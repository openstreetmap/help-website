Tag line of floating buoys near weir or dam

3

What is the preferred way to tag a line of floating buoys such as near a weir or dam to keep boat traffic away. On rivers these are often polystyrene or plastic and anchored or linked together. Should the way marking the line of buoys be tagged barrier=buoy or barrier=boom or other.
