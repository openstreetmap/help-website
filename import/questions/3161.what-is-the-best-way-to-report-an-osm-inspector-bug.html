It looks as though your addition to the wishlist section on the wiki was only made a few hours ago. You might only have to wait a few days to see if you get a response.

In addition, the OSM Inspector page on the wiki suggests that you post questions and comments about OSM Inspector to talk@, if you don't wish to join the OSM Inspector mailing list

But really, "just give it a few days" is probably enough to get an answer if you have made a detailed bug report.
