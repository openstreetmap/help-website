How to tag a haystack with a hexagon roof ?

0

I came across several farmyards with hexagon haystags. I tagged them, "building - just a roof and named it haystack. The roof is at 3 or 6 poles up or downwards adjustable so without hay, its just a empty roof. Has anyone another idea or suggestion ? There nothing in the WIKI about haystacks AFAIK. Take a look here, https://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=52.129844&lon=4.973271&zoom=18&layers=M Greetz and Best Wishes Hendrik

Building=roof: Sounds good

The name-tag is only for official names, you might want to include the haystack as a note or description instead (note= / description=)
