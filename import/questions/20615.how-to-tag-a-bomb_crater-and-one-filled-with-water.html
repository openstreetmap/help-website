How to tag a bomb_crater and one filled with water ?

0

I came across an old airfield, used during 1942 - 1945, some historic buildings, traces of runways and several craters made by falling bombs. The craters were made during 2 raids and are specific pointed at during guided tours since theres not much else left to see. I did not find any trace of such in the Wiki. Should I use key and value, military - bomb_crater and if filled with water ad natural - water as well ?

The water part of the question is easy - yes, if it contains water tag it natural=water, see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:natural%3Dwater .

I would tag the bomb craters as historic=bomb_crater
