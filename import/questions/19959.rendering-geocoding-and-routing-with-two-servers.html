2

Hi guys,

At the company where I work, after a year of having a tile server running Mapnik with excellent results, we want to add geocoding and routing services. For this purpose, we have two servers available.

I was trying to find if it was possible to use the same database for Mapnik and Nominatim but apparently that's not the path to follow even though it might be a way to do it.
