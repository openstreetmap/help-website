  1. Rivers should be tagged with a way tagged with waterway=river in the centre to show the flow of the water and optionaly a way or multipolygon outlining the river tagged with waterway=riverbank. See the wiki for documentation.
  2. Coastlines are not updated as frequently as the other features as it requires a lot of resources to find out what is sea and what is land.
