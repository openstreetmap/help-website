For mapping highway destinations/exits see:

  • https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:destination
  • https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dmotorway_junction
  • As always: look at existing highway objects at other places (well mapped places) to see how other mappers handled this situation there.

Traffic signs themselves (as additional node objects) in general (I could not find those signs there): https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:traffic_sign
