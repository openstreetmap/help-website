I have tried GraphHopper and OSRM ( car mode) they are the two routing systems available on the map page and they both seem to work correctly to me. I suspect the mapping data on your GPS device is incorrect, can you install an update? The data on your device may not get updated to what is on OSMs database that quickly. This is what one router does when routing through your linked point. https://www.openstreetmap.org/directions?engine=graphhopper_car&route=-34.7610%2C138.6742%3B-34.7982%2C138.6416#map=13/-34.7778/138.6811 From your comment below changing OSM data won't fix your device's routing until that device has that new data installed, be aware that there is always a delay in updates being used in the map data incorporated into data assembled for an update. So what is the device, the app and does the device use OSM data, and if so how old is the data? and where did/will you download it from.
