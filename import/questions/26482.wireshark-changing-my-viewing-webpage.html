The fact that a page shows up differently on a PC than it does on your phone is completely normal. Many sites send a totally different page to a phone so that it can be properly formatted to the smaller screen.

As to why the competitor's name is showing up in the PC version, I can't say. I have far too little information to state that it has been hacked or anything else.

I am fairly certain it is not a Wireshark problem - Wireshark is showing you what it receives from the site. The fact that you are getting a different version on your phone means nothing.
