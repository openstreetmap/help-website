See Lacking_proper_attribution (you need a new account for the wiki if you want to add it to the page). However, quite easy what to do now, just continue your emailing - mail mapometer. They even use the osm.org tile server (which is somehow okay, and somehow not). However, nice that they offer OSM! Use a positive tone in your email (e.g. mention this).

And, yes, I cannot see a attribution, too (just "© Mapometer Ltd 2013." and "using Google maps"). "OpenSM" is a quite uncommon abbreviation which should either be OSM or OpenStreetMap, but that is unrelated to the license issue.
