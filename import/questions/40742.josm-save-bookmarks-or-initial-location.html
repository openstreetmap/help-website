For the problem with position changing after download there was a ticket about it but I do not find it, atm.

As workaround you can still select last selection through the selection history in the selection list (small arrow within "select"-button) or use the utilsplugin2 which even offers a shortcut for last selection. Then you can "zoom on selection" again.
