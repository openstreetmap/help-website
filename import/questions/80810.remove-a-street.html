Hi Micol,

InsertUser has described what needs to be done. To actually do that please locate your property on www.openstreetmap.org and press the edit button in the top menu. You might have to create a user account first. The editor opens a short tutorial that shows you how you can edit the road in question.

If you do not feel like doing it yourself you can also leave a map note at the location. Press the add note button on the right hand side (little speech balloon with a plus sign) and move it to the right location. Please describe exactly what needs to be changed. Be aware that it may take some time until one of the volunteer mappers reacts on the note.
