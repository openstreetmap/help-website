If you can't wait until the issue is fixed, there is quick and dirty solution. Just add referer into http request:

tms[3,17]:http://{switch:a,b,c}-globalheat.strava.com/tiles/both/color7/{zoom}/{x}/{y}.png?v=6{header(Referer,strava.com)}

You can find more about TMS options at https://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Maps in section Tile Map Services (TMS)
