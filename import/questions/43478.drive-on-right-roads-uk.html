I didn't set it up. I found the driving_side page on the wiki https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:driving_side then clicked the "uk" link under "more tools for this tag" in the box on the right and noticed there was an overpass turbo link. I suspect the tag has been added deliberately and in those instances you end up driving on the right because of circumstances linked to one way systems.
