For me http://www.maposmatic.org/ shows a red box on the left side mentioning "The GIS database is not available. Renderings cannot be processed at this time." Which means that the service is not working currently, you only can get into the rendering queue. I guess you could advance until the step " Waiting for rendering to begin... ", right?

There are many alternatives.
