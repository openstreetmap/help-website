Merging platform area and railway station point for light rails?

0

When using Osmand to look if a public transport platform ist buggy/wheelchair friendly you get mostly 2 points.

  1. for the platform Area (which is dominant on rendering and selction)
  2. the POI for the station/halt

Mostly both points contain different information, sometimes even conflicting. After consulting the wiki I am not sure if merging the POI into the area for these small stations (1 platform, 2 rails, no switch) is the right fix.

Example:

  • https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit#map=18/52.43681/13.23303
  • https://wheelmap.org/map#/?lat=52.4368959721313&lon=13.233051002025604&zoom=19 (a Desktop example)

What is the right fix for this? I would suggest merging the POI into the area of the platform. Using 2 points is very counter intuitive.
