Register on OpenStreetMap if you haven't already: https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new

If you have a username, log in.

Find the place of your business, and click Edit.

If your business is represented by a dot, just click it and move to the place where you want it to be.

If your business is on a building, remove the tags of your business on that building, and add them to a building where your business is now.

Click Save two times.
