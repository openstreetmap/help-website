Look again - most are SVG. If you want the same projection as OSM uses, limit your search to those with "mercator" in the name/description. www.naturalearthdata.com is a more sophisticated option but you'll need to use a GIS software like QGIS or ArcGIS to make a PDF/SVG from the shapes you can download there.
