It is your decision. You may look at the taginfo numbers to see what others have chosen.

I have used the generic pitch with the sport specification for the tables I've mapped. The interative refinement of this tag combination seems to be useful to me. E.g. a map could display sport pitches regardless of all the sport types just by filtering for leisure=pitch. One example.
