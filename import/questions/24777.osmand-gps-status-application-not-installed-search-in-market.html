Despite the name, OsmAnd is not officially part of OpenStreeetMap. OsmAnd does use maps based on OpenStreetMap, but it is a separate project. So it may be more useful to check the guides on the OsmAnd website, or ask on their forum.

Anyway, to answer your question: For the "Show GPS Status" option to work you need to install the GPS Status and Toolbox app. This is a separate app, available to download for free on Google Play. Then when you choose the Show GPS Status option in OsmAnd, it will show this app.
