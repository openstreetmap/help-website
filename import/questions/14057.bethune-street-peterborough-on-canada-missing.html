Hi karate,

Welcome to OSM. The answer to your query is that the way is tagged as "highway=path" rather that "highway=residential", as the northern section of Bethune Street is.

As you've not started contributing to the mapping as yet I have changed the tagging to "highway=residential", hopefully in a short while the rendering will be correct.

Regards Bernard
