At the price of giving a slightly unpopular answer, the shortest version is likely

https://www.openstreetmap.org/welcome

plus

the intro for the iD editor (the default editor on openstreetmap.org).

This is far from the whole story naturally. The two sources given in the first answer for example will explain more, but you should be aware that they tend to be mired in rather old tech (at least learnosm.org has historically not been geared towards an audience in developed countries). For example by far the easiiest way to collect data today is to use a mobile phone, in its simplest form simply by taking georeferenced photographs.
