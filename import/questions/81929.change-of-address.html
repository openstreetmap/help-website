You will need to contact Google and Instagram to change that.
If you want to add your address to the OpenStreetMap.org you will need to sign in and do the introductory to mapping https://learnosm.org/en/
You should then be able to draw the shape of you house and add tags for housenumber, street, suburb, etc.

If you are listing your business:
Using something like https://osmybiz.osm.ch/#/9/-27.49122/152.67700 or https://www.onosm.org/# to initially help adding your business listing at the building you operate from in to the OSM with suitable tags may assist.
