Mueschel has replied to your question on the key:socket discussion page: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Talk:Key:socket#socket:gb.2Ft_20234

TL/DR: yes, but only use alpha-numeric characters, don't use the /. And keep the code as simple as possible (which might be simpler than in 'real life')
