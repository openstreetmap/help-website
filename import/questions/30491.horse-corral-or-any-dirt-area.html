I'm afraid something has got lost in translation. A paddock is nearly always a grassy area for horses in the UK. We have many hectares locally (https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=16/51.5306/-0.7697), which have been mapped and labelled as such (somewhat naughtily using addr:housenumber). It's really hard as a native speaker to tag something which is totally counter to ones common sense. The image on the wiki labelled paddock is an exercise area or arena. See this page for UK use of terms: http://www.nationalhorseandponynetwork.co.uk/stablepaddockyard.html
