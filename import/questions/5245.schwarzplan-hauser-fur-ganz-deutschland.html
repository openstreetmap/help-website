For a map of Germany with only all buildings you should have first a look at another topic here dealing with rendering such big maps.

When you only want to render a smaller area I recommend Maperitive .. there you can edit the style file only to render buildings. Have a look there at "Sample Renderings"

(When you want to dig deeper in that topic in German language there is also the OSM forum with a german sub section. Log in there with your OSM account.
