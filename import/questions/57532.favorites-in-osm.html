openstreetmap.org itself is primarily designed for contributors to the project and is not intended as a gmaps replacement. You can however use umap.openstreetmap.fr or any other umap instance to create a private "map".

Yes - every time you browse to a location in OSM the URL changes. Just add wherever you are as a browser bookmark, and if you want share browser bookmarks between devices by using a browser that allows you to sign in to it and carry your bookmarks with you
