I suspect many of the new surfaces we see are concreate based with chips of attractive stone added into the surface. The surface is then ground and polished, Often encountered in supermarkets and new public buildings. See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granolithic perhaps Granolithic is the tag we should use. Previously used tags can be searched for and found here. https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=+surface+%3Dconcrete but it seems a little sad to just call it concreate.
