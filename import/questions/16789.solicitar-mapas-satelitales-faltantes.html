As of 22/Dec/2012, there are good quality aerial images of the two locations listed in the original question. Someone already traced details for Cocula, but not Ameca yet.

AFAIK, Bing allows for the use of all satellite images, but for some places, simply there is no such imagery. I work on maps mostly for Brazil, and I noticed that the quality of the images have been steadily improving in the last months.

I would advise to re-check problem areas every few weeks when good quality imagery is not available, or to use other sources of data. Not Google, of course...
