Why my editions are visible only when the zoom is 16 or less?

0

Hi, I have a problem with my work on OSM. Some of my edits can only be seen when the zoom is 16, but not closer. Why is this happening?? You can see the problem following the links:

https://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=4.60133&lon=-74.06543&zoom=16&layers=M

https://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=4.60133&lon=-74.06543&zoom=17&layers=M

Note how the "universidad de los andes" appears and disappears. This is the link to the change set: https://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/changeset/12687084

If you've just uploaded your edits, it can be the case that not all tiles for the different zoom levels are already rendered. You can right-click on a tile to open it in a seperate tab, append /dirty and thus force it to rerender, though.
