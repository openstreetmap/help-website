I use myOpenID for login in OpenStreetMap, can I change the authentication method? I wouldn't lose my profile

Login to http://www.openstreetmap.org, click on your username on upper right corner and choose "My settings". There you can select new OpenID (created first at another provider, of course) and/or change your email/password which you can use for logging in. You need to do that ASAP while your myOpenID still works, of course.
