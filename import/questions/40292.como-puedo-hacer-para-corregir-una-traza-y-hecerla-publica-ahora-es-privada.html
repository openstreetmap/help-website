Hi, If you do mean gps traces, then:

  • Open the page of your gps traces:http://www.openstreetmap.org/user/La%20Geogu%C3%ADa/traces.
  • Choose the relevant trace by clicking on the filename of that race.
  • Two options: Left button (Change the track) to modify the visibility of trace or the right button to delete it.
