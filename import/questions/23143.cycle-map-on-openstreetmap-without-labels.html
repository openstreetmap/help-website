You can visit www.opencyclemap.org and ask any contact person named in the docs there whether your aim is possible without to much effort.

Or you can try to render map tiles similar to cyclemap.org on your own. Do a search on this FAQ site with keywords like "render own map style" or similar. There are many topics here, and many possibilities to do so.
