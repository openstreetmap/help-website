Currently the maximum zoomlevel seems to be 22. You need to ask the operator of that service if they could implement this. Osmbuildings.org ist not the same as openstreetmap.org - they just use our data to provide their service/map.

Note that there are other OSM-based services for 3d maps: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/List_of_OSM-based_services#3D . Maybe you find something more suitable, or build your own (nearly all offer free materials – this is "free" as in freedom).
