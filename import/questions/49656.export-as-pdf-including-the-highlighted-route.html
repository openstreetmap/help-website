I'd start by going direct to the routing service that's giving you the route in question. The OSM homepage gives access to a number of routing services - but you don't need to use the homepage to access these. This article might help if you don't immediately get what I mean: https://blog.openstreetmap.org/2015/02/16/routing-on-openstreetmap-org/
