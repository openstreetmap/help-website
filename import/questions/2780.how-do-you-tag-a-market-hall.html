How do you tag a market hall?

1

I would like to know how I should tag a market hall.

For examples of market halls please see:

Central Sofia Market Hall

Great Market Hall (Budapest)

La Boqueria (Barcelona)

Commons Category:Market halls

You can tag that as amenity=marketplace + building=yes.

You can also tag the name and the opening hours etc. See amenity=marketplace
