There simply are no bars and pubs entered in our data for this city (see overpass turbo query). Instead try airport near Moses Lake, Grant County, Washington – that gives you a result. Or try bar near Frankfurt am Main – many results.

I hope you help to fill this gap if/when you are there to make a survey! Just adding your favourite bar would be a great start!
