Do you want to easily find out tile numbers in your web browser?

  1. Just go to http://mc.bbbike.org/mc/?lon=51.5273&lat=25.2806&zoom=14&num=1&mt0=mapnik (or any other slippy map with OpenLayers) (on the OSM homepage this does not work anymore),
  2. navigate to your desired location,
  3. right click the desired tile on the map,
  4. select the first entry "show image"(Firefox)/"open image in new tab"(Chromium),
  5. then just look at your browser's address/URL bar which will show e.g. https://a.tile.openstreetmap.org/14/10537/7001.png .
  6. In this address you find the tile numbers (14/10537/7001) in a format which you can paste directly into the JOSM download window.

Other interesting tools are at https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Slippy_map_tilenames#Tools
