nüwi 255W cannot find the housenumbers of many adresses in the garmin.osm.nl map although they are existing on the OSM.org map

0

Hello, I downloaded this week a map of Austria from http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/ and installed it on my Garmin-Navigator nüwi 255W. When I tried it out, it became clear, that the navigator couldn´t find the housenumbers of many adresses. I looked it up on the map of openstreetmap.org and saw, that in THIS map all the housenumbers were ok?

Here´s an example screenshot:

If I search for "Schüttelstraße 27" (which is right of the river), my Garmin nüwi 255W will only find the "Schüttelstraße" as a whole and will send me to the beginning, although you can see, that openstreetmap.org knows the housenumbers of this street.
