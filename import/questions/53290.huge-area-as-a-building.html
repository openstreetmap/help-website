Huge area as a building

0

Some large areas (several kmxkm size) are uploaded/tagged as buildings. Some layers show these areas as buildings like here: http://osm.org/go/tzdfL~Rr--?layers=N and some as water like here: http://osm.org/go/tzdfL~Q?layers=CN . Obviously, none of these are correct.
So, my question is what are these areas and which tag(s) should be used?
Thanks!

Since these are obviously not buildings, I would remove the building tag.

As to what these are, the area to the east (Tambak Garam) seems to be salt ponds, which would be tagged landuse=salt_pond

The areas to the west (such as way 244163704) are tagged farmland:type_idn=sawah, and sawah is rice paddy. Tagging for these seems to be landuse=farmland, crop=rice.

As Hendrikklaas said, it would be good to contact the mapper too (or make a comment on the changeset) as locals might have more accurate or detailed information than we could gather remotely.
