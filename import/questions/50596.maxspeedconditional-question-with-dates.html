maxspeed:conditional question with dates

0

Hello! I've been quite a while in the community but I have a question for you, since I don't like making mistakes obviously.

There is an area in my neighbourhood that has the following terms according to the signs: * Between Aug 20-Jun 15, the speed is reduced during the following times: ** (Mo-Fr 07:00-09:00)

I'm not entirely sure what to do.

maxspeed conditional highway

The is (as things go in the realm of opening hours and conditional restrictions) quite simple to enter:

maxspeed:conditional= nn @ (Aug 20-Jun 15 Mo-FR 07:00-09:00)

with nn the target maximum speed.
