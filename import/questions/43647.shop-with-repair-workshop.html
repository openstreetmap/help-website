For bicycle shops there is the option of adding additional tags like;

service:bicycle:repair=yes

service:bicycle:retail=yes

from: http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:shop%3Dbicycle#Additional_keys

Would something similar be suitable for musical instrument shops that offer repair service?
