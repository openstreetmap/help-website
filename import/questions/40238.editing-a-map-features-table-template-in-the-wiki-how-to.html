There is a link to help you with editing just below the long table …

  1. follow it (then you are at the template's page)
  2. click "edit" (at top of the page – those on the page or from the template's docu)
  3. add your entry (example) (alphabetic ordering seems to be useful)
  4. use the preview (since editing tables easily leads to errors because of their syntax)
  5. save

General help about editing templates is there: Help:Template (quick version). And there about tables.
