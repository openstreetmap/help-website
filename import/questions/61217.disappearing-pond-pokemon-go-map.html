Hi, the pond is OK in the OpenStreetMap database from which I think your PokeMon map is derived. It won't rendered on your map until your map is updated. So you'll have to be patient for a while.

On looking at the pond it seems to have been drawn much larger than the Bing image suggests it should be, literally touching one building. I would also question why the road name was changed from Presidents Lane to Presidents pond, was that inadvertent and should it be changed back? The property address on the road are for Presidents Lane.
