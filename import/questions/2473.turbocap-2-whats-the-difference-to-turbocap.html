You and Chris are correct. The original 2-port card was named TurboCap. When the 4-port card came out we had to change its name to TurboCap 2 to distinguish it from TurboCap 4.

I tend to duck my head and keep coding when pricing is discussed so you'll have to ask the sales team about that.
