There are no "official OSM experts", since this is a (mostly) volunteer organization.

However, there are several ways to get in touch with people who know about OSM:

  • Contact a local user group, there may be someone who can help. A map of user groups is available.
  • Ask on one of the local mailing lists
  • There are many companies who offer services around OSM. They may be available for consulting (of course this will usually not be free).
  • Finally: Ask your (concrete) question here on help.openstreetmap.org. Maybe someone can help.
