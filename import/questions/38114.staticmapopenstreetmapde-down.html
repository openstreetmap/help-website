The server has been reinstalled after a crash and it is unclear how long it will take to re-install all services, or indeed who is/was responsible for running them in the first place, see also https://lists.openstreetmap.org/pipermail/talk-de/2014-October/109675.html . The machine in question, gauss.openstreetmap.de, was a development server and not a production machine, and many of the services had been installed by someone and then more or less forgotten …
