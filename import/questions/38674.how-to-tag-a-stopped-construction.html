The bulidings had been built but they are unfinished. No painting at all and most parts of it doesn't have insulation just bare walls or just the supporting structure. The area is ingrown with weed and only the guard lives there.

I also thought of "abandoned:". In fact this one: abandoned:building=construction (5 times in taginfo) (since it was a building under construction when which was abandoned – matters for the look if it was finished or not, IMHO). And construction=house/apartments/… to specify the building type which was in construction.

The whole area was landuse=construction before, so, now, it should be abandoned:landuse=construction (9 times in taginfo), shouldn't it?
