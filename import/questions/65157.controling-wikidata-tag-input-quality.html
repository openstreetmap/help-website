We don't have many bots in OSM and we prefer to keep bad data from getting into OSM rather than writing bots to fix it ;) the edit you are referring to has been made with the ID editor. It would be good to open an issue at https://github.com/openstreetmap/iD and recommend adding a check to the editor.
