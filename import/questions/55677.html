Why are the bays, straits, coves created as a "points" in the sea? After all, it is possible to cut off the a piece from the great sea multipoligon and give it a name. Then through the communication-relation "outer" add to the multipoligon the sea.

Creating such polygons will protect you from creating duplicate points of such objects. Mapnik will make the rendering in map after a month and a half. Well, God be with him. The main thing that will make the map beautiful
