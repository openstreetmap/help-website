Tagging mode of transport that was interesting to me, e.g. MTB, bike, car, walking

Place names are very popular to tag, but you really need to add lots of tags for that to be interesting e.g. the suburb, the city, the county, the state, country. (to be extreme)

Tagging it nice because it's easy to search your traces, but as you may have seen it's not use that much.

The tags on traces are simple single-word tags which mean you can tell where and how those traces were gathered. You can put any words you like in as tags, but useful things to use could include the names of any settlements they cover, which route numbers you travelled along, which mode of transport you were using, and so on.

You can then filter your own and others' traces by tag at openstreetmap.org
