It's been renamed/moved to another menu option. Use "File" -> "Export Specified Packets".

I.e., "saving" means saving the entire capture, including what changes you've made (adding frame comments, editing frame comments, deleting frame comments).

For writing some of the packets to another file, use "Export Specified Packets".
