Many things simply don't get rendered. They're either not important enough or haven't been noticed yet.

As an example, radio towers are not rendered on the OSM main page because there are so many of them they clutter the map if displayed.

While a floating barrier is important it's also the type of object that will need a push to get the rendering folks to look at implementing it as a project.

Cheers, Dave
