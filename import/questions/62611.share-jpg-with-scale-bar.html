Hopefully this explains how. Click on third icon up ( green in pic ) choose jpeg format, then drag the outline to include the scale bar. Click the link near the top and save it for emailing. Or on a win 10 PC use the screen snip tool, and save as a jpeg and email it as an attachment. For example here is a short link with a marker of a car park for a start point of a walk that a group could use as the meeting point. https://osm.org/go/eu6_3vj4?m=
