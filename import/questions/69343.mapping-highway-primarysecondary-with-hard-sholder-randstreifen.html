How are mapped hard shoulders of highway=primary/secondary. No signs. The "Randstreifen" is 1 meter wide. I have a foto, but how upload?

There is a key to attach to the highways: shoulder=xyz. Have a look at that page for examples. You could tag something along:
highway=primary
shoulder=both
shoulder:width=1
shoulder:surface=asphalt
