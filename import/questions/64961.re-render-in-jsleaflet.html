I guess you mean the default map on https://www.openstreetmap.org/ ...

https://www.openstreetmap.org/ uses Leaflet since November 2012. Leaflet also uses tiles. However, you cannot (since Sept 2016) simply do a right click on a tile to get its address. Please see the older question "requesting-re-render-of-a-tile" for workarounds.

Note that due to a current server move updates may be slower than usual.
