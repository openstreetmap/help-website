OSM screenshot in scientific publication?

3

Hi, I'm a phd student at a public university.

Am I allowed to include one graphic based on a OSM screenshot in a scientific publication - of course mentioning it as being based on OSM material?

Usually all conference papers are released together in printed conference proceedings by a scientific publisher. So I doubt I could formally put my single entire article under any license since it undergoes some standard handling by the scientific publisher...
