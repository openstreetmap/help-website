I do not know of any " management system ". It would be best to edit our own traces before upload but unless we drop them over a map to see if they make sense first it would be hard to know what to cut out. We can cut the start and ends, the "birds nest" tangles and maybe some phantom plots where the a trace jumps an unreasonable distance. While someone could program this I doubt it is worth the work involved as it probably would not be that successful. Use your own judgement to average and plot a way.
