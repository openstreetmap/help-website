At least http://opengts.sourceforge.net/ likely does what you want and supports an OSM based map (naturally you should add attribution to the map if you are building a public service).

There naturally may be other solutions, please have a look at https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/tracking/ , http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Software and naturally do a search with google.
