Yes, there's been a spam problem on the help site. The spam messages have been removed but since emails are sent immediately, we can't do anything about that (you probably wouldn't approve of us deleting messages from your inbox even if we could ;)

In the footer of any of these notification emails, there's a link that says "P.S. You can always fine-tune which notifications you receive here." -- Click on that and you can unsubscribe from the new question notification.
