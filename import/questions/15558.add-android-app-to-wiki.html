I'd like to add my app (GPS Essentials) to the list of supporting apps here: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Android Is there any information available on how to do this?

First you should create a wiki page for your app. Then you have to add Template:Software2 to that page, filling in the appropriate details for your app. A bot will read the details in the template, and automatically add it to the Android wiki page, probably within a day or so.
