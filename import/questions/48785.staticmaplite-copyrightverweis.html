No, the integrated notice is probably not sufficient. The Legal FAQ says:

3a. I would like to use OpenStreetMap maps. How should I credit you?

What credit to use

Our requested attribution is "© OpenStreetMap contributors". You must also make it clear that the data is available under the Open Database Licence. This can be achieved by providing a "License" or "Terms" link which links to www.openstreetmap.org/copyright or www.opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl.

The rest of the FAQ gives more details about the attribution. You do not necessarily need a clickable link (though that would be nice), but you need the text "© OpenStreetMap contributors", and you need to inform users about the license, typically either by linking to it, or by naming it.
