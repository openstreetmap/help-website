Export a route relation as GPX in the sorted order

0

Hi I've a circular route relation & want to export it as GPX so that the ways (<trkseg>) are in clockwise order.

I checked in JOSM that the relation's ways are sorted, but JOSM's 'export to GPX' & 'saveas GPX' jumbles up the order for some reason. How can this be be solved in JOSM or using another program.

josm export gpx relations

This might be worth a try...
Open a new layer in Josm, then select the route and ‘merge selected’ into the new layer. Then select all the route segments and ‘edit > combine’ and try exporting the combined route to gpx. Be careful not to upload to osm on exiting.

Also some alternative ideas in this query...
