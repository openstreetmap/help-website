I had a look at the data. I found a lot of connected waysegments with natural=water enclosing the bay. As far as I know such a thing would not be rendered as water (contrary to natural=coastline). Interesting there should have been water rendered at all.

I have been so bold and put these segments into a multipolygon and added all the inner natural=land to it, too.

Still there is some work to do. Some of the ways are not too exact and one long way in that region misses tags at all. And several islands are not mapped, but doing that will be quite some work.
Having mentioned that, the rest of the region looks good to me as far as I looked. :)
