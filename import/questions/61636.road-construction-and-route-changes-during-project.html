The decision whether to keep up with construction updates is subjective, and depends on what access you have. Anything that can be verified is fine, even though it would require restricted access to the construction site (via a worker or special permission) or a refreshed view on OpenAerialMap. Locally I have subscribed to a daily work notice for an interchange construction zone and when I see announcements of a changed exit or slip road, I drive to acquire a new GPS trace and update accordingly, but haven't sketched any highway=construction.

Map data consumers have their own update rate and the changes will appear later.
