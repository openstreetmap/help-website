How do I merge two identical accounts

1

Somehow I seem to have ended up with two OpenStreetMap accounts: "davefiddes" and "David Fiddes". Both are tied to my email address but with different cases. I have no recollection of setting these accounts up. Some of my edits are on one account and the rest on the other.

Is there any way to merge these two accounts?

Short answer: no.

You can easily write in the description of the accounts that you are also known by the other account. There is no harm in having several accounts and some people prefer having several accounts to keep diferent edits on diferent accounts.
