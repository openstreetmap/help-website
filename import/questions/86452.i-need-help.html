Hi, As commented above they probably are notes which are meant to ask folk to have a look at a potential problem, and hopefully resolve the problem. The notes can be turned off to avoid clutter if you only want to just peruse the map. On the right-hand side of the map select the layer icon, and from the drop-down untick the notes box.
