You have two alternatives.

First one is to upload to our wiki:

  1. Log in
  2. click "Upload file" in the left menu - or use this link: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Special:Upload
  3. read the info
  4. fill in the form
  5. upload

If you cannot see "Upload file", then your wiki account is too new. It must be at least 4 days old and have at least 10 edits.

Second alternative is to upload to Wikimedia Commons (needs an account there). You can use the image in our wiki pages like if it was uploaded in our wiki directly - see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Collaboration_with_Wikipedia#Using_pictures_from_Wikipedia_for_tag_description.2FMap_Features for more info.
