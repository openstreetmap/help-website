To login at the forum:

  1. goto http://forum.openstreetmap.org/login.php
  2. enter your Openstreetmap user name and password

This works for me, the username is case sensitive, which is good to keep in mind.

You should also clear the password database for the site, so it doesn't autofill your username/password.
