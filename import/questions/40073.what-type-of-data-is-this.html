well, by looking at the text export of the data, I can literally see a bad slice. The reason could be a grip that's too soft. Furthermore the launch angle is a bit low with 17 degree. I guess you are hitting the ball to early. A better stand and a better alignment to the ball could help to optimize the launch angle.

Seriously, there is no easy way to decipher the communication as that's a proprietary binary protocol. With a lot of reverse engineering (especially by looking at the iOS app internals), one could probably figure out how their protocol works, but that's gonna cost a lot of time and/or money. I guess it's much easier to contact the vendor and simply ask for that information and/or ask them to add data export features into their tool.
