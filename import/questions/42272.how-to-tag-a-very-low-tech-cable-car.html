how to tag a very low tech cable car

0

Where I'm travelling, it's quite common to have pedestrian crossings over a river with low tech cable cars. Take two steel cables, make a wooden cart and hang them underneath. These are usually tagged as cable cars, like here: https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/333694395

However, since this tag is usually used for quite a different kind of structure (see the wiki) it might give people the wrong impression, and get rendered on low zoomlevels or a dedicated skiing map.

Any suggestions for a better tag?
