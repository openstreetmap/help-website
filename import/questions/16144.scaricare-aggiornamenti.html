I'd like to know how much time will pass before I can download an update to my mobile phone after the correction of a map.

This depends on the organization that provided you with the data for your mobile phone. Look at the information on the site where you downloaded it, or ask the provider if you bought it - there is no general answer.

And if possible I'd like to know which is the best way to update the mobile.

That is a question about how your mobile phone and/or the map software on it works, not a question about OpenStreetMap, so is off-topic. Please contact the appropriate forum / support hotline etc. for the map software you use on your phone.
