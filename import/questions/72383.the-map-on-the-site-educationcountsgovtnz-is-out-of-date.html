The map on the site educationcounts.govt.nz is out of date

0

The map we have on https://www.educationcounts.govt.nz/ is missing new sub divisions, and doesn't show house numbers:

https://www.educationcounts.govt.nz/find-an-els/els/profile-and-contact-details?region=2&district=7620&area=523920&ece=47764

But on the OpenStreetMaps site, it does show new sub divisions and has house numbers:

https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=18/-37.03841/174.90679

Can you please advise how to go about having up to date maps with house numbers?

The mapping on your site is served by Mapbox, who take OpenStreetMap data and resell it. You should contact them and ask them when they'll use the latest OSM data.
