How to make iD show several GPS tracks?

2

I know how to make iD show a GPS track.

But how to make iD (or any other OSM editor, but preferably iD) show several GPS tracks?

Why? Smartphone GPS sometimes get way off, making the GPS track unreliable. But since I carry two phones (in different pockets, with time perfectly synchronized), by seing the two tracks I would be able to see what parts are reliable (both tracks are close) and what parts are unreliable (tracks differ).
