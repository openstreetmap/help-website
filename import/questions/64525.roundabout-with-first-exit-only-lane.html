We currently likely don't have a way of accurately modelling this situation in OSM.

I suspect the best way to get a roughly working solution is to map the dual lane sections of both the roundabout and the streets leading up to them with two lanes and make the right-hand lane with "right"

So in the first example that would be

turn:lanes=none|right on the road leading up to the roundabout (ignoring the other side of the road for now)

turn:lanes=none;right|right on the section of the roundabout it merges in to.

YMMV
