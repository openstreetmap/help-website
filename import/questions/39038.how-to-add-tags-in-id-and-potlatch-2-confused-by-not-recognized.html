How to add tags in iD and Potlatch 2? Confused by "not recognized"

0

I have found several items with regard to charging stations for EV or E-bike. Most with a link to https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dcharging_station (Link to Wiki)

However I don't see a possibility to use these tags as such in iD and even in Potlatch 2 I get the warning that the tag is not recognised/UNKNOWN.

  • Is the wiki out of date?
  • Is it only for a different editing tool?
  • How do I go about with either iD or Potlatch?

You can use "tags" in both, iD and Potlatch. See the last section of https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tags .

Regarding "Is the wiki out of date?" and "not recognised/UNKNOWN": see the other question tags discrepancy: map features vs. editor vs. QA tools (OSM Inspector). Then it should be clear to you. In short: likely you will want just to use the tags even if the editor says it is "unknown" because it is just the editor saying this.

In iD, just press the "+" under the all tags. section. You might have to click the little triangle first to expand that section. There are so many possible tags in OSM, that the editors only offer a selection, typically the most popular ones. As those charging stations become more important, I expect that the editors will include it in the near future.
