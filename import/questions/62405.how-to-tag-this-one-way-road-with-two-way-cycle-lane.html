Example T3 also suggests to add oneway=no to the cycleway. Alternatively don't draw a separate way, instead add cycleway:left=track + oneway:bicycle=no (or cycleway:left:oneway=no?) to your highway.

When there's two different mapping/tagging approaches to a situation, I normally look around the local area to see which one is the most commonly used. However both methods (separate ways and single way) are used almost equally, but I think I'll just map it as one way for now.

  • highway=residential
  • oneway=yes
  • sidewalk=both
  • cycleway:left=track
  • oneway:bicycle=no
