I presume you mean this changeset : http://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/23861924.

The best way is to use JOSM and the revert changeset option. If you are not familiar with JOSM it is best to ask someone to do this for you. It is fairly simple once one is familiar with the process, but lack of familiarity could make things worse.

There are a number of other questions of a similar kind on this site.
