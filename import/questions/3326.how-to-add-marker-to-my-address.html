An answer to your orginal question (in the title) is there :

The simplest way to get a marker is to click 'permalink', then modify lat and lon to mlat and mlon respectively. If you want the marker to appear in a specific position without knowing the exact coordinates, simply double-click a point on the map. The map will be zoomed in to that point and the point will be centered.

The content of your question seems to not concern OSM.
