Hi.

Please share the exact process and the error messages.

But are your gpx files from a personal GPS recording session ? Then please upload them following this guide. Otherwise you probably don't have to right to upload them (license issue), and you can't be sure they are correct.

Then you need to add all the paths and tracks the trail goes on, either from aerial imagery or on-the-ground knowledge. And add these to the relevant route=hiking relation.

You might want to talk with your local community, and mappers you already edited this trail, to see what's possible.
