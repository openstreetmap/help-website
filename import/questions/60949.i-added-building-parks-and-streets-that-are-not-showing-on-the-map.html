Hi, you're welcome! Have a look a this node tags https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/5161537961 , there is still a leisure=park tag on it, it means that if I look at the map I do still see two parks https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/543793633 and https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/5161537961 . Open the map editor you're using, select that skatepark node, scroll down and remove that leisure_1 tag, then replace the leisure=park with leisure=pitch, that would transform the park into a pitch.

Is this one the road you're referring to? https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/544139640 If this thing is just a road used to access a parking area you should tag it as a highway=service https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dservice and then create an area to tag as amenity=parking, in order to point out that this area is a parking, just like that www.openstreetmap.org/way/213844285 .

If this one is the building you're referring to https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/5161537963 , it doesn't appear on the map because it is mapped as a node, if you want to make it appear you have to draw it as an area and then you can merge that area with that node or just move that node on the building.

Hi, the building appeared on the map but it looks like it has a strange shape. Most of the times buildings have a square shape (90 degrees angles), if you think this one makes no exception you should square it off (right click on it and you'll find the square button).

Parking lot looks good.

The park needs still some work instead. Seems like you created a relation https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/7779517 but relations shouldn't be used in cases like this one. Relation are used when two or more objects of the map are someway related, it may be the case of a hundred of road sections that together creates a bus route https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/3785488 or it may be the case of a building with "holes" in its shape https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/6648729. Here you can find out more about relations https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Relation

Right now I think you should delete that park relation, merge this two lines https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/543793633 and https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/544394128, then tag that area as a leisure=park, give it the S.Simao Park name and disconnect it from the nearby road
