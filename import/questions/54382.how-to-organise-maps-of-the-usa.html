I guess you were downloading from http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/ .

Did you see that you can custom-select areas on garmin.openstreetmap.nl ("Enable manual tile selection")? That may help to reduce the file size and/or split the desired area into slices which fit on one of your SD cards each.

There are many other OSM-based Garmin maps - see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download . Some may show less detail and are smaller that way.+

Third option: as far as I know (but I newer used Mapsource myself) you can select map areas to be transferred in mapsource, too.
