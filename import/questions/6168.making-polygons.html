If I understand you right you want to match polygons from a list of city boundaries with OSM data to get the names of the boundaries. You could do this with a postgresql database and spatial functions. There are different tools available to get OSM data into a postgres db, search the wiki for osmosis, osm2pgsql, imposm and postgresql.
