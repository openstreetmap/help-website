how do you tag the happy hour of a pub, bar, restaurant, etc?

1

Dear all,

I have seen that Google Maps now provides information on the happy hours of bars, pubs, and restaurants. I think this is very important information along-side the opening hours.

So how do you add information on the happy hour of a pub, bar, restaurant?

The tag happy_hours=* is in use and documented for this purpose: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:happy_hours

Same syntax as opening_hours, eg "happy_hours=Mo-Fr 17:00-20:00".
