If the information is showing up correctly on OpenStreetMap.org then there isn't much the OSM community can do.

Map apps only update their data periodically. If the download maps screen shows that you have the latest version then you will have to wait until their next update. I'm not sure how often Organic Maps aims to update. Their site lists a Russian telegram group that might be a go place to ask.
