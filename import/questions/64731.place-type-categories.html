OSM allows freeform tags, so there is no "complete" list - people can just enter what they like. That said, the wiki "Map Features" page (as suggested by Mateusz) will cover most of what you're interested in. If you want to see how many of a particular item there are just use taginfo to search for whatever it is you're interested in.
