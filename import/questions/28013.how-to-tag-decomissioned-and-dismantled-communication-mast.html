Hi are the transceivers removed ? Is the object (mast) still standing ? Is the foundation present and not demolished ? Consider adding these tags, man_made=tower, tower:disused=transceiver / GSM station, material=wood/steel and tag the foundation building=yes, material=concrete. If the tower is no longer present, foundation:dismantled=GSM tower. We / OSM are tagging what is there, even an underground / covered (pipe) / lines are added.
