I think the postcodes for The Netherlands are imported into Nominatim and are not part of the OSM data, see http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128717184 . This means that the data cannot be updated by a mapper. You have to contact the Nominatim maintainers.

If you compare this with a Belgian postcode, see http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128098718 , you will notice that there is a boundary relation with a clickable link.
