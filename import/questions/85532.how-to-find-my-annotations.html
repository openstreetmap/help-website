OpenStreetMap as a project consists of several different ones - there's the map data at openstreetmap.org, and changesets, changeset discussions, diary entries, diary entry comments and private messages there, but also there is wiki.openstreetmap.org, community.openstreetmap.org, help.openstreetmap.org, forum.openstreetmap.org and many others not run by OSMF directly (e.g. various Discord / Slack / Telegram instances, everything created by HOT et al, etc.)
