Why does `highway=track` render more promenently than `highway=service`?

0

In my understanding of highway=track and highway=service, I would describe track as more minor. And yet, in the default rendering, tracks render at zoom 13, and service roads do not. Was this an intentional decision?

Tracks would tend to occur in less dense areas, like farm fields, forests, etc., whereas service roads would tend to be in more dense suburban and urban areas (in general). I suspect the Standard OSM Carto rendering takes this into account and renders the tracks one level earlier because there's less chance of clutter.
