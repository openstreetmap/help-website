OsmAnd, major cities not found

0

Hi, OSM-community! Please, help me: I've download a new offline-map of Belarus (because old version is not supported by 0.8.1 beta), but no one from regional centers (such as Minsk, Gomel, Grodno, Brest, Mogilev and Vitebsk) are not marked on the map. Everything else is OK, but this.. even the smallest city is selected and these very large and important - not! Please, can anyone help to fix it? Thanks.

In many cases, this is due to the redaction. The maps were compiled just after the redaction and thus there are some issues.

A city should have a city node and a closed boundary around it with the same name as the city node before it's indexed.

If it is indeed the fault of the redaction, it will most likely already be repaired. So you only need to wait for new OsmAnd maps, or compile them yourself (see the FAQ)
