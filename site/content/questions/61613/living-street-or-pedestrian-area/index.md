<p>This isn't a living street, as those are a car-accessible roads with specific rules and signs that don't seem to be present here.</p>
<p>Pedestrian fits a bit better, but I'd argue it isn't a good choice either. This may be different in your local community, but around here, a typical pedestrian road would be <em>far</em> wider – like a regular street, but reserved to pedestrians (and maybe the occasional delivery or emergency vehicle).</p>
<p>Because of that, and assuming there's no bicycle access, I'd map your example from the picture as a <strong>footway</strong>.</p>
