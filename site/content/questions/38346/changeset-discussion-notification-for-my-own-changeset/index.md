<p>Okay, I will answer my own question to summarize some things:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>You're automatically subscribing discussions at your own changesets since the new feature was introduced (November 2nd, 2014)</p></li>
<li><p>Currently you're not subscribing to your own changesets that were made before that date (there is a <a href="https://github.com/openstreetmap/openstreetmap-website/issues/823">ticket on GitHub</a>)</p></li>
<li><p>Unsubscribing and Resubscribing to my own changesets is possible</p></li>
<li><p>If you're commenting you're automatically subscribing to a discussion (although you previously may have unsubscribed)</p></li>
</ul>
