<p>You will need to contact Google and Instagram to change that.<br />
If you want to add your address to the OpenStreetMap.org you will need to sign in and do the introductory to mapping <a href="https://learnosm.org/en/">https://learnosm.org/en/</a><br />
You should then be able to draw the shape of you house and add tags for housenumber, street, suburb, etc.</p>
<p>If you are listing your business:<br />
Using something like <a href="https://osmybiz.osm.ch/#/9/-27.49122/152.67700">https://osmybiz.osm.ch/#/9/-27.49122/152.67700</a> or <a href="https://www.onosm.org/#">https://www.onosm.org/#</a> to initially help adding your business listing at the building you operate from in to the OSM with suitable tags may assist.</p>
