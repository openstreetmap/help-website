<p>They are extremely long, 100s of miles and stay near each other. They tend to follow streams roads railroads and pretty much any linear feature seemingly at random. The lines are not tagged, and the history seems to show them as never have having a tag.</p>
<p>They both start and end at the exact same place.</p>
