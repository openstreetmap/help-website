<div class="answer-body">
<p>The standard map at openstreetmap.org doesn't render windsocks. It is a general-purpose map that does not attempt to display everything in the OpenStreetMap database. Many correctly-mapped features are not shown, but they are in the database for anyone interested in that particular feature.</p>
<p>Perhaps there is a more specialised renderer that does display this, but I am not aware of one.</p>
