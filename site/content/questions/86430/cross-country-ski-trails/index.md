<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you are looking at <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/">https://www.openstreetmap.org/</a> you will not see any ski trails. They simply cannot render everything that is mapped so they render what they think most people would like to see.</p>
<p>For ski trails look at other renderers like the one at Open Snow Map: <a href="http://opensnowmap.org">http://opensnowmap.org</a></p>
<p>If I recall correctly, for portable offline use OsmAnd can display ski trails (it might need a plug-in/add-on for this).</p>
