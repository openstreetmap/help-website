<p>That's because it's trying to export all the data from the current area (bounding box).</p>
<p>Exporting the data just for the route could be done this way:</p>
<ol>
<li>Go to the <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu">http://overpass-turbo.eu</a></li>
<li>Open the Wizard</li>
<li>Type in "route=bus and ref=52 in Cardiff"</li>
<li>Run the query</li>
<li>Go to the Export tab to download in a format suitable for you</li>
</ol>
