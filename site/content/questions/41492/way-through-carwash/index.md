<div class="answer-body">
<p>I have used that too – for a wash where you really can stay in the car while ordering the wash type, paying, getting the service. I think for this type of car wash it fits. And <code>highway=service</code>, of course.</p>
<p>I have used <code>access=customers</code>, <code>oneway=yes</code>, <code>tunnel=building_passage</code> in addition. You could go <em>crazy</em> with whatever other highway-related attributes, too.</p>
