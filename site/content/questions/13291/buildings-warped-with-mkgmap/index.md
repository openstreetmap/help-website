<div class="answer-body">
<p>The coorordinates in Garmin maps are accurate to about 2.5m depending on direction and latitude.</p>
<p>That's quite small, but a small building is not many times larger than that and so can be quite distorted.</p>
<p>So I am afraid there is nothing simple that can be done about this.</p>
