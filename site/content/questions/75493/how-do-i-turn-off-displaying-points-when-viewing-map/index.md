<div class="answer-body">
<p>In iD there are some pre-set filters that you can use:</p>
<ol>
<li>Expand the Map data panel using the second button in the second group of buttons on the right hand side of the map area.</li>
<li>Expand the "Map Features" list.</li>
<li>Deselect categories you don't want to see.</li>
</ol>
<p>Try to remember not to re-add things you have hidden.</p>
