<p>You just need to provide a clear attribution and you are all set on the OpenStreetMap side of things:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright">https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright</a></p>
<p>It's probably a good idea to check with your publisher about whether they have strange thoughts about the ODBL license the map data comes under.</p>
<p>In addition to the ODBL, you need to check on CC BY-SA (which the default tiles on openstreetmap.org are licensed under), there are other styles available: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tile_servers">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tile_servers</a></p>
