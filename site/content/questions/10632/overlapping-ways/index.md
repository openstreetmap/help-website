<p>I suspect it depends:</p>
<p>Is the boundary defined as following the course of the river, or is it defined separately using a series of coordinates that happen to be where the river once was?</p>
<p>Where the boundary really does follow where the river is now, is it really in the middle of the river or on one bank of it?</p>
<p>How do you know where the boundary is with respect to the river, when boundaries often aren't marked on the ground?</p>
