<p>When tagging a way with shared lanes for bicycles in both directions there are three ways of doing to my understanding:</p>
<ol>
<li>cycleway:both=shared_lane</li>
<li>cycleway=shared_lane</li>
<li>cycleway:left=shared_lane and cycleway:right=shared_lane</li>
</ol>
<p>Which is the most appropriate one? 1) is the most compact and has no ambiguities, but some tools (including JOSM) do not render it. 2) is also compact, but does not explicitly state that there are two ways. 3) has no problems but requires two keys.</p>
<p>Thanks!</p>
