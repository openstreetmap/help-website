<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<ul>
<li>Using Ctrl key select both ways. (You can add ways to relation one by one also)</li>
<li>click on "Add to" button on the lower left corner.</li>
<li>In the appeared window click on "New relation..." button to create new relation.</li>
<li>Or if you are adding members to existing relation, then choose that relation from appeared window and click "Select" button.</li>
<li>Choose Multipolygon as the type of relation from dropdown menu.</li>
<li>Click on "Members" tab on the bottom of window.</li>
<li>Write either word inner (islands inside an area) or outer (ways that form the outer area) as the type of way.</li>
</ul>
<p>You have a multipolygon.</p>
