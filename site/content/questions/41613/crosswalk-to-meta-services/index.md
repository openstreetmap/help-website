<p>Hello,</p>
<p>I believe OSM can, or should support 'crosswalk' urls for physical locations to popular services within the tag structure. For example, and specific to restaurants, Yelp ratings have become the standard for ratings across most restaurants. I dont propose the Yelp rating added, but instead the Yelp URI that would allow a developer link from a physical Place (Restaurant) to a yelp object via the Yelp API. This could be applied to a variety of useful meta services and and added to tags like meta:yelp=yelp.com/blah</p>
<p>This would be incredibly useful for folks making geographic databases based on OSM data instead of Google Places or the like. This seems like a powerful feature that is needed.</p>
<p>Is this something the community would find useful?</p>
<p>A</p>
