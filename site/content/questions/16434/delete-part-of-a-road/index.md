<div class="answer-body">
<p>You need to split the road in three parts, and then remove the middle part. You can only split a way at a node so if the road doesn't have nodes to either side of the creek that you could use as your splitting points, you have to insert these nodes first.</p>
<p>(The JOSM editor has a convenient "delete segment" feature, where you select the trashcan icon at the left and then shift-click on the piece of way that crosses the creek; the way will then automatically split and the middle bit removed provided that there were nodes to either side of the creek. In Potlatch, click the first node then press X to split the way, click the second node and press X again, then click the middle way bit and shift-delete.)</p>
</div>
