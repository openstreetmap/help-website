<ol>
<li>Some historical milestones are recorden on the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/History">wiki</a>. You may also look at the mailing list archives and possibly interview Petter Reinholdtsen, Steve Coast or any of the early developers.</li>
<li>The servers at <a href="http://openstreetmap.org">openstreetmap.org</a> are run by the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Foundation">OSM foundation</a> and is funded by donations and memberships. The development are mostly done by volunteers, but some have jobs in companies like Bing, GeoFabrik and CloudMade.</li>
<li>As far as the success we are <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Humanitarian_OSM_Team">saving lives</a>, gaining support by Microsoft and giving developers a better map then Google.</li>
</ol>
