<p>The best place to begin so that the common questions can be addressed before work begins is the <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/listinfo/imports">Imports list</a>.</p>
<p>The most common issue with this many business points is how was the location information obtained? If it was derived from a licenced commercial geocode database, it would not be compatible with OSM.</p>

<p>General note CC BY licences are not directly compatible with OSM, the data owner will have to provide a waiver as in <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B3PN5zfbzThqeTdWR1l3SzJVcTg/view">https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B3PN5zfbzThqeTdWR1l3SzJVcTg/view</a></p>
<p>The other point to understand is that once the data is in OSM it is "OSM data" it will be maintained (or not) by our contributors and will develop in one way or the other a life of its own.</p>
