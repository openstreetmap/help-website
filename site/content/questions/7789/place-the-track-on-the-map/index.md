<p>Your track is uploaded correctly.<br />
</p>
<p>To put it on map you need to manually draw it (not just upload it as is). To make this easier you can display your recorded track under your drawing to know where the track should be placed.</p>
<p>Click Edit (this opens an online editor called Potlatch) -&gt; open "GPS Data" combobox -&gt; selct "My Tracks" -&gt; click "Load" for each track you need to display -&gt; by clicking mouse button draw the track along the recorded GPS trace -&gt; tag it.</p>
