<div class="answer-body">
<p>I'm not to fond of the start_date tag, but I usually tag the construction years, architect and start_date anyways.</p>
<p>This would be a building for me:</p>
<ul>
<li>building=residential</li>
<li>construction_date=2008-2011</li>
<li><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:start_date">start_date</a>=2011</li>
<li><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:architect">architect</a>=Åwl; Frans Bengtsson</li>
</ul>
<p>And the landuse=construction would have been tagged with:</p>
<ul>
<li>opening_date=Autumn 2010</li>
<li>start_date=2008</li>
<li>architect=Åwl; Fran Bengtsson</li>
<li>website=<a href="http://www.awlark.se/">http://www.awlark.se/</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The architect tag is a bit free form, but so is the date tags unfortunately. I also like to add websites to construction areas, which tend to be a bit free form as well.</p>
