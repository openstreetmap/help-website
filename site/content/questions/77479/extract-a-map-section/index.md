<div class="answer-body">
<ol>
<li>Go to openstreetmap.org</li>
<li>pan/zoom to the area you want to extract</li>
<li>click the share button (a square with an outgoing arrow) (in the buttons menu on the right)</li>
<li>choose "image" and then the format ("jpeg" or "pdf") - you may also further restrict the extract area by clicking "set custom dimensions". Use a small area.</li>
<li>click "download" button</li>
<li>enjoy.</li>
</ol>
