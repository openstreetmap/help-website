<div class="answer-body">
<p>When you created your account, you had to fill in an email address. I assume this is your regular email address. With which program do you read the emails normally ? Use that one, depending on the mail program and your provider, you might have to look in the "spam", or "updates" folder besides the regular inbox folder.</p>
<p>In case no mail arrives, it is possible that you made a typo in the email address. I'm not sure whether an admin can correct that for you. Otherwise you have to make another account.</p>
