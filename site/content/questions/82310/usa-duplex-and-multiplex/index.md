<div class="answer-body">
<p>Normal "side by side" duplexes are normally <code>building=semidetached_house</code>s.</p>
<p>If by multiplex you mean "townhouse style" houses like a normal duplex but with additional houses in the middle of the row then the building as a whole may be tagged as <code>building=terrace</code>.</p>
<p>The best summary of the various options is probably <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:building">this page</a> on the wiki.</p>
<p>Either of the above can alternately be mapped with a split at the dividing walls and each individual dwelling tagged as <code>building=house</code>. This would allow the individual houses to be given their own addresses. If this is not feasible or would require too much guesswork then multiple address nodes at roughly the correct locations within the overall building is also acceptable (and preferred in some places with government opendata), some people prefer to put them on the front doors in which case they would get <code>building=entrance</code> too.</p>
