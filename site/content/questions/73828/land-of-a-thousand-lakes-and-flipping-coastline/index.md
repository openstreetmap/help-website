<div class="answer-body">
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Coastline">Coastlines</a> should always be drawn so that the land is on the left according to the order of the ways.</p>
<p>There is a map that shows coastline errors <a href="">here</a> (currently showing a potential error in Sweden, but not Finland).</p>
<p>Lakes are drawn with the same direction convention, but different tags.</p>
<p>What you show in your image looks like it could be a rendering issue for the editor, not necessarily an issue in the data itself. Is this a multipolygon that isn't fully loaded?</p>
