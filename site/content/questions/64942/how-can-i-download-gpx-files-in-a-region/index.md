<p>The simple answer is you can't.</p>
<p>What you can do is download data for small areas via the OSM API, see: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/API_v0.6#GPS_traces">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/API_v0.6#GPS_traces</a> I believe JOSM is the only popular editor that supports this currently.</p>
