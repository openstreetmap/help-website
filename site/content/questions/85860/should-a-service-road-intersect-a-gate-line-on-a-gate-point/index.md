<div class="answer-body">
<p>I believe it should, assuming the service road passes through the gate (not just near it). The advice on the wiki seems sensible: "Add a node on the intersection between any footways and the gate, also tagged with barrier=gate, much like how crossings are mapped. (This helps routers as many will just ignore the gate if there is no node)". <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:barrier%3Dgate">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:barrier%3Dgate</a></p>
<p>Note that only about 1% of "barrier=gate" are mapped as ways, so it's relatively uncommon.</p>
