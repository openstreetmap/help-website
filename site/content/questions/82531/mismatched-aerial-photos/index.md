<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is unfortunately an issue we cannot do much about, having to rely on "donated" material. I have even found aerial images where the alignment changed from zoom level to zoom level.</p>
<p>It is good practice to align aerial images either with existing map objects (in already well mapped areas) or with several GPS traces. All major editors have functions to adjust the position of the background images.</p>
<p>Please have a look at <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Using_Imagery">our wiki</a> for some more hints and background.</p>
