<div class="answer-body">
<p>I think these features in Flickr (machine tags for openstreetmap) ceased to be supported long before (say, around 2015) Flickr was acquired by SmugMug. I suspect it was more a side project of some members of Flickr's engineering team. Flickr also changed their underlying mapping tech around the same time.</p>
<p>Some of the photos may still exist, providing they are tagged as CC-BY-SA, but any photos not so tagged and not owned by a subscribed Flickr member will have been deleted. I could probably find some relevant Flickr emails if I search my email history.</p>
<p>Either way Flickr pretty much restricts access to any group of photos unless signed in.</p>
