<div class="answer-body">
<p>What seems to work for the motorway near me, that is JOSM and other tools seem to accept it) is:</p>
<pre><code>lanes=5
hov:lanes=designated|yes|yes|yes|yes
hov:minimum=2</code></pre>
<p>This is for a 5 lane section of the motorway. There are, of course, some other tags for HGV, maxspeed, etc.</p>
<p>In this area you are allowed to enter and exit the designated HOV lane at any point. There are some other areas where changing into or out of the HOV lane(s) is not allowed. In those areas the change:lanes=* tagging can be used to indicate what lane changes are permitted.</p>
