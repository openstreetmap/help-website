<div class="answer-body">
<p>First of all, if there is any <code>highway=</code> inside, they can already be checked for being <code>access=private</code>, and have <code>informal=yes</code> added together for unofficial ones.</p>
<p>You need to distinguish whether the area is the immediate <code>landuse=reservoir_watershed</code> around a waterbody, or a larger <code>boundary=water_protection_area</code> surrounding it. It is preferable there is a visible border (eg landscape, or obstacles), or they release data in a compatible license. Otherwise, an approximate area around the terrain can be acceptable.</p>
</div>
