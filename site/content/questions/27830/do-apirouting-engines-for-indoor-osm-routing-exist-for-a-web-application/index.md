<p>In case you do not yet know the following – a first help …</p>
<p>Routing engines are listed at <span>Routing</span> (and sub/linked pages) in our wiki. Likely some may also be used for indoor routing or could be adapted for. If you found something, please mention it here for future reference.</p>
<p>Also there is <span>Indoor Mapping</span> described in general. Note that indoor mapping is very very incomplete in our data and experimental.</p>
