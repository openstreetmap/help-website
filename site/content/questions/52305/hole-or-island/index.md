<p>It's a dry space ;-)<br />
Additional tags like place=island/islet are optional, not mandatory. This makes no real difference, just avoids possible confusion for later editors.<br />
All holes you stamp into a multipolygon area are completely free space again, just like the space outside that MP.</p>
