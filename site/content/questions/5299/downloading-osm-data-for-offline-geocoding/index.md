<p>Planet.osm is all we have. If that's not good enough for you, then OSM is not good enough for you, and you will have to go and pay a load of money and get a commercial dataset for Poland.</p>
<p>Having said that, your conclusion "need to geocode 14k addresses" =&gt; "have to install offline geocoder" is not necessarily right; the <a href="http://developer.mapquest.com/web/products/open/nominatim">MapQuest Nominatim instance</a> can easily deal with 14k requests if you don't make them all at once.</p>
