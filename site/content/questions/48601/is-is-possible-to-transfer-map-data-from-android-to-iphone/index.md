<p>How should transferring the <em>data</em> to your iPhone help if there is no corresponding <em>app</em> available?</p>
<p>Instead take a look at our <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Apple_iOS">wiki page about iOS</a>. There are lots of different OSM-based apps available.</p>
