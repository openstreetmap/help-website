<div class="answer-body">
<p>Instead of edit, use Query Features in openstreetmap.org (the ? icon on the right). Click on the edge of the lake to see a list of features in the left sidebar. Pick the "water" item there. Now you will see the details of the lake, including the most recent changeset information at the top of the sidebar. At the bottom of the left sidebar (scroll down) you will see View History. Click there for more history details on that feature. (There seem to be only 2 for that lake.)</p>
