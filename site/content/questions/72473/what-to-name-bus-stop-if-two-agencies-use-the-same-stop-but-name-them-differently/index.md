<p>In general use the name as shown on the sign ("the flag") at the bus stop. If this does not carry a name use your discretion (I'd regard the two options above as more or less equivalent). The tag unsigned=yes can be useful in such situations, implying the source lies elsewhere (e.g., in announcements on the bus).</p>
<p>You can also use alt_name for one of the alternatives.</p>
