<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetMap doesn't generally have postcode sectors mapped.</p>
<p>However, when you ask "is it possible to use postcode sectors with OpenStreetMap" - you can use any geographic data with OSM. It's all just latitudes and longitudes. OSM isn't really a "mapping tool" or a platform, it's a database of mapping information that you can incorporate into other tools.</p>
<p>If you want to download postcode sector polygons for free, you can do so from <a href="http://postcodes.mapit.longair.net">http://postcodes.mapit.longair.net</a> .</p>
<p>If you provide more information on what you're actually trying to achieve, I'm sure people here will be happy to help further.</p>
