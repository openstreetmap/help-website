<div class="answer-body">
<p>If it actually exists, but isn't accessible by the public, I'd suggest changing the access tags to "private" rather than removing it altogether. That way, if it's visible from aerial imagery, no-one will add it back by accident.</p>
<p>You can do this yourself by zooming in to the area in question in a PC web browser and clicking "edit".</p>
