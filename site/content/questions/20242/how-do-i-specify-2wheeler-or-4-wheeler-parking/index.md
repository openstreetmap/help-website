<div class="answer-body">
<p>For 2-wheel parking use <code>amenity=motorcycle_parking</code> has 1016 uses on <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/amenity=motorcycle_parking">taginfo</a></p>
<p>For car parking where motorcycles are not permitted I'd suggest using <code>motorcycle=no</code>. The <code>motorcycle</code> access tag has 636 uses in combination with parking on taginfo.</p>

<p>Hi Devdatta, It depends on local laws. In some country's theres a law stating no parking allowed for 2 wheel motor vehicles (mc) in any parking space.
