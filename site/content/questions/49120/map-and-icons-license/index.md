<div class="answer-body">
<p>In case you want to produce a map (with some special icons) out of <em>OSM data</em>: Please read the full ODbL license and e.g. section 3c in <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal_FAQ">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal_FAQ</a> . Therefore, I see no problem with your icons for use in the produced work – the map. Just obey the requirements (e.g. attribution) of the ODbl.</p>
<p>In case you want to use the default map on osm.org as basis, you need to obey the license restrictions of this map's license too. It is Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0. This may not work with your non-sharable icons.</p>
<p>In both cases see <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright">https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright</a> and read the full legal license texts (not the summaries).</p>
<p>(note: I am not a lawyer and do not, and cannot, speak for the OSM community. The license texts are binding.)</p>
