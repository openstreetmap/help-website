<p>Use standard OSM naming conventions "height:left" and "height:right", left &amp; right are with respect to the direction of the way. These have very low usage, e.g., <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/height%3Aleft">13 examples</a> of <code>height:left</code></p>
