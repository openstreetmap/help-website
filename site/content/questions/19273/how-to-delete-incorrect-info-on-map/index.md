<div class="answer-body">
<p>The OSM map data is editable, everyhwere.</p>
<ol>
<li>Create an account on <a href="http://openstreetmap.org"></a><a href="http://openstreetmap.org">http://openstreetmap.org</a> and log in</li>
<li>Pan and zoom the map to the location where you want to change data. Zoom as close as possible</li>
<li>Choose "Edit" (or your local translation, second entry after "map") and then "Potlatch 2" to edit the map data.</li>
</ol>
<p>Ther are more advanced editors once you are more experienced, but Potlatch works well to remove duplicates or correct incorrect values.</p>
