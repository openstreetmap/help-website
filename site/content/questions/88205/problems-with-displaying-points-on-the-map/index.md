<p>The rendering on the standard layer is offered by Carto and is a blue dot as with all the other offices I checked.<br />
<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Aoffice%3Dit">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Aoffice%3Dit</a><br />
Shops tend to have more interesting icons.<br />
I expect the one that does not display the name is due to spacing from the other pois. If you want the name to display, I would shift it a bit further away from the others until you get a better result.</p>
