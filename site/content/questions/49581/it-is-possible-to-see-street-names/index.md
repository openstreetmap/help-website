<p>No. We are not allowed to copy street names from <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Bing">Bing</a> or similar services. Instead, go out to make a survey and write down the street names. This will also allow you to add other interesting details such as shops, post boxes, bus stops and so on.</p>
