<p><a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/150/mtmail">@mtmail</a>: there doesn't seem to be a consistent format for postcodes in UK official data: choices are &lt;out&gt;&lt;space&gt;&lt;in&gt;; no space, or the outcode padded to 4 or 5 with spaces. Most people expect the first format. Often it is best to take the rightmost 3 characters &amp; then trim what is left. There should always be a number followed by two characters for the last part of the code, the outcode can have from 2 to 4 characters starting with a letter.</p>
