<div class="answer-body">
<p>My first thought was community_centre but it seems more specialized than that.</p>
<ul>
<li>If you're taking the word "wellness" at face value, <code>healthcare=alternative</code> seems good, though none of the specialties listed in the wiki seem to fit.</li>
<li>The website's emphasis on dance classes makes me think <code>amenity=dancing_school</code>, maybe with <code>dance:style=belly_dancing;kundalini</code>.</li>
<li>There are also yoga classes listed, which most people seem to tag as <code>leisure=fitness_centre+sport=yoga</code>.</li>
</ul>
<p>If you're really into piling on tags, you could add all of these.</p>
<p>Sorry I don't have any mapped examples -- there's nothing on my radar in this particular grey area.</p>
<p>(There's also <code>amenity=meditation_centre</code> which seems to fit ok but it's very uncommon.)</p>
