If another email only results in a reply from an under-resourced support person who does not seem to understand the issue, you can drop the DWG an email (see <a href="https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Data_Working_Group">https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Data_Working_Group</a> for details) and we can also try.</p>
