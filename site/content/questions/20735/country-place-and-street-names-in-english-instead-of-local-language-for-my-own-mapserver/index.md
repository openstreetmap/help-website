<p>One approach would be to ask the people behind the <a href="http://toolserver.org/~osm/locale/en.html?zoom=6&amp;lat=31.2222&amp;lon=113.00537&amp;layers=BT">localised map on Wikimedia's Toolserver</a> how they did it. There are no obvious contact details on the page but they do crop up on mailing lists every now and again, so that's where I'd start looking.</p>
<p>Another (very quick and dirty) option would be to add "if name:en exists use that instead of name" directly into osm2pgsql itself.</p>
