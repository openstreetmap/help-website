<div class="answer-body">
<p>If the way joining the roundabout has a barrier island separating the opposing lanes, then split the way into 2 oneway sections / carraigeways (<a href="http://osm.org/go/zDDCr2SD1--">see this example</a>). In this example the road running north - south is a dual carraigeway while the road running east - west is a single carraigeway with barrier islands at the roundabout junction</p>
<p>If the way does not have a separating island between the 2 opposing lanes at the junction with the roundabout, then do not split the way (<a href="http://osm.org/go/zCXcdcm44--">see this example</a>). In this example, all 4 roads intersecting with the roundabout are single carraigeways without islands at the roundabout junction.</p>
