<div class="answer-body">
<p>The OSM wiki has helpful pages about <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Public_transport">Public Transport</a> and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Buses#Adding_a_bus_route_to_OpenStreetMap">Adding a bus route</a>.</p>
<p><a href="http://learnosm.org">LearnOSM</a> has a rather short section about <a href="https://learnosm.org/en/josm/josm-relations/#route-relations">Route Relations</a> with the OSM editor JOSM.</p>
<p>OsmAnd (an OSM-based Android/iOS app) published a <a href="https://osmand.net/blog/guideline-pt">guide for adding public transport routes</a> with JOSM.</p>
