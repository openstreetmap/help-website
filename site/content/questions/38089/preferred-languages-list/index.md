<p>As far as I understand, Arabic is the official language in Algeria. This means that I would expect the standard "name" tag to reflect Arabic, with a potential "name:fr" or "name:en" or "int_name" thrown in for variants in other languages.</p>
<p>If you want to open a feature requests against the iD editor which is used on openstreetmap.org, then <a href="https://github.com/openstreetmap/iD">https://github.com/openstreetmap/iD</a> would be the best place for that.</p>
