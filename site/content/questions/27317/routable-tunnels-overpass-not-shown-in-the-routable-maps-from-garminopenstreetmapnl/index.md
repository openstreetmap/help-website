<p>It's only for buses right? access = no highway = unclassified motor_vehicle = no name = South East Busway psv = permissive tunnel = yes</p>
<p>Tunnels that are not accessible for 'normal' traffic (car, bicycle, foot) are not rendered on these maps. See <a href="http://www.mkgmap.org.uk/websvn/filedetails.php?repname=mkgmap&amp;path=%2Ftrunk%2Fresources%2Fstyles%2Fdefault%2Flines">http://www.mkgmap.org.uk/websvn/filedetails.php?repname=mkgmap&amp;path=%2Ftrunk%2Fresources%2Fstyles%2Fdefault%2Flines</a></p>
<p># Hide unaccessible tunnels</p>
<p>highway= <em>&amp; tunnel=yes &amp; (access=private|access=no) &amp; foot!=</em> &amp; bicycle!=* {delete highway;delete junction}</p>
