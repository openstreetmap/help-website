<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can do this using JOSM with the Measurement plugin. Load the plugin, and it will add an icon to the left-hand toolbar, with a picture of a ruler. If you click on that it will open the measurement window.</p>
<p>Then select a closed way you are interested in, and it will give you the area in m².</p>
