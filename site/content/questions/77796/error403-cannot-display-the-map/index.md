<p>Wikimedia stopped allowing third-party websites like yours to use the map tiles they provide at maps.wikimedia.org. That's why you get a 403 error.</p>
<p>So either use the openstreetmap.org tiles while respecting the OSM tile usage polices (SomeoneElse posted the link already) or look out for a tile provider here: <a href="https://switch2osm.org/providers/">https://switch2osm.org/providers/</a></p>
