<div class="answer-body">
<p>highway=proposed roads are not rendered on the "Standard" rendering shown on openstreetmap.org. This is intentional, because many such roads have been mapped that often never make it beyond the proposal stage and there's nothing visible on the ground. Some other renderings do show proposed roads, like the Humanitarian map.</p>
