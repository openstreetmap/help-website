<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>If I am not mistaken, all osm services run over http or https. So that would be the standard ports 80 and 443. You can see the complete assignment of IPs and their corresponding names in the git repository that OSM uses to manage their DNS servers. ( <a href="http://git.openstreetmap.org/dns.git/blob/HEAD:/src/openstreetmap">http://git.openstreetmap.org/dns.git/blob/HEAD:/src/openstreetmap</a> )</p>
<p>The IPs for the tile servers might change more frequently because they are behind a geographically distributed CDN that can change depending on demand and availability. You can access the individual caches directly with their IPs, but this is not encouraged, as that then bypasses load balancing and high availability mechanisms.</p>
</div>
