<div class="answer-body">
<p>After you have installed the plugin "imagery" or "slippymap" in JOSM you will see a new light green button in the left hand toolbar with a cross-arrow pointing in 4 directions. After you clicked it you can move the imagery while holding the mouse-button and moving the mouse. Click on the "Select" or "Add" button in the left hand toolbar to leave that mode again.</p>
