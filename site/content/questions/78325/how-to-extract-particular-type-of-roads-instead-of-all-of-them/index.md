<p>For large areas you'll have to download either the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Planet.osm#Planet.osm_mirrors">whole planet</a> or <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Planet.osm#Country_and_area_extracts">an extract</a> and process locally.</p>
