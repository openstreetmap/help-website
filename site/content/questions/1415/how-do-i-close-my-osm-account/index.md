<p>I think that there should be a proceedure for closing an account. I agree with the reasoning of mk408. I don't know how many registered users there are (probably &gt;200,000) but how many are really active.</p>

<p>9 with no contribution</p>
<p>9 contributions within the last 12 months</p>
<p>2 with none since 1-2 years</p>
<p>12 with none since 2-3 years</p>
