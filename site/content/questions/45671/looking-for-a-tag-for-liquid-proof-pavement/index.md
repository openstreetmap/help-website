<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is no widely-used tag to describe this; I would simply tag them as <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:surface"><code>surface=paving_stones</code></a>. I would also argue that this level of detail is probably not necessary for OSM, as the users of the petrol station care whether the surface is paved or not; whether it's impermeable to liquids isn't really relevant for them.</p>
