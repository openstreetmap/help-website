<div class="answer-body">
<p><code>building=roof</code> for that part of the building. If for some reason you don't want to change the outline, <code>building:part=roof</code>.</p>
<p><code>covered=yes</code> for the way. <code>tunnel=building_passage</code> is for when there are walls on both sides.</p>
