<div class="answer-body">
<p>Use both S3DB (<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Simple_3D_buildings">Simple 3D buildings</a>) and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Simple_Indoor_Tagging">Indoor</a> mapping. You need 2 building:part elements with roof:shape=flat. Doors, windows etc are usually part of Indoor mapping, but in practice windows are far too fiddly to be practical in most situtations.</p>
