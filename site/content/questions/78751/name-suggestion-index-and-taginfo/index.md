<div class="answer-body">
<p>Name Suggestion Index accepts entries for any chain of stores, and also tracks brands that may no longer actively exist (these brands are not added to the data used by OSM editors).</p>
<p>So while it is likely that an entry in Name Suggestion Index would appear at least once in OSM, it may not be the case.</p>
<p>You can also look at the tags that NSI tells taginfo it uses, and how they are generated, if that is what your question is:</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/osmlab/name-suggestion-index/blob/main/scripts/build_dist.js#L346">https://github.com/osmlab/name-suggestion-index/blob/main/scripts/build_dist.js#L346</a></p>
