<div class="answer-body">
<p>That sounds more like a "shop" for me (instead of a restaurant). Maybe <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Ashop%3Dfood"><code>shop=food</code></a> because <code>shop=deli</code> does fit less? I am not sure of this kind of shop should have an own shop category instead.</p>
<p>It may be a good idea to also add "<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:description"><code>description</code></a><code>=shop selling freshly prepared pizza that is meant to be taken home and baked</code>" to describe what the object is about in free language</p>
