<p>You need to know that OSM usually focus on long term, almost not movable amenities and (IMHO!) wifi hotspots are not.</p>
<p>If you want to map them please check out dedicated <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Related_Project">related projects</a> as openBmap.</p>
<p>If you just want to present some of your own hotspots, please have a look at various dedicated online maps e.g. <a href="http://berlin.freifunk.net/network/map/">some of the Freifunk movement</a>.</p>
