<div class="answer-body">
<ul>
<li>Log in (and <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/ticket/5268">maybe</a> you also must have a validated email address)</li>
<li>Depending on what you want to do … add an answer to an existing question or ask a new question</li>
<li>click on 6th button from left above the text you're typing (labelled "image")<br />
<img src="/upfiles/toolbar-image.png" alt="editing button row with image button highlighted" /></li>
<li>browse to the image on your PC (it'll fill in both boxes)</li>
<li><p>supply the alt text (what people without graphics will see).</p></li>
<li><p>If you want to "comment", convert the answer to a comment.</p></li>
</ul>
