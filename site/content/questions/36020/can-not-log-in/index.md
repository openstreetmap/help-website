<div class="answer-body">
<p>To log in to OpenStreetMap at <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/login">https://www.openstreetmap.org/login</a> ...</p>
<p>you can use either your "Display name" or your email address. It is possible to change you display name, so that could be something you have confused yourself with.</p>
<p>You should be able to look through your old emails and find a confirmation email which you used when you first signed up. Take the email address that this was sent to, and use this when you're signing in.</p>
<p>Still not working? Time to try a password reset: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/forgot-password">https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/forgot-password</a></p>
