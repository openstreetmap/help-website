<div class="answer-body">
<p>I assume your shapefile layer consists of solid objects, which hide the underlying objects from the OSM-layer.</p>
<p>You can change the order of the layers, as well as change the opacity. This explained on the <a href="https://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Dialog/LayerList">JOSM wiki LayersList page</a>.</p>
