<p>I've used Graphviz for lots of simple things over the years like flow charts, block diagrams of network wiring, etc. Quick and relatively easy to do for the job it was originally developed for.</p>
<p>But if it is possible to to make something as nice as the tube map you linked to in Graphviz it would really, really surprise me. I'm pretty sure a good graphic designer and/or cartographer put in some hours to make it look that good.</p>
<p>I hope someone else chimes in with a software tool that will do that for you as I'd like to learn about it but would not be surprised if it either does not exist or is very expensive.</p>
<p>Looks like your Inkscape effort is quite good, congratulations on that.</p>

<p>I <em>think</em> you could use mapnik's <a href="https://www.mapbox.com/blog/expanding-mapnik-carto/">vertex converters</a> to do the work.
