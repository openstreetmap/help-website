<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is probably caused by Potlatch failing to load the stylesheet because your browser has cached the old stylesheet list.</p>
<p>You should clear your browser cache or bypass cache when loading potlatch. How to do it depends on your browser. For all major browsers it is well described on this <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Bypass_your_cache">wikipedia</a> page.</p>
