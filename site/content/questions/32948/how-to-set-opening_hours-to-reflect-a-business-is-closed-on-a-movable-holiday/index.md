<div class="answer-body">
<p>The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:opening_hours:specification">opening_hours specification</a> accepts a comment enclosed in "". So your tag could look like <code>opening_hours=24/7 "except Easter"</code>.</p>
<p>But in my opinion this is only a workaround because it is hard to parse. Instead the specification should be extended to accept more holidays than just <em>PH</em> and <em>SH</em>. But it would have to be somehow generic in order to accept any local holiday in the world and not just the common ones.</p>
