<div class="answer-body">
<p>Trying to update your 8-month-old data is going to take vastly longer than a full re-import. As a rule of thumb, as soon as you're more than 2 weeks out of date, a new import is usually faster. Only reason to try an update would be if you cannot afford the database to be down for 1.5 days - but running an 8-month update will likely make your database veeeery slow for 15 days ;)</p>
