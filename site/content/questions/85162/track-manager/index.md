<div class="answer-body">
<p>What I do in this situation is email the tracks I have recorded in OSMand to myself and then import them into Garmin's Basecamp program. As long as you have a map of your area of interest in Basecamp, the tracks will be visible on the map. They can be arranged or grouped to your liking within the Basecamp data structure and stored in Basecamp indefinitely.</p>
<p>Basecamp is available free from Garmin. You would need some sort of map inside Basecamp in order to see your tracks overlaid on something other than a white background but Garmin-compatible maps can be purchased or even custom-made if you are oriented that way.</p>
<p>Cheers,</p>
<p>Dave</p>
