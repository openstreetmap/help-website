<p>Hi David, welcome to OSM!</p>
<p>I see the building even in close zoom levels. This is likely a issue of caching in your browser. When the buildings disappear hit Ctrl+F5 on your keyboard to do a cache-circumventing refresh of the map images. The buildings should show up then.</p>
<p>See also <a href="/questions/18390/">road-appears-and-disappears-at-different-scales</a> and <a href="/questions/178/">how-often-does-the-main-mapnik-map-get-updated</a> (mainly discussing server-side issues).</p>
