<p>I suspect many of the new surfaces we see are concreate based with chips of attractive stone added into the surface. The surface is then ground and polished, Often encountered in supermarkets and new public buildings. See <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granolithic">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granolithic</a> perhaps Granolithic is the tag we should use. Previously used tags can be searched for and found here. <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=+surface+%3Dconcrete">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=+surface+%3Dconcrete</a> but it seems a little sad to just call it concreate.</p>
