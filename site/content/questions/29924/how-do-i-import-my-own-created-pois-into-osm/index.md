<p>Hi welcome to OSM and thanks that you like to contribute :)</p>
<p>First I highly recommend to get in contact with your local OSM community, as they can help you by reviewing/adding/commenting your dataset and teach you in person on the import process <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Contact">http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Contact</a></p>
<p>Please also make sure that the legal aspects (source etc.) of your material is ok, then you can use e.g. the <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/JOSM">JOSM Editor</a> (with <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/JOSM/Plugins/OpenData">OpenData plugin</a>) to convert your data to OSM model. Please be aware that the OSM community is very <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Import/Guidelines">skeptical about imports</a>, so really spend a lot of time on reviewing/merging your data before you upload and merge them.</p>
<p>P.S: If you only want to display your POIs on top of a OSM baselayer, you can use desktop applications as <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Marble">Marble</a>, <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/QGIS">QGIS</a>, ... or as webmaps using <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Frameworks#Webmaps">Leaflet, OpenLayers</a>, ...</p>
