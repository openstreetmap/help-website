<div class="answer-body">
<p>Please don't publish tables based on pure numbers like this. They don't help to improve the quality of mapping and are easily <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaming_the_system">gamed</a> thus lowering OSM's overall quality. The ODbL tool you link to is not a league table, but a tool used during the licence change process to target users who had contributed large amounts of data but had not agreed to the change.</p>
<p><a href="http://neis-one.org/">Pascal Neis</a> has produced some nice tools and visualisations based on users' edits, but they reflect some more useful qualities than just raw numbers of edits. Take a look at those before you do anything that might harm OSM.</p>
</div>
