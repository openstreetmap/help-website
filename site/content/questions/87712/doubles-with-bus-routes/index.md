<p>There are two ways to map bus routes in OSM. The newer method uses a relation for each direction and a route master to relation to tie the two together.</p>
<p>More complicated routes also have a relation for each variation of the route.</p>
<p>See <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Buses">here</a> for more information.</p>
