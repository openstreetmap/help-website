<div class="answer-body">
<p>I think you've resized the window down to a small size where it doesn't show anything but the title bar.</p>
<p>JOSM remembers the window size between invocations of the history function.</p>
<p>To fix it, hover over one of the corners until the pointer changes and then resize the window.</p>
</div>
