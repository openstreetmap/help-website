<div class="answer-body">
<p>The inner part of a relation is a member of the same relation as the outer part. If you want to have different tags for the inner part of the relation you have to make a new relation with the inner parts of the first relation as the outer part of the seccond relation.</p>
<p>If you for intstance have a multipolygon of a forest with a lake in it you have the borders of the forest as outer part of the forest relation and the lakeshores as inner part of the forest relation and as outer part of the lake relation.</p>
