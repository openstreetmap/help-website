<p>I’m not near my computer now but I am almost certain if you right click on the relation listed in a window on the right side, then select relation. Then select edit, copy tags. Then select your node or way and then edit, paste tags... it should work. You will need to delete those relation specific tags not suitable for your node or way.</p>
