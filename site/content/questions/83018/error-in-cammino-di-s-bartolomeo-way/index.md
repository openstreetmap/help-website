<p>Hi.</p>
<p>Please share the exact process and the error messages.</p>
<p>But are your gpx files from a personal GPS recording session ? Then please upload them following this <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Upload_GPS_tracks">guide</a>. Otherwise you probably don't have to right to upload them (license issue), and you can't be sure they are correct.</p>
<p>Then you need to add all the paths and tracks the trail goes on, either from aerial imagery or on-the-ground knowledge. And add these to the relevant <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Hiking#Tagging_walking_and_hiking_Route_Networks">route=hiking relation</a>.</p>
<p>You might want to talk with your local community, and mappers you already edited this trail, to see what's possible.</p>
<p>Regards.</p>
