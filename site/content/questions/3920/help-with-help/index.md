<div class="answer-body">
<p>A "how does this site work" section at the top of the FAQ would help. Perhaps something like:</p>
<p><strong>How does this help site work?</strong></p>
<p>It's simple - you ask an OSM question, and other people answer it. You then choose which one is the best answer to your question and select that, so that it's available for easy referenence.</p>
<p>People can also vote up and down good questions and answers, and can add comments to both questions and answers.</p>
<p>It's important to keep both questions and answers relevant (don't post follow-up questions in answers, for example), and make sure that you accept an answer if it worked for you. Also, up and down votes are essential for telling good questions and answers apart from the rest - vote early, vote often!</p>
</div>
