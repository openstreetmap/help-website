<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:historic%3Dwreck">historic=wreck</a> is the recommended way to tag shipwrecks according to the wiki. You could also add <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:seamark:type%3Dwreck">additional tags</a> specific to OpenSeaMap to provide more information.</p>
