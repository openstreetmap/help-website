<p>The editor should always be up-to-date, so my guess is that you did not look carefully enough. A supermarket may have been added as a node, a building, an area, a relation etc.</p>
<p>Another, maybe less likely, reason may be that the supermarkets you saw on the map had already been deleted by someone when you entered the editor.</p>
<p>If you supply links to the supermarket objects or the map where the problem occurred it may be easier for someone to give you a more authoritative answer.</p>
