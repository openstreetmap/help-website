<p>According to <a href="http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=201619152">http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=201619152</a></p>
<p>Smallingerland has admin_level 8 and Drachten has admin_level 10. So the nominatim result is correct. Since the boundaries were last edited by a Dutch mapper (sebastic) who knows what he is doing (he keeps track of all boundaries in the Netherlands, based on official data), I expect this is the way the Dutch community wants it to be.</p>
<p>Of course you can contact him to see whether the data in that area is correct.</p>
