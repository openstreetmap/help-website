<p>The is (as things go in the realm of opening hours and conditional restrictions) quite simple to enter:</p>
<pre><code>maxspeed:conditional= nn @ (Aug 20-Jun 15 Mo-FR 07:00-09:00)</code></pre>
<p>with nn the target maximum speed.</p>
