<div class="answer-body">
<p>I assume you want to use the <span>iD editor</span> on your website. You can customize everything in iD - it is opensource (code linked on the wiki page), I am not aware (did not see at OSM.org's use) of a method to pre-fill some fields by URL parameters.</p>
<p>However, do you want people to use the editor on your website to make changes to OSM data? Do you want to have an own database? Then you need much more than only a customized version of iD. It may be helpful for you to describe in mooooore words what you want to do.</p>
