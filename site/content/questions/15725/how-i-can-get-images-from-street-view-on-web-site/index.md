<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>Assuming that you're talking about a link to an open service that provides street-level photography, then there isn't an "official" OSM service that does that.</p>
<p>People have suggested such things in the past (see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OpenStreetView">here</a> for example) but I'm not aware of any that have actually taken off. If you're after a fully-formed service that you can deploy, then you're probably out of luck. If you're interested in developing something, it's probably worth searching through the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Main_Page">OSM wiki</a> to see what's already there and adapting / collaborating. It might also be worth asking about <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Video_mapping">video mapping</a>.</p>
<p>(I'm assuming that you're not asking about Google Streetview because (a) you don't mention Google in the question and (b) you're asking here and not at Google Help).</p>
