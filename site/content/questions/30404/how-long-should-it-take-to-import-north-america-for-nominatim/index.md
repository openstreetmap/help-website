<div class="answer-body">
<p>The numbers mean "I have completed 6.6 million objects on rank 26 in 126k seconds which means I can do 52 per second; I assume that it will take another 126k seconds to complete rank 26." - this means that rank 26 is going to take another 1.5 days. The ranks that come after 26, in my experience, aren't such a big deal but you'll have to wait and see.</p>
<p>The "slow place_id" is just a debug message that can help you find out which objects took exceptionally long to index. I usually ignore it - but if you have very many of these then it simply means your system is slow.</p>
</div>
