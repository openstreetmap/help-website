<p>Could you give an example of your tagging either by giving the exact tags used or by providing a link to the area? Cycling routes (as opposed to cycling ways) are often mapped with relations, which might be quite tricky for a beginner. Also, the tag is highway=cycleway (no space) and it can't be used on the SAME way as highway=pedestrian.</p>
