<p>The process, if you are using Potlatch2 editor is: - edit, choose the potlach2 editor. click on down arrow to the right of gps tab, then my tracks. Refresh list then tic the track you wish to see, it will show as a cyan (pale blue) line.</p>
<p>An alternative from map page is to click more: GPS traces : See Your Traces : Login :to see your traces : Click on the edit tab of trace you wish to edit. see screen grab below.</p>
