<p>I created a map and used the 'secret link' edit option. I was not logged into my OSM account. This was fine as I don't want the editors of the map to have to sign into umap.</p>
<p>I made the mistake of logging in to umap using my OSM login. Now all of my maps have transferred ownership and I do not have the option of removing myself as an owner. It seems a little crazy that just because I logged in and had been editing a map that it suddenly became linked to my account.</p>
<p>Any ideas on how to remove this ownership? Deleting the owner from the map settings page does not then allow me to hit submit.</p>
