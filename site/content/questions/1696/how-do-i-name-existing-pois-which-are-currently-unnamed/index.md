<div class="answer-body">
<p>My suggestion would be the following:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log into your account on <a href="http://OpenStreetMap.org">OpenStreetMap.org</a>, click on your user name (top right) and go to the "My Settings" page.</li>
<li>Scroll down and change the "Preferred Editor" to "potlatch2". This is the new, enhanced and much easier to use version of our online editing tool</li>
<li>Now go back to the main page, and find the area you want to improve. When the map is in the right place, click "Edit"</li>
<li>Wait for the editor to show up, and load all the information in your area</li>
<li>Click on a POI that you want to name. On the left-hand panel, find the name field and enter the name</li>
<li>When you're happy with your changes, press Save</li>
</ol>
<p>That's it! Changes will show up on the map after a few minutes. There are many other options for making edits to the map, but this is probably the easiest way to get started.</p>
