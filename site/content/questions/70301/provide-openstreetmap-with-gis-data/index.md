<div class="answer-body">
<p>Besides Simon Poole's good recommendations, if you are a GIS shop using ESRI software, you might consider submitting your aerial imagery to ESRI for inclusion in the "ESRI World Imagery" layer, which is actually a pretty big mash up of imagery of many different sources. They have the community maps program for that.</p>
<p>The "ESRI World Imagery" layer is currently available in iD and JOSM for OpenStreetMap users to base edits on, just like Bing has been for years.</p>
