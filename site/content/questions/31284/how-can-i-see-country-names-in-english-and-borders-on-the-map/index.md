<p>If your own language is English then you might want to have a look at <a href="http://open.mapquest.com">open.mapquest.com</a> which also uses OSM data, but tries to display everything in English where possible, rather than using the local names.</p>

<p>Note that OpenStreetMap (OSM) is not only one map or service. OSM at its core is just data. This data is used by <span>many services</span>. And if you are looking for routing, look at <span>page routing</span>.</p>
<p>An easy way to see the some of the different maps: Have a look at the "layers" button (fourth from the top on the right side) on <span>www.osm.org</span>. All those maps are based on the same OSM data but have a different styling.</p>
