<p>What do you mean by "overlay"? Do you mean to add data to the OSM database? Do you mean to do such a change to your own copy of the database and then render your own map of it? Do you mean to manually edit a static (screenshot) of a OSM-based map? Are you aware that some streets may have English names in the data (but not shown on our default map)? Note that <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Names#Avoid_transliteration">automatic transliteration</a> is possible if you just want to change the alphabet.</p>
