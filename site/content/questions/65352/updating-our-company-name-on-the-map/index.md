<p>The images on openstreetmap.org are not generated fresh each time, and old image will be stored for a little bit of time ("cached"), this improves rendering time. It will also be stored on your local web browser. Can you try clearing your browser cache, or opening a private browsing/incognito mode, or trying on another computer? That might show the updated details</p>
