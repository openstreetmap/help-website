<div class="answer-body">
<p>The GPX upload facility in OSM is to upload traces made with a GPS. This is to show you have actually surveyed what you are mapping.</p>
<p>If you create a route with RouteYou and upload it to OSM, then you are copying data from RouteYou to OSM. This is not allowed. It is both a violation of <a href="http://www.routeyou.com/page/view/23/routeyou-terms-of-use.en">the terms of use of RouteYou</a> and of the <a href="http://www.osmfoundation.org/wiki/License/Contributor_Terms">contributor terms of Openstreetmap</a>.</p>
