<div class="answer-body">
<p>Well, just remove those queries in the area rules file, which you don't need.</p>
<p>For a much stripped down version, you might want to take a look at the following wiki page: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Areas">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Areas</a> - that's the original version before Martin started working on <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_turbo/Polygon_Features">polygon features</a>. That wiki page also explains in more detail, how this all works.</p>
<p>If that's still too much, you might also want to remove some further queries:</p>
<pre><code>  &lt;query type=&quot;relation&quot;&gt;
    &lt;has-kv k=&quot;type&quot; v=&quot;multipolygon&quot;/&gt;
    &lt;has-kv k=&quot;name&quot;/&gt;
  &lt;/query&gt;
  &lt;query type=&quot;relation&quot;&gt;
    &lt;has-kv k=&quot;postal_code&quot;/&gt;
  &lt;/query&gt;
  &lt;query type=&quot;relation&quot;&gt;
    &lt;has-kv k=&quot;addr:postcode&quot;/&gt;
  &lt;/query&gt;</code></pre>
