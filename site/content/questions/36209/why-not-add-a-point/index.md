<div class="answer-body">
<p>Thanks for your first contributions.</p>
<p>There are a number of reasons. First it takes some time for the server to rerender the map with the additional data (please search this site for more elaborate answers), another one might be that the thing you added never gets rendered on the osm.org map. And a third reason might be that you did something wrong.</p>
<p>I just saw your supermarket and the restaurant of your last change appearing on the map. So just be patient, and give the poor server the time to rerender the area :-)</p>
<p>happy mapping</p>
