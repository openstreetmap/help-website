<p>This sounds like a bicycle version of a rumble strip to me. The 'road versions' are typically mapped as <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:traffic_calming"><code>traffic_calming=rumble_strip</code></a> which would probably be appropriate in your case as well.</p>
<p>I am not aware of any tags for the height of these sorts of features, they are normally in places that would place a surveyor at risk from the traffic.</p>
