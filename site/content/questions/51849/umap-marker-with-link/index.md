<p>In my example, I suppose that your data have an attribute "genus". Here is an example where the whole link is contained is an attribute "test_url" in the data. <a href="http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/fr/map/untitled-map_103107#7/49.074/7.218">http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/fr/map/untitled-map_103107#7/49.074/7.218</a></p>
<p>In this example, the popup content is:</p>
<pre><code># {name}
 [[{test_url}|link]]</code></pre>
