<div class="answer-body">
<p>Remember, Nominatim is fundamentally about <strong><em>names</em></strong>, i.e. named objects in OSM. Not every spot on the world's surface has its own name. So if you enter a location, I guess Nominatim will take you to the nearest named object it can find. There are lots of streets with no name in the vicinity, but Nominatim doesn't know about them: it's all about names.</p>
<p>At least, that's my best guess.</p>
