<p>Hello! I'm rather new to OSM and I need a little help choosing the tags/type for a specific kind of path I'm having trouble categorizing. Here are the specifics:</p>
<ul>
<li>These are paths within private garden allotments. But they're always open and non-members are allowed to walk through them.</li>
<li>A narrow gate at the entrance allows pedestrians and bicyclists access, but the gates are sometimes opened for cars if someone needs to bring something heavy to their garden.</li>
<li>Never seen snowmobiles, horses, or wheelchairs being used there.</li>
<li>Some paths are named and have signs like roads.</li>
<li>The surface type is mowed grass.</li>
<li>Example photos:</li>
<li><a href="http://pzd.pl/uploads/images/1/IMG_2107.JPG">http://pzd.pl/uploads/images/1/IMG_2107.JPG</a></li>
<li><a href="http://pzd.pl/uploads/images/P1040604.JPG">http://pzd.pl/uploads/images/P1040604.JPG</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=16/50.9379/17.2841">Region I'm working on.</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Any help will be greatly appreciated.</p>

<p>Probably...</p>
<p>highway=track, bicycle=permissive, foot=permissive, motor_vehicle=private, surface=grass, name=(whatever the name is)</p>
<p>(you could make an argument for highway=footway instead, but the width seems to merit =track to me. There are some Maoists out there who will suggest highway=path)</p>
