<p>I came through or stumbled over a cut that’s made in a dike and can be closed with temporarily doors? It is made to shorten the road, that otherwise has to go over the dike to reach the industrial development on the other side, with the risk to get flooded. The cut is closed with doors or timber to prevent flooding the low lands.</p>
<p>The wiki gives me no solution, Wikipedia has some pics.</p>
<p>I would consider; <code>man_made = dike / embankment</code> with <code>man_made = cut</code> or the French <code>coupure</code>? Include the wide and depth of the cut.</p>
