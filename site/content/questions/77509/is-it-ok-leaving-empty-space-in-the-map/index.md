<div class="question-body">
<p>I've noted that it's quite common that empty space is left in the map. A typical case is where forest cover don't go fully up to the shore line, then empty space is left to the water. Or if the forest cover does not go all the way up to the roadside, empty space on both sides of the road is left. Yet another example is when the road is very broad somewhere for some unclear reason (ie it's not strictly a parking) empty space is left for the road width.</p>
<p>My understanding is that a complete map really should have cover everywhere of something. On the other hand I have no idea what to tag those wide empty road areas with.</p>
