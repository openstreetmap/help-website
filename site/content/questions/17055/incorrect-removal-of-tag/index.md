<div class="answer-body">
<p>Adding tourism=hotel is acceptable (whatever you mean with acceptable), because that is just adding missing information.</p>
<p>Removing building=yes is (in this case) an error. Since nobody is infallible, it is acceptable that people make errors. Nobody is stopping you from fixing the error (by adding building=yes again) though.</p>
<p>You could also write a friendly message to the other mapper explaining that he made an error, so he is less likely to repeat it.</p>
