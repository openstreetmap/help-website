<p>No, it can't be done in JOSM. It is not a matter of an editor, it is a matter of the database.</p>
<p>Just build the 'T' by two lines which share a common point (not two points on the same place). Do the same for your island problem. Use different ways. That way, the two ways can also be tagged seperatly, e.g. with appropriate "oneway".</p>
