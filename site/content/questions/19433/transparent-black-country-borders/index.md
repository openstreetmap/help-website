<p>"OpenStreetMap" is really just the data (with a couple of example maps available on the home page).</p>
<p>The Cloudmade tiles are rendered by Cloudmade using OSM data - you'd need to talk to them about any change to the way that they look.</p>
<p>Other tile providers are available, and you can of course render your own and make them look however you want.</p>
