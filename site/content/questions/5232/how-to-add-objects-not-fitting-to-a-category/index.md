<p>If you really can't find a matching tag even after searching the wiki and consulting other mappers you may invent your own tag. Please make sure that the choosen tag matches other similar tags, doesn't clash with any existing tag and is extensible.</p>
<p>For a large (research) institute I'd suggest <code>amenity=institute</code> <code>institute=research</code> and <code>discipline=material sciences</code> or some such.</p>

<p>No you have not done anything wrong. The default rendering (as well as the simple edit mode) simply don't know about rare objects like research institutes and consequently don't render them. As the reseach institute is in a building you should also add "building=yes" to give the renderer a rough idea what the object is.</p>
</div>
