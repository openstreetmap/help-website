<p>How do we tag such symbols on the road. lanes=2 turn:lanes=?</p>
<p>360 image mapillary, drag to see the road symbols. <a href="https://www.mapillary.com/map/im/j5idU0L_U1s2kQzGfpRJEA">situation 1</a></p>
<p>The lanes are separated by a kerb. People want to draw two lanes, because you can not changes lanes.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.mapillary.com/map/im/dZNIxfgPLzer3ToMCzf_KQ">situation 2</a></p>
<p>You need to choose the right lane in front.</p>
<p>I did see this page <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:turn#Turning_indications_per_lane">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:turn#Turning_indications_per_lane</a> and the JOSM turn lanes plugin. Like to see the symbols in the JOSM plugin.</p>
<p>For me straight over (roundabout) is not through. Through is following the roundabout lane and the turn in to the direction.</p>
