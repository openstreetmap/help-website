<div class="answer-body">
<p>Most mappers would likely use <code>name</code> for the official name and <code>loc_name</code> for the local name. It's a grey area though, and at your (the mapper's) discretion - if you feel that the local name is so well established that somebody asking for directions to "Nathan Strong Park" would be met with a shrug, then maybe you should use <code>name</code> for the name that almost everyone knows, and record the official name as a side note under <code>official_name</code>.</p>
