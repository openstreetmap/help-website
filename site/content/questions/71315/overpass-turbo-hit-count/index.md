<p>Based on the pharmacy example <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Overpass_API_by_Example#Count_Pharmacies_per_County_.28updated_0.7.54.29">on this page</a> I tried</p>
<pre><code>[out:json][timeout:25];
(
  node[&quot;highway&quot;=&quot;bus_stop&quot;]({{bbox}});
  way[&quot;highway&quot;=&quot;bus_stop&quot;]({{bbox}});
  relation[&quot;highway&quot;=&quot;bus_stop&quot;]({{bbox}});
);
out count;</code></pre>
<p>which for the area selected returned</p>
<pre><code>{
  &quot;version&quot;: 0.6,
