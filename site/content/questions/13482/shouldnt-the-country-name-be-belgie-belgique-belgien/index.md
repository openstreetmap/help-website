<p>Hi,</p>
<p>I just recently discovered openstreetmaps, and i quite enjoy using it. (Actually I also made a small donation to help you guys :D )</p>
<p>However, there's this one thing that bothers me tremendously. I just don't understand why the name "BELGIQUE BELGIË BELGIEN" is used for Belgium.</p>
<p>With 60% of the population, Dutch/Flemish-speakers are the majority in Belgium. Whereas French speakers and German-speakers count for respectively 40% and 1% or so. And even when you sort the names alphabetically "België" should come before "Belgique".</p>
<p>Since language can be a rather sensitive issue in Belgium, I hope someone can make the effort of changing the name to "BELGIË BELGIQUE BELGIEN".</p>
<p>thank you in advance. with kind regards, ben</p>
