<p>You can rotate an object in JOSM by selecting it, pressing ctrl-shift and then using the mouse.</p>
<p>This is described in the JOSM status bar at the bottom of the edit window.</p>
<p>Or did you want a numeric rotation angle value? I am not aware of being able to enter "turn 7 degrees".</p>
