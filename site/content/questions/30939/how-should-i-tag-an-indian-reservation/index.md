<p>I doubt that protected_area is correct.</p>
<p>As I understand it, reservations are semi-automous self-governing areas where the state(s) they are in have limited authority. And the boundaries for them are not generally the same as the conventional counties that are under a state in hierarchy. And in some cases a reservation may extend across state boundaries. So they are, in some respects, at the same level as states in the legal hierarchy.</p>
<p>I think they should have a boundary relation but haven't got a clue what the boundary should be tagged as.</p>
