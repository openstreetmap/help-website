<p>Sorry, no : turning an area (farmyard, residential, or similar) into a multipolygon just to punch "building holes" into it is not considered good OSM practice. The first reason is that those buildings actually are part of the farmyard/residential/etc. The second reason is that using multipolygons for this would mean to use them <em>everywhere</em>, and that is so cumbersome that mappers prefer other solutions.</p>
</div>
