<p>In the OpenCycleMap style that you're looking at, a dashed line indicates a track.</p>
<p>Styling decisions like this are based on the underlying OSM data for that road/path. You can always see the underlying data by clicking the "?" tool on the right (the bottom one) and then clicking the road/path.</p>
