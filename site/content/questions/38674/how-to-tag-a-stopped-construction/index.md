<p>I also thought of "<code>abandoned:</code>". In fact this one: <code>abandoned:building=construction</code> (5 times in taginfo) (since it was a building under construction when which was abandoned – matters for the look if it was finished or not, IMHO). And <code>construction=house/apartments/…</code> to specify the building type which was in construction.</p>
<p>The whole area was <code>landuse=construction</code> before, so, now, it should be <code>abandoned:landuse=construction</code> (9 times in taginfo), shouldn't it?</p>
