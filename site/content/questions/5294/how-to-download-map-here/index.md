<p>Well I dont have navdroyd but I assume it works the same way as mapdroyd....</p>
<p>You download the map data inside the app itself on your phone. Press 'menu', 'Manage maps' and chose the download you want.</p>
<p>The map data comes from OpenStreetMap (originally) but you don't need to download it from here yourself.</p>
<p>The neat thing though, is that when you see things which are missing/wrong you can edit the map on <a href="http://OpenStreetMap.org">OpenStreetMap.org</a> Make corrections and eventually (with a delayed update cycle) you'll see your improvements inside the app.</p>
