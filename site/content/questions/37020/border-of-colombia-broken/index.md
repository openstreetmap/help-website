It still had a large portion of the border missing on the eastern side, and i realized that most of it was missing on top of the amazon river. I then drew rivers in also and only the border with Brazil was missing (not including the rivers along the border as they were drawn from the rivers).</p>
<p>Heres what it looks like:</p>
<p><img src="http://i.imgur.com/v9wLb2R.png" alt="alt text" /></p>
<p>Is there a tag I am missing? How can I get the rest of the border? Thanks in advance.</p>
