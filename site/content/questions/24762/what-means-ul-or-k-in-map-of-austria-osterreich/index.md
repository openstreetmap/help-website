<div class="answer-body">
<p>Always try to provide a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Permalink">permalink</a> to the area in question.</p>
<p>I did find two bicycle routes. One called <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/relation/2404945">Über drüber Landpartie</a> which has the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:ref">ref</a> <em>ÜL</em> and one called <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/relation/1611520">Kultur-Radweg</a> which has the ref <em>K</em>. These references just serve as identifiers and are mostly locally unique. But you will only <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=48.0769&amp;lon=14.3084&amp;zoom=12&amp;layers=C">see them on the map</a> with the OpenCycleMap (in German Radfahrerkarte) layer activated. The standard layer <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=48.0769&amp;lon=14.3084&amp;zoom=12&amp;layers=M">doesn't show</a> bicycle routes.</p>
