<p>The map layer on www.openstreetmap.org does not have speed limits, although you can of course see the speed limit if you inspect an individual road that you have identified in the <span>"data" overlay</span> (provided that a speed limit has been entered at all).</p>
<p>There are several third-party maps that show speed limits as tagged in OpenStreetMap; one is the <a href="http://www.openstreetbrowser.org">OpenStreetBrowser</a> which has a maxspeed display under Transportation -&gt; Individual Traffic -&gt; Maxspeed.</p>
</div>
