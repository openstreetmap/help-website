<p>Hello, heck of a job working that one out.</p>
<p>To display the three routing items, ('+flag' (add points)'-flag'(remove points)and 'drag points') in the left panel you must click on the double right pointing symbol, (something like this &gt;&gt;). That brings up a new panel on the left where you can enable the three buttons, tick beside the item means its enabled/displayed.</p>
<p>This routing is new to me, took a while to figure it out and IMHO it's not worth the effort. Much better options dedicated to routing.</p>
<p>Some tips I found out, download a layer of data to more than cover your intended route. Open the Mapnik image layer to better show the streets, click on the green routing nodes.</p>
<p>Regards Bernard</p>
