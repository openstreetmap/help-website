<p>Hi - I've recently caught the OSM bug and wanted to check some details.</p>
<p>There are a few private gardens and the seem to be tagged differently and I wanted to check/find out the best method.</p>
<p>example1 - <a href="https://osm.org/go/evW~qx8Av">https://osm.org/go/evW~qx8Av</a> Here the garden is tagged as an area but lacks the barrier tag</p>

<p>What is the preferred method to map&amp;tag so the garden is tagged with barrier?</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is no requirement for anyone to add all detail that exists. That is, one is free to tag a garden and tag or not tag the fence that surrounds it (or vice versa).</p>
<p>As to the correct tagging if you want to map a garden that is bounded by a fence, you could:</p>
<ol>
<li>draw one way, and add both leisure=garden and barrier=fence tags to that way</li>
<li>draw two overlapping ways, adding leisure=garden to one, and barrier=fence to the other</li>
</ol>
<p>I've seen both methods used. With either method, a small gate or entrance can be tagged on one of the nodes (with barrier=entrance, barrier=gate, etc). If the fence does not completely surround the garden, or there is a large gap in it, the second method (separate ways) is probably best.</p>
