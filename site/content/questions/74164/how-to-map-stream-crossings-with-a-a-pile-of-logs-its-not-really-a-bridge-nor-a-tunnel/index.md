<p>What's happening when that stream carries some water? Does it run between the logs or is the path flooded with water running across it?</p>
<p>If you don't get your feet wet I would say the current tagging with <code>tunnel=culvert</code>on the steam is ok. Tagging it as a bridge wouldn't be out the way either. It's one of those corner cases.</p>
<p>If the path gets flooded, though, I would neither tag bridge nor tunnel but a ford. To do that you have to connect highway and waterway with a common node and tag it with <code>ford=yes</code>.</p>
