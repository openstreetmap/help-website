<div class="answer-body">
<p>When you are logged in here on this help site, there should be* a check mark/tick symbol (<img src="/m/default/media/images/vote-accepted.png" alt="check mark" />) just below the answer up/down vote buttons (<img src="/m/default/media/images/vote-arrow-up.png" alt="up" />, <img src="/m/default/media/images/vote-arrow-down.png" alt="down" />) left to each answer. Looks like this:</p>
<p><img src="/upfiles/tick.png" alt="screenshot of the vote section left to an answer" /></p>
<p>Click that check mark (<img src="/m/default/media/images/vote-accepted.png" alt="check mark" /> → <img src="/m/default/media/images/vote-accepted-on.png" alt="green check mark" />), and you have accepted this answer and set your question to "answered".</p>
<p>* <span class="small">This does <a href="/questions/7416/unable-to-accept-own-answer">not (yet) work for own answers</a>.</span></p>
