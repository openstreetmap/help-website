<p>Hi, To start with obviously highway=(path/track) and bridge=yes, layer=1, handrail=no, surface=wood and width=?(in metres ie 0.5).</p>
<p>You can also add the allowed or restricted traffic ie foot=yes, bicycle=no wheelchair=no as the case may be.</p>
<p>A description=??? tag can say for example "Rough log bridge might be difficult to cross. The stream underneath may be passable at the peak of the dry season but even then not always. If you are very good at jumping from rock to rock, it may be an option."</p>
<p>Any combination or all of the above are OK</p>

<p>You might also consider adding the <code>hazard=*</code> key, especially if there is any kind warning posted on the trail or in official descriptions of the trail.
