<div class="answer-body">
<p>Normally in OSM, only bus route path and stops are mapped. Since the route is the same for both AM and PM, a different start/end terminal information would be included in the GTFS for that route. GTFS includes many attributes that do not fit into the OSM geographic data.</p>
