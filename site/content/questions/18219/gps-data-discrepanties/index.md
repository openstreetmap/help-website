<p>You were at 52.25836°, 5.14985° in the decimal degree notation. Note that there is only one decimal dot in each number. The number you got from your Garmin seems to be in <em>another degree notation format</em> ("52.15.464 - 5.09.049"). With correct symbols that would be 52°15.464', 5°09.049'. Note that there is a &gt;'&lt; sign in addition to the &gt;°&lt;. Solution: Go into your Garmin's settings and change the display format for coordinates. That is what andy mackey referred to above (06 Dec, 10:38).</p>
