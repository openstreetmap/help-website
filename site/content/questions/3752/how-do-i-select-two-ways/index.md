<div class="answer-body">
<p>In Potlatch 2 (hover over the 'Edit' tab and select Potlatch 2), you select two ways by control-clicking (or command if you're on a Mac).</p>
<p>But if you're creating a turn restriction, you don't want to do that anyway.</p>
<p>Select the node where the roads meet. <em>Make sure that the ways end at this node - you can't create a restriction if the ways don't end there.</em> Press Enter just to make sure you're not in 'extend the way' mode.</p>
<p>Click 'Restrictions' on the left. Using the menus, choose the road that you're not allowed to turn from; the road you're not allowed to turn to; and the type of restriction.</p>
<p>Hey presto. You have a turn restriction.</p>
