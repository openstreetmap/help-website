<div class="answer-body">
<p>With physical separation, you are allowed to do either, as in <code>highway=footway</code> + <code>footway=sidewalk</code> vs <code>highway=</code> + <code>sidewalk=</code>. On this topic, some are using <code>cycleway:*=separate</code> to replace the <code>cycleway:*=track</code> once the <code>highway=cycleway=</code> has been drawn, as a counterpart to <code>sidewalk:*=separate</code>.</p>
<p>If you choose <code>cycleway:*=track</code> for now, it is helpful to add <code>cycleway:*:oneway=no</code>.</p>
