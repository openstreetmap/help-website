<div class="answer-body">
<p>Since all 3 things are located in separated buildings / places, you can place 3 different nodes. Some people will map the amenity=fuel to the whole area of the gas station though. The 2 others can then be mapped as areas/buildings inside the gas station area or as nodes. This depends on how much detail you want to add.</p>
<p>I would not map them all 3 on the same node.</p>
