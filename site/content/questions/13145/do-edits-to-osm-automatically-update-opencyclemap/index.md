<p>I have just added a Road fron Asurias, North of Balamban in Cebu, heading East to 'Danasan Eco Adventure Park'.</p>
<p>This edit seems to appear immediately when viewed in Open Street Map view.<br />
However if one switches to Open Cycle Map, the added Road/Track can't be seen.<br />
</p>
<p><strong>Is the update to OSM, reflected in Open Cycle Map, later?</strong> <em>(or is some action required to implement an update there)</em></p>
<p>We had '<strong>Green Trail Ride #25</strong>' on Sun 20th May and used the GPS Track Log of the ride, from the Eco Adventure Park to West Coast, and then down to Balamban, to update the <strong>Open Steet Map</strong>.</p>
<p>Sorry we did not note where the dirt Track changed to Paved, and then back to Dirt/Gravel again.<br />
</p>
<p>Seems a lot of Road Improvements, with widening evident and grading and some sections. Concreted also (single lane initially) in sections.</p>
<p>Check out these Road Improvements on <strong>YouTube Video</strong> taken by <strong>Brett</strong> on his <strong>GoPro 960</strong> helmet-cam:-</p>
<p><a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RZlKnXt-8ks&amp;feature=g-all-u">GTR25 Danao Eco Park to Asturias.wmv</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcWrfsQoRiA&amp;feature=g-all-u">GTR25 Danao Eco Park to Asturias (part2).wmv</a></p>
