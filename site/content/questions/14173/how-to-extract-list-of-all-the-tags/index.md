<div class="answer-body">
<p>You probably do not want do extract the complete list of tags being used in OSM.</p>
<p>If you really wanted to then you could do this:</p>
<pre><code>wget -O- http://planet.openstreetmap.org/planet-latest.osm.bz2 | grep &quot;&lt;tag&quot; | cut -d\&quot; -f2 | sort -u &gt; my-tag-list.txt</code></pre>
<p>This will yield a list of about 35,000 tag keys currently in use.</p>
<p>A better presented tag listing is available on <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org">http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org</a>, and there you can also download sqlite files that contain lists of tags.</p>
</div>
