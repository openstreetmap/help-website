<p>Buna tuturor, lucrez la un proiect care implica realizarea locurilor de parcare in orasul Viena. Ce incercam noi sa facem este sa aducem cat mai multa informatie posbila in OSM, pentru a le putea furniza oanemilor care folosesc aceasta aplicatie. Intrebarea mea este: exista vreun tool in OSM care sa sa imi permita sa fac acest lucru? De exemplu, in momentul in care dau click pe un punct, sa primesc informatii in legatura cu: numele strazii, districtul, GPS data etc...Ce vreau sa fac este sa pot importa, intensifica si modifica datele deja existente, sau chiar incarca alte noi date. Va multumesc anticipat!</p>

<p>OpenStreetMap isn't just the website that you see at openstreetmap.org - lots of people take openstreetmap data and create websites with entirely different functionality from it. Some also use map tiles from openstreetmap.org (or another provider) and display those within a different website. One example that does something a bit like what you seem to be asking for is <a href="http://openpoimap.org/">http://openpoimap.org/</a>, but I'm sure that there are lots of others. See also the list <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/List_of_OSM-based_services">here</a> for other examples.</p>
