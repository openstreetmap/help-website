<div class="answer-body">
<p>What technique did you use to download the map?</p>
<p>Garmins typically only recognize maps named gmapsupp.img. The roads you see in Central America may be on the basemap. Try renaming your North America map and then renaming the new map back to gmapsupp.img. Some units allow two different copies of gmapsupp.img, one internal and one on SD but try that later after you confirm the problem.</p>
