<div class="answer-body">
<p>There's a few ways you can do this:</p>
<ul>
<li>If the relation exists already, download the relation itself by downloading one member the usual way. Then select that relation via its member, click <code>edit</code> in the tags/membership sidebar, and click the <code>download all incomplete members</code> or <code>download selected incomplete members</code> button in the relation editing dialog.</li>
<li>If you know the obect ids, you can download them "manually" using the <code>file-&gt;download object</code> menu.</li>
<li>You can use the <code>download_along</code> and/or <code>waydownloader</code> plugins to dowload osm data along a way</li>
<li>You can download osm data area by area (as long as your machine is powerful enough). Using the <code>file-&gt;download in current view</code> menu (consider adding it to your toolbar or as a keyboard shortcut) makes that pretty easy.</li>
</ul>
