<p>Oh sorry I misunderstood the question. Thought it's asking about how to show a shop provides a kerb-side pickup.</p>
<p>One can simply add a <code>=parking_space</code> + <code>capacity=</code> to represent the entirety. <code>parking_space=pickup</code> is likely a synonym with passenger pick-up. <p>I doubt pedestrian is fully relevant here. It's common for take-aways to be transferred outside a restaurant.</p>
