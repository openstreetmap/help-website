<div class="answer-body">
<p>OsmAnd will show opening in the search and Open/Closed status when you tap on a POI if the information is present in the OSM data. You can also use "Open now" as a filter when searching (slider icon on right in image below).</p>
<p><img src="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/w/images/thumb/d/db/Screenshot_osmand_2.0.4_POI.png/270px-Screenshot_osmand_2.0.4_POI.png" alt="Screenshot from wiki" /></p>
<p>I don't know about service times though, nothing is showing in the search, but I also haven't found anything near me that has the information in OSM.</p>
<p>Note that I am using the Android version of OsmAnd, but it seems like an odd thing to be missing from the iOS version.</p>
