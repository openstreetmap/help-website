<p>Hi, The four sections of the M/S Norppa II route are held in Relation: Hurissalo - Lintusalo (12584568) <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/12584568">https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/12584568</a></p>
<p>Any corrections or additions to the route must be made via this relation.</p>
<p>I cannot open/access the Visit Finland DataHub, I think you have to subscribe to get in. Therefore I cannot see the image or circle you describe so cannot physically help further.</p>
