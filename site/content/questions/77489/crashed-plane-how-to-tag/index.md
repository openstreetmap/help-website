<blockquote>
<p>historic=aircraft it's not perfectly suitable, since it isn't a tourist attraction</p>
</blockquote>
<p>I can't see any reason why "historic" tags can only be attached to tourist attractions? At least in the UK, where I've looked at the correlation between historic and tourism in some detail, there are lots of "historic" things with "tourism" tags but also many without.</p>
<p>The current tagging looks OK to me, but I'm not familiar with the area. Depending on what state it is in, <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=historic%3Daircraft">taginfo</a> suggests possibly "aircraft_wreck".</p>
