<div class="answer-body">
<p>You need to visit the page of the relevant proposal and see if the voting stage is open. I think <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Proposed_features/shelter_type">shelter type</a> is the only proposal with voting open currently. If you visit that page and scroll down to the Voting section you will see the information about how to vote on that proposal, the key bit being</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Please vote with {{vote|yes}} or {{vote|no}} and sign with ~~~~</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p>so you would click [edit] to the right of the Voting title, scroll down to the end of the section and add either</p>
<blockquote>
<p>{{vote|yes}} ~~~~</p>
</blockquote>
<p>or</p>
<blockquote>
<p>{{vote|no}} ~~~~</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The ~~~~ bit inserts your wiki login name and the date and time you voted. You will have to have created a wiki login and logged in before being able to edit, as these are different from the OSM login.</p>
<p>Voting on any proposal is done in a similar way, but not all remember to include such helpful instructions as on the shelter type one.</p>
</div>
