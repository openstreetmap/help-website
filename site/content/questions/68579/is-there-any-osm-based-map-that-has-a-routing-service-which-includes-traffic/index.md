<p>To answer the question in the title: Magic Earth uses live traffic in routing. Maps.me at least shows traffic but I am not sure it uses it in routing.</p>
<p>For the second question: I think TomTom HD traffic can be used by third party apps. There is also Open Traffic. But I cannot tell of either how easy to use or reliable they are.</p>
<p>There may certainly be more.</p>
