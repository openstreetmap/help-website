<p>According to Taginfo there are 58 uses of shop=wholesale. The disadvantage with this is obviously that the information "supermarket" is lost.</p>
<p>So I would suggest combining the two.</p>
<p>shop=wholesale</p>
<p>wholesale=supermarket</p>
