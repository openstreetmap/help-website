<div class="answer-body">
<p>Interpreting freely, <code>=yes</code> would mean a yes anywhere, <code>=no</code> a no everywhere. You could try <code>outdoor_seating=yes</code> + <code>outdoor_seating:dog=yes</code> + <code>indoor_seating:dog=yes</code> but this has no support, only informative use to editors and future reference. It would be best to add <code>dog=yes</code> to a <code>leisure=outdoor_seating</code>, and <code>dog=no</code> to eg the <code>building=retail</code>. This doesn't replace the <code>dog=yes</code> on the <code>amenity=restaurant</code>.</p>
