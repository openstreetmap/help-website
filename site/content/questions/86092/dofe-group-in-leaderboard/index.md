<p>It looks as though these edits should appear on the MM Leaderboard, but for some reason they are not (I checked other hashtags on the edits and they do not appear under the other hashtags.</p>
<p>An alternative way of searching for the changesets would be to use OSMCha (login needed using OSM id): <a href="https://osmcha.org/?filters=%7B%22comment%22%3A%5B%7B%22label%22%3A%22%23aliensesu%22%2C%22value%22%3A%22%23aliensesu%22%7D%5D%2C%22date__gte%22%3A%5B%7B%22label%22%3A%222022-11-01%22%2C%22value%22%3A%222022-11-01%22%7D%5D%7D">this</a> is a query which should pull changesets with your hashtag. It many need a bit of tinkering with (area, time etc) to meet your needs.</p>
