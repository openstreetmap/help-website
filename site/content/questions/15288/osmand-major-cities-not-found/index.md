<div class="answer-body">
<p>In many cases, this is due to the redaction. The maps were compiled just after the redaction and thus there are some issues.</p>
<p>A city should have a city node and a closed boundary around it with the same name as the city node before it's indexed.</p>
<p>If it is indeed the fault of the redaction, it will most likely already be repaired. So you only need to wait for new OsmAnd maps, or compile them yourself (see the <a href="http://code.google.com/p/osmand/wiki/HowToArticles#How_To_Prepare_own_data_to_use_offline">FAQ</a>)</p>
