<p>I think I begin to understand your question. You want to add a new article to that specific display ?</p>
<p>As I was saying before, this has nothing to do with OSM, it just provides the background.</p>
<p>If you check the neighboring <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Banora">Banora</a> article, it has coordinates in the infobox, and top right corner. IIRC these coordinates might be stored in the associated wikidata element.</p>
<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tomba_Kanssa">Tomba kanssa</a> on the other hand, has coordinates in the body of the page, which might not be parsed by automatic tools.</p>
<p>Or maybe it's just that it is located at the bottom of the page ? Or that the database of <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/WIWOSM">WIWOSM</a> is lagging behind ? The documentation is outdated, so it's hard to know for sure.</p>
<p>I see that you've just added a <code>wikipedia=</code> link to <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/10961683446">Tomba Kanssa</a>, but <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/2535980101">Banora</a> doesn't have one, and already shows on WIWOSM.</p>
<p>So I think this is a Wikipedia question, I hope you'll find more precise help there.</p>
<p>You may not have found that <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:How_to_add_geocodes_to_articles">documentation</a>.</p>
<p>Regards.</p>

<p>Bonjour,</p>
<p>Pour associer un élément de la base de données OSM à un article wikipedia, il y a un tag dédié : <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FR:Key:wikipedia"><code>wikipedia=</code></a> (et l'équivalent pour <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FR:Key:wikidata"><code>wikidata</code></a>).</p>
<p>Il y a quelques projets qui permettent d'afficher ces liens sur une carte.</p>
<p>Par exemple <a href="https://map.openseamap.org/">OpenSeaMap</a>, en cliquant le menu "View" puis "Wikipedia-links".</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetbrowser.org/">OpenStreetBrowser</a> propose aussi une catégorie "Wikipedia" dans le menu "OpenStreetMap Quality Control".</p>
<p>Il y en a probablement d'autres, mais je ne les retrouve pas.</p>
<p>Cordialement.</p>
