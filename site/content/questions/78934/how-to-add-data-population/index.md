<p>I assume you are referring to adding an (estimated?) population tag to place elements as in <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:population">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:population</a></p>
<p>I don't see a reason why you shouldn't, but give your source (estimation, or other way you arrived at the number) in the changeset source field and on the object itself with source:population .</p>
