<p>I found a wrong writing for a mountain ridge in AUSTRIA/Vorarlberg leading to the "MÖRZELSPITZE" with <strong>Laubachgrad</strong>. I was hiking there about 3 weeks ago. The correct writíng should be <strong>Laubachgrat</strong>. So, I was quite happy being able to change the wrong to the correct writing, i.e. from <strong>grad</strong> to <strong>grat</strong> (<em>-grad</em> means "degree" in german, <em>-grat</em> means "ridge" in german). Funny enough, when I look up now, the writing is still wrong down to the scale of 1:30. When I go further down to say 1:20, 1:10 or even as low as 1:5, it is only then that appears the corrected and correct spelling, i.e. <strong>Laubachgrat</strong>. So, why is that so??</p>
