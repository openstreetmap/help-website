<p>See <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/?key=bridge#combinations">http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/?key=bridge#combinations</a> The key <em>material</em> is used 534 times with the bridge key. Or <em>bridge:material</em> / <em>building:material</em> (if a bridge should be regarded as a building ...). The key <em>material</em> is common at benches: <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/amenity=bench#combinations">http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/amenity=bench#combinations</a></p>
