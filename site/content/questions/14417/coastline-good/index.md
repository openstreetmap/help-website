<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetMap itself does not publish a shape file named "coastline good". You might be referring something made available by MapBox, in which case the proper place to discuss that would be their support forums.</p>
<p>Jochen Topf publishes a series of coastline shape files on his <a href="http://openstreetmapdata.com/">http://openstreetmapdata.com/</a> site and he claims that these are correct (or at least, as correct as possible with OSM data).</p>
