<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hello and welcome to OSM, I see you're using the iD editor so in that case click the first section to highlight/select it, shift+click to highlight/select the next section, (shift+click any other section to be merged), with all relevant sections highlighted/selected click "merge these lines". The area's perimeter should be merged into one way. Regards Bernard</p>
<p>Edit. Having answered the question I then looked at the building in JOSM (my preferred editor) and see that the building is in fact a multipolygon. The two sections of it's perimeter are joined in a relation, so there is no need to physically join them.</p>
