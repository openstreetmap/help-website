<p>Update:</p>
<p>I bought one, a Huion H580 (about $50 US), and have been experimenting with it for a few days. For many routine edits, it's not much help, the mouse does as well. For curving roads and rivers and lakes, it's faster and you can make smoother lines.</p>
<p>It worked right out of the box on Potlatch. You can use your regular mouse at the same time.</p>
<p>The tablet recognizes its pen when it's about a cm above the tablet. When you touch the surface, it makes a node. You can drag and select same as a mouse.</p>
<p>Conclusions:</p>
<p>Great toy, not much documentation, lots of potential to customize, if you can figure out how. Works well for drawing lakes and canals and curved roads. Easy. Should also have other uses.</p>
