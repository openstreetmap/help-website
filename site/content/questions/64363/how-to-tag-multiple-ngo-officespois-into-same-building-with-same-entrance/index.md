<div class="answer-body">
<p>The exact semantics of the building tag values are a bit murky, and often building "style" is mixed up with use. IMHO adding building=school and building:use=office (see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:building:use)">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:building:use)</a> is the best solution.</p>
<p>And yes, tagging individual points is the thing to do for multiple offices (add level tags if they make sense).</p>
