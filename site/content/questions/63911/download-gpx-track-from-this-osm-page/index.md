<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can download GPX routes from <a href="https://waymarkedtrails.org">https://waymarkedtrails.org</a>.</p>
<ul>
<li>Go to <a href="https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org">https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org</a></li>
<li>Enter "Greenway Praha-Wien" into the search field and select your cycle route from the results -&gt; <a href="https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org/#route?id=108494">https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org/#route?id=108494</a></li>
<li>Click on "Show on map" to verify if it matches your expectations</li>
<li>Click on "GPX" to download the track</li>
</ul>
