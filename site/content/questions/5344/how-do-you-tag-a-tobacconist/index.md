<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>Click 'Edit' for the area of the map you're looking at.</p>
<p>Double-click the spot on the map where you want to place the tobacconist. A green dot will appear. Click 'Advanced' in the bottom left.</p>
<p>Find the tags that are suitable. The easiest way to do this is by searching <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.de/">taginfo</a> to see what other users have done. Look at both the 'Keys' and 'Values' tabs. In this case, <code>shop=tobacco</code> is the most common combination, with 372 uses so far.</p>
<p>In the Advanced panel, add a row with 'shop' and 'tobacco'.</p>
<p>Save. You're done!</p>
