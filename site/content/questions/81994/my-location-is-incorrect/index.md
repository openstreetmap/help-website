<p>Just a guess - but this could be about the <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/profile/edit">OSM user profile</a> and the "Show my location" (for Wifi/IP geolocation) button on OSM's main slippy map.</p>
<p>These are two completely different things - one is meant for community interaction between nearby mappers, the other is supposed to move the map to your <em>current</em> location.</p>
