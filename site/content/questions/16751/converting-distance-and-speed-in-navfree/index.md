<p>Navfree is software that uses OpenStreetMap data. This help site is typically for OpenStreetMap itself.</p>
<p>Normally, you'll have the best chance for support/help by visiting the author's website. Navfree's is <a href="http://www.navmii.com/support/faq#q9">http://www.navmii.com/support/faq#q9</a></p>
<p>The answer to your question is found on their website: <em>"The setting is available from the International Preferences menu Menu -&gt; Advanced -&gt; Preferences -&gt; International In the further updates this menu item will be renamed to "Distance Units" "</em></p>
