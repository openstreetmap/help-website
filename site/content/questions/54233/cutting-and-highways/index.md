<p>The explanation most likely is that those who edited the highways you are looking at just didn't care to add cutting=yes tags as these tags doesn't really have any importance for navigation. If you feel for it though, you are welcome to add them. Please be aware of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/en:KISS_principle">The KISS principle</a> though (avoid adding too much unneeded complexity).</p>
