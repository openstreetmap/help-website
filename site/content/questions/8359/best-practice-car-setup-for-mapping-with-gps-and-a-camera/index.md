<p>No need to get too fancy. You can mount gps devices on your dashboard. And cameras can be mounted on the windshield. See <a href="http://www.ehow.com/how_4431289_mount-camera-car-filming.html.">http://www.ehow.com/how_4431289_mount-camera-car-filming.html.</a> If you want you can add a swivel mount for a laptop so you could be sitting in the driver seat with the laptop on the side. Not recommended while driving of course. Using an ap like OSMand, you can post directly to OSM without using josm.</p>
