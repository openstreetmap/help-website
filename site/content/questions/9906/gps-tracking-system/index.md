<div class="answer-body">
<p>This depends on what system you are using. If you have a Garmin device you can <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin">convert OSM data to the garmin format</a> and use that. If you have a tablet or a TomTom you can use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Navit">Navit</a>. Other devices may be possible.</p>
