<p>Hi, We have big buildings with several entrances. Building has its number/name, each entrance is labeled A, B, C... In wiki it is not quite well explained how to address each entrance in that case. Building has addr:housenumber tag. Entrances are tagged with entrance=yes. Except that, how to add <code>addr:</code> to entrances?</p>
<ol>
<li><code>addr:unit</code></li>
<li><code>addr:housenumber</code> (different from housenumber of the building?)</li>
<li><code>addr:housenumber</code> (building's number) and <code>addr:unit</code></li>
<li><code>addr:housenumber</code> and <code>addr:entrance</code> (proposed, but inactive tag)</li>
</ol>
<p>Or simply <code>ref</code> or <code>name</code> is enough?</p>
<p>Thanks.</p>
<p>EDIT: Behind each entrance there are abt. 20-30 flats (apartments). <code>addr:flats=1-20</code> could be added as suggested in the wiki. Each apartment has a postal address in the form: "BUILDING 1, ENTRANCE "A", FLOOR 1, APARTMENT 1".</p>
