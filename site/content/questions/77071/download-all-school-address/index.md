<p>First you need to get the area to search within. Then ensure your searching for all types (NWR means Nodes,Ways,Relations) Output to both centre-points &amp; perimeter boundaries so they can all be viewed when zoomed out.</p>
<pre><code>area[name=&quot;Airdrie&quot;];
nwr[amenity=school](area);
out center;
out geom;</code></pre>
<p><a href="https://overpass-turbo.eu/s/Z37">https://overpass-turbo.eu/s/Z37</a></p>
