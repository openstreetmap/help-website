<p>No, but the passage for the water could be tagged <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:tunnel%3Dculvert">tunnel=culvert</a>.</p>
<p>As there are two non-intersecting things passing at different elevations, setting the appropriate <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:layer">layer=*</a> tag(s) would also be useful.</p>
