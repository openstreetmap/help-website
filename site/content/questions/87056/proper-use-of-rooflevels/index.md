<p>Hi, Yes a roof level is a roof level whether it has windows or not, and there can be several levels within a roof. The two pictures in your link above show, (from the arrangement of the windows), that there are two levels within the roof, hence roof:levels=2 in those instances. Without windows as a guide, you can only assume that there is one roof level.</p>
