<div class="answer-body">
<p>The update schedule of any of the renderings of OSM are set by the people rendering them. The default rendering on openstreetmap.org is updated unusually frequently, often in minuets, so that people doing the mapping can see how their edits have changed things. Other renderings are usually every couple weeks or months, depending on the goals and resources of the people who are rendering them. I can't answer specifically for the daylight map, but you may be able to find more information from the website of the group doing the rendering.</p>
