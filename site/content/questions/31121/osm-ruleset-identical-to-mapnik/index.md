<p>Not a direct answer to your question, but an alternative solution might be to use a desktop alternative to Maperitive such as <a href="https://www.mapbox.com/tilemill/">TileMill</a>. That can use the actual OSM stylsheet (so maps will look identical), but you'll probably need a small local database to store the data in. There's an extensive walkthrough at the TileMill site, but it will take a little while to get up to speed with it.</p>
