<p>Just split the way once more so that you can add the name to the matching parts of the way. With Josm you just mark the node where you want to split the way and press "P". (Or have a look at "Tools")</p>
<p>Please do not merge the way where you think it is split "incorrectly". There are a lot of reasons why ways are split, e.g. changing maxspeed, surface, name or a turn restriction.</p>
