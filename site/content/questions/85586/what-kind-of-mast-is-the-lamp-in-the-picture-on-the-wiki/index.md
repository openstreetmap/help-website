<p>I would call this straight. Not sure why mast has been chosen rather than pole or post for regular streetlamps, where standard EN-GB calls them lampposts.</p>
<p>In this case the lamp &amp; it's housing makes up nearly all (probably all) of the non-straight part of the lamp post. These designs seem to be increasingly common, replacing ones with a small bend near the top.</p>
