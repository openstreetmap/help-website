<div class="answer-body">
<p>OK... Here's an ugly proof of concept-hack I've written that at least works for me in JOSM:</p>
<pre><code>cat 7.gpx | sed -n &#39;/^&lt;extensions&gt;/!{p;d};:l;n;/^&lt;\/extensions&gt;/d;s@^&lt;gpxdata:startPoint \(.*\)/&gt;@&lt;wpt \1&gt;&lt;/wpt&gt;@;/^&lt;wpt/p;bl&#39; &gt; 7b.gpx</code></pre>
<p>It transforms start points into way points.</p>
