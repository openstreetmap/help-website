<p>The main database is designed for editing and therefore indeed doesn't allow to export more than a very tiny piece of the map.</p>
<p>You can either download a Garmin map directly <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download</a></p>

<p>I guess you are using the <span>export tab on osm.org</span>. It simply may be that the area is still too big, yes. Furthermore, exports are very resource demanding and restricted more during high-load times.</p>
<p>You want an export for your Garmin? As far as I know you would not even be able to use the map formats offered by export in Garmins (at least not without processing on your own).</p>
<p>Download a MapSource map installer (+ possibly a pre-made gmapsupp.img for your device) from <span>OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download</span>. See e.g. <a href="/questions/4592/basic-guide-to-download-osm-to-garmin-gps">basic-guide-to-download-osm-to-garmin-gps</a> and <a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/garmin/?sort=mostvoted">list of garmin questions</a> for more info.</p>
