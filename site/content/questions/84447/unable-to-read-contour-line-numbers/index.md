<p>Basically if compare with a map company (Ordanance Survey) that does things well, we are getting a few things wrong:</p>
<p><a href="https://getoutside.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/guides/understanding-map-contour-lines-for-beginners/">https://getoutside.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/guides/understanding-map-contour-lines-for-beginners/</a></p>
<p>1) The contour line should not strike out the number!</p>
<p>2) Numbers need to be bigger;</p>
<p>3) Numbers need to be in a dense/solid colour;</p>
<p>4) In many cases it is not necessary to put numbers on every single line - that declutters the map;</p>
<p>Any comments?</p>

<p>No, there is no such setting, as what you are looking at are composite raster images.</p>
<p>If you have issues with the style as presented you need to take that up with the creator and operator of the the specific map layer. In this case as a few clicks on the attribution lines will show you <a href="https://github.com/cyclosm">https://github.com/cyclosm</a></p>
