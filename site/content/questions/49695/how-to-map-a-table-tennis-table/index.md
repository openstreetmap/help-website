<p>It is your decision. You may look at the taginfo numbers to see what others have chosen.</p>
<p>I have used the generic <code>pitch</code> with the <code>sport</code> specification for the tables I've mapped. The <span>interative refinement</span> of this tag combination seems to be useful to me. E.g. a map could display sport pitches regardless of all the sport types just by filtering for <code>leisure=pitch</code>. <span>One example</span>.</p>
