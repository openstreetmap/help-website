<p>It appears that the lake consisted of one circular "way" and you split it into three ways. None of the three forms a complete circle, so none are colored blue as water.</p>
<p>I just fixed this by creating a "multipolygon" including all three ways. Since together they form a complete circle, the multipolygon as a whole is colored blue.</p>
