<div class="answer-body">
<p><code>hazard=curve</code> from the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Proposed_features/hazard">hazard proposal</a> has been <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/hazard=curve">used almost 900 times</a>.</p>
<p>However aseerel4c26 has a valid point. Sharp curves can be calculated very easily by navigation software if the roads are mapped with sufficient detail.</p>
<p>Of course there can be additional conditions making a curve particularly difficult, such as a bad surface or frequent wetness due to high humidity, a stream running over the road and similar causes. In these cases just looking at the road geometry isn't enough. See also <code>hazard=slippery_road</code>.</p>
