<p>Hi.</p>
<p>It doesn't seem possible. You can edit it though, and remove all content if necessary.</p>
<p>An admin of the OSM website could probably do it for you.</p>
<p>Be aware that the diaries are available through RSS feeds, which might be cached elsewhere.</p>
<p>(If it's not about your own posts, you should report the diaries, moderators will delete inappropriate content.)</p>
<p>Regards.</p>

<p>As H_mlet said, if it's your diary you should be able to edit it to remove any content you no longer want people to see. <a href="https://blogs.openstreetmap.org/">https://blogs.openstreetmap.org/</a> will pick up the new version, but of course other versions may be cached elsewhere. It's not possible to report your own diary entry, but a quiet word with the DWG or the admins should be able to get the (now empty, after you've edited) one hidden.</p>
