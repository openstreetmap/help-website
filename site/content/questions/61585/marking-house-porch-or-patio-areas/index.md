<div class="answer-body">
<p>I don't think patios are normally mapped. However, the small wall surrounding the patio can be mapped. In this example, I would draw a wall and enter a height value of about .5 (meters). Then, add a fence on top of the wall; min height = .5 and height 1. This way you'l have a wall of a half meter and a fence of a half meter on top for a total of one meter. After about 24 hours you can view the results in 3D here: <a href="http://demo.f4map.com/#camera.theta=0.9">http://demo.f4map.com/#camera.theta=0.9</a></p>
