<div class="question-body">
<p>So I've recently been working on fixing up the boundary of Los Angeles City. It turn out that the boundaries imported in the area included separate boundaries for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Census-designated_place">census-designated places</a> (CDPs).</p>
<p>With the understanding that CDPs exist solely for census purposes, I've dealt with the first issue I ran into (the contiguous <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ladera_Heights,_California">Ladera Heights</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/View_Park%E2%80%93Windsor_Hills,_California">View Park–Windsor Hills</a>) by eliminating the separate relations and leaving only nodes (as <code>place=suburb</code>) and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/33172221">a single boundary way</a>. However there are likely many more similar situations, so I'd to know what people think about dealing with such cases.</p>
