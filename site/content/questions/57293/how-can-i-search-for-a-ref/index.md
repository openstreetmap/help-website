<p>The Wizard interface on Overpass Turbo splits the difference between simplicity and power. Here's your ref using the Wizard:</p>
<p><a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/qCV">http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/qCV</a></p>
<p>The <code>global</code> in the Wizard input tells Overpass Turbo not to insert any geographic bounds into the Overpass script.</p>
