<div class="comment-text">
<p>You must first ask yourself a more important questions: are you allowed to import that data to OSM. Licensing is a problematic topic and, unfortuantely, in many cases goverment-published data is not compatible with OSM license.</p>
<p>If the license is OK, then the rest depends on the format in which the data is published. Could you give us more information on the data you have in mind?</p>
