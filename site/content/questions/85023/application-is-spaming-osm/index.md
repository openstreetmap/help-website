<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you think it's vandalism or spam you'll have to contact the DWG (Data Working Group) here: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Data_working_group#Contacts_in_case_of_problems">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Data_working_group#Contacts_in_case_of_problems</a> . They will look into the behavior and can decide to block users or revert edits.</p>
<p>But in regard to some of the changesets, e.g. this one: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/118310523,">https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/118310523,</a> how is adding a power pole there (if there is one) spam? I would think this is relevant information, esp. for e.g. blind people a power pole is an obstacle on a sidewalk.</p>
