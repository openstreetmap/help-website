<div class="answer-body">
<p>OSM nor OSMF provide such services. You have to contact one of the many companies <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Commercial_OSM_Software_and_Services">listed on the wiki</a></p>
<p>The services offered by OSM/OSMF are free, but with restrictions. Those restrictions are typically OK for hobbyist and mappers, but not for commercial purposes. You cannot buy a license to lift those restrictions.</p>
