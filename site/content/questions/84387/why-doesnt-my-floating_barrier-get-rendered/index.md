<p>Many things simply don't get rendered. They're either not important enough or haven't been noticed yet.</p>
<p>As an example, radio towers are not rendered on the OSM main page because there are so many of them they clutter the map if displayed.</p>
<p>While a floating barrier is important it's also the type of object that will need a push to get the rendering folks to look at implementing it as a project.</p>
<p>Cheers, Dave</p>
