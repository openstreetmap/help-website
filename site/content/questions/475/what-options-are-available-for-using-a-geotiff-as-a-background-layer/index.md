<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can load GeoTIFFs in Merkaartor.</p>
<p>This doesn't seem to be explained very well in the documentation (I think its a fairly new feature), but to do this:</p>
<ul>
<li>Click on the Layers menu, then "Add new image layer".</li>
<li>Then right click on the "Map - None" layer in the layers dock, select GeoTIFF, then "Load image", and pick your .tiff file.</li>
<li>Then your image should be loaded. Note the map view will not change to where the GeoTIFF is, you will have to manually scroll/zoom to the correct place.</li>
</ul>

<p>To display your GeoTIFF as a background layer, you need a way to convert it to a coordinate reference system (CRS) suitable for the editor you are using. <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/JOSM/Plugins/ImportImagePlugin">For JOSM, the ImportImage Plugin</a> will take care of transforming your images to the CRS used by JOSM.</p>
