<div class="answer-body">
<p>The tag <strong><code>happy_hours=*</code></strong> is in use and documented for this purpose: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:happy_hours">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:happy_hours</a></p>
<p>Same syntax as <strong><code>opening_hours</code></strong>, eg "<strong><code>happy_hours=Mo-Fr 17:00-20:00</code></strong>".</p>
