<div class="answer-body">
<p>Sea routes (especially long-distance) are not something fixed. They vary to some extend on weather, traffic, type of vessel, fuel used, etc. It is useful however to map <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:route%3Dferry">ferry routes</a>. This could help navigation systems used in cars.</p>
