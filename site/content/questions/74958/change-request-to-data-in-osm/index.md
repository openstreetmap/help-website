OSM currently shows it as, Under Construction. I have the plan sheet that shows how it should currently exist. I did see in the Maxar Premium Imagery (beta) that it could be digitized in from the imagery. I don't feel comfortable making adjustments to this Interchange because of existing relationships with other roads. Any help would be much appreciated.</p>
