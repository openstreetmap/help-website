<div class="answer-body">
<p>Ah yes - "Fix My Address".<br />
</p>
<p>What this means (at least when I see it) is that the addr:street of a POI hasn't been set. It's possible to tell mkgmap (the software used to create this map) to infer the "addr:city" by passing a flag such as "--location-autofill=3" (see the mkgmap documentation for what values are supported and what they actually do).</p>
<p>Garmin eTrex devices show the POI name and city when you're searching; Nuvis show name and street (at least mine do). This does mean that the POI list on a Nuvi can be unhelpful. I'm not aware of a way to get mkgmap to infer street as well as city (although getting it to display city when street is unavailable in place of "Fix My Address" would be about 3 lines of Java).</p>
</div>
