<p>Your friend didn't assign the correct properties to these buildings, for example at <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/659928207">https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/659928207</a> . They are tagged with <code>area=yes</code> which is too unspecific to get rendered. Instead they have to be tagged with <code>building=yes</code>, <code>building=house</code> or something similar like your buildings. I guess Geofabrik doesn't include objects that don't have any relevant tags.</p>
