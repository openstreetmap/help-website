<p>(I think we have a very similar question here already, but I cannot find it now.)</p>
<p>As far as I know you cannot customize. bug reports / feature requests should go to our trac system (you can login with your www.osm.org account), but be aware that your suggestion could be just closed/denied. Lists: <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/query?status=accepted&amp;status=assigned&amp;status=new&amp;status=reopened&amp;component=website&amp;max=200&amp;order=priority">All open website bugs</a>, <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/query?status=!closed&amp;keywords=~user_location">all user_location bugs</a> … and here is the one you are asking for: <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/ticket/3895">suppress inactive users "near you"</a>.</p>
<p>Instead use the other, better tools mentioned at <span>wiki page about this feature</span>.</p>
