<p>Instead of the <em>history</em> (ctrl-h) try the <em>advanced info</em> (ctrl-i) where the ID should be easily copyable. If not, then either open the <em>history (web)</em> (ctrl-shift-h) or the <em>advanced info (web)</em> (ctrl-shift-i) where you can copy the ID directly from your browser.</p>
