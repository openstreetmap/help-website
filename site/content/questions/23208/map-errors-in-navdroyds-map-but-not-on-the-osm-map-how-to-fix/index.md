<div class="answer-body">
<p>Maybe you need to update the map on your device.</p>
<p>Maybe the details got fixed just recently and Navdroyd did not update the data yet. You could look when a detail was last changed in the OSM data with the <span>data browser</span> (if you have problems, please describe which detail you mean and paste a <span>permalink</span> of <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org">http://www.openstreetmap.org</a> in a comment here and someone will tell you).</p>
<p>Maybe the Navdroyd map is created differently ("rendered") than the standard map on osm.org - it may interpret our data differently or may not show some kinds of data at all. This ''may'' be caused by (partly) incorrect/uncommonly tagged OSM data. Again: show us a example "error" location and "error" description and we can have a look and ''may'' fix our data (side note: we will not change the data if the <span>only</span> reason would be to let it appear on the Navdroyd map).</p>
<p>I don't know if you can insert external maps into Navdroyd - but, as alternative, you could use another <span>Anrdoid app</span> which uses OSM data.</p>
