<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you want to encourage a developer to work on something by paying them, there's nothing stopping you advertising this in the places where developers are to be found - such as the dev@ and rails-dev@ mailing lists.</p>
<p>Remember, though, that the decision on whether a particular patch is accepted rests with the project maintainer. Even if you can get a patch put together, you will also have to convince the maintainer of that project (whether it be the <a href="http://osm.org">osm.org</a> site, Potlatch 2, JOSM, or whatever) that the patch is well coded and appropriate.</p>
