<div class="answer-body">
<p>Someone has broken the coastline polygon and the renderer has let the sea in. This has now been fixed in the OpenStreetMap database that all these maps use.<br />
The standard and humanitarian maps now show the data correctly and are rendered more often that the cycle and transport maps.<br />
There is nothing you can do to render the cycle and transport maps ahead of the schedule that the map providers use to update their maps.<br />
The <a href="https://www.opencyclemap.org/">OpenCycleMap</a> is updated every few days with the latest data from OpenStreetMap</p>
