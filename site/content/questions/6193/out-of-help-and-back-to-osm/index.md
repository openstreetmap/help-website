<p>If you just viewed a few pages on help.osm.org: Just use the "back" feature of your browser (that is a basic functionality which all should have; usually accessible by a leftward arrow button or by some rightward swipe gesture for touch interfaces). Well, or open the help site in a new tab.</p>
<p>Yes, that may have room for enhancement. To report bugs and feature requests I think it is best to use <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/">https://trac.openstreetmap.org/</a> (component "website") instead.</p>
