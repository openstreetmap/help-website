<div class="answer-body">
<p>The answer is simple in the end. Komoot is using somewhat outdated data. The culprit in the routing lies on <a href="https://www.komoot.de/plan/tour/d08AsxxHABxq4A=FoSABNxEQA==/@46.9526928,7.4495204,19z">a small piece of road</a> as you can find out by moving start and end of the route around. Routing works the other way round. <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/608434857/history">This stretch of road</a> was falsely tagged as a oneway road until two months ago. Komoot apparently uses data from before the correction,</p>
