<p>He is more asking about how to resolve <code>amenity=parking</code> and <code>amenity=bicycle_parking</code>, than micro-mapping or indoor mapping. In principle, at least an <code>amenity=bicycle_parking</code> area can be drawn overlapping the <code>building=parking</code> of <code>amenity=parking</code>. Cycling-through overlapping objects is as much as an editor issue as an ease of maintenance issue. (In iD's case, this here can be filtered relatively easily by difference in building and land-use feature.)</p>
