<div class="answer-body">
<p>That is not how OSM works. Everything is editable by everybody and in general that is a good thing (forgetting about the occasional vandal), because that means that other contributors will build on your edits, will improve them, just as you can with other contributors edits.</p>
<p>If you want to have "private" data you are going to have to store it in a private server (which btw isn't so difficult, but does require a non-neligble amount of hardware).</p>
