<p>I find the MotionX GPS app very good. It displays OpenStreetMap and OpenCycleMap data and allows large areas of the map to be pre-loaded onto the phone, so you don't need a network signal when you are out on the hills.</p>
<p>It also does nice track recording, so you can import your GPS track back into OSM :)</p>
