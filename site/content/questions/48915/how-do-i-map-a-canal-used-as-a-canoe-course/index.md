<p>I would go with:</p>
<p><em>waterway=canal</em> as it is a canal, and if it is used often for canoeing then add <em>sport=canoeing</em>.</p>
<p>If the water body is wide enough you can map additionally the area using tags: <em>natural=water</em> and <em>water=canal</em></p>
