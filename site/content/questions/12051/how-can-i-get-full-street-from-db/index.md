<div class="comment-text">
<p>To find the intersection between two streets of which you know the name, try</p>
<p>SELECT st_intersection(a.way,b.way) FROM planet_osm_line a, planet_osm_line b WHERE a.highway is not null AND b.highway is not null AND a.name='FirstName' AND b.name='SecondName' AND a.way &amp;&amp; b.way;</p>
<p>This query will benefit from an index on the "name" column. It could occasionally return empty geometries, or more than one geometry.</p>
