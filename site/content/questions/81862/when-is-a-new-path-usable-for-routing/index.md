<p>There's a link on <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/directions?engine=fossgis_osrm_foot&amp;route=29.7628%2C-95.6085%3B29.7620%2C-95.6081#map=19/29.76257/-95.60737">the routing page</a> that goes to <a href="https://routing.openstreetmap.de/about.html">here</a>. That says:</p>
<pre><code>Overview of routing data updates
&#10;The routing data are updated daily. There may be delays in the internal processing and creation of the routing diagram.</code></pre>
<p>The downloadable "foot Europe Asia" timestamp file contains "2021-09-21T15:00:00Z", which is just over a day ago. Once that date is after your update, it'll be using your data.</p>
