<div class="answer-body">
<p>I would use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Relation:multipolygon">multipolygons</a> to map this, which are able to model areas with holes.</p>
<p>In your example, there seem to be some buildings(?) together with the steps in the center of the pedestrian area. Those should all be excluded from the pedestrian area by using a multipolygon. You can then connect the steps to the inner ring of the multipolygon with a shared node.</p>
