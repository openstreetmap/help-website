<div class="answer-body">
<p>If the entirety of GR5 is indeed part of European path E2, there is no need for two separate relations in this instance; it's needless duplication and makes the path harder to edit (and thereby more prone to mistakes). Instead, just add the GR5 relation as a member of the E2 relation.</p>
<p>If parts of GR5 aren't in E2, however, then you probably do need two separate relations.</p>
<p>Note that the FFRP appears to claim copyright over these routes in some kind of bizarre dog-on-lamppost ritual, and so you may (sadly) not be permitted to add these to OSM in any case. Check with the French OSM community and forum/mailing list threads such as <a href="http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?id=3665">http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?id=3665</a> .</p>
