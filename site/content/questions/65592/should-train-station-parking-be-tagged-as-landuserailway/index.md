<div class="question-body">
<p>In the city that I am focusing on right now, there is a decently sized train station. The rail corridor and maintenance infrastructure is properly encased in an area tagged with landuse:railway, but that are also includes public parking around the station.</p>
<p>While the parking's primary use is for the station and it is directly attached to it, the wiki page about landuse:railway mentions that the zone is for railways, the station itself and any maintenance equipment or buildings supporting the operation.</p>
<p>Should the parking stay in the landuse:railway area or should it be removed from it, making the zone cover only equipment and buildings related to the operations of the trains, as stated on the wiki ?</p>
