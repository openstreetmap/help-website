<div class="answer-body">
<p>You could</p>
<ol>
<li>use a semi-colon: barrier=cattle_grid;gate on 1 node</li>
<li>use a short way (part of the track/path) for the cattle_grid and a node on that way for the gate</li>
<li>use 2 nodes next to one another (my least preferred solution)</li>
</ol>
