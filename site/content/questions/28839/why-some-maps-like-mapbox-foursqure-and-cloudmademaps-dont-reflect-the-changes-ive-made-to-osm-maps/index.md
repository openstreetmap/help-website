<div class="comment-text">
<p>The coastline update process for OSM-hosted maps (e.g. the standard layer) changed recently, in the last few months I think. It's possible that external maps are still kicking coastlines updates off manually - you'd have to talk to the external map provider concerned. Also be aware that some data downloads from external sites may be very old indeed, so it's resonable to assume that maps from those sites may be too - always check the date of map tiles that you're using before scratching your head about why things haven't updated.</p>
