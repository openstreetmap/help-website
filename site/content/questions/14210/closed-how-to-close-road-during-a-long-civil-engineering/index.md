<div class="answer-body">
<p>you can use the following ways: add construction=yes</p>
<p>or change to highway=construction</p>
<p>or add some appropriate access-tags (e.g. vehicle=no foot=yes if the road is closed for vehicles but pedestrians can still use it).</p>
<p>Don't forget to change the tags accordingly, once the construction is finished.</p>
<p>How long are the roads closed for reconstruction? If it is only for a short time (e.g. a couple of days up to some weeks) and everything will be like before afterwards, imho don't tag the construction. If only the surface or sewer pipes are renewed during a long weekend, this does not justify tagging and re-tagging.</p>
<p>Reason: people might download data for offline-navigation in the meantime and then they are stuck with supposedly closed roads for a long time after the construction is finished. But there is an ongoing discussion about this topic - 2 lawyers have 3 different opinions :D</p>
