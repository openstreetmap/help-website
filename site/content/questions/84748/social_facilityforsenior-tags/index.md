<div class="answer-body">
<p>Interesting question. I don't know if there is a consensus approach.</p>
<p>I'd be tempted to classify these retirement villages as a type of residential area and map individual facilities within the area as <code>social_facility</code> separately (as has been done with the retirement home for clergy). The reason being that there is a whole continuum of residential areas for older people ranging from US style retirement communities, sheltered housing, almshouses, group homes for people with substantial independence etc. Many of these will not obviously be dedicated social facilities either from aerial imagery or, necessarily, on the ground.</p>
