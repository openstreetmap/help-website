<p>Is this a one-time task? Then you can use <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/">Overpass Turbo</a>. There's an <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Overpass_API_by_Example#List_of_streets">example for what you are looking for</a> on its help page.</p>
