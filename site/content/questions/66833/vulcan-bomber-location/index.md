<p>When I looked at this a while back (not just for Vulcans) "<a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/?key=historic&amp;value=aircraft">historic=aircraft</a>" was the most common legitimate way to tag this sort of thing. There's a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:historic=aircraft?uselang=en-GB">wiki page</a> for it too. Unfortunately there's a fair bit of "tagging for the renderer" going on with static aircraft in the UK; e.g. there's at least one "imaginary fence" that has been used to define the outline.</p>
