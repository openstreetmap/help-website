<div class="answer-body">
<p>That seems to be either a problem at wikimedia maps or an already corrected problem that is not updated yet in wikimedia maps.</p>
<p>On my rendering of the OSM data in Portuguese, Portugal is displayed as Portugal in Portuguese.</p>
<p>And Nominatim tells the same:</p>
<p><a href="https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/search.php?q=portugal&amp;polygon_geojson=1&amp;viewbox=&amp;accept-language=pt">https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/search.php?q=portugal&amp;polygon_geojson=1&amp;viewbox=&amp;accept-language=pt</a></p>
<p>And here: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/295480">https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/295480</a> you see the name:pt tag also displayed as "Portugal".</p>
<p>So this is not in OSM data, but somewhere broken in wikipedia maps (where I see what you mean).</p>
