<div class="answer-body">
<p>My instinctive answer, which seems to be used a bit according to <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=phone">taginfo</a>, is to use "phone:XXX" where XXX qualifies the usecase ("mobile", "emergency", etc). You might want to tag the default phone twice, once with and once without the ":XXX" suffix.</p>
<p>Taginfo shows a lot of other schemes are in use, such as "phone2" and "alt_phone", but I feel that they do not follow best practices for new tag creation. There is also "contact:phone" as a IMHO-superfluous synonym of "phone".</p>
