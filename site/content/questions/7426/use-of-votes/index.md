<li>If you like an answer you click on the thumbs up icon to the left of the answer.</li>
<li>If you don't like the answer click on the thumbs down icon instead</li>
<li>If you change your mind just click on the icon again.</li>
<li>You may vote for any post except your own posts.</li>
<li>The votes are <em>not</em> anonymous.</li>
</ul>
<p>If you are the one that asked a question, and you and find an answer the be the correct one, you may click the tick icon and the question will be marked as answered.</p>
