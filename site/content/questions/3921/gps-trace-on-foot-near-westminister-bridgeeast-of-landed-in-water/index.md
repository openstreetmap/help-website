<div class="answer-body">
<p>(as I'm sure you're aware, but other people may not be) the Vista Hcx does report a "location +- X m" figure on the "Satellites" screen. I turn to try the "IT Crowd fix" (turn it off and on again) if that +- figure is more than I'd normally expect.<br />
</p>
<p>Re the "South" bit GPS satellites aren't geostationary (see <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPS_%28satellite%29">obligatory wiki link</a>) so a tall building (or hill) on any side could impair reception.</p>
