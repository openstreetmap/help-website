<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are already <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/shop=video_games">109</a> objects tagged as <em>shop=video_games</em> (note the <em>s</em> at the end) in the database. Although the wiki doesn't have a description for this tag yet you can <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Any_tags_you_like">use it nevertheless</a>.</p>
<p>If you can't find a tag for a specific object in the wiki or your editor's presets you can always search on <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/">taginfo</a> to see which tags were used by other users.</p>
