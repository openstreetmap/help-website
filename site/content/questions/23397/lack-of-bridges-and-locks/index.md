<div class="answer-body">
<p>I always add bridges on paths so others know they don't have to detour, wade or jump the water. When we survey we will usually remember the bridges but not remember the other details unless we take geo-tagged jpegs. Water users will probably notice water features but I guess most surveys are done on bike or foot. You can, as you know, add any features that you consider important, but they may not get rendered, they may come in useful to others users, so if you wish map them.</p>
