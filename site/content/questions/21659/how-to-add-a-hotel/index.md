<p>Hi, you can add it yourself: Go to our main page <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org">http://www.openstreetmap.org</a> , Login, and click edit. Drag the hotel icon to the correct place, name it, save with a comment. If you need it: <span>Docu wiki page about hotels</span> (for detail info). Hopefully you will also <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/node/2273134513/history">add more objects</a> in the surroundings of the hotel, but don't use google maps or other maps/databases as source, just your local knowledge).</p>
<p>Further/more info: Please see <a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/6/how-do-i-get-started-with-openstreetmap">how-do-i-get-started-with-openstreetmap</a>. In essence: You get help on how to edit at the <span>Beginner's guide</span> or at <a href="http://learnosm.org/">LearnOSM</a>. Also search this site for questions/answers, or browse by tags (<span>example</span>).<br />
</p>
<hr />
<p>Since you asked me via mail what is wrong with your edits (you simply should have mentioned here that already tried)... here is my answer:</p>
<p>Your hotel is here: <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/node/2273133595">http://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/node/2273133595</a></p>
<p>Please understand that "OpenStreetMap" is just a big database. <span>Many different map styles</span> are made of the data. In our main style the name of your hotel is not shown since there is no space to display it (name of the street collides). Please so not change the position of the hotel to a wrong position just to display the name in our standard style. The hotel will be found in search results. Enter "hotels near Kozle, Skopje" (without ") in the search box on <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org">http://www.openstreetmap.org</a> However, in the aerial image ("sat image") in the editor's background it looks like there is no house at the current position of your hotel icon (if I assume that the aerial image's position is not too wrong). Move the icon to the centre of the hotel building. Maybe, your name will be shown then, too.</p>
<p>Please also understand that all maps need time to update to new data. So: if you change something it will not be changed on maps directly afterwards.</p>
