<div class="answer-body">
<p>In principle the methods described for <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Guidelines_for_pedestrian_navigation#Ways_inside_buildings">pedestrian navigation within buildings</a> could be used for the parking aisles in multi-storey buildings.</p>
<p>In practice these are generally very difficult for hobby mappers to do with any degree of accuracy and most mappers will terminate the service roads at an <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dparking_entrance"><code>amenity=parking_entrance</code></a>.</p>
<p>Exposed "bridges" looping outside of the main parking building can be tagged with the usual <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:layer">layer</a> tags.</p>
