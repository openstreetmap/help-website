<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you are a OSM contributor and are already using our advanced <span>editor JOSM</span>, then it is quite handy to also use it to make tracks. This way the known interface can be used, by chance you can spot problems in the OSM data or even plan your route based on bing to collect some new data (surveying) while being on the ride.</p>
<ol>
<li>Add a "Mapnik", "CycleMap", bing, … background layer,</li>
<li>add a new (empty) data layer,</li>
<li>draw your track in it,</li>
<li>export it as gpx.</li>
</ol>
<p>Of course, be sure to not accidentally upload your track to OSM.</p>
