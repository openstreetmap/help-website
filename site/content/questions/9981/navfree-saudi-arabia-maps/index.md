<p>As NavFree is a closed source program, there is no possibility to make your own map files. So you'll have to contact the developer: support AT navmii DOT com</p>
<p>If you want apps that offer about the same functionality (offline navigation), check out OsmAnd and ZANavi.</p>
<p>OsmAnd has more online services (next to the offline ones), and ZANavi has better performance in the routing engine.</p>
