<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is no "the map". There are many Garmin-compatible maps which are made by using our OpenStreetMap data.</p>
<p>See the sections for devices with a <span>very similar model number on our Garmin/Nüvi series info page</span> (1350T and 1390 T) (I have just added some basic info about your device). Yes, map and routing could work on your device – just try it and please add your findings to the mentioned wiki page section (or here).</p>
<p>Follow the general instructions <span>OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download</span> (just try the first entry there – <a href="http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/">http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/</a> ) and/or <a href="/questions/4592/">basic-guide-to-download-osm-to-garmin-gps</a>. You may need to use a microSD card if there is not enough space on the device itself.</p>
