<div class="answer-body">
<p>Popular <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Search_engines">geocoders for OSM</a> are Nominatim and Photon, among others.</p>
<p>For donations see <a href="https://donate.openstreetmap.org/.">https://donate.openstreetmap.org/.</a> However donating money to OSM doesn't mean you can use OSM services without any restrictions. You will still have to comply with the usage policy like of the particular service you are accessing, like any other user, too.</p>
<p>If your requirements don't match the service's usage policy then either choose a commercial provider (see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Commercial_OSM_Software_and_Services">Commercial OSM Software and Services</a> and <a href="https://switch2osm.org/providers/">switch2osm.org/providers</a>) or install your own geocoder instance. For the second option take a look at the <a href="http://nominatim.org/release-docs/latest/admin/Installation/">installation instructions for Nominatim</a> and the <a href="https://github.com/komoot/photon#installation">installation instructions for Photon</a>.</p>
