<div class="comment-text">
<p>Firstly, sedum green roofs are not grass. Secondly, a 'green roof' is nearly always an additional structure built and supported by the actual roof. The combination green_roof=yes has been used 30 times so far (<a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/5jj).">http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/5jj).</a> For traditional roofs which are indeed constructed of turves laid over the supporting structure I would suggest roof:material=turf is more accurate than roof:material=grass as the former correctly identifies the importance of the soil layer for insulation, water-proofing and structural integrity.</p>
</div>
