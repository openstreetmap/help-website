<div class="answer-body">
<p>Yes and no.</p>
<p>If you mean "the" map displayed at the main openstreetmap.org site, there is the possibility of designing a new shop icon and then filing an issue with the openstreetmap-mapnik stylesheet (via <a href="https://github.com/openstreetmap/mapnik-stylesheets/issues">https://github.com/openstreetmap/mapnik-stylesheets/issues</a> ).</p>
<p>Also, you could build a website yourself where you display an OSM view with OpenLayers and overlay that with your shop icons.</p>
