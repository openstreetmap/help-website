<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>Which objects are visible and at what zoom levels is primarily a cartographic decision; for the standard OSM map style, the decisions are made over at <a href="https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto">https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto</a> but many other map styles exist that make different decisions. It is never possible to display <em>all</em> information.</p>
<p>In addition to the cartographic decisions by the map style team, sometimes the rendering engine itself will omit icons or labels if they would overlap with another icon or label placed earlier. In rare circumstances this can lead to almost random-looking results, where one of two competing labels is shown on one zoom level and another on a different zoom level.</p>
</div>
