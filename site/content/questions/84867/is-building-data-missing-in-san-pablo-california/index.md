<div class="answer-body">
<p>Openstreetmap is almost totally mapped by volunteers. Some areas are more complete. Mappers have different interests and tend to work on those features. Drawing building shapes from aerial images, and doing a good job, can take a lot of time even with practice. You have highlighted something that needs work, someone may pick it up. Do you want a job?</p>
