<p>There is a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Camino_de_Santiago">wiki page on the Camino de Santiago</a>. From Tours you would follow <a href="https://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/#route?id=187781&amp;map=6!44.9408!2.1935">Via Turonesis</a> and then <a href="https://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/#search?query=Camino+Francés&amp;map=7!41.9188!-1.7878">Camino Francés</a> (the overall way is not visible on that page, click the segments on the right).</p>
