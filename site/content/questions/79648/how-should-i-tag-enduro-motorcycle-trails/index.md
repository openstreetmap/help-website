<div class="answer-body">
<p>If what you want to map is a racetrack you can use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Draceway"><code>highway=raceway</code></a> with an appropriate <code>sport=</code> key.</p>
<p>If this is more some sort of marked trail not primarily for racing you should map the ways as <code>highway=path</code> or what better indicates their nature and add appropriate access tags, e.g. <code>access=no</code> and <code>motorcycle=designated</code>. You can then also add a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Relation:route#Others">route relation</a> combining the individual highways into the complete trail.</p>
