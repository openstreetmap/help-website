<div class="answer-body">
<p>Data tagged according to the SIT schema (<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Simple_Indoor_Tagging)">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Simple_Indoor_Tagging)</a> is no different than any other OSM data. So the question would be what kind of specific support are you looking for?</p>
<p>Note that the "IndoorOSM" tagging schema is long defunct and you shouldn't be considering adding data in that format.</p>
