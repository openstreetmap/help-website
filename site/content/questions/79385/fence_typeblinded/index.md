<p>Hi, I've seen some of these. It's called slatted or preslatted. I think slatted is a chain link fence with slats added to it, preslatted looks like a ready made chain link fence with slats incorporated. So the fence is a combination of chain link and slats, probably a good tag is fence_type=chain_link,slatted.</p>
<p>For information and pictures <a href="https://swwire.com/preslatted-chain-link-fence/">https://swwire.com/preslatted-chain-link-fence/</a> and <a href="http://fitzpatrickfenceandrail.com/slatted-chain-link-fence/">http://fitzpatrickfenceandrail.com/slatted-chain-link-fence/</a></p>
