<div class="answer-body">
<p>Add whatever marks the start of the route to the relation and give it the role "start". This does not have to be a node. It could just as well be a building or a parking lot.</p>
<p>Note that a route can have multiple start points, so you can't always look at the ordering of the ways. See e.g. the routes here: <a href="http://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/en/?zoom=15&amp;lat=52.11171&amp;lon=5.2137">Panbos/Beerschoten</a></p>
