<p>Hello all,</p>
<p>I recently came across the service <a href="http://www.mapiful.com">mapiful.com</a> where you can style your own map and have them print it. I ordered a map and really liked it. I think they use OSM data to create the map. Do they not need to give attribution on the printout of the map?</p>
<p>What If I created some maps myself by downloading data from <a href="http://download.geofabrik.de/">http://download.geofabrik.de/</a> and applying my own style to it? Do I need to give attribution here? Can I just print it on anything and sell it because I created the style? Would it be enough to give attribution on my projects website?</p>
<p>Hope you can help me :)</p>
<p>cheers</p>
<p>Edit: <a href="https://www.cartida.com/en/">cartida.com</a> is a similar site to mapiful.com. On their website on the bottom they give the attribution "Map data: OpenStreetMap contributors". Is this enough?</p>
