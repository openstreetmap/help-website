<p>I found a navigation application based on OpenStreetMap data. The application is enriching OSM data with the companies own data to provide better navigation.</p>
<p>The company is promoting this on it's website. Also I wrote a mail to the developer support. They confirmed to me that they combine OSM data and their own data and use it for the navigation app.</p>
<p>Asking if that wouldn't mean it has to be published according to the ODbL I never recieved an answer. This made me a bit suspicious so I sent other mails to the company asking for the data in terms of ODbL. After a couple of weeks I still did not get any answer.</p>
<p>At least I'd wish to get an explanation why the company doesn't have to publish their data according to ODbL. But I never get an answer. What to do?</p>
<p>Is there a formal procedure to request the data to be published?</p>
