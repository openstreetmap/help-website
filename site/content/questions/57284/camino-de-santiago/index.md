<div class="answer-body">
<p>Waymarked Trails (overview map <a href="https://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/#?map=7!43.4177!-0.3955">here</a>). Pilgrimage ways to Santigao de Compostella are shown with the pilgrims scallop at higher zoom levels. Select a particular route and you can download a GPX file.</p>
<p>Note many routes are divided into numerous sections. OSMers have been mapping the various routes fairly intensively for years now, so there are a lot of variants. See the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Camino_de_Santiago">wiki page</a> for more detail.</p>
