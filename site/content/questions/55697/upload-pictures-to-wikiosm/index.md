<div class="answer-body">
<p>You have two alternatives.</p>
<p>First one is to upload to our wiki:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log in</li>
<li>click "Upload file" in the left menu - or use this link: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Special:Upload">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Special:Upload</a></li>
<li>read the info</li>
<li>fill in the form</li>
<li>upload</li>
</ol>
<p>If you cannot see "Upload file", then <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Wiki_Help#Upload_(New_user_restrictions)">your wiki account is too new</a>. It must be at least 4 days old and have at least 10 edits.</p>
<p>Second alternative is to upload to <a href="https://commons.wikimedia.org">Wikimedia Commons</a> (needs an account there). You can use the image in our wiki pages like if it was uploaded in our wiki directly - see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Collaboration_with_Wikipedia#Using_pictures_from_Wikipedia_for_tag_description.2FMap_Features">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Collaboration_with_Wikipedia#Using_pictures_from_Wikipedia_for_tag_description.2FMap_Features</a> for more info.</p>
