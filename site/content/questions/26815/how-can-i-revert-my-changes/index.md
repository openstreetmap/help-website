<p>I just restored the deleted landuse. The reverter plugin was only partly helpful since it reverted the linked changeset only to a part of that way and 100+ unconnected nodes.</p>
<p>With iD you cannot make reverts. As above was pointed out, the JOSM reverter plugin is so far the most user friendly tool existing for this task.</p>
<p>And citing myself from an older answer:<br />
PS: Don't get too upset about your error. Only who does nothing does nothing wrong. Maybe it soothes your mind if you have a look at <a href="http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?id=22014&amp;p=3">errors other people made</a>. :)</p>
