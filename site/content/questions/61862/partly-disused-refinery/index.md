<pre><code> disused:
&#10;    not currently available for use, but could be reinstated easily
    usage: ~37,000 in taginfo as of 08/2016
&#10;abandoned:
&#10;    still visible but fallen into serious disrepair and which could only be put back into operation with considerable effort</code></pre>
