<p>If you want a static site, that works fast, the better is to download the data of each "layer" as GPX (or another geo format), through overpass-turbo for exemple, and load them over an OSM background using <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Leaflet">leaflet</a> and one of its <a href="https://leafletjs.com/plugins.html#overlay-data-formats">plugins</a>. There are also plugins for layer management and such.</p>
