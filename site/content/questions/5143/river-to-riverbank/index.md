<p>Don't move the river way. Even rivers where the river banks are mapped should have a <code>waterway=river</code> way as centerline.</p>

<p>The flowline of a waterbody will often be what people are interested in using rather than it's surface area. Questions like "How long is River X?" can't be answered trivially if there is not a flowline. The direction of flow is not available from a riverbank polygon either. If I wanted to do a hydrographic simulation you've removed the most important data.</p>
