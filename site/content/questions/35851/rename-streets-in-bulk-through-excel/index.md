<p>You certainly do not want to reupload your "random list of names", since then everybody would see your names on the map and not the original ones!</p>
<p>If you want to create your own map with changed names, load the OSM data into the JOSM editor, change the names there, save the file, and then use a local rendering software like e.g. Maperitive to make a map from that.</p>
