<p>I guess the nominatim reverse-geocoding for <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/2648597457">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/2648597457</a> is meant. This is displayed via a search for "Free wi-fi Palazzo Barbieri":</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Free wi-fi Palazzo Barbieri, Piazza Bra, Quinzano, Verona, VR, Veneto, 37125, Italy</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This is also visible at <a href="http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=9161955595">http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=9161955595</a><br />
</p>
<p>I <em>guess</em> (I am really not a Nominatim expert) this may be because <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/2m2">there are place=suburb</a> outside Verona, but not (more near) inside Verona. And the next suburb to the Palazzo node is the one named "Quinzano". So nominatim uses this suburb as a refinement of the location description. Maybe there are also some boundary relations not correctly mapped.</p>
<p>It is not a problem for you, linda, just do not care about it now. It is not related to your edit. Please better ask such highly specific questions in our <span>forum or chat</span> next time.</p>
