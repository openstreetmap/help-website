<p>Does anyone know of any common OSM tagging for areas of coastline protected by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Riprap">riprap</a> or rock armour? I can't find anything on the wiki (or I don't have the right synonym).</p>
<p>For now I have tagged the <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/654263224">examples</a> <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/654263223">I</a> <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/654263225">ran</a> <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/654263226">across</a> as:</p>
<p>barrier=wall + wall=seawall + area=yes + natural=rock</p>
<p>However:</p>
<ul>
<li>'natural=rock' seems wrong for something clearly artificial and</li>
<li>'barrier=wall + wall=seawall' seem a little misleading for something so unstructured.</li>
</ul>
