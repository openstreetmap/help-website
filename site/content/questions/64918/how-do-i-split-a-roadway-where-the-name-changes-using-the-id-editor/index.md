<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>To split a street, click on the point where you want to split the street and then right click and click the scissors button. You can also just press "x" on your keyboard after clicking to select the point.</p>
<p>To see existing points, you can click on the street to select it, the circles visible when the street is selected are the points where you can split the street (In OpenStreetMap jargon, they are "nodes").</p>
<p>If none of the existing points are a good place to split the street, you can add one by clicking and dragging one of the triangles visible with the street selected.</p>
