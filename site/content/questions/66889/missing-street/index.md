<p>Hi, do you know the place in <em>reality</em>? Then:</p>
<ol>
<li><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/login">login</a> (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new">register</a> first if you do not yet have an account),</li>
<li>go to <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/welcome">https://www.openstreetmap.org/welcome</a> for a short introduction,</li>
<li>start the editor (big "edit" button on top or "start mapping" on the welcome page),</li>
<li>take the walkthrough in the editor,</li>
<li>and add it yourself, please! :-) OpenStreetMap is made by its users.</li>
</ol>
<p>Only if you really cannot do it, please add a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Notes">note</a> on the main map at the problem's location.</p>
<p>Thank you!</p>
