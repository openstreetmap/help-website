<p>The data is fine in OpenStreetMap. Mapbox has also picked up the changed data, at least in <a href="https://api.mapbox.com/styles/v1/mapbox/streets-v11.html?title=true&amp;access_token=pk.eyJ1IjoibWFwYm94IiwiYSI6ImNpejY4M29iazA2Z2gycXA4N2pmbDZmangifQ.-g_vE53SD2WrJ6tFX7QHmA#19.26/52.3547519/6.6661523">one of the maps</a> provided on their web page. If the business is not listed on snapchat maps you should contact them to ask when they will do that. There's nothing more to be done from OSM side.</p>
<p>On a side note: The description for <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/7884869791">the barber shop</a> exceeds what should go in there in my opinion. As indicated on the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:description">description tag's wiki page</a> it should be used for short, descriptive information only. Please review what you have added there and align it with the intended use.</p>
