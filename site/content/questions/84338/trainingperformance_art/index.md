<p>I would suggest <code>amenity=college</code> for this type of facility (e.g., <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/460552917">this one</a> which my nephew attended last year). These may offer both shorter courses, and longer vocational ones closer to a degree programme in length, but are generally post-secondary education which is why college fits. The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:isced:level">ISCED codes</a> may be useful tags too.</p>
