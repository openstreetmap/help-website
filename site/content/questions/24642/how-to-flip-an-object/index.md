<div class="answer-body">
<p>I only know such functionality from JOSM.</p>
<p>In JOSM you can mirror an object on the vertical axis by selecting it and pressing <em>Shift-M</em> on the keyboard or by choosing <em>Mirror</em> from the <a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Menu/Tools">tools menu</a>. Mirroring at the horizontal axis doesn't seem to be possible without an additional plugin. But the <a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Plugin/UtilsPlugin2">utils2 plugin</a> allows you to specify two nodes as a symmetry axis and mirrors the selected ways on this axis by pressing <em>Alt+Shift+S</em> on the keyboard or by choosing <em>Symmetry</em> from the <a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Plugin/UtilsPlugin2">More Tools</a> menu.</p>
