<div class="answer-body">
<p>You cannot. With the exception of pedestrian areas and some experimental road-area mapping in Germany, OSM only ever maps the street centreline and not the curb. The maps you see will draw the street using a line width that is derived from the street type (<code>highway</code> tag), and in some cases also taking into consideration the <code>width</code> or <code>lanes</code> tags, but never based on actual curb line mapping.</p>
