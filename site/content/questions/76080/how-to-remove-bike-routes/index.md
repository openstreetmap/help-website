<p>Hi,</p>
<p>Please don't remove anything from OpenStreetMap to suit your personal desire, this would detrimentally affect the database, to say the least.</p>
<p>It looks like you made 12 changesets additions to OpenStreetMap, none of which were bike routes. You are depicting an image from the Maps.Me app, if this app is causing the problem then it is there that you should make amendments.
