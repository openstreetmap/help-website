<p>If it is a restaurant and offers a variety of dishes including a sitting area, then it can be tagged as amenity=restaurant, cuisine tags might probably added to describe what is sold there. If it is just a take out, then takeaway=yes/no can be mentioned with the restaurant or fast food. Depends on how the deli functions or what they sell. If it is just a shop selling meat then shop=convenience can work.</p>
