<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you have a suitable Garmin, or Garmin-compatible, map for your region, you can drag and drop photos (jpgs) onto the map and Basecamp will tag them with the lat and lon.</p>
<p>Basecamp is a free program from Garmin that manages GPX data and interfaces with Garmin devices.</p>
