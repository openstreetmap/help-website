<p>If <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/526875023">this stretch of road</a> is primarily a junction to turn into/from the building access to the west I would leave it as it is, i.e. as a <code>highway=service</code>.</p>
<p>If it is primarily a u turn opportunity you can chose to promote it to <code>highway=residential</code>. Remember to split the way at the appropriate location. In that case I would name it as <em>Carrera 21</em> as it clearly belongs to that road then. As Richard mention it is no error to leave it unnamed. On the other hand routers might produce clearer turn-by-turn instructions if <em>Carrera 21</em> is in place.</p>
