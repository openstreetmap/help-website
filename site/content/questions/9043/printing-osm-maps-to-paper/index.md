<p>What's your ultimate goal? If you want to print a tile, then print the tile image from the online map that has the rendering you want ( <a href="http://osm.org">http://osm.org</a>, http:// <a href="http://open.mapquest.com">open.mapquest.com</a> , <a href="http://opencyclemap.org">http://opencyclemap.org</a> et al ). Alternatively, try the service at <a href="http://maposmatic.org/">http://maposmatic.org/</a> which allows you to generate and then print a traditional paper map for an area.</p>
