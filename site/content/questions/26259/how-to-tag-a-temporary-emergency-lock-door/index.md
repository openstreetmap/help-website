<p>A temporary lock door is made by a set of stoplogs or beams and filled with dirt or soil like a coupure. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coupure">http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coupure</a> It’s used in case of emergency, for instance to close a river or canal with a damaged lock door. There’s as far as I know no sign or mentioning of it in OSM. I consider man_made stoplog together with man made – dike for the filling. Its a vertical or upright dike with 2 reinforced slopes.</p>
