<p>As of 22/Dec/2012, there are good quality aerial images of the two locations listed in the original question. Someone already traced details for Cocula, but not Ameca yet.</p>
<p>AFAIK, Bing allows for the use of <em>all</em> satellite images, but for some places, simply there is no such imagery. I work on maps mostly for Brazil, and I noticed that the quality of the images have been steadily improving in the last months.</p>
<p>I would advise to re-check problem areas every few weeks when good quality imagery is not available, or to use other sources of data. Not Google, of course...</p>
