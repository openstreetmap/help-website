<p>A friendly plea from New Zealand re correct use of the word 'kiwi'.......... Kiwi is firstly a flightless bird native to New Zealand and also a colloquial term for a New Zealander <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiwi">http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiwi</a></p>
<p>Although the fruit is often referred to as a 'kiwi' (generally outside of New Zealand) its correct name is 'kiwifruit' (originally known as Chinese Gooseberry). <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiwifruit">http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiwifruit</a></p>
<p>Please can the trees be tagged as 'trees=kiwifruit' and 'kiwifruit_tree:count=150'</p>
<p>If one wants to be really pedantic about it kiwifruit are actually grown on vines like grapes rather than on trees but that is probably being far too picky :)</p>
