<div class="answer-body">
<p>The correct tag would be <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:natural%3Dpeak">natural=peak</a> (usually as a node). But Potlatch 2 does indeed not recognize this tag.</p>
<p>You can report this bug on <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/">trac</a> by creating a <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/newticket">new ticket</a> (login with your regular OSM account) and selecting the <em>potlatch2</em> component.</p>
