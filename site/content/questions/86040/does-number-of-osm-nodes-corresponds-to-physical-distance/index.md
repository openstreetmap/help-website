<div class="answer-body">
<p>No, there is no relationship at all.</p>
<p>Nodes are generally needed where a an object is not a straight line. So a long straight might be drawn with only 2 nodes, while a short curved street might have many nodes to draw the curve. Nodes are also needed to represent intersecting objects, so a street with many junctions will tend to have more than a street with no intersections.</p>
