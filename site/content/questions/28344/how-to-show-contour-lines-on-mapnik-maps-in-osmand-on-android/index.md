<p>I'm not familiar with OSMAnd, but a quick web search finds this answer:</p>
<p><a href="https://groups.google.com/forum/#!searchin/osmand/contours/osmand/ZmQltT8ss-U/kxGC26__hlMJ">https://groups.google.com/forum/#!searchin/osmand/contours/osmand/ZmQltT8ss-U/kxGC26__hlMJ</a></p>
<p>which sounds like a similar issue. However, I'm confused when you say both "mapnik" and "offline". OSMAnd's offline maps are its own vector format and aren't based on any of the raster stylesheets available at osm.org. When configured to use Online maps OSMAnd does refer to the OSM standard map as "mapnik", but it's NOT offline.</p>
<p>Certainly, people in the <a href="https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/osmand">OSMAnd google group</a> are more likely to be familiar with the application than here.</p>
