<p>Hello and welcome to OSM.</p>
<p>I'd say please just stop for a bit. While nothing is drastically wrong with what you've added it could be a whole lot better. If you go back on your edits and compare them to similar features already mapped you'll see a lot of anomalies. I've only looked at a few edits and can see several things that can be improved. Also comments are a great help in understanding what has been added, why, and from where.</p>
<p>Improvements can be made to comments, building and area outlines, phone numbers, check permission to use data from websites, (there are tags for areas, others for nodes (the landuse tag is area not node)) and why remove tags from ways.</p>
<p>So there's several hours needed to be spent going over those things. Regards.</p>
