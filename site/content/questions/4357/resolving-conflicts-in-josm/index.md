<div class="comment-text">
<p>Be careful, to blindly use your version. The reason that there is a conflict is that another mapper changed the map that you downloaded a few weeks back. By taking your version, you undo whatever was done by the other mapper. If you made a small number of changed, I recommend that you start all over by first downloading the area from the server and make your changes on an up-to-date version of the data,</p>
</div>
