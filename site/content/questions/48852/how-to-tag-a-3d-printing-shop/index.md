<div class="answer-body">
<p>if you search <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/shop#values">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/shop#values</a> for "3d" you find 4 shops which are likely like yours – tagged with different tags. Similar to other tags (no caps, underscores instead of spaces), I would use <code>shop=3d_printing</code> for your shop. As always, just add (in addition) a description (e.g. <code>description=shop for doing 3D prints</code>) and its website.</p>
</div>
