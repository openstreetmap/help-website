However, my map wasn't finished and I stored it under "Favorite" in my web browser. Today I opened the favorite but unfortunately, the toolbar on the Right of the map window is missing. So I cannot work on my map. In this context I do have two Questions: 1. How can I re-activate the toolbar to edit my data/map? 2. Unfortunately, I did not Login to my osm account before I created my map. So, is there a chance to transfer my data/map to my account? I always stored the map, but fair to say I did not know the path where I can find my map. Hope you can help me. Thanks a lot. Chiao. Rainer</p>
