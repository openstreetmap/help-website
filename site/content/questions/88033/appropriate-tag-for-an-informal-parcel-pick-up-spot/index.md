<p>Hello,</p>
<p>What is the correct to way to tag a spot where delivery companies are waiting for the customer to come pick up their parcels ? It's on a small island with no post services and it's an approximate position since it's not always the same companies and they are usually waiting somewhere on a small place.</p>
<p>There the node in question, and the tags I've used :</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/11382932146">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/11382932146</a></p>
<pre><code>amenity=post_depot
description=This is where you can pick up your parcel from the delivery companies
informal=yes
outdoor=yes</code></pre>
