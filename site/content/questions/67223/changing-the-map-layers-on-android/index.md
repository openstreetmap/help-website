<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is no standard OpenStreetMap app. Any map apps that you find on Android (or iOS or any other platform for that matter) are created by third parties, and you should contact them for help.</p>
<p>If you are referring to using the OpenStreetMap website on your Android, then changing layers is done through the layers icon on the right hand side of the screen. Open the layers screen, select a new layer, and close the screen with the "x".</p>
