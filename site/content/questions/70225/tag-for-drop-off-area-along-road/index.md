<p>Is there an established tag for a drop off area alongside a road?</p>
<p>These are often seen next to bus stops an look similar to <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:passing_places">passing places</a> but exist to allow buses or other vehicles to pull out of the flow of traffic while passengers get on and off.</p>
<p>These are distinct from <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Drest_area">laybys</a> and accesses to larger bus stations in that they are widened areas of an existing road rather than separate ways.</p>
<p>I'm sure I saw something for this once somewhere in the wiki but I'm getting nowhere in maze of public transport pages.</p>
