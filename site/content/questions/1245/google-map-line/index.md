<p>The streetpatterns on the maps will look the the same on every map. What is copyrigthed is how the information is rendered. For example an Michelin map.</p>
<p>I suspect the legal issue is not copyrigth, but the use of data. You can use any individual telefoonnumber out of the telefoonbook, but you are not allowed to copy the whole book or sort it by telefoonnumber.</p>
<p>Back to the issue: Is there any way or format to store a list of geografic coordinates (route) and project it on a map such as OSM? Such an object exist of course in a OSM editor, but can it be externalized or exported? My idea is to store such an object in wiki commons and then to project it on any desired map. Any image location out of the wiki commons can now be projected on any desired maps. Why not a route?</p>
