<p>Imagine I have downloaded all ways with railway=rail of a country. Now I would like to select all nodes that should contain <code>railway=switch</code> or <code>railway=railway_crossing</code>. The list of situations is:</p>
<ol>
<li>three ways end in one node</li>
<li>four ways end in one node</li>
<li>one node is in the middle of one way and at the start of another way</li>
<li>one node is in the middle of two ways</li>
</ol>
<p>My search queries for cases one and two are:</p>
<ol>
<li><code>ways:3- type:node</code></li>
<li><code>ways:4- type:node</code></li>
</ol>
<p>My question to you: how can I select case three and case four?</p>
