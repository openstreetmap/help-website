<p>I'm not a Garmin user myself these days, but <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Garmin/Dakota_series">this page</a> indicates that there has been general success with your model.</p>
<p>Premade maps of regions are available from the sources listed <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download">here</a>. Note that the <em>garmin.openstreetmap.nl</em> site has been unreliable enough recently to generate quite a few questions on this help site, so you may wish to try another provider first.</p>
<p>I have not tried their Garmin maps, but I have found <a href="https://extract.bbbike.org/?format=garmin-osm.zip">extract.bbbike.org</a>'s other offerings quite useful for custom areas. They offer several Garmin map <a href="https://extract.bbbike.org/extract-screenshots.html">styles</a>.</p>
<p>If you would like to generate your own files or need instructions on how to transfer them to your device I think <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin">this page</a> is probably the place to start.</p>
