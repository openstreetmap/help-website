<p>Is there anything special about a "bike resting place" opposed to general resting places?</p>
<p>I'm not aware of a specific tag for such area. But there are a lot of tags available for sites or the ojects, e.g.</p>
<p><code>tourism=picnic_site</code><br />
<code>leisure=picnic_table</code><br />
<code>amenity=drinking_water</code><br />
<code>amenity=bench</code><br />
<code>amenity=table</code><br />
<code>amenity=waste_basket</code><br />
<code>amenity=bicycle_repair_station</code><br />
<code>amenity=bicycle_parking</code></p>
