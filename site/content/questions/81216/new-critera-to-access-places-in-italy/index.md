<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is a well-established protocol of using keys with <strong><code>*:covid19</code></strong> added for potentially short-lived covid pandemic related features.</p>
<p>If it is deemed appropriate to tag such things (one may be able to simply identify them by POI type) I would recommend such an approach. There are already over 6000 instances of <strong><a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/access%3Acovid19"><code>access:covid19</code></a></strong>, most with yes as a value. For a green pass I would suggest possibly something like permit as the value.</p>
