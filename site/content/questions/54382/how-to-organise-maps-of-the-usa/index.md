<div class="answer-body">
<p>I guess you were downloading from <a href="http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/">http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/</a> .</p>
<p>Did you see that you can <em>custom-select areas</em> on garmin.openstreetmap.nl ("Enable manual tile selection")? That may help to reduce the file size and/or split the desired area into slices which fit on one of your SD cards each.</p>
<p>There are many other OSM-based Garmin maps - see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download</a> . Some may show less detail and are smaller that way.+</p>
<p>Third option: as far as I know (but I newer used Mapsource myself) you can select map areas to be transferred in mapsource, too.</p>
