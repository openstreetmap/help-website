<div class="answer-body">
<p>Openstreetmap.org recently changed to fastly for tile delivery in some regions.</p>
<p>As fastly may treat your request differently than the old osm tile cache network, have you tried to disable the</p>
<pre><code>proxy_set_header Host $http_host;</code></pre>
<p>line in your nginx location conf? As fastly reports, they don't know the tile.korbyt.com domain, so you shouldn't send that as a host header. I assume the old tile cdn did not look for a host header value while fastly does.</p>
</div>
