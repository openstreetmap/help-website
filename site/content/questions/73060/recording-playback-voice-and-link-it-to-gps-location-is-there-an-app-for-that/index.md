<p>i would like to find an app, which records your voice and links it to the gps location , exemple : if on a race-track you could have the feedback from the app, and you can modify/add the recorded information in the log, just by speaking</p>
<p>for example :</p>
<p>voice-start-cmd : START -&gt; starts recording/playing your voice/recording during , if no entry and silence no recording, if gps position has voice recording, plays it , but still listens to voice and changes/adds the recorded information linked to that particular gps position</p>
<p>pause-cmd ; PAUSE -&gt; pauses the recording only plays gps-location-linked information</p>
<p>stop-cmd : STOP no voice play / recording</p>
<p>the final result will be if for example you say at the beginning of the track PAUSE, it will play the instructions/info you entered during a previous pass-by ………. 90 degrees turn to right ahead , go in breaks smoothly , ….. slight turn to left,</p>
<p>or whatever instructions a rally co rider gives to the pilot</p>
<p>it could be some gadget , does not need to be an app</p>
