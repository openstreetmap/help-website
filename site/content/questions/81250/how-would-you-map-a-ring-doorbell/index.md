<p>I have been adding surveillance cameras to the map as [Points] Surveillance Type: Camera Mount Type: (Doorbell for Ring). When I know they employ facial recognition, I add that to the description.</p>
<p>Developers of mapping apps such as #Organicmaps have expressed interest in adding this to the display. Remember the main feature that made Waze popular was the identification of speed traps. These face traps will help us know where to avoid or approach with a paper bag over head. Keep adding!</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>Personally I would map it the same way as I'd map any doorbell, i.e. not at all.</p>
<p>However, I'm still at the "how do you get to an address" level of mapping, and haven't yet (generally) progressed to mapping "how do you get in" or whether the occupants might spot you arriving.</p>
<p>Having said that, taginfo finds 222 instances of <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/camera%3Amount=doorbell">camera:mount=doorbell</a>, so I suspect you would want to add <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:man_made%3Dsurveillance">surveillance related tags</a> to the adjacent door node (not sure you'd need a separate node for something as small as a doorbell).</p>
