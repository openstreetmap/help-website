<div class="answer-body">
<p>This question is not really related to Potlatch2. The only issue is that Potlatch2 does not help you with special characters <em>itself</em> (which is really not wrong). As far as I know the other OSM editors also do not offer a help themselves with this.</p>
<ul>
<li>There is the method described by Hendrik: use a "special character insert" tool/table from another program and copy &amp; paste then.</li>
<li>You can use whatever your operating system supplies — special character tables.</li>
<li>You also could copy the character from a website like Wikipedia (<span>article about "o"</span>).</li>
<li>But you may also just press the correct keys on your keyboard (which and if possible depends on your keyboard mapping).</li>
</ul>
