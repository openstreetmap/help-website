<div class="answer-body">
<p>For the currently selected <em>node</em> you can view the coordinates if it is <em>already uploaded</em> to OSM. Just click "View on openstreetmap.org" on the left bottom.</p>
<p>If the currently selected node is <em>not yet uploaded</em> then your only chance is to zoom to the maximum (currently zoom level 26) with the node in the centre and then look at the url bar of your browser. Five digit WGS84 precision is shown there, only.</p>
