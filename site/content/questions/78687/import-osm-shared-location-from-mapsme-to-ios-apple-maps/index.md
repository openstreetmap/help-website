<div class="answer-body">
<p>To share a location from OpenStreetMap:</p>
<ol>
<li>In a web browser go to openstreetmap.org</li>
<li>Move around the map until the location that you want to share is displayed</li>
<li>Copy the contents of the URL bar</li>
<li>Create a new email</li>
<li>Paste into that email</li>
<li>Send email</li>
</ol>
