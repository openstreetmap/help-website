<p>I'll try to give this a start...</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/449220">https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/449220</a> this is "Taiwan" - as you see we have all sorts of names available in out data.</p>
<p>What you or your tile provider makes out of our data is his choice. Map <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tiles">tiles</a> are usually bitmap images, so if you want to change a name, you would need to change this image. There are many tile providers which use OSM data, and all make their own choices. E.g. <a href="http://open.mapquest.com/?version=1.0&amp;hk=6-hurlYmyd">mapquest open displays</a> "Taiwan".</p>

<p>a) ask your provider whether they can give you tiles with a different content b) set up your own renderer and make your own tiles</p>
