<p>I can't be sure what is needed in your case, since I don't use those plugins, but standard Python interpreter can be downloaded from here:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.python.org/downloads/windows/">https://www.python.org/downloads/windows/</a></p>
<p>When you click on link to one of the stable versions (basically from 2.x series or 3.x series), you need to choose proper installer, basically 32-bit (called also x86) or 64-bit (called also x86-64), depending on your Windows version.</p>
