<div class="answer-body">
<p>For the charging station: see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity=charging_station">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity=charging_station</a> (an additional <code>bicycle=yes</code> is suggested if it is also for E-bikes). For the single associated parking space you can use <code>amenity=parking_space</code> and <code>capacity:charging=1</code>. A (car) example: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/3746157582">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/3746157582</a> and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/370957639">https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/370957639</a> .</p>
