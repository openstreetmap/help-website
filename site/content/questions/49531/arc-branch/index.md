<div class="answer-body">
<p>Please link to a location you are looking at. Lacking this, here is a example of which I hope it is what you mean: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit?editor=id#map=20/50.00573/8.77318">https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit?editor=id#map=20/50.00573/8.77318</a> Or schematically:</p>
<p><span><img src="/upfiles/arc_branch.png" /></span></p>
<p>Street junctions on OSM are always at least two ways which share the same node at the way intersection. <span>Ways</span> are an ordered list of nodes. There is no arc junction in <span>our data model</span> (which could possibly mean a angle of 0° ad infinitum). So, there always is some angle &gt; 0° at the intersection of two diverging ways.</p>
