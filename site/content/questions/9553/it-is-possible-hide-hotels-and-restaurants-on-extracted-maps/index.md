<div class="answer-body">
<p>There's one example in the wiki for osmfilter that I customized for you:</p>
<p>./osmfilter input.osm --drop="amenity=hotel" -o=output.osm</p>
<p>For multiple ones, you may be able to do: ./osmfilter input.osm --drop="amenity=hotel,restaurant" -o=output.osm</p>
