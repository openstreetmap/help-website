<p>There are a number of ways that have a maximum weight limit, combined with some exclusions.</p>
<p>Taking two examples, that I can think of.</p>
<ol>
<li>A road is signed as Maximum weight 7.5 tonnes, between 22:00 and 06:00</li>
<li>A road is signed as Maximum weight 7.5 tonnes, except access</li>
</ol>
<p>Obviously the maximum weight bit is relatively simple with a tag of maxweight=7.5, but how should I handle the exceptions?</p>

<p>With regard point 1, there is a way of tagging access restrictions with effective hours, see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:access">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:access</a>, maybe this can be of help to you tagging maximum weights?</p>
