<p>Most towns in UK have a Shopmobility centre which is a charity where people can hire wheelchairs (manual and electric) and sometimes other mobility aids, sometimes run by local council as part of their commitment to equal access.</p>
<p>What is the best way to tag this?</p>
<p>Couldn't find anything in the wiki other than shop=medical_supply=mobility but this is not a shop but a hire place.</p>
<p>Searching for "Shopmobility" on the map shows in the past people have tagged it as building, shop, social_facility or mobility_equipment_hire</p>
