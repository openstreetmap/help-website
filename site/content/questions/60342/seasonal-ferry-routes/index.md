<p>The wiki <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/route=ferry#combinations">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/route=ferry#combinations</a> is silent on the topic and taginfo doesn0t help either <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/route=ferry#combinations">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/route=ferry#combinations</a></p>

<p>Note the opening_hours specification currently doesn't have shorthand for the season so you will need to express it with months.</p>
