<p>The "Related questions" section (lower right side) provides a link to the question <span>how-do-i-tag-a-skywalk-skyway-sky-bridge</span>. The proposed tagging is what I would have proposed here. By the way: The <span>bridge tag</span> is quite general (at least in the English version: "man-made structures used to carry a road, railway, path canal or similar over another feature or across a valley"). If the building part is not used as walkway then I would leave away the key "bridge".</p>
<p>You may also use the <span>key building:min_level</span>.</p>
