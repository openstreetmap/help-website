<div class="answer-body">
<p>Thank you for the response and the helpful advice. I have found a work-around for Version 13576.</p>
<p>By manually moving the green "active layer" icon to the imagery I am moving, moving the imagery and pressing "OK" then manually moving the "active layer" icon back to the Data layer. This process allows JOSM to accept the moved imagery and revert back to mapping mode.</p>
