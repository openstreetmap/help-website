<div class="answer-body">
<p>Existing tag:</p>
<p><code>amenity=vending_machine</code>? Just need to indicate it provides batteries, is for vehicles, and is an exchange.</p>
<p><code>vending=battery_swap</code><br />
<code>bicycle=yes</code> / <code>moped=yes</code> (or look into recent proposals for these small electric vehicles)</p>
<p>Compare <code>vending=fuel</code> for self-serviced fuel pumps, <code>vending=bottle_return</code> for reverse vending machines recycling used bottles, and <code>vending=parcel_pickup;parcel_mail_in</code> for parcel boxes. In relevance, there's already <code>vending=bicycle_tube</code> for bikes.</p>
<p>The issue would then be making this variety of vending machine especically obvious compared to charging station.</p>
<p>Problems include contributing to a potential abuse of "vending".</p>
<p><em>Personal thought: I now wonder how to tag "charging stations" that lend you a mobile battery-charger to charge elsewhere. (for vehicles, and also mobile devices)</em></p>
