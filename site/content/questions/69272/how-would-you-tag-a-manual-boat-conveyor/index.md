<p>The term for that is a "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portage">portage</a>". While only used a few times, the tag <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Awaterway%3Dportage">waterway=portage</a> has been documented in the OSM wiki and would be the most appropriate.</p>
