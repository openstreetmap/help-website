<p>the manipulaion is called fieldwork ;)</p>
<p>What you can do, if you don't have the time or will to do surveys outside: map the buildings as good as possible from the photos and leave the enhanced details for later, when you or someone else finds time for fieldwork, or better sources become available.</p>
<p>Possibly add a fixme=* tag to the houses.</p>
