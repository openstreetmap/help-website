<p>In England and Wales, the legal restrictions on "<code>restricted byway</code>" are pretty similar to your sign (though <a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/158/scai"></a><a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/158/scai">@scai</a></a>'s point is a good one to see if yours are actually legal restrictions or just "be aware of what you might find here"). I typically tag restricted byways as something like "<code>motor_vehicle=no;vehicle=yes; foot=yes;horse=yes;bicycle=yes</code>".</p>
