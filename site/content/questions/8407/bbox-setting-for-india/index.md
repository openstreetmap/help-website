<div class="answer-body">
<p>The easiest way to find the numbers that you want (stolen shamelessly from <a href="http://weait.com/content/make-your-first-map">Richard</a> <a href="http://weait.com/content/map-tiles-and-bounding-boxes">Weait</a>) is to click the "export" tab, then shift-click and drag until the area that you want is selected. Then just read the numbers out and stick them in the appropriate place in "generate_tiles.py". Make sure you comment out stuff that you don't want to generate tiles for, of course.</p>
