<div class="question-body">
<p>I am editing a large medical center that has many building and kinds of roads. There is a large parking structure (building) for general patient/family use. The rest of the parking spaces that are spread around the complex are restricted to various reasons that you are there.<br />
</p>
<p>I don't see these attributes for the amenity=parking [lot] or service=parking_aisle :</p>
<ul>
<li>valet (an employee parks your car for you but you must leave the key--these spaces are for the valet's use)</li>
<li>emergency room (a patient is receiving urgent care in the nearby emergency room)</li>
<li>ambulance (the vehicle is stopped to carry a patient in)</li>
<li>handicapped (a patient can't walk far and has a wheelchair sticker on the car)</li>
<li>permit (an employee or frequent visitor has permission to park in the same lot/space each time)</li>
<li>reserved (saved spot)</li>
<li>20-minute drop-off (someone is parking for less than 20 minutes)</li>
<li>courier (for someone locally delivering packages, documents, medical equipment)</li>
<li>deliveries (for packages delivered by a shipping company)</li>
<li>employee (works at the medical center, ie a doctor)</li>
</ul>
<p>Is there an existing way to specify this type of parking restriction (to reflect the posted signs) or would this need to be a new feature/attribute? Note that there can be different parking restrictions on each side of the road or parking aisle.</p>
