<p><a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/647/sk53">@SK53</a> Both are possible. Eg: <code>kerb=</code> on a a feature for what kerb has; and on a <code>barrier=kerb</code> for what it is. <code>guard_rail:type=</code> is not meaningful enough, especially when it may be mistakened as a double-sided guard rail (<code>guard_rail=double</code> is only a generic solution for now).</p>
