<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are regular meetings of OpenStreetMap folks in Toronto, Windsor and Ottawa. Some less-regular meetings in other cities. Look for MappyHour YourCityName on a search engine to find a group of locals you can meet with for peer coaching. Or email me directly.</p>
<p>Also, check the <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/listinfo/talk-ca">Canadian mailing list</a>, in English or en français.</p>
<p>Or a local <a href="http://www.meetup.com/OpenStreetMap-Toronto">Meetup Group for OpenStreetMap</a></p>
