<p>The established tags for levels (from <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Simple_3D_Buildings">Simple 3D Buildings</a>) would be</p>
<pre><code>building:levels = 2
building:min_level = 1</code></pre>
<p>This goes along with <code>building=yes</code>, or <code>building:part=yes</code> if you consider it part of another building. I'm not aware of an established building value for these.</p>
<p>Add <code>layer = 1</code> to establish the vertical stacking with the features below.</p>

<p>There's no obvious tag for enclosed air-conditioned "indoor" walkways (like do we consider there's an indoor walkway within the bridge structure). I must admit I "abuse" <code>bridge=covered</code> with appropriate <code>layer=*</code>, adding <code>air_conditoning=yes</code>.
