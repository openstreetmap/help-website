<div class="answer-body">
<p>Which instructions are you following? It sounds like those instructions are out of date, and referring to Potlatch v1.</p>
<p>This has now been replaced with Potlatch v2, which doesn't have an 'edit live' mode. So just go to openstreetmap.org and click edit, it will start Potlatch 2 in 'edit with save' mode. None of your edits will be saved until you click the "Save" button in the top left corner.</p>
