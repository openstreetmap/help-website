<p>To elaborate: It's extremely common to see incorrect location pins drawn over OpenStreetMap maps embedded in third-party sites. The map underneath is generated from OSM data, but the location of the pin is very often from entirely different data which neither OSM mappers nor the OSM organization can control.</p>
<p>Facebook (owner of Instagram) is notorious for this -- search for "facebook" here and you'll see dozens of similar problems.</p>
