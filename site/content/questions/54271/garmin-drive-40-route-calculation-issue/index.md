<p>It may in fact be a incompatibility. As far as I know, Garmin did not release the specifications for their map format.</p>
<p>We do not <em>yet</em> have OSM-relevant information about <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Garmin/Drive_series">this device series in our documentation wiki</a>.</p>
<p>I would try other OSM-based maps for Garmins. Info: there is no single official download of OSM maps for Garmins. Have a look there: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download</a> .</p>
