<p>Hi, I have put that route into OSRM and it works fine, indicating that the OSM data is correct. See <a href="http://osrm.at/6Jy">http://osrm.at/6Jy</a></p>
<p>If you had missed the turn, or GPS data was in error and thought you had, it does take you on the long detour <a href="http://osrm.at/6Jz">http://osrm.at/6Jz</a> .</p>
<p>Were you driving this, or just planning?</p>
<p>At the turn around however some very strange stuff happens. On this one, the sharp right turn onto the secondary loop looks very, very wrong to me: <a href="http://osrm.at/6JA">http://osrm.at/6JA</a> .</p>
<p>I suspect missing oneway and turn restriction tags.</p>
