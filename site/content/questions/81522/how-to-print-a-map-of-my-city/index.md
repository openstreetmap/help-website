<p>The usual jumping off point for printed maps is <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_on_Paper">this page</a> on the wiki.</p>
<p>There are a variety of options depending on how you want to handle this and what software you prefer to work in. I would assume for something that big it would be best to go with something vector based, but this may depend on how close your delivery people will be looking at it.</p>
