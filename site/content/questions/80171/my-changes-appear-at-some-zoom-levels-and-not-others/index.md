<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is a recurring question: the renderer for the standard map usually takes only few minutes to process the changes, especially when they do not involve huge parts of the map, but the fresh tile might not be shown to you if something, somewhere between the source and your screen, is acting as a cache (your browser is one of these, so refreshing the page or clearing its cache, often does the trick).</p>
<p>Read the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FAQ#I_have_just_made_some_changes_to_the_map._How_do_I_get_to_see_my_changes.3F">FAQ</a> for more information.</p>
