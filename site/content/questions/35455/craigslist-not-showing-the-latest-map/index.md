<div class="answer-body">
<p>They probably have their own database instance of OSM-data. This means that they have to copy data from the main OSM server, which is driving osm.org, to keep the map on Craigslist up-to-date. When they do not do this, one will not see recents updates on their map.</p>
<p>The only team that can solve this problem is Craigslist. I'm sorry to hear they are not responsive</p>
