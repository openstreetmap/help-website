<div class="question-body">
<p>How should houses with multiple addresses be tagged (e.g. houses at intersections)? I'm talking about houses for a single family, not apartment buildings.</p>
<p>For example, the house might have the <strong>mailbox</strong>, the <strong>entrance</strong> and a <strong>driveway</strong> on different streets. And some houses actually have <strong>two number signs</strong> on them (not on the entrance, but on the house itself). I think it would be nice to have all of them searchable. Is this possible?</p>

<p>Try to find out which address is the most official (used by post office and so on) and use that one. I guess address issues like this might stem from former two family houses that have been rebuilt into one family homes and the old number for the other house is simply left on the other door without being in use.</p>
<p>You can probably check with local authorities to see which address is currently in use.</p>
