<div class="question-body">
<p>On the main web page of <a href="http://openstreetmap.org">openstreetmap.org</a> (where the slippy map is displayed) the search box is placed at the left bottom. When you use OSM on devices with a small screen you have to scroll down to see the search box at all. I even met new users who didn't know that there is a search box!</p>
<p>There have been already many efforts to move the search box more to the top of the screen. (see e.g. <a href="http://trac.openstreetmap.org/ticket/3100">bug #3100</a> or <a href="http://trac.openstreetmap.org/ticket/2038">bug #2038</a>.</p>
<p>So what are -in these days- the real reasons that prevent us from moving the position of the search box to the top? Many users would be happy!</p>
