<p>You can find some sites reporting statistics but you shouldn't trust them. Since some contributors perform mainly imports, they stats numbers can be very high in amount of edits. These numbers in terms of OSM "quality and progress" is low. Unfortunatelly, it is not easy to separate real ground contributions from imports. So instead of the 10 most amount of edits, we miss a system where we can count contributions coming from local knowledge.<br />
Differently said, 'quantity' doesn't mean 'quality'.</p>
