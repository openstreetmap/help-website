<div class="answer-body">
<p>The clue is in what you've written.</p>
<p>Select a closed way which is approximately circular and click make way circular for it to make it circular.</p>
<p>Select a way where the angles are approximately right angles and click the button for them to be made right angles.</p>
<p>You could try this in Potlatch 2 (if you then close the browser window without saving, for example) to see how it works.</p>

<p>The circle tool is good for making tidy roundabouts and the square tool is useful for buildings. The parallel tool is useful with the circle tool to make lots of circles that can be dragged to map lots of circular tanks when chemical works, oil tank refineries, wastewater plants or formal garden features. The undo tool or the bin can be used when testing techniques.</p>
