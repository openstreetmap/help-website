<p>For info, when I was looking at rendering these I came up with <a href="https://github.com/SomeoneElseOSM/SomeoneElse-style/blob/9350fcbfa5133245688918b0e77c531976701467/style.lua#L1835">this</a> list of how people map embankments on highway and non-highway, non-railway ways. I can't show you an example rendering of anywhere in the Netherlands, but an example in the UK is <a href="http://map.atownsend.org.uk/maps/map/map.html#zoom=18&amp;lat=53.198982&amp;lon=-0.789726">here</a> - you can see an embankment with a footpath on it going to the east and the embankment with no footpath going north.</p>
