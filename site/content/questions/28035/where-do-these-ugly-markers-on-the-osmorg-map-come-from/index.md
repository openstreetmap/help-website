<div class="answer-body">
<p>The markers you are seeing are coming from the "new" <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Notes">notes feature</a> which has been introduced on April this year. They are disabled by default and I guess you followed a link where they have been activated (there is also a <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/ticket/5027">ticket</a> addressing this issue). You can change this setting via the sidebar at the layer selection.</p>
