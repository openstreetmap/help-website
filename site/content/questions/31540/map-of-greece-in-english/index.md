<p>If a place has names in other languages they will typically be present in the OSM data and it depends solely on your application if they will be displayed or not.</p>
<p>The "standard" map style simply uses what is in the "name" tag, for example the MapQuest Open style uses English names where present.</p>
<p>In other words it depends on what MapFactor does with the data.</p>
