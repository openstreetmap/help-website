<div class="answer-body">
<p>Your business is not mapped at all in OpenStreetMap yet. You can ask someone to do that by adding a map note (icon with a speech balloon an plus sign) at the correct location on <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org">www.openstreetmap.org</a> (it may take quite some time to get added depending on volunteers mapping in that area) or by adding it yourself on the same site by hitting the edit button and following the instructions/tutorial.</p>
<p>Even then Instragram will likely still show the wrong location. While they take the underlying map from OpenStreetMap they use address information from unknown other sources. You need to contact Instagram directly to get that fixed.</p>
