<p>Hello! This happens sometimes in Potlatch 1. In Potlatch 2 I haven't noticed yet this behaviour. So you might want to use the new version of Potlatch. You can choose the default editor clicking on your login name and then changing it under "my settings" -&gt; "preferred editor" or you can choose the editor when hovering the mouse pointer over the edit button and then selecting the preferred editor (Potlatch 2).</p>
<p>I doubt that this bug (?) of Potlatch 1 will be corrected, considering that we have now a new version of Potlatch.</p>
