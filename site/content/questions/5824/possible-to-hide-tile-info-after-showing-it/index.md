<p>You can hide/show layers (including a tile layer) in the Layers" display (Alt+L will show it).</p>
<p>From there you can hide/show/remove a layer.</p>
<p><a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Dialog/LayerList">http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Dialog/LayerList</a></p>
