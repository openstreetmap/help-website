<div class="answer-body">
<p><span>Do</span><span>ne</span> as requested.</p>
<p><span>Way 68600885</span> was/is again the "outer" member of the <span>relation 1077083</span>. This relation here is a <span>multipolygon</span>. For this reason the way itself has no tags, which may have been the reason why you thought it is "useless". The tags are on the relation to which it belongs to.</p>
<p>Undeleting and reverting is not that easy (but also not hard). Just do what you are comfortable with. Asking for help is not a problem.</p>
