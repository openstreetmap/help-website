<div class="answer-body">
<p>The most recent way of tagging a ford is using the same technique as tagging a bridge: for the short section of road that is a ford, add the tag <code>ford=yes</code>. This has the advantage that the length of the ford crossing can be discerned and the original road type (<code>highway=*</code>) is preserved.</p>
