<p>To login at the forum:</p>
<ol>
<li>goto <a href="http://forum.openstreetmap.org/login.php">http://forum.openstreetmap.org/login.php</a></li>
<li>enter your Openstreetmap user name and password</li>
</ol>
<p>This works for me, the username is case sensitive, which is good to keep in mind.</p>
<p>You should also clear the password database for the site, so it doesn't autofill your username/password.</p>
