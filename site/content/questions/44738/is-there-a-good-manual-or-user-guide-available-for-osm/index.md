<p>There are a couple of printed books available on openstreetmap one by Frederik Ramm/Jochen Topf and another by Johanthon Bennet. Your local library may have one or google them. I also found that Steve Coast ( he dreamt up OSM ) may do one. and there is a pdf as well.
