<p>You should decide between two ways at least:</p>
<p>1) have those spots of interests added to the whole OSM database, and keep an eye on thgem by storing their OSM object ID number,</p>
<p>or 2) have an external dataset of your objects in geoJSON or any other geo format, and display them asa separate layer on an OSM based map.</p>
<p>For that purpose, have a look at the mentioned uMap or <a href="http://geojson.io">geojson.io</a> or <a href="http://cartodb.com">cartodb.com</a></p>
