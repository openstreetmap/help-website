<div class="answer-body">
<ul>
<li>Draw the outer way</li>
<li>Set the natural=woodland tag</li>
<li>Draw the inner way</li>
<li>Select both ways at the same time (by holding ctrl or shift key while selecting)</li>
<li><strong>In Potlatch 2</strong>: Click the multipolygon toolbox icon (top right)</li>
<li><strong>In JOSM</strong>: Choose the 'tools' menu -&gt; 'Create multipolygon'</li>
<li><strong>In iD</strong> - (The new editor) Choose the 'merge' tool in the context menu</li>
</ul>
