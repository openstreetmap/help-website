<p>In the first case, when boundaries are missing and no other tags such as addr:city are present, Nominatim just does a guess. This can be a lucky one, but it can also fail. It's not because Nominatim makes a lucky guess that other services make the same guess.</p>
<p>Secondly, a lot of boundaries are broken: missing roles, self-intersecting ways, non-closed ... Nominatim interprets these in a way, again as a lucky guess.</p>
<p>I don't know citymaps2go, but it's very well possible that you need to enter an address as city -&gt; street -&gt; number instead of free search.</p>
