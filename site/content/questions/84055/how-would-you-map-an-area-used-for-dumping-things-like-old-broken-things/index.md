<div class="answer-body">
<p>From your description, maybe <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:landuse%3Dlandfill"><strong><code>landuse=landfill</code></strong></a>.</p>
<p>From the aerial imagery, though, it might just be some land with some buildings and junk piles on it. I'm not sure how I'd categorize it -- some people just like to put buildings and junk on their land. It might be residential or industrial, but if you don't know it's better not to force fit a possibly false tag IMO. There isn't a mandate to categorize every square meter of the Earth. (Some mappers disagree of course. Always.)</p>
