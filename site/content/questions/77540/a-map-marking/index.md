<div class="answer-body">
<p>These are boundaries for cities/towns/municipalities. For example, <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/5652724">here</a>'s the relation for San José, which corresponds to the lines you're seeing. It isn't uncommon for geopolitical boundaries to follow major waterways, which is why you see some of them running down the middle of rivers.</p>
<p>In the same area are other boundary lines using a "dash dot dash" scheme, which indicates a district/county (or in the case of Costa Rica, a canton).</p>
