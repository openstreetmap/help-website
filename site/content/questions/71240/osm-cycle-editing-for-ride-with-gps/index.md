<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hi and also a warm welcome from me. Thanks that you want to contribute to the project. There are two aspects in your question. First you'd like to share your knowledge on objects like trails and bike shops. These are things we map in OSM and the <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/help">web page Bernard mentioned</a> is a good place to start.</p>
<p>The other thing you mention are "preferred routes used by locals". What we map are <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Cycle_routes">marked routes</a>, i. e. routes that ideally are signposted or at least described on information boards, printed maps or such. Such routes are for example shown on <a href="https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org">Waymarked Trails</a>. We do not map personal route suggestions, just commonly used routes or any informal suggestions. There are plenty of other services on the web that do that job (and may or may not use an OSM map as background).</p>
