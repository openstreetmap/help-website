<div class="answer-body">
<p>To associate an openID with your existing OSM account, you log in to your existing OSM account as always and then go to your <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/this/account">account details page</a>. There you will see an OpenID text box under password where you can add your OpenID address.</p>
<p>If you happen to be using a Google account as your OpenID, which uses "cryptic" URLs, you can just type "google.com" or "gmail.com" as your openID in that box and once you hit save it goes through the OpenID process to determine your true one which is then saved. I think this works with some other generic URLs as well, like me.yahoo.com</p>
</div>
