<p>There is also the ESRI OpenStreetMap vector tiles viewable on the web in a range of styles:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.arcgis.com/home/group.html?id=5e0d56e27d7e4a68955d399ca96c41cb&amp;view=list#content">ESRI OpenStreetMap vector tiles on ArcGIS Online</a></p>
<p>This is the one for the more-or-less clone of the "Standard" map:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.arcgis.com/apps/mapviewer/index.html?webmap=fae788aa91e54244b161b59725dcbb2a">Standard map in vector tile</a></p>
<p>Alas... not without the typical "vector tile" inherent defects like missing landuse on lower zoom scales due to the limitations of most current vector tile frameworks and compromises needed to be made for avoiding rendering milions of vector objects in a typical view port on a large screen...</p>
