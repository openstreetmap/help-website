<div class="answer-body">
<p>If by "all elements of a city" you want all of the data for an area, then there are <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Planet.osm#Country_and_area_extracts">extract services</a> that will provide this.</p>
<p>If by that you mean you want something that will extract all examples of a particular feature with in a specific area then your best bet is something like <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_turbo">Overpass Turbo</a>.</p>
