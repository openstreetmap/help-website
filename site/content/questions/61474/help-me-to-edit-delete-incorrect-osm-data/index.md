<p>Thank you for your interest in improving OpenStreetMap.</p>
<p>See <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/fixthemap">https://www.openstreetmap.org/fixthemap</a> Most of the time new contributors simply used the in-browser editor "iD" which is what you will get when you click the "Edit" button on openstreetmap.org as default.</p>
