<p>Tracks would tend to occur in less dense areas, like farm fields, forests, etc., whereas service roads would tend to be in more dense suburban and urban areas (in general). I suspect the Standard OSM Carto rendering takes this into account and renders the tracks one level earlier because there's less chance of clutter.</p>
