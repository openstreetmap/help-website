<p>I am presently mapping the E4 (Europeon Long Distance Path) in Cyprus.</p>
<p>You should indicate which device (e.g. Garmin, Android etc.) your using for more specific help.</p>
<p>For hiking <a href="http://openmtbmap.org/">OpenMTBmap.org</a> on Garmin is adequate.</p>
<p>For Android devices <a href="http://osmand.net/">Osmand</a> is adequate.</p>
<p>Take a look at <a href="http://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/de/?zoom=9&amp;lat=35.3416&amp;lon=24.738">Lonvia's Waymarked Trials</a> to see what is available/missing in Crete.</p>
