<p>Unfortunately, the usage of the name tag for route relations by mappers is a mess. Some use real names like your example but many routes have descriptions as names, e.g. public transport routes with names like "Bus 45 Central Station =&gt; Zoo". That's why Nominatim does <em>probably</em> not support them.</p>
