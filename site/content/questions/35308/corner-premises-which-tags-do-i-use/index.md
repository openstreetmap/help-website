<div class="answer-body">
<p>Here in Germany we usually don't use that kind of adressing, I guess it's related to the structure of (old) cities. Thus the 'Karlsruhe schema' for addresses at OSM doesn't reflect it.</p>
<p>Usually I would expect <strong>the geocoders</strong> to deal with such kind of requests, as all nessesary information are already there and we avoid data redundancy (that is difficult to maintain). But it seems, that at least <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Talk:Nominatim#Find_road_intersection">nominatim can't handle it</a>.</p>
