<p>I have been mapping my village in France and got all the roads done so started adding buildings. In Potlatch2 the satellite picture is not very clear and at least eight years old. So I set about adding the buildings mostly as simple rectangles with some additions of recent buildings. This is quite laborious so I left it a few days and went back to it tonight.</p>
<p>I can't believe what has happened, most of the buildings I put in have changed into beautifully edited complex shapes and they a very accurate to. Even my house, entered as a simple rectangle is showing minor details that are completely invisible from the satellite picture.</p>
<p>I can only think some other person has done this but I don't feel convinced that is the explanation because most of this area is almost completely UN-mapped. How can this be? Newbie naturally.</p>
