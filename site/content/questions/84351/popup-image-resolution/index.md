<div class="answer-body">
<p>It appears that the photos in horizontal orientation are linked to the "h" size images while the vertical photos are linked to "c" or "z" size images. Some examples:</p>
<p>horizontal:<br />
<a href="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/52027916780_0f56affd81_h.jpg">https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/52027916780_0f56affd81_h.jpg</a> <a href="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51982755773_afd3bb814b_h.jpg">https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51982755773_afd3bb814b_h.jpg</a></p>
<p>vertical:<br />
<a href="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51983244240_8f61869f0a_z.jpg">https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51983244240_8f61869f0a_z.jpg</a><br />
<a href="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51982758638_9cb43bbcc7_c.jpg">https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51982758638_9cb43bbcc7_c.jpg</a> <a href="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51981683532_7fe1ce0f92_z.jpg">https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51981683532_7fe1ce0f92_z.jpg</a></p>
<p>While their originals may all have the same resolution you seem to have linked to various lower resolution copies. In flickr you can click on the download button and then "View all sizes" to see the different options.</p>
