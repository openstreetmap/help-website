<p>The changes are available in the database immediately.</p>
<p>The rendered tiles take a while to update. Other services based on OSM like the routing on the main page and others downstream providers also update at their own pace.</p>
<p>For tiles clearing your browser cache for the site can be useful to force a reload. The English <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FAQ#I_have_just_made_some_changes_to_the_map._How_do_I_get_to_see_my_changes?">FAQ on the wiki</a> has some further information on this.</p>
