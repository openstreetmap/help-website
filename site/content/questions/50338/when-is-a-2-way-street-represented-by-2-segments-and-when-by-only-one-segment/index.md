<p>Generally - for streets - we map 2 parallel ways when there is some kind of physical barrier, and one way only when the divider is simply a line. The boundary is blurry though. I suggest that you choose which form of mapping works best for you and apply that, without attempting to change the work of others to fit your personal style.</p>
