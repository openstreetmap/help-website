<p>I'm not sure what you did, but it still in the middle of the sea as far as I can see (see <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/762380470/history#map=17/46.19626/38.20465">this node history</a>). I've commented on the <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/43171930#map=8/47.030/36.660">changeset</a>; I'm sure the original mapper will fix it shortly.</p>
<p>The more general answer is "try and see what went wrong and then (a) talk to the original mapper about it, so they don't do it again, and (b) try and undo what went wrong rather than just deleting".</p>
<p>The steps involved in "try to undo" might be different depending on what else happened in the changeset - it might just need a revert in JOSM, or more manual tidying up might be needed.</p>
