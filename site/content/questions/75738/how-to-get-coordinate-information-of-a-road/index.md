<p>There's a tricky method: when you are on <a href="http://osm.org">openstreetmap.org</a>, right click on the point you want to know the coordinates of and select "Center map here". Then, in the URL bar the URL will be changed to something like <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=ZZ/XX.XXXXX/YY.YYYYY"><code>https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=ZZ/XX.XXXXX/YY.YYYYY</code></a>:ZZ is the map zoom level, X and Y are the N/S coordinates and E/W coordinates respectively.</p>
