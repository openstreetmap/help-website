<p>Both the projected globe and the tiles are square (-&gt; w:h=1) in standard web Mercator projection. Most screens and therefore windows shown on them are rectangular, typically with a width to height ratio between 4:3 and 16:9. To fit the world seemlessly in to such a window you either need to distort the tiles horizontally or use a projection that fits better.</p>
<p>See <a href="http://www.progonos.com/furuti/MapProj/Dither/TOC/cartTOC.html">http://www.progonos.com/furuti/MapProj/Dither/TOC/cartTOC.html</a> , <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Map_projection">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Map_projection</a> , <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Map_projection">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Map_projection</a> ,</p>
