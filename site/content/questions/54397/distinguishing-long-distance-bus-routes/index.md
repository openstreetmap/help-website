<p>In many parts of the world buses are the main ways of travelling long distances. Some simple examples: Rio Gallegos to El Calafate (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/3670030">one</a> of several), St Petersburg to Riga, Kuala Lumpur to Ipoh, Madrid to most towns &amp; cities in Spain. Often these long distance routes are operated very differently from regular local services: infrequent stops, higher quality seating, in-service refreshments etc.</p>
<p>Is there any tag or set of tags which allows one to distinguish local, regional, national and international level bus services?</p>
