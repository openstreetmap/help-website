<div class="answer-body">
<p>I don’t think there is world wide consensus on how to map this. In theory a pedestrian router should be able to route across a square but apparently this is not an easy thing for some reason. I am not aware of any pedestrian router that can do that.</p>
<p>So many people add “virtual” footways across or around the square to connect things together. When that is done, it seems that they usually put the footways along the routes that would typically be used by people traversing the square.</p>
