<div class="answer-body">
<p>(assuming that you're based in the England or Wales here)</p>
<p>Personally, I'd tag the road as "highway=whatever" (if it's a proper road, probably "highway=service"), and if appropriate stick other tags on it for surface, width, etc.</p>
<p>I'd tag it as "designation=public_footpath" to reflect the legal status.</p>
<p>I'd also add "access=private" to reflect the general access sign, and add "foot=yes" to reflect the fact that foot access is definitely allowed.</p>
<p>However.... There was a thread on the talk-gb mailing list (I think last year) that was essentially "does this look like an official public footpath sign to you" and some "access=private" signs may not reflect the legal reality but merely the landowner's wishes, so it'll probably end up with you deciding "what does this look like to me"?</p>
