<div class="answer-body">
<p>"Potlatch automatically simplifies tracks on import" (mentioned <a href="http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?pid=10768#p10768">in the forum</a> by Richard). This is what I have experienced many times (annoyingly) in the past with Potlatch2 (I guess Richard's post is about Potlatch 1). I see no option in Potlatch2 to turn it off. Annoying if you do high precision gps recordings (e.g. if you walk around a tree).</p>
<p>Workaround1: it seems (tested with two traces by me and your example 1425846.gpx) that gps traces are not simplified by Potlatch2's display engine if you open a trace locally from your computer (via Potlatch2 button "background" -&gt; "vector file" -&gt; "File open").</p>
<p>Workaround2: use another <span>editor</span>.</p>
