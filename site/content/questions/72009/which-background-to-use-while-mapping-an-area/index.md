<p>See the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Good_practice#Align_aerial_imagery_before_tracing">Align Imagery Before Tracing</a> section of the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Good_practice">Good Practice</a> page on our wiki and follow some of the links there. The short answer is that none of the imagery sources are reliable, and which one is the best can vary from region to region.</p>
