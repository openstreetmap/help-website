<p>Where does gCarta / GeoFlyer 3D Maps have their DEM data from?</p>
<p>On their <a href="https://www.geomind.it/en/solutions/terrain-big-data/gcarta/">website</a> they only say <em>highly-detailed 3D terrain</em>, but not the source. I have asked them already, but didn’t get an answer. And there is no article or information about this nice to view (but difficult or complicated to use/move around) app in the <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org">OSM-Wiki</a>.</p>
<p>The map data is from OpenStreetMap.</p>
<ul>
<li>Advantage of this app: really detailed digital elevation model data.</li>
<li>Disadvantage: no vector data for the ways, only for the names of some POIs, rest is raster graphics</li>
</ul>
