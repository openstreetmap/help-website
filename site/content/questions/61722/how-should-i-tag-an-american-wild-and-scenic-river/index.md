<div class="answer-body">
<p>The correct tag would be <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:boundary%3Dprotected_area">boundary=protected_area</a>, protect_class=5. On the wiki, see the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:boundary%3Dprotected_area#Nature-protected-areas">table for nature-protected areas</a>, and scroll down to the US section.</p>
<p>I'm not sure where exactly the tag should go though--on the river way? riverbank? does the Wild &amp; Scenic protection include area outside the riverbank?</p>
