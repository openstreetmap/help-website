<p>This is not pretty, but you can do it this way in iD:</p>
<ol>
<li>Select one of the areas</li>
<li>Click on one node which joins it to the adjacent area, and use the Split (scissors) tool</li>
<li>Click on that node again and use the Disconnect tool</li>
<li>Repeat with the other node</li>
<li>Then click on that node and delete it, the middle line should disappear.</li>
<li>Repeat with other three areas.</li>
<li>Then shift-select all the remaining outer ways and Merge them.</li>
</ol>
