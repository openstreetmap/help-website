<p>I'm mapping in Alaska and have some questions about the proper tagging of POIs for air taxis and adventure tour guide services. There are many of these small businesses scattered around Alaska primarily catering to the tourist industry. Also, because many places in Alaska cannot be reached by road those places receive supplies and passengers by air so it could be very useful to tag the air taxi businesses appropriately.</p>
<p>I found nothing at all about air taxis or float plane services in the Wiki or in Taginfo. And nothing about guided tour operators. Usually I would be tagging the building that contains the office for these businesses but shop=* doesn't seem right for this sort of operation.</p>
<p>Any suggestions?</p>
