<div class="answer-body">
<p>You need to <em>be logged in</em> the wiki first (top right). Then there is a "Upload file" (or "Datei hochladen" in German) in the "tools" box on the left side panel.</p>
<p>If you still cannot see this link there, then maybe your account is too new. <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Wiki:User_confirmation">Your wiki account must be 4 days old and have 10 wiki edits to be autoconfirmed</a></p>
