<div class="answer-body">
<p>Most Aerial imagery is donated for our use. You can probably guess it is expensive to make. Companies will invest in new images only if they can make a profit. I met a guy on a group walk who had mapped a village in 2000 several years prior to openstreetmap. He had contributed to the fuel cost of a pilot who needed to keep up his annual flying hours, He then photographed the village from the air and mapped it. Areas of expanding economies may be a candidate for new surveys, but don't hold your breath. Cambridge UK images are I think at least 4 years old. Note most images are i believe made with aircraft not sats.</p>
