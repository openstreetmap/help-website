<div class="answer-body">
<p>If I have understood the question correctly, it is asking how to show the direction of a one-way street.</p>
<p>In Potlatch 2, select the way (click on it), and in the 'Oneway' drop-down box, select 'One way' or 'One way reverse' to set the direction.</p>
<p>Please accept my apologies for replying in English. Here is an attempt at a Spanish version.</p>
<p>En Potlatch 2, seleccione la forma (haga clic en él), y en la casilla desplegable de la 'Oneway', seleccione 'One way' o 'One way reverse' para establecer la dirección.</p>
