<div class="answer-body">
<p>Using <strong>Potlatch 2</strong>, here's how you move all the ways from relation B to relation A. Note that both relations will need to have been loaded - in other words, you need to pan to an area that has both in.</p>
<ul>
<li>Switch to Advanced view.</li>
<li>Select one of the ways in relation A.</li>
<li>Add it to relation B (using the 'Add to' button).</li>
<li>You should now have both relations showing in the Relations panel of the Advanced view.</li>
<li>In the dropdown menu to the right of relation B, choose 'Select all members'.</li>
<li>In the dropdown menu to the right of relation A, choose 'Add selection to this relation'.</li>
<li>Finally, select relation B, and click 'Remove from'.</li>
</ul>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>In <strong>JOSM</strong> you open both relations for editing in the relation-editor (on the right, select the relation from the list and click on the icon with the sheet of paper, eventually you have to make the <a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Dialog/RelationList">dialog</a> visible by clicking on the left vertical icon list, cog wheel icon.).</p>
<ul>
<li>select all elements of one relation in the <em>relation editor</em> (click on one element in the right column and hit <code>ctrl+a</code>)</li>
<li>select then these elements in the map editing window by clicking on the icon with the <code>blue arrow pointing right</code> in the <em>Selection</em> column</li>
<li>now go to the <em>relation editor</em> window of the other relation and add all selected elements.</li>
<li>eventually assign <em>roles</em> to the newly added elements</li>
<li>now delete the first relation</li>
<li>confirm the modifications by clicking on the <code>OK</code> button of the relation window</li>
</ul>
