<div class="answer-body">
<p>I assume that by "midpoint" you mean the point on the half distance between two neighbouring nodes of a way which is symbolised by a "+" in JOSM.</p>
<p>I know no direct way to do that. I often zoom in very much to the "+" and then insert a new node into the way at approximately that position. Afterwards I can use it as a snap target or tag it (e.g. a building entrance).</p>
