<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hello,</p>
<p>I don't think it's possible, you could raise an issue at the <a href="https://github.com/umap-project/umap/issues">issue tracker of umap</a>, but I don't think anyone is going to spend time working on this kind of functionality, as it's not usually the point of this application.</p>
<p>When you can edit the map, you can download the data as GPX, which is really convenient, but here it seems edition is closed.</p>
<p>If you click on some circuit, clicking "Détails parcours" will lead you to a page where individual GPX tracks are downloadable.</p>
<p>Once you have the GPX files, you can use some app to read them, I really like OsmAnd. Organic Maps is a simpler choice, but you have to convert gpx to kml first. These app are really made for usage on the go, the data is fully available offline, and much more.</p>
<p>Regards.</p>
