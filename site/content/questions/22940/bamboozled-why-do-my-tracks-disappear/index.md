<p>Many mappers use a program called gps babel to down load and process their traces. Time stamps are required. They are a witness to a walked, driven or ridden survey and not just a tracing from another map. It is not a good idea to create them with a text editor. see <a href="http://www.gpsbabel.org/">http://www.gpsbabel.org/</a></p>
