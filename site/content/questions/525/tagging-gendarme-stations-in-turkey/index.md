<p>Since I'm living in Turkey I started using <code>amenity=police</code> and <code>name=Jandarma</code>. Actually it's a military subdivision which is responsible for the maintenance of the public order in areas that fall outside the jurisdiction of police forces (generally in rural areas), (see <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gendarmerie_(Turkey)">wikipedia article</a> )</p>
