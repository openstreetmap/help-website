<p>It's rather straight forward. Go the website, enter your id NNN into the field <em>relation id</em> and press <em>Go!</em>. On the next page you have some orange buttons on the top labled <em>Deep History</em>, <em>History Browser</em> and so forth. Clicking those takes you to different views for the history of your relation.</p>
<p>Take for example the <em>history browser</em>. You get all the versions of your relation listed. Select two to compare (left and right radio buttons) and click <em>compare selected versions</em>. You see a list of members for each compared version.</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can view history from Josm.</p>
<p>Select the <strong>relation</strong>, View, History and you can view the changes that were made to the tags of the relation.</p>
<p>There is a dropdown box next to each listed changeset and you can select to view the changes in that particular changeset... I normally view in Achavi which displays the changed ways.<br />
I think this is the way you want to view but am not sure if it shows all the changes.</p>
<p>You can instead <strong>select members</strong> of the relation and view the tag changes on the members. Beware of doing this on a large relation with many members as it can be unmanageable. Sometimes it may be better to select just one or few members to view.</p>
