<p>Actually - the <em>isolated dwelling</em> tag is used for named places that are smaller than a hamlet - no more than a few buildings really. If it doesn't have its own place name, just use building=yes on a node, or (much) better yet, draw the building as a polygon with building=yes.<br />
In short, <strong>place=isolated dwelling</strong> will never render without a placename, and will in fact be classified as an error/issue without one!</p>

<p>I agree with the first part. I wouldn't go so far to call an isolated dwelling without a name an error, though. There could also other tags like <code>ele</code> or <code>population</code> be added for dwellings without a name.</p>
