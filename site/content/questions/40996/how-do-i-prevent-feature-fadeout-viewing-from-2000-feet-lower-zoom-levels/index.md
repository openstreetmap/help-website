<div class="answer-body">
<p>You cannot easily modify the Standard map style.</p>
<p>An option would be to use a <span>specialized map/service</span>. → Have a look at the OSM-based energy maps by ito: <a href="http://www.itoworld.com/map/group/16">http://www.itoworld.com/map/group/16</a> I guess "electricity distribution" should be good for you.</p>
<p>Another option would be to use <span>overpass turbo</span> (optionally together with uMap) or just to view them in an OSM editor like JOSM.</p>
<p>Of course, you always could <span>render</span> your own custom map, based on OSM-data.</p>
<p>Also see the suggestions on <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Power">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Power</a> .</p>
