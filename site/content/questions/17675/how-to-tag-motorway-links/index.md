<div class="answer-body">
<p>In Germany, many links are tagged using the keys <code>destination</code> and/or <code>destination_ref</code> - the former naming the places listed on the exit sign, the latter naming the number of the highway you get to when you use the link. Needless to say, this only works with oneway links! Links rarely have a <code>ref</code> or <code>name</code> tag of their own.</p>
