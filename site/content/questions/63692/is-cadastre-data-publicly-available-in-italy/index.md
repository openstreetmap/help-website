<div class="answer-body">
<p>There has been multiple imports of government data in Italy however as far as I know these have always been datasets from local/regional administrative bodies.</p>
<p>I suspect you will get the best answer from asking the community in question, giving your area of interest, on <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/listinfo/talk-it">https://lists.openstreetmap.org/listinfo/talk-it</a> which seems to be fairly active, or <a href="https://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewforum.php?id=39">https://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewforum.php?id=39</a> which seems to be fairly quiet.</p>
<p>In any case you should have a look at <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/WikiProject_Italy">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/WikiProject_Italy</a> for more information.</p>
