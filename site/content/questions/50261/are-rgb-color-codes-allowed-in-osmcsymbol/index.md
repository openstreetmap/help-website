<div class="answer-body">
<p>Taginfo indicates that there are ~2 objects that use color codes:</p>
<p><a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/?key=osmc%3Asymbol#values">http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/?key=osmc%3Asymbol#values</a></p>
<p>(search for <code>#</code> in the search box that is to the right of the page below the <code>Values used with this key</code> header)</p>
<p>So the de facto situation is pretty clear, use the named colors.</p>
<p>Looking at the wiki, it looks like consumers of the tag expect it to follow the format given there.</p>
