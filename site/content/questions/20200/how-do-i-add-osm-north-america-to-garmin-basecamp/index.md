<p>Our docu wiki page <span>"OSM Map On Garmin/Download"</span> says: "As .gmapi for Mac OSx Roadtrip/Basecamp." So choose a map provider which also supplies a gmapi file for your <span>BaseCamp</span> on MacOS. (I don't use MacOS)</p>
<p>If that does not help, please better describe your problem (what/where goes something wrong) - just edit your question.</p>
<p>Note that there is not only one "OSM map". OSM is just data which can be used in various ways. Choose a map which best fits your needs.</p>
