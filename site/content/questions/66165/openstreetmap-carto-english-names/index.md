<p>This is the best solution. A more primitive approach is to simply replace queries for the "name" column with <code>COALESCE(tags-&gt;'name:en', tags-&gt;'int_name', name) AS name</code> which will then replace the local name with the name:en or int_name where these are set. Note that this, like the above osml10n solution, depends on having imported with <code>--hstore</code>, otherwise these names would not even be there.</p>
