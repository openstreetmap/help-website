<div class="answer-body">
<p>It's simple.</p>
<p>Split the way in two places, 1) where the bridge starts, and 2) where it ends.</p>
<p>Click on the new segment you just created to select it and add the tags</p>
<p>bridge=yes</p>
<p>layer=1</p>
<p>There are other tags you might use to enhance this simple scenario, e.g.,</p>
<p>bridge:name=Memorial Bridge</p>
<p>bridge:structure=suspension</p>
<p>but the first two tags will do the trick</p>
