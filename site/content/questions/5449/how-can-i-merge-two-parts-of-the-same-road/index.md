<div class="answer-body">
<p>Not entirely magic key presses:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Potlach</strong>. Select both ways whilst holding the CTRL key down. Then in the tool panel at bottom right click on the chain icon and the two ways become one. If you are in advanced view it shows whether the two ways have different tag values (its a good idea to check in case the ways are split for a reason).</li>
<li><strong>Josm</strong>. Select both ways, and the short-cut "c" will combine the two ways. In JOSM a dialog box prompts if the tagging is different or the ways have different orientations.</li>
</ul>
