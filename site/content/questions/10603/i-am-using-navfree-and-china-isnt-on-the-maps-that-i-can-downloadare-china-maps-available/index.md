<p>Generally you can download raw OSM data for any place or region you want. But to use it in a certain navigation app, this raw data has to be converted in an appropriate format.</p>
<p>If you obly have the choice to use the navfree app, and the guys of navfree do not offer map data for your needed region, and you do not have the possibility to create your ofn map files for navfree, then that is bad luck for you.</p>
<p>If you can use other apps like Navit or Osmand (to name but a few) then you can even create your own map data in their specual format.</p>
<p>Unfortunately you have not given any information what device you are using, so we cannot give you more hints.</p>
