<div class="answer-body">
<p>At least in the US, ZIP codes don't line up with geographic areas, they line up with postal routes. In a few cases, there's situations where two zipcodes checkerboard an otherwise coherent polygon. Worse, zip codes, particularly in rural areas, tend to be in a continuous state of flux.</p>
<p>It's best to stick to the postal_code key on known addresses in the US.</p>
