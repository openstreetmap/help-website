<div class="answer-body">
<p>It means that when routing for pedestrians, you can use the ferry. It doesn't meant that you can walk on the water.</p>
<p>Some ferries take cars, some don't take pedestrians, so these tags are necessary. A routing engine must not treat everything tagged "foot=yes" as meaning that it is a path, without checking the other tags.</p>
<p>This is the same thing as using a cable car tagged foot=yes - it means that pedestrians can take the cable car, not that you can walk along the wires.</p>
