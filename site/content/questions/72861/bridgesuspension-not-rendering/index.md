<div class="answer-body">
<p>Historically, the "approved" <code>bridge=*</code> tags with a "structure" value rendered (cantilever, trestle) are proposed together with <code>bridge:structure=</code>. <code>bridge=*</code> is basically reserved for significantly distinct types of bridges. There are 2490 <code>bridge:structure=suspension</code> uses, more than 973 <code>bridge=suspension</code> now. The latter would be "misunderstandings".</p>
</div>
