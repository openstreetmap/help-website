<div class="answer-body">
<p><a href="https://mapserver.org/">MapServer</a> and <a href="http://geoserver.org/">GeoServer</a> are two open-source server-side software products, which can be used for managing all sorts of different map data from different sources.</p>
<p>The two projects are completely unrelated to OpenStreetMap.</p>
