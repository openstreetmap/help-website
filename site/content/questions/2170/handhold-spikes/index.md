<p>I am very new to OpenStreetMap, so if I am asking a dumb question, please let me know and point me in the right direction.</p>
<p>I have been entering walking tracks around some local woodland. In general there is nothing special about them. However there is one section where the path stops being a regular track and turns into a set of handhold spikes and footholds to traverse a cliff-face on a river.</p>
<p>This seems to be an important feature - it makes the trail effectively impassable with a bike, or a dog, or if you have limitted use of arms, and probably undesirable with small children.</p>
<p>I can't figure out how to tag this section to indicate this.</p>

<p>highway=path<br />
rungs=yes/amount</p>

<p>wheelchair=no<br />

bicycle=no<br />
