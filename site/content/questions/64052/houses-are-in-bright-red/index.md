<p>Somehow <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/59594185#map=18/52.30525/0.49063">this changeset</a> seems to have uploaded duplicates of many of the buildings such as <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/594524157">this one</a> duplicated as <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/594521616">this one</a>. I don't know how this has happened or whether his might be why you are seeing what you are seeing - I didn't get to see anything disappear, although buildings weren't visible until I zoomed far enough in. I'm guessing you could try deleting the duplicates and seeing if it helps. It might be easier to edit this in Potlatch 2 as it is fairly obvious where two nodes are exactly on top of each other (by the red dots on the corner of the buildings), but I know that better than iD (the default editor). I notice in iD that the particularly red houses, such as <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/594536651">this one</a> have more than one duplicate - this example is one of 7 ways on top of each other.</p>
