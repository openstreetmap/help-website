<p>Didn't mean to discourage you! It's not too bad, really. :)</p>
<p>In iD, you can do this by:</p>
<ol>
<li>click on the lake outline</li>
<li>in the left sidebar, look down a bit for where it says Water Havash Lake (in the relations section)</li>
<li>change the name</li>
<li>click Save.</li>
</ol>
