<p>Hi, Have a look at Tower Bridge, London, that's drawn with two leaves, description=southern leave and description=northern leave. This link to the bridge:- <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=17/51.50578/-0.07377&amp;layers=N">https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=17/51.50578/-0.07377&amp;layers=N</a> If the triangle shape of the two leaves is to be indicated that can be done by sectioning the bridge highway and tagging each section as incline=up (to the centre point).</p>
