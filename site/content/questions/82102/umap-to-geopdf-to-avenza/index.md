<p>Hello,</p>
<p>you can <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/UMap/Guide/Export_data_of_my_uMap">export umap data</a> as geojson, and then there are online converters (like <a href="https://mygeodata.cloud/converter/geojson-to-geopdf">this one</a>), or GDAL probably can do it.</p>
<p>But to combine the data with existing OSM data might prove difficult.</p>
<p>Also, I don't see how you'll get navigation, as geopdf is mostly an image.</p>
<p>I suggest using OsmAnd, where you can download OSM data, and display GPX data (which you can also export from umap) as an overlay, and even route along a GPX track.</p>
<p>My 2 cents.</p>
