<p>You probably meant different tag (wetland=saltmarsh instead of natural=saltmarsh):</p>
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Awetland%3Dsaltmarsh">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Awetland%3Dsaltmarsh</a></p>
<p>This white polygon is just a bare land - together with clear water area around, because your polygon has no defined rendering for standard map style (OSM Carto) and is treated as empty space over land and the water.</p>
