<div class="answer-body">
<p><code>landuse=residential</code> is used to describe land that is used for housing.</p>
<p><code>place=neighbourhood</code> is for a small, named residential area. As the boundaries between individual neighbourhoods are often quite blurry they are often mapped as nodes.</p>
