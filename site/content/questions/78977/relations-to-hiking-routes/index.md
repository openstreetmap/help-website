<div class="answer-body">
<p>[Slightly simplified] Routes are modelled in OSM as a list (the Relation) of ways (for example a trail segment).</p>
<p>To add a way to an existing Route relation in iD:</p>
<ul>
<li>select the way</li>
<li>in the pane on the left hand side, go right to the bottom (that is to the "Relations" section) and click the "plus" button. That will offer you existing Relations in the vicinity* or allow you to create a new one.</li>
</ul>
<p>* I don't believe there is currently an easy way to access a Relation that doesn't have an existing member nearby.</p>
