<p>Using <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/JOSM">JOSM</a>, the normal process is :</p>
<ol>
<li>Download data for your area of interest</li>
<li>Edit this data locally</li>
<li>Upload changes</li>
</ol>
<p>You can just replace the last step by saving the data locally, for future offline use.</p>
