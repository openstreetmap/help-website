<div class="answer-body">
<p>OK, worked example using the war memorial <a href="http://osm.org/go/0EHzEvKoW--">here</a>.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Click the + in the top right of the map area, then tick next to Data.</p></li>
<li><p>Click on the green dot marking the memorial; brief details are available on the left.</p></li>
<li><p>Click "Details" next to the node number in the left hand panel - this will show details about who edited it most recently</p></li>
<li><p>Click "view history" to see all versions, and in particular who added the original version.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>On either the "Details" or the "view history" screens you can click on the username next to "Edited by" to get to that user's profile page, and the first option on that page under the username is "Send message" which will allow you to send them a message.</p>
