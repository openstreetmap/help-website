<p>The wiki has an article on <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Conditional_restrictions">Conditional restrictions</a> where such a speed limit is used as an example.</p>
<p>Note that routing engines are unlikely to process the conditional restriction because they will usually not allow you to specify which time of day your route is for.</p>
