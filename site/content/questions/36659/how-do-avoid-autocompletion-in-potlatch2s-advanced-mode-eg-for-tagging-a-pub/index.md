<p>Well, strictly speaking pub <em>nodes</em> autocomplete to "pub" but pub <em>ways</em> autocomplete to "public_building" if you press return.</p>
<p>However, if you don't press return, but "tab" after typing "pub" if doesn't autocomplete.</p>
<p>Also for me "clicking anywhere else after typing pub" doesn't delete the whole tag. Depending on what you click on it might select something else, but the pub is still there. Perhaps this is a browser-dependant thing (it's working for me on Seamonkey on Windows 7)?</p>
