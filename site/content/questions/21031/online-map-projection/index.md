<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>The OSM map, just like most other world-wide online maps, uses a projection called "<span>Spherical Mercator</span>" a.k.a. "Popular Visualisation CRS" or "<span>EPSG:3857</span>" (formerly, EPSG:900913 and EPSG:3785). It is not entirely correct to call this "the Mercartor projection".</p>
<p>OSM doesn't change the projection at any zoom level.</p>
