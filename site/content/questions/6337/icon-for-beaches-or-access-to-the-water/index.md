<p>There is a tag for beaches, <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Beach">natural=beach</a>. You can add supervised=yes if there are lifeguards, and you can add swimming=no, but those tags won't be visible in Mapnik. Only beach will be visible, as a yellow dotted surface. I don't know if other icons are planned.</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>I'd consider mapping the physical object used to provide access to the water, such as a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway=footway">highway=footway</a> or <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway=steps">highway=steps</a> to the water edge, or if there is a ladder to get down to the water level use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:ladder">ladder=yes</a> on the node.</p>
<p>If there is something on the ground indicating a particular place to enter the water then even <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:entrance">entrance=yes</a> on the node of the waters edge.</p>
