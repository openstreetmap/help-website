<p>The short answer is no. The imagery you see is provided by Bing, and they have their own schedules for which areas get updated when.</p>
<p>You may be able to buy more up-to-date imagery of the area you're editing, but it won't be cheap and it gives you quite a limited set of information compared with a ground survey.</p>
