<p>There is a symbol for power generators in the Potlatch 2 presets. Select a node then go to the menu section for "Power", and choose the icon for <code>Plant (station)</code>. This is the symbol for a power generating station (also known as a power plant), and it will add the tag <code>power=generator</code>. You can also specify the energy source, eg wind, hydro, coal etc.</p>

<p>Same procedure as for any tag not present in Potlach 2:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>find a place with an already existing such tag (here a windmill)</p></li>
<li><p>switch to editing with P2, click on the object, switch to the "advanced" tag, remember which tag ord is used</p></li>
<li><p>quit editing without saving (anyway you didn't change anything)</p></li>
<li><p>go to the actual place you want to add a new tag</p></li>
<li><p>click on the target object, switch back to edit, then to the "advanced" tag</p></li>
<li><p>add the right tagword, the one you just remembered above</p></li>
<li><p>save.</p></li>
</ul>

<p>A single wind turbine is one of many "parts" of a "wind farm" ("wind farm" = "plant"). I would place a single point in the geographic centroid of the entire wind farm and attribute according to Power, Plant, etc. However, I would also place a "Tower" for each wind turbine on the "farm". That's what I've been doing, anyway.</p>
<p>CORRECTION: There will be a fairly central (not necessarily in the geographic center) "hub" (not sure what it's called) for the wind farm. The wind turbines are spread out over many, many square miles of farm land. This "hub" may look like (or even be an addition to) a substation. When I have placed a Tower for each wind turbine, I have added Designation = "wind turbine" since there is presently no choice other than "Tower" that fits a wind turbine.</p>
