<div class="answer-body">
<p>You didn't say where it isn't visible, but I assume you're talking about the "Standard" rendering at OpenStreetMap.org. Each different map rendering of the OSM data can choose to show whatever they want. In the case of the Standard rendering, the maintainers of that style either have consciously chosen to not render leisure=dance objects, or simply haven't considered that tag for rendering. If you have a convincing case for why a certain tag should be rendered on any particular map, you'll need to contact the maintainers of that map and make your case. If it's the Standard rendering, you would do that <a href="https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto">here</a>.</p>
