<p>How to tag an area which is</p>
<ul>
<li>Used to train solders</li>
<li>Large-ish, several square kilometres, mostly forest and countryside (including cattle!)</li>
<li>Not, strictly speaking, off-limits to the general public, but</li>
<li>may be noisy and risky ("blank" ammunition firing, combat vehicles doing "tactical" driving, ...)</li>
</ul>
<p><code>landuse=military, military=training_area</code> looked promising, but, judging by the wiki, <code>training_area</code> is more of a research/test facility and not so much about squaddies doing the same ambush over and over again... And if call this a <code>danger_area</code>, what, then, do I use for the areas where there actually are live shells dropping out of the sky, and where you get prosecuted if you enter?</p>
