leisure: pitch<br />
sport: ice-stock<br />
surface: paving-stones<br />
lanes: 4<br />
lit: yes.</p>

building: yes<br />
club: sport<br />
sport: ice_stock.</p>

name, club, operator, addr:street, addr:housenumber, addr:postcode, addr:city, website</p>
