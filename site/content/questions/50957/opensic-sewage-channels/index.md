<p>Hi rabon, use waterway=drain for the smallest and lined waterway being a neither canal nor river. Use natural=water and water=sewage as well to make clear that the water is not clear. The ditches you’re mentioned are not lined as I expect the sewage drains are. Otherwise the streets should or could be wasted by the next water flow.</p>
