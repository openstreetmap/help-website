<p>Routers know not to go against a one-way flow. You can map 'forward' and 'reverse' roles for correctness, but any halfway competent router will get it right anyway. Several routers use route relations as an influence on their weighting - <a href="https://cycle.travel/map">https://cycle.travel/map</a> (my site!) certainly does and there are others.</p>
