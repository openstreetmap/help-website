<div class="answer-body">
<p>Apparently, Maps.me adds certain tags correctly to OSM but displays them differently inside the app itself. I recently used Maps.me to add a Thai restuarant and when I looked at the cuisine menu it offered Thai as a choice, which is incorrect because cuisine tag values are never capitalized in OSM. I checked the tagging later from within JOSM and found that it had added "thai" as cuisine.</p>
<p>I'm not sure why they do this but it does tend to mislead those of us who are familiar with OSM. Perhaps because they're playing to an audience that is either new to OSM or only use it occasionally?</p>
<p>As for the grammatical error you reported, it is probably just that the programmers simply don't know any better. Many people use incorrect grammar these days. Perhaps you've noted the very common misuse of "your" for "you're" and "there" for "they're" or "their"? One often sees signs offering "Burger's to Go" and other similar misuses of possessive nouns.</p>
<p>Cheers,</p>
<p>Dave</p>
