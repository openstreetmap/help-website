<p>There are many different workflows possible outside of joting down the information on paper</p>
<ul>
<li>take geo-referenced fotos (for example with OpenCamera) and then use JOSM (unluckily this is not possible with iD) to add the information at home by using the photo import support in JOSM.</li>
<li>use mapillary (supported in both iD and JOSM)</li>
<li>use OsmAnd or maps.me to add POIs (both have significant disadvantages compared to proper editors, which may or may not be relevant for you)</li>
<li>use vespucci <a href="http://vespucci.io/">http://vespucci.io/</a> to add the POIs directly</li>
<li>and various combinations of the above</li>
</ul>
