<p>Nice searching on taginfo! I'm not necessarily advocating shop=supplements, but I don't think either of these options is better, though. shop=health is really vague, and health_food as a value is really subjective. For example, a food cooperative that sells organic food, etc could be considered as shop=health_food, but IMO makes a lot more sense as a shop=supermarket.</p>
