<div class="answer-body">
<p>I have reviewed some more-or-less randomly picked cases in Germany: JOSM and Potlatch2 were used. The tag is used to mark that there really is <em>no building</em>. In one case it was an address (housenumber). In another case it was a wayside cross (which sometimes may reside in a small building). In another case the same with a amenity=place_of_worship. In two cases it was a demolished building (along with a description tag).</p>
