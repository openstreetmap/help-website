<p><code>bridge=viaduct</code> is a continuous long many-short-span bridge; often elevated, or even on flat ground. Long "bridges" you think of are only a few spans to a single main span, with <code>bridge:structure=</code> usually <code>=suspension</code> or <code>=cable-stayed</code>.</p>
