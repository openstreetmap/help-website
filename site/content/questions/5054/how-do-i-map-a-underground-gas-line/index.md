<div class="answer-body">
<p>Have you read the <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Pipeline">pipeline</a> section in the wiki?</p>
<p>Something like this may be used for an underground gas line:</p>
<ul>
<li>man_made=pipeline</li>
<li>location=underground</li>
<li>type=gas</li>
</ul>
<p>Can you please post a link to the way describing the pipeline so we can take a look at it? (Or an object ID, coordinate or something that can make us find it.)</p>
<p>Just because something is entered in OpenStreetMap doesn't mean it will show up on every map made from OpenStreetMap. It's up to the map designer to choose what to render and not.</p>
<p>Even if some kind of data will be rendered on a specific kind of map i may take a while before it's actually rendered. It may take minutes, days, weeks, months etc.</p>
