<p>When you are in the online editor, select area. Draw a polygon over your house. The editor will automatically show "Building features". Click that. Then house. Fill in your address details in the form. Save</p>
<p>From then on your address is in the database. Depending on the refresh rate of the navigation app, your address will be available to the users of the app after 1 day or 1 month or...</p>
