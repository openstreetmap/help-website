<div class="answer-body">
<p>The way the navigation software interprets OSM data is beyond our control. All that we can do is tag the roads as correctly as possible and then hope that the Nav software is smart enough to notice.</p>
<p>For reserved lanes of freeways, known by different names in different places, the tag "hov" (High Occupancy Vehicle) is the preferred tag in OSM. I have seen the term "Hov" used in California, but it may be used in other places as well.</p>
<p>See this wiki page: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:hov">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:hov</a></p>
