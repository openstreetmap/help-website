<p>There is an article in WIKI somewhere regarding the difference between landuse and landcover (grass would be better tagged under landcover rather than landuse). But it was only a proposal, right now landuse is used as far as I know. Generally speaking you can use whatever tags you think are appropriate, I think that landuse=plant_nursery and plant=tulips or plant=bulbs, plant=lilies etc sound OK to me.
