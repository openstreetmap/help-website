Sometimes they are mapped as buildings by contributors who map via an imagery and don't have local knowledge, as they appear very similar to buildings on imageries. There is <code>historic=tomb</code> tag but tombs aren't always historical and mostly in a continuous use (common for the whole descendant and a place of rituals). So</p>
<ol>
<li>What is the proper way to tag a common tomb?</li>
<li>Should they be tagged as buildings?</li>

<p>There is currently no really well established tagging, but building=tomb (for non-historic tombs) seems to be a good choice and already has a fair number of uses.</p>
<p>See <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=tomb#values">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=tomb#values</a></p>
<p>The value is currently undocumented and could do with a write up.</p>
