<p>The system that runs help.openstreetmap.org, OSQA, does not offer combined searches like the one you are looking for, and the software project is largely abandoned. Therefore, we're seeking volunteers to move this to a more modern, feature-rich, and maintained platform: <a href="https://github.com/openstreetmap/operations/issues/149">https://github.com/openstreetmap/operations/issues/149</a></p>
