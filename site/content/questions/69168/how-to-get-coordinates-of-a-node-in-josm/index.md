<div class="answer-body">
<p>It's as simple as Edit -&gt; Copy Coordinates.</p>
<p>This will copy the coordinates in decimal degree format.</p>
<p>This works for all nodes, including ones that have not been uploaded yet (for uploaded/existing nodes one could also hit Ctrl+H and extract node coordinates from the browser).</p>
<p>The keyboard shortcut, depending on the platform, will be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Shift-Control-C</li>
<li>Shift-Command-C</li>
</ul>
