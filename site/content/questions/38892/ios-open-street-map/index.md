<p>"where i should start for using open street map"? Definitely at the "apps" section <span>of our wiki page about iOS</span>. Of course, also just visiting a website like <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/">https://www.openstreetmap.org/</a> works too. And, just view <span>the list of old iOS questions here</span> – there are quite very similar questions.</p>
<p>I am not sure what exactly you mean by "using", if you mean editing OSM too, then you need to look at those apps which provide editing features (also a table there). You might be able to also use the default editor ("iD") on <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/">https://www.openstreetmap.org/</a> with your browser app, although this will not be very comfortable. A short mobile-specific intro/guide is at <a href="http://learnosm.org/en/mobile-mapping/">http://learnosm.org/en/mobile-mapping/</a> (the text there. The list of the apps is not useful for you since it is Android only).</p>
