<ol>
<li>For instance a shop with its address written on its window display ? Or a doctor's practice ?</li>
<li>Or on a post box with its own address written on it ?</li>
<li>If the name of the bus stop is the name of the hamlet ?</li>
<li>If the address is not written on the building itself but can be found from an advertising in a newspapers ?</li>
</ol>
