<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is a rather common situation with tracks, even if not necessarily in this combination. In previous discussions about this topic, people have suggested a <strong>surface:middle=</strong>* key for the surface in between. At the time of writing, it has <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/surface%3Amiddle">about 1000</a> uses mostly in and around Germany, so it isn't established or anything, but it fits your situation perfectly.</p>
<p>So my tagging recommendation is:</p>
<pre><code>surface        = cobblestone
surface:middle = paving_stones</code></pre>
</div>
