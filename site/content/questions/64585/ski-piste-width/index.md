<div class="answer-body">
<p>Yes, of course:</p>
<p>Map the center line as a way with piste:type=downhill and also the area as a closed way piste:type=downhill and area=yes.</p>
<p>Like here, for instance: <a href="http://www.opensnowmap.org/mobile.html?zoom=16&amp;lat=45.39234&amp;lon=6.6456">http://www.opensnowmap.org/mobile.html?zoom=16&amp;lat=45.39234&amp;lon=6.6456</a></p>
