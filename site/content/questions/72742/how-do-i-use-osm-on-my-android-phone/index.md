<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are quite some apps that use OpenStreetMap data for their maps:</p>
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Android#OpenStreetMap_applications">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Android#OpenStreetMap_applications</a></p>
<p>I'm most familiar with OSMAnd. It has on-device maps and routing, with profiles for cars, bikes and pedestrians. Maps.Me has similar features, with a less cluttered interface.</p>
<p>As a practical matter, you may find that OpenStreetMap doesn't have a lot of address information in your region of interest (there are countries with excellent coverage and countries with almost no coverage and so on, it varies a lot).</p>
