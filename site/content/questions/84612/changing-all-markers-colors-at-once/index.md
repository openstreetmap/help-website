<div class="answer-body">
<p>Not a direct answer to your question but what to look out for for the future: You should group objects which are to be displayed the same into separate layers. Then don't set colors and forms for each individual object but for the layer.</p>
<p>So when you now have to go through all your objects rather than changing the color reset it to undefined and set the new color on the layer.</p>
