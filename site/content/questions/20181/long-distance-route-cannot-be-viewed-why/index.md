<p>This view your are talking about has problems with large relations and fails to display them, most probably because of internal timeouts. This doesn't indicate any tagging problems and your relation is probably fine. A nice way to verify it is to take a look at it on <a href="http://hiking.waymarkedtrails.org/de/relation/2167509">waymarked trails</a>.</p>
