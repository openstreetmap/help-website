<p>Not rendered: amenity=swimming_pool</p>
<p>Rendered: leisure=swmming_pool.</p>
<p>As you can read in the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:leisure%3Dswimming_pool">wiki</a>, the amenity-variant is discouraged.</p>
