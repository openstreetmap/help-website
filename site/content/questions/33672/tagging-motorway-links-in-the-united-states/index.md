<p>First off, the junction in the example image doesn't have a name, just a ref, so the name tag shouldn't be used.</p>
<p>As for the exit_to vs. destination debate, it sounds like the resolution was that there's no resolution. In the US, exit_to seems to be the tag of choice, while other parts of the world use destination. Basically, use whichever ones suits your fancy.</p>
