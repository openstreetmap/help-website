<p>That's one possibility, indeed.</p>
<p>There are many more maps made of the same OSM data. The layer menu on our homepage is just a selection. See <span>Tiles</span> or <span>services: transport</span>. Just for example: <a href="http://www.öpnvkarte.de/?zoom=17&amp;lat=59.91859&amp;lon=10.7961&amp;layers=TBTTT">ÖPNVKarte</a> (link to your location) is similar to the transport map and also shows the name. Our standard style simply does not show names of highway=bus_stop nodes. Likely this is intended to not add tooo much information into this map. Or was it different in the past? I do not remember.</p>
