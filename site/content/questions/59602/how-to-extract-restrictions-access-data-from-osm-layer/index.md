<div class="answer-body">
<p>The geofabrik shapefiles currently don't contain any information on turn restrictions, you should likely talk to geofabrik directly to see if it is possible to model this in a reasonable way.</p>
<p>To get the data directly out of OSM you need to extract relations of the type "restriction" (they will contain information on what kind of turn restriction it is) and the member elements. However if that is useful depends on what you want to do with the information in the end.</p>
<p>Overpass-turbo example: <a href="http://osm.li/3E">http://osm.li/3E</a></p>

<p>Hi,</p>
<p>turn and access restrictions are only available in our paid "routeable" shape files. In addition, linestrings in our routeable shape files are split at each intersection. Our free shape files only contain unsplit linestrings (identical to the OSM ways they are based on).</p>
<p>Michael (from Geofabrik)</p>
