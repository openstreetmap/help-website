<div class="answer-body">
<p>The question sounded like a place and confused me. In English it's spelt canoe/kayak.</p>
<p>OpenStreetMap.org does display weirs if they are mapped, <a href="http://map.openseamap.org/?zoom=17&amp;lat=46.33165&amp;lon=13.57623&amp;layers=BFTTFFFFFTF0FFFFFFFFFF">example of weirs</a> on the River Wear (grey dotted lines).</p>
<p><a href="http://map.openseamap.org/">OpenSeaMap</a> is said to display a whitewater chart if you use the menu View-&gt;Sport. However that didn't show when I looked at the example spot.</p>
<p>There is some effort to map <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/WikiProject_Whitewater_Maps">whitewater</a> elements, but no online maps of the data currently.</p>
