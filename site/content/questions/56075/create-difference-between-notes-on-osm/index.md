<p>In OSM (at least the Netherlands) the map is flooded with notes.</p>
<p>Many of these unfortunately by anonymous and often senseless (too vague, repetitive etc.)</p>
<p>These too many notes discourage most users to have a look at the map for resolving notes.</p>
<p>It would be nice to extend the colouring scheme of notes: Anonymous without reactions: grey Anonymous with reactions: dark grey Non anonymous: red Non anonymous with reactions: orange resolved: green (as is)</p>
<p>Also the stay-alive-time of anonymous notes without answer could be limited to, say, four weeks?</p>
<p>Just my thoughts</p>
