<p>Almost certainly because the townland appears on GSGS 3906 as a single entity, rather than two split by parish, and has therefore been <a href="https://www.townlands.ie/westmeath/fore/glore/ballynagall/">mapped</a> as such. It's a fairly simple operation to split townlands which cross parish boundaries in most GIS tools, but it may now be difficult to do on townlands.ie.</p>
