<div class="answer-body">
<p>Just for clarification (probably only for other readers of this question): openstreetmap.org does not provide routing functionality, it does not even provide a <span>map service for everyone/every use</span>.</p>
<p>I guess you could find a service that offers an embeddable map with routing function at <span>List_of_OSM_based_Services#Routing</span>. E.g. <a href="http://open.mapquestapi.com/sdk/js/v6.1.0/routing.html#draggableroutingcustomizable">http://open.mapquestapi.com/sdk/js/v6.1.0/routing.html#draggableroutingcustomizable</a> (oh, interesting, it looks as if this <em>code</em> example misses the required OSM credit and license). <strong>Update:</strong> I found an ''easy'' way (probably of many): go to <a href="http://open.mapquest.com/">http://open.mapquest.com/</a> , make your route, and then click "Send to" button and select "to your website". <a href="http://open.mapquest.com/embed?hk=W8iJsh">Example route</a> (would be inside an iframe usually).<strong>/Update</strong></p>
<p>Please look for licensing and usage restrictions for each service.</p>
<ul>
<li><span>Questions about setting a <em>marker</em> on a map</span> and</li>
<li>the <span>Deploying_your_own_Slippy_Map</span> page may be helpful, too.</li>
</ul>
<p>Please mention it here (as "answer") if you found a service/solution for your problem.</p>
