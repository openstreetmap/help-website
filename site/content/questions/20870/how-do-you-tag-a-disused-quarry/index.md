<div class="answer-body">
<p>If the quarry is disused, but still clearly visible (blocks of rock, no vegetation, maybe old machinery standing around), I would tag it as a normal quarry and add a note.</p>
<p>IMHO, landuse does not have anything to do with the current status of a business. As long as no new landuse is assigned, or it gets so destroyed that it would not be useable as a quarry (then it is maybe a brownfield?), it is still a quarry after all.</p>
