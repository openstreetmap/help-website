<div class="answer-body">
<p>For parking spaces <em>along</em> a road I would almost always go for <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:parking:lane">parking:lane</a>. It is a little bit difficult to understand at first but has good <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:parking:lane/Examples">examples</a> and is really powerful for most situations.</p>
<p>However currently <em>parking:lane</em> only supports <em>left</em>, <em>right</em> and <em>both</em> but not <em>center</em>. Of course you can introduce this value yourself but since this is a really rare case I would not expect any tool to support it.</p>
<p>Alternatively you could split the road since there is now some kind of physical separation. In this case you can map the parking spaces with <em>parking:lane:left</em> and <em>parking:lane:right</em>.</p>
