<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can edit the style that the GPS trace is displayed with in Potlatch 2 by waiting for the trace to load, then selecting background / vector file and changing the style "GPS" to something else (so that the GPS trace is displayed in another style).</p>
<p>One that might work for you is to tick the box in the "select" column for it and change "GPS" to "Potlatch". Then when you click on the trace it'll show as a wide blue line.</p>
