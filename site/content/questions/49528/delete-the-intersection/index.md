<div class="answer-body">
<p>=Increase the zoom level to see exactly the one node of the intersection<br />
=Select that node<br />
=Drag it a little bit to further inspect<br />
=Click Edit =&gt; Undo to restore the original situation<br />
=If it is the right node :<br />
=Select that node once more<br />
=Click Tools =&gt; UnGlue Ways<br />
=You now have all the ways that connected at that intersection separate</p>
<p>=If you only want to remove just 1 way from the intersection :<br />
=Select that way<br />
=Slowly move the mouse pointer to the intersection node and if the node lits up :<br />
=First press the Ctrl key (the mouse pointer will get a +sign) and than the left mouse button<br />
=Now both the way and the intersection node are selected<br />
=Click Tools =&gt; UnGlue Ways</p>
<p>If you change an intersection the ways nolonger connect. They nolonger route !<br />
The bicycle-, walking-, and bus routerelations nolonger work are corrupted !</p>
<p>So be care full before you upload the Change Set to Osm<br />

Gys</p>
