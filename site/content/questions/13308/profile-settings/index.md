<p>Make sure you are logged into openstreetmap.org</p>
<ol>
<li>Click on your display name at the top-right of the page</li>
<li>Click on 'my settings' in the links just below your display name</li>
<li>Change any settings you want to change</li>
<li>Click Save changes at the bottom-right of the page</li>
</ol>
