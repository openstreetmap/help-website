<div class="answer-body">
<p>I have mapped this type of thing using three ways: One for the fence on the left, one for the trail/track/footway, and another for the fence on the right.</p>
<p>It does not render well and getting things all lined up with GPX data and aerial imagery can be a pain. But it is all the better I know how to do.</p>
<p>I suppose one could get a discussion going on the tagging email list about using a modifier tag on the traveled way, somewhat like the <code>sidewalk=no|left|right|both</code> tag that can be added to roads. But at present I know of no widely used, documented or discussed way to do this other than drawing the three different ways.</p>
