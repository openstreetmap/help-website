<p>The simple answer: not at all.</p>
<p>For regularly reoccurring situations or ones that are going to be in place for at least a couple of months, it might make sense to record them in a suitable fashion in OSM.</p>
<p>However in the case at hand the information will long be stale before it even reaches potential users.</p>
<p>There are other approaches to making such data available that could be considered, but directly adding it to OSM clearly isn't.</p>
