<p>Hi Patrick, welcome to OSM! Great that you want to help the project.</p>
<p><span>OpenCycleMap</span> and the "outdoors" layer of OpenCycleMap are all based on the same OpenStreetMap data. Also the standard map on openstreetmap.org is based on this data. And <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/List_of_OSM-based_services#Biking.2C_Geocaching.2C_Hiking.2C_Sport">even more maps/services</a> are based on this data. It is just displayed differently and different aspects/parts of the data are displayed.</p>
<p>So, contribute, update your surroundings and it will (after some days - this depends on the operator of OpenCycleMap) be reflected also in OpenCycleMap and its outdoors style.</p>

<p>There are a couple of layers on openstreetmap.org, the standard, cycle map, transport map and humanitarian, but no outdoor/hiking etc.</p>
<p>All the renders get the data from the same place so it's just up to the people behind it what is show and what is not.</p>
<p>There is <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/List_of_OSM-based_services#Biking.2C_Geocaching.2C_Hiking.2C_Sport">a nice table on the wiki</a> showing which renders show/focus on what.</p>
