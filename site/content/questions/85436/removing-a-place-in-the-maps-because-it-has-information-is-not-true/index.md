<p>OpenStreetMap can be edited by anyone, and that includes deleting places. Just log in, select the object, and choose "delete".</p>
<p>When saving your changes, be sure to explain the reason for the deletion (e.g. "XY hotel has closed, there is now a private residence") so that people can see that you know what you're doing.</p>
<p>Attention: Sometimes you do not want to delete the whole thing but just an aspect of it. For example, if a hotel has an address, and stops being a hotel, then it still is a building and still has an address! Deleting the hotel outright would lose this address information - in such a case you would just remove the "hotel" aspect (or, in OSM parlance, the <code>tourism=hotel</code> tag and any additional stuff about rating, website, contact details) and not the whole object.</p>
