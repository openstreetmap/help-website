<p>Re-rendering takes different amount of time depending on the zoom level, specifically low zoom tiles are not re-rendered on demand and will take longer to update.</p>
<p>If your change is correctly rerendered at high zooms and you can search for the new name, then there is nothing to be concerned about.</p>
