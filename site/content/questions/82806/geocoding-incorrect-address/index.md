<p>There are a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Search_engines">variety of geocoders</a> using OpenStreetMap data. Some of them may have better "fuzzy" matching than others.</p>
<p>As a (mostly) volunteer contributed map OSM does have variable levels of completeness from region to region, so some of the information you need may not be available.</p>
