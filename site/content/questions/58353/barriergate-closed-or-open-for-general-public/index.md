<div class="answer-body">
<p>The usual <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:access">access tags</a> should be used.</p>
<p><code>access=private</code> means the gate is closed for the general public. <code>access=yes</code> means it is open for everyone.</p>
<p>For time-based restrictions see the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Conditional_restrictions">conditional restrictions scheme</a>. Example: If the gate is closed during the night then use <code>access=yes</code> + <code>access:conditional=no @ (20:00-06:00)</code>.</p>
