<div class="answer-body">
<p>Garmin Basecamp should solve the issue,it is available as a free download. BC will allow you to export a section of a Garmin map to your Garmin device. Most OSM on Garmin map downloads can be used with Garmin Basecamp on a Windows PC and maybe a Mac. Opentopomap have a a Win pc version as well as a Garmin one <a href="http://garmin.opentopomap.org/">http://garmin.opentopomap.org/</a> Here's a link for Basecamp <a href="https://www.garmin.com/en-GB/software/basecamp/">https://www.garmin.com/en-GB/software/basecamp/</a> Note: If Garmin offer to update your firmware do NOT accept, some users have suggested that third party maps, such as OSM may not work on the device if you up-date the firmware.</p>
