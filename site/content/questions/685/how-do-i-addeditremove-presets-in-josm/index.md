<div class="answer-body">
<p>In Preferences &gt; Map Settings (Grid and globe) &gt; Tagging Presets you have many other presets to choose from. You can activate any presets you want from the list or add a custom preset file or url.</p>
<p>To create custom presets you can try to follow the simple guide at the <a href="http://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/TaggingPresets">josm wiki</a>.</p>
