<div class="answer-body">
<p>The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API">Overpass API</a> allows you to limit your query to a bounding box, an area already in OpenStreetMap or an <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Overpass_QL#By_polygon_(poly)">arbitrary polygon</a>.</p>
<p>Note that the "completeness" of OpenStreetMap varies by area, and even if the building are traced they may only be tagged generically as <code>building=yes</code> or <code>building=residential</code> rather than as one of the house specific tags (<code>=bungalow</code>, <code>detached</code>, `semidetached_house', <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Buildings">etc.</a>).</p>
