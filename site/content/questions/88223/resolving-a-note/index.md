<blockquote>
<p>In adding a note, what does it mean to resolve it?</p>
</blockquote>
<p>"Resolving a note" means "marking it as no longer needing local mappers to investigate". This is done initially by changing the colour from red to green. After 7 days, it'll disappear from the default "notes" view (though you can still locate it by URL if you wish).</p>
<blockquote>
<p>is there an Android version of Openstreetmap?</p>
</blockquote>
<p>That rather depends on what you mean by "OpenStreetMap". There certainly are OSM maps and apps for <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Android">Android</a>.</p>
