<div class="answer-body">
<p>This doesn't answer the question, but the best I've found is this:</p>
<ol>
<li>Click the Hamburger Menu to open the settings</li>
<li>Click "Configure Map"</li>
<li>At the top of the new menu, change from the "globe" tab to the "person-walking" tab. This changes the profile from "Browse map" to "Walking Profile"</li>
<li>Scroll down and enable "Hiking routes"</li>
<li>Scroll down and click "Details" -&gt; Enable "More Details"</li>
</ol>
<p>In my experience, this will show the trails at three (3) more zoomed-out zoom levels.</p>
<p>However, this doesn't buy you much. I'm not sure how to make trails more visible at <em>all</em> zoom levels.</p>
