<p>You use the <a href="https://export.hotosm.org/en/v3/">HOT export tool</a> to export data. I doubt however, that e.g. bathroom facilities &amp; fences are mapped in detail. If they are mapped, you can see them on osm.org, so you know beforehand if the information will be available. Feel free to start mapping them now yourself before exporting the data.</p>
<p>Concessions is something we typically do not map.</p>
