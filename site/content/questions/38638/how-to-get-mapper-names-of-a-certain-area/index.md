<div class="answer-body">
<p>You're probably going to need to use several different tools depending on your definition of "ever contributed". ITO's <a href="http://www.itoworld.com/static/openstreetmap_tools/osm_mapper.html">OSM Mapper</a> may be part of what you want though - you can define an area of interest and see who was the last-modifying user of ways there. It won't spot node or relation edits, or edits that have been superceded by other edits, but it's more than a start.</p>
<p>If you want to do something similar but looking at node edits, try Zverik's <a href="http://zverik.osm.rambler.ru/whodidit/?zoom=12&amp;lat=53.08823&amp;lon=-0.68141&amp;layers=BTT">WhoDidIt</a>.</p>
