<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetMap is not a business directory. You might just have misunderstood what this site is about.</p>
<p>For OpenStreetMap, the actual <strong>location</strong> of something is important. We don't care if there is a certain shop at a certain address in Kansas. We want to know <strong>where exactly that address is, on the map.</strong> The whole time, essentially, you are fighting against a system that wants you to pinpoint a location on the map, whereas you simply want to list a business against an address. That's why you find it frustrating - but the answer is simple, you're trying to use the wrong system (Openstreetmap) for the wrong purpose (business listing).</p>
</div>
