<p>In that sentence, what do you mean by "osm"?</p>
<p>The OpenStreetMap project consists of a large lump of centrally hosted data. There are many, many different uses for that (rendering as maps and in other forms on servers both centrally and externally), and tools developed both internally and externally so without a bit more details in the question it's difficult to say more than "most things that you can think of" as an answer.</p>
