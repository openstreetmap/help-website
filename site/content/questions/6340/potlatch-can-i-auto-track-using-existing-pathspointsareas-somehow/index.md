<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can do this using the <strong>Follow</strong> feature in Potlatch 2.</p>
<p>To use this feature to draw the forest:</p>
<ol>
<li>Start drawing the forest.</li>
<li>Click on one of the nodes on the lake.</li>
<li>Click on the next node on the lake, in the direction that the forest goes.</li>
<li>Press <strong>F</strong>, and the way will be automatically extended to the next node.</li>
<li>Keep pressing F for as far as the ways share nodes.</li>
<li>Continue drawing the rest of the forest as usual.</li>
</ol>
