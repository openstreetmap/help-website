<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are many different people and companies who provide Garmin maps based on OpenStreetMap data. It is up to each person what options they choose when they are making the maps, and one of the options is whether or not to include routing information in their maps.</p>
<p>You should confirm with whoever made your particular Garmin map as to whether it supports routing.</p>
<p>If it does, then you should check the routing settings on your 62s. From the main menu, select Setup -&gt; Routing. Look at the "Guidance Method" - it should be set to either "On Road for Time" or "On Road for Distance". If it is set to "Off Road" you should change it.</p>
</div>
