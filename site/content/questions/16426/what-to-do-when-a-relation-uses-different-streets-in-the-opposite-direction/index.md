<p>osm.org generally doesn't aim to provide data in a consumer-friendly format - it's principally a site for the use of mappers. Rather, we rely on third-party websites to do that.</p>
<p>I would hope that, if there were enough demand, a third-party site would be established to supply route relations properly ordered in .gpx format. Certainly it would be wrong to impose an extra burden on mappers (or our hard-pressed tool authors) just to suit the needs of one particular group of users.</p>
