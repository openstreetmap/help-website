<div class="question-body">
<p>HelioFelix has added a large number of survey points in Hong Kong recently, such as <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/4980813289">this one</a>. I am not really sure of the copyright status of these, but the linked PDFs show that there is probably government copyright. HelioFelix has <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/50727248">replied to my changeset comment</a>, but the license provided by data.gov.hk doesn't really seem ODbL-compatible (particularly the <a href="https://data.gov.hk/en/terms-and-conditions">terms and conditions</a>, which have a long list of things which control commercial and non-commercial use of the data). Reversion would probably be a bit complicated since each node was uploaded in a separate changeset; an alternative would be asking the government if the conditions could be waived. I'm not sure if he has made any other edits which may be license-compatible.</p>
