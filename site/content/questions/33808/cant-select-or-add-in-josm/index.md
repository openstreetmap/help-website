<div class="answer-body">
<p>Usually there will be a dialog named "Layers" on the right. Exactly one of all loaded layers has a green check mark next to it; that one is the active layer (the one you are editing). If for some reason you have managed to make your GPX layer active rather than your OSM layer, selecting and editing will be greyed out. Right-click on your OSM layer and select "activate" from the context menu, or use the green check mark button below the layer list, to make your OSM layer active.</p>
