<p>Where did you download that map without buildings ? The map on osm.org always had buildings. That is : as soon as they were mapped by someone. The same applies e.g. to the maps that you can download from garmin.openstreetmap.nl. No buildings when they were not mapped, but when someone adds them, they appear on the map. Of course this makes the files containing the maps bigger</p>
<p>You could create your own map (be it for a website or a Garmin device) without buildings. If you cannot make your own maps, or do not want to so, you will always depend on the map maker for including or excluding certain features.</p>

<p>" <a href="http://www.openfietsmap.nl/downloads/worldwide">Worldwide</a> (Openfietsmap Lite)</p>
