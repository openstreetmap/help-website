<div class="answer-body">
<p><a href="https://www.scribblemaps.com/">https://www.scribblemaps.com/</a> just seems to use OSM (or Google, or others) as a tile background. Your KML is just what you drew over the top. If you want to change what that tile background contains, you'll need to generate your own tiles. If you want the "scribblemaps" website to work differently (have different background options or a "no background" option) you'll need to ask them. They're not connected to OpenStreetMap other than using tiles created using OSM data.</p>
