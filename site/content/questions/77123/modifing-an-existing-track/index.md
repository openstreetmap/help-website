<p>If you are sure the line of the path is incorrect you can move it. But you will need to check the image against GPXes and survey as well to be sure. I would not duplicate it, but there may be an additional but close by path. Sometimes there may be highway with a separate cycle and/ or footpath and in that case both could be mapped.</p>
