<div class="answer-body">
<p>As far as I can see...</p>
<p>The only needed <span>turn restriction</span> (by usual mapping standards) is at <span>the merge of the left turn road</span> (this node gets the "via" role). Also add the <span>left turn road</span> with the "from" and the <span>Abbey Road</span> with the "to" role to the restriction relation. The <code>restriction</code> should be <code>only_left_turn</code> (if that is more usual in your country you could convert that to a <code>no_right_turn</code> restriction).</p>
<p>The other straight ahead lane does not need a (usual) turn restriction as it is not mapped separately (and should not as it is just a non separated lane).</p>
<p>So, there is nothing really special due to the roundabout here.</p>
