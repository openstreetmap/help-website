<p>Besides a traditional geodetic (horizontal and vertical control) network of passive <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:man%20made=survey%20point?uselang=en">man made=survey_point</a> many nations (and even global agencies such as the <a href="https://igs.org/network">IGS</a>) have active GNSS reciever/antenna's that collect satellite observations; and contribute towards the foundation of modern geodesy.</p>
<p>These <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real-time_kinematic_positioning#CORS">CORS</a> are often co-incident with more traditional beacons -&gt; <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/7204350246">TRIGSURVEY</a></p>
<p>How would we tag this?<br />
<code>gnss=yes</code> or would <code>constellation=gps;galileo;beidou</code>, etc. that the reciever logs be more appropriate?</p>
