<div class="answer-body">
<p>if you do not know the position of the parking space (which you could tag <span>amenity=parking</span>), then this is better than nothing, in my opinion. Adding tags in that style is usual. We have 1100 <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/?key=parking#values">objects with this tag</a> and <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/bEL">49 hotels with this tag</a>. However, mapping the parking separately should be preferred.</p>
<p>How to add such a tag? See <span>the bottom section "Tag#Use_in_an_editor"</span>.</p>
