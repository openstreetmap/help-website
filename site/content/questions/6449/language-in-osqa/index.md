<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are also some questions in other languages than English, although this system is not very prepared for this situation (there are no namespaces, everything ends up in the same pool). Please be also aware that you can only get a reply if there is someone who knows the answer AND can understand the language the question is in.</p>
<p>The possible solutions might be to</p>
<ul>
<li>moderate the questions on the local mailing list and then have someone who speaks English transfer it here (and retranslate the answers back),</li>
<li>or ask the question in your local language here and see what happens,</li>
<li>or wait till someone somehow extends this system to deal with different languages.</li>
</ul>
<p>The second solution will probably not augment the clarity of this site.</p>
