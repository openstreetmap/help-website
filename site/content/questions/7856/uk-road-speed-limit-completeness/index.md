<p>Is anybody looking at the completeness of speed-limit data for UK roads? Unlike Finland, Netherland and some others the UK has no central map of speed limits.<br />
Route planning with OpenRoute might be enhanced, knowing the limits. Incorporating speed limit warnings into SatNavs would be better if the data were complete. I am mentioning OpenStreeetMap to The Institution of Engineering and Technology <a href="http://www.theiet.org">www.theiet.org</a> to see if that sparks an interest.</p>
