<p>This is likely to be the site you are looking for <a href="http://strava.github.io/iD/#background=Bing&amp;map=14.93/153.0330/-27.2279">http://strava.github.io/iD/#background=Bing&amp;map=14.93/153.0330/-27.2279</a> You can change the background to display the stardard osm layer instead of the satellite imagery.<br />

<p>Strava labs site <a href="https://labs.strava.com/slide/demo.html#16/-207.12448/-27.35314">https://labs.strava.com/slide/demo.html#16/-207.12448/-27.35314</a></p>
