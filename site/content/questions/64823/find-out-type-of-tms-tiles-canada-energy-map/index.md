<p>These are the sources listed, most of which are commercial:</p>
<p>Oil and Gas Pipeline data provided by Pennwell Corporation. Electrical Power Line data derived from tabular data provided by Pennwell Corporation, and spatial data derived from CanVec 1:250K data, © Department of Natural Resources Canada. All rights reserved. Oil, Gas and Electrical Facility data derived from data provided by Pennwell Corporation and The Centre For Energy. Oil sands facility data derived from Alberta's Oil Sands Projects and Upgraders Map, Oil Sands Operations, Alberta Department of Energy, January 2013. Coal Mine data derived from Atlas of Canada 6th edition: Coal Resources, 2009. Uranium Mine data derived from Saskatchewan Mining Association online map, April, 2013: www.saskmining.ca/map/Map/map.html#top</p>
