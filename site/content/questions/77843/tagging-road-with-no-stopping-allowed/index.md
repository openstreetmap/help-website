<div class="answer-body">
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:parking:lane"><code>parking:lane=*</code></a> has values <code>no_parking</code> and <code>no_stopping</code>.</p>
<p><em>edit:</em> As Kovoschiz pointed out. It needs to be <code>parking:lane:both=*</code>, <code>parking:lane:left=*</code> or <code>parking:lane:right=*</code> depending on local situation.</p>
</div>
