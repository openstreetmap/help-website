<p>Hi, I use ID.</p>
<p>In a village in Argentina, my GPS told me I was driving on the left side of the road (the road is in 3 parts : the left side, a separation with grass and the right side with one way directions). Then later it told me stupid things because it believed I was on another road than the one I was on and I needed to stop my car to understand where to go.</p>
<p>I upload my GPS track on OSM and I can see that all the village was made with satellite view of Bing but the images are 5 or 6 meters from the real places : so all the village is at a bad place on osm.</p>
<p>The village is great on osm : a lot of POI, lines (roads), polygons (buldings).</p>
<p>My question is very simple : is there a way to select all the objects in the bad area then to move all the objects at once ?</p>
<p>Best regards</p>
<p>Fred</p>
