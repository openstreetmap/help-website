<p>If such data is in OSM it would be a very small amount, the majority of the buildings in Puerto Rico are either from imports that did not include such information or from HOT activity related to Hurricane Maria.</p>
<p>There is a data portal: <a href="https://data.pr.gov/data">https://data.pr.gov/data</a></p>
<p>And cadastral data with building footprints: <a href="https://www.satasgis.crimpr.net/cdprpc/">https://www.satasgis.crimpr.net/cdprpc/</a> (turn on the "Estructuras" layer), but the metadata shown is limited to the building height.</p>
