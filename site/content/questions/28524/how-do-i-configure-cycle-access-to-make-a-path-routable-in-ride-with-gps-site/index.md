<p>If I look in my neighbourhood, I notice that ridewithgps.com use old, very old OSM data (more than one year old!). Something to bear in mind when you plan routes with this tool. Better download Garmin Mapsource or Basecamp and a more recent map from <a href="http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/">http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/</a> (use my Openfietsmap Lite for bicycle routing). Latest maps are from November 21 this year.</p>
