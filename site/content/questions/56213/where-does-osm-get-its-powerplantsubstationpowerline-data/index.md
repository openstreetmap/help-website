<p>A lot of it is just from imagery (many features have no further information beyond what is observable in imagery). I think a lot of people input information based on their understanding of the power grid and from sources like permits.</p>
<p>The EIA has a pretty massive amount of data on US infrastructure:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.eia.gov/state/maps.php">https://www.eia.gov/state/maps.php</a></p>
<p>A review of which layers are suitable as OSM sources (appropriately licensed and so on) would be a useful thing:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.eia.gov/maps/layer_info-m.php">https://www.eia.gov/maps/layer_info-m.php</a></p>
