<p>Probably the simplest place to retrieve the data is via Jonathan de Ferranti's site <a href="http://viewfinderpanoramas.org/dem3.html">viewfinderpanormas.org</a>. These have voids filled (from a range of sources, which may have licence implications) and so are likely to be rather more useful than raw SRTM data.</p>
<p>An alternative is ASTER: <a href="https://asterweb.jpl.nasa.gov/gdem-wist.asp">https://asterweb.jpl.nasa.gov/gdem-wist.asp</a></p>
