<p>What you are describing is a custom render, that is a map custom created for your needs. You can do ths for yourself. You can download the data for an area and render that area, or part of it, the way you want it, including railways but ignoring buses. A custom render could use Maperitive or Tilemill to make the map. More info on switch2osm, <a href="http://switch2osm.org">http://switch2osm.org</a></p>
