<p>The best source of country boundaries ("Admin 0") and their first-level internal boundaries ("Admin 1") is Natural Earth data.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.naturalearthdata.com/downloads/10m-cultural-vectors/">http://www.naturalearthdata.com/downloads/10m-cultural-vectors/</a></p>
<p>It's approximately a bazillion times easier to use these, than extracting the same information from OpenStreetMap.</p>
