<div class="question-body">
<p>'Cause I've seen them all happen. Personally, I like the lines to be at the bottom of the slope, thereby really marking the boundary of the terrain feature. But the arrows on the line indicate "lower terrain". If you read that as "ALL lower terrain" instead of "the lowest terrain", the lines should be placed at the top of the slopes. But I don't like that cause it gives no indication of the volume of the embankment. In the middle of the slope: that just seems sloppy, but I've seen it happen.</p>
