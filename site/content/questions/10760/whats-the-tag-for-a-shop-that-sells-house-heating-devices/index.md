<p>"shop=furnace" isn't used for that in English (it is in American, though). The fact that someone has created a wiki stub for a key does not make it a good reason to use it. In English the nearest equivalent would be "heating" or "hvac" (and along with "furnace", none of these have been used in OSM much, though).<br />
</p>
<p>In English a "furnace" is something that you find in a factory that makes steel, not in a house.</p>
