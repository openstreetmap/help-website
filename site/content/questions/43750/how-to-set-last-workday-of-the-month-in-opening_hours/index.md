<p>I don't think there is syntax support for first/last weekday of the month. You could use a comment:</p>
<pre><code>Fr-Sa 18:00-06:00; &quot;Closed last workday of each month.&quot;; PH off</code></pre>
<p>Which isn't perfect, but it gets the information to a human looking at the entry.</p>
