<div class="answer-body">
<ol>
<li>Yes. You are free to copy, distribute, transmit and adapt our maps and data, as long as you credit OpenStreetMap and its contributors. Make sure that you are not breaking the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tile_usage_policy">tile usage policy</a> if you are using map tiles from the osm server (other map tile providers may have different policies).</li>
<li>If you alter or build upon our maps or data, you may distribute the result only under the same licence. But any data not derrived from OSM is not subject to this licence.</li>
</ol>
