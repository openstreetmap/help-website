<div class="answer-body">
<p>"Coal" is either short for charcoal, or is not used alone as the fuel coal without charchoal. Should be <code>=charcoal</code> for starters. Avoid abbreviations or nicknames.</p>
<p>Although there are 25 <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/waste=hot_coals">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/waste=hot_coals</a> instances that you may follow, I kinda think its status is already assumed to be hot. Like you won't specify <code>=hot_cigarettes</code> (even though they may be extinguished), when only <code>=cigarettes</code> is used.</p>
