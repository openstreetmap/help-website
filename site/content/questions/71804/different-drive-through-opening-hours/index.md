<div class="comment-text">
<p>If you don't mind a personal comment being added, to me <code>drive_through:opening_hours=</code> sounds better. Sure, <code>opening_hours:drive_through</code>, <code>opening_hours:kitchen</code> (which is an odder key, v <code>service_times=</code> or meal time keys) are being used. However, as you pointed out, support/comparability is an issue. <code>drive_through:opening_hours=</code> would go better with <code>drive_through=</code>, like how there's <code>atm:opening_hours</code> "competing" with the Wiki-documented <code>opening_hours:hours</code>.</p>
