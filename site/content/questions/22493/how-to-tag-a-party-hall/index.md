<p>From Pieren's and moszkva-ter's replies, I think I have the answer:</p>
<ul>
<li>leisure=playground, access=private (maybe operator= if it's a chain of places) for for-rent children't party facilities.</li>
<li>amenity=community_centre, access=private for privately-owned "social halls" - where you would hold a wedding reception or something.</li>
</ul>
<p>Thanks to all.</p>
