<div class="answer-body">
<p>While we haven't laid down any hard and fast rules for using the logo and OpenStreetMap trademark, when we eventually get around to doing it (there is such an effort underway that has been stalled), one thing is clear: use by the community/community members to identify products/data from OSM will be supported.</p>
<p>In other words as long as you don't imply that you have any "official" status and you make clear that your project uses OSM data, but is not a product of the OSMF or similar, your use should be completly ok.</p>
