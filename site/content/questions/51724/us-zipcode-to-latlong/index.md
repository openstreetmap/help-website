<p>You can try asking nominatim, like so:</p>
<p><a href="http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/search.php?q=14556">http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/search.php?q=14556</a></p>
<p>But there is no assurance that the answer will be a zip code, and zip codes don't really have a center as such.</p>
<p>You will probably get more useful results from something using a non OSM source like <a href="https://www.census.gov/geo/reference/zctas.html">https://www.census.gov/geo/reference/zctas.html</a> .</p>
