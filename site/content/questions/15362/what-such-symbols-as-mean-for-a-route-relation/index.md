<div class="answer-body">
<p>The ways making up the route are members of a relation. A member of a relation may have an role. In your case the following roles are editable in Potlatch:</p>
<ul>
<li>&lt;empty&gt; or route: way used in both directions (Symbol &lt;&gt; in Potlatch)</li>
<li>forward: way used in following the route forward (Symbol &gt;&gt; in Potlatch)</li>
<li>backward: way used in following the route backward (Symbol &lt;&lt; in Potlatch)</li>
</ul>
