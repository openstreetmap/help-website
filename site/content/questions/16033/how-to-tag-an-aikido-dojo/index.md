<div class="answer-body">
<p>Reading the wiki, I believe the best options is to tag the dojo with <code>sport=aikido</code>, and possibly with <code>name=Jane's dojo</code> as appropriate.</p>
<p>Place the tag on the building if the dojo occupies the whole building, or place a node inside the building if there is more inside.</p>
