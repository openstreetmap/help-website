<p>We currently likely don't have a way of accurately modelling this situation in OSM.</p>
<p>I suspect the best way to get a roughly working solution is to map the dual lane sections of both the roundabout and the streets leading up to them with two lanes and make the right-hand lane with "right"</p>
<p>So in the first example that would be</p>
<p>turn:lanes=none|right on the road leading up to the roundabout (ignoring the other side of the road for now)</p>
<p>turn:lanes=none;right|right on the section of the roundabout it merges in to.</p>
<p>YMMV</p>
