<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are naturally more than one way you could do this. However the easiest is likely to use overpass-api queries to get the indoor and display that. You won't get a layer switcher or similar though.</p>
<p>I expect that re-using OpenLevelUp (<a href="https://openlevelup.net">https://openlevelup.net</a> <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OpenLevelUp">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OpenLevelUp</a> <a href="https://framagit.org/OpenLevelUp/OpenLevelUp)">https://framagit.org/OpenLevelUp/OpenLevelUp)</a> in some form might be more appropriate.</p>
