<div class="answer-body">
<ol>
<li>Rivers should be tagged with a way tagged with <em>waterway=river</em> in the centre to show the flow of the water and optionaly a way or multipolygon outlining the river tagged with <em>waterway=riverbank</em>. See <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:waterway%3Driverbank">the wiki</a> for documentation.</li>
<li>Coastlines are not updated as frequently as the other features as it requires a lot of resources to find out what is sea and what is land.</li>
</ol>
