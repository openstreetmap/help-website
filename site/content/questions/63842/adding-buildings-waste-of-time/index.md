<div class="answer-body">
<p>As far as I know, there is no automatic building import on a global level.</p>
<p>In some places, e.g. France, there are building imports from official sources (cadastre), but in other regions, e.g. Sweden, there are no such efforts underway.</p>
<p>Sooner or later, there will probably be an AI that just grabs buildings and everything else off aerial imagery, but I would not hold my breath waiting. :)</p>
