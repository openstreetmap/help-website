<p>Hello, I have the latest Osmand Version 1.9.2 and the latest available maps for Austria and with one route (for car) Osmand is searching with no finding a route. But every some seconds I see one of two error codes. 1) "Schnelle Routenberechnung fehlgeschlagen (length=2; index=2), Rückfall auf langsame Berechnung." or 2) "Fehler während der Routenberechnung: length=2; index=2".</p>
<p>Now I was searching a long time and I know which junction is the problem, but I can't find the problem. It is here if you left the highway at "Hirschstetten" (coming from the city) and if you are on the ramp and like to drive left or right to "Hirschstettner Straße". In both directions I have the same problem.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit?way=23411145#map=20/48.24057/16.46007">https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit?way=23411145#map=20/48.24057/16.46007</a></p>
<p>Do you have this problem also and do you know where the problem is?</p>
