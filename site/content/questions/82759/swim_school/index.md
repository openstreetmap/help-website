<div class="answer-body">
<p>I'm going to promote <code>amenity=training</code> + <code>training=sport</code> + <code>sport=swimming</code> here. Better than proliferating an ever longer list of <code>=*_school</code>. For comparison, there's 10 <code>=swimming_school</code> and 21 <code>=sport_school</code> instances</p>
<p>Could try <code>training:for=child;toddler;infant</code> and <code>min_age=3 months</code>. (great another time period format)</p>
<p>You should put the <code>note=</code> as <code>website=</code><a href="https://www.swimmable.net/"><code>https://www.swimmable.net/</code></a>. Discard the <code>*?gclid=*</code> tracking parameter.</p>
