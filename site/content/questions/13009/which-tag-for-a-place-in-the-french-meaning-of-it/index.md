<p>You can also tag an area as a highway, with area=yes. Example:</p>
<p>highway=pedestrian</p>
<p>area=yes</p>
<p>name=XYZ</p>
<p>In Potlatch 2 "simple mode", click on the second register (the one, that looks like a checkbox), expand the menu "physical" and check the "open area" checkbox.</p>
<p>You can also add other tags as needed, e.g. amenity=parking, amenity=marketplace and so on</p>
