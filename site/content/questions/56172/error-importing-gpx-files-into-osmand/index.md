<p>Hi</p>
<p>I am using OSMAND free version. Perhaps this can help.</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Download the GPX fils on your "Download" folder of your handset.</p></li>
<li><p>You can also copy paste them on Android --&gt; data --&gt; net.osmand --&gt; files --&gt; tracks --&gt; import folder.</p></li>
<li><p>Open OSMAND</p></li>
<li><p>Go to Menu --&gt; Configure Map --&gt; GPX Track (Turn it on) and it will show all the GPX tracks that are in the Tracks folder on your handset.</p></li>
<li><p>If they are not in the Tracks folder, click on "Add track" and find them from your Download folder.</p></li>
</ol>
<p>Hope that helps.</p>
