<p>Correct, this will require a manual edit. Which should always be preferred, see <a href="estimated%20particles%20per%200.01%20cubic%20feet">automated edits</a>. Another reason is that the speed might apply only to a part of the road, requiring the road to be split. Or it applies to multiple consecutive ways, i.e. having a single way id is not enough. Or it applies only to a specific vehicle type, or only to the forward or backward direction, or only during specific times of the day, or only to specific lanes. The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:maxspeed">maxspeed key</a> can be quite complex.</p>
