<div class="answer-body">
<p>The change was instant, but the map you see can take some time to refresh (in part because it is heavily cached). You can change to the "Humanitarian Map" layer to see it with the directional arrows pointing North. Other maps may take a week to 10 days to catch up, routing engines even longer (most update on a monthly cycle).</p>
