<p>Sorry, mapping for OSM is almost always unpaid volunteering. The OpenStreetMap Foundation has a very small budget and that's mostly dedicated to hardware and to legal support.</p>
<p>But if you want to make a living out of OSM, there are several companies that produce OSM-based maps or software, and a few of those have a "data team" (or similar) which employs mappers. Mapbox and Telenav are two such companies.</p>
