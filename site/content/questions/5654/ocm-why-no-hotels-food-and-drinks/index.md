<p>It's not possible to show everything on the one map - there has to be choices as to what is shown and what is left out, in order to map the map easier to read.</p>
<p>In saying that, most cartographic questions with OpenCycleMap can be answered with "because I haven't got the time to deal with it. Running the server, dealing with mass downloading applications and so on takes up all my time".</p>
