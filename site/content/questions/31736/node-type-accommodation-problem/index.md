<p>If "<code>guest_house</code>" is an appropriate translation for "Pension" in your country, I'd use "tourism=guest_house" for that. There are <a href="http://taginfo.osm.org/tags/tourism=guest_house">36,347</a> of them currently.</p>
<p>For "Free room" you might want to look at other "<a href="http://taginfo.osm.org/keys/tourism#values">tourism</a>" values that people have used.</p>
<p>Another option is to ask on a <a href="http://wiki.osm.org/wiki/Contact">mailing list (or other forum) for your country</a>.</p>
<p>In the iD editor, typing "guest" into the search returns "tourism=guest_house" as the top result.</p>
<p>EDIT:</p>
<p>I'd tag the "smaller than a hotel, larger than a guest house" as a hotel.</p>

<p><strong>Guest-house</strong> is built for commercial use, for accomodation.</p>
<p>"<strong>free room</strong>" or "<strong>private accomodation</strong>" is different cathegory. It was originaly classic family house, which was slightly, partially re-built. It is stil family house, where live original family members, but it has more or less separated part with one or two rooms and restroom for accomodation. Main difference is, that it usually offers less privacy than guest-house, but it is very cheap.</p>
<p>ps: there are almost no <strong>hostels</strong> for bacpackers and young travellers in my country (i think in central and eastern europe too), so this cathegory is useless for me. This is nice example of regional differences. If some cathegory fits in one part of world, in other it is useless (sometimes even next country).</p>
<p>pps: <strong>chalet</strong> is something different, it is average only for high mountain regions (other private chalets are not for immediate accomodation, you must book them weeks or months ahead)</p>
