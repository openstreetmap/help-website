<p>I also find signs like these or posts helpful when trying to locate or assist others to locate the start of bushwalking/hiking or mtb tracks as well as conveying the intended information.<br />
I don't know what the correct answer is but suggest that sign=yes, sign=information, sign=nature_reserve, sign=notice, sign=description may assist. Pehaps man_made=sign, sign=* is a better way to tag them.<br />
I expect they unlikely to render on the standard maps.<br />
The usual tourism and traffic signs are not suitable.</p>
