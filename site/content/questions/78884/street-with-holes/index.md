<p>Holes cannot be mapped as an element in the OSM database (since they are extremely volatile). You could, however, use the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:smoothness"><code>smoothness</code></a> tag to alert routers to the state of the street.</p>
