<div class="answer-body">
<p>Ignoring tagging for renderer:</p>
<ol>
<li><code>amenity=police</code> was tagged on the building, instead of the compound</li>
<li><code>landuse=commercial</code> is "misinterpreted" as any office and non-industrial service activity.</li>
</ol>
<p>It's true there are no widespread-enough <code>landuse=</code> tag for similar complexes hosting multiple POI-s/functions with common properties (although not a problem here), when you look at <code>=institutional</code>, <code>=police</code> (cf <code>=military</code>), <code>=prison</code>, <code>=civic</code>, etc.</p>
