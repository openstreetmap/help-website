<p>Hi,</p>
<p>I am having a problem with <a href="http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl">http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl</a> when approaching roundabout. I understand the problem, and why it is happing and I am hoping there can be a fix or a work around. (EDIT: It only happens when I pick "Generic Routable (testing new style) )</p>
<p>When I approach roundabouts it gives me the wrong exit number say "take the first exit" when the map and the route clearly shows it is the third exit. This seems to be happening because in Ireland and UK we drive in the left and go around roundabouts clockwise while in the rest of Europe they drive on the right and go around roadabouts anti-clockwise. On the main screen on the Garmin it shows the correct way around the roundabout, but on the small icon in the top left hand side it shows to go around the roundabout the wrong way. So the exit number is being counted in the wrong direction.</p>
<p>I saw a similar problem in the android OSM AND, but there was an option in the settings, driving region, for country=UK which switched the roundabouts the correct way around.</p>
<p>Is there a similar workaround or can it be fixed for UK (UK, Ireland, Northern Ireland, India, Australia, Gibralter (near spain) where they drive on the left?</p>
<p>I also noticed that mini roundabouts seem to be ignored as if no roundabout exits at all.</p>
<p>If I use <a href="http://mapas.alternativaslibres.es/downloads.php#">http://mapas.alternativaslibres.es/downloads.php#</a> or the normal options the same roundabout shows the correct exit.</p>

<p>Dowload map directly. This direct link may work <a href="http://osm.pleiades.uni-wuppertal.de/garmin/generic_new/18-02-2014/c589f89a2b2f7a1c1d9aca87a0538c30/">http://osm.pleiades.uni-wuppertal.de/garmin/generic_new/18-02-2014/c589f89a2b2f7a1c1d9aca87a0538c30/</a></p>
<p>Then when I approach a roundabout it speaks the wrong exit number.</p>
