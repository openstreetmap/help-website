<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is a good trick to editing only existing ways in JOSM:</p>
<ol>
<li>Open the "filter" dialogue (filter button on the left bar)</li>
<li>In the "filter" dialogue add a new filter (button +). This will open a search window</li>
<li>Write "type:node" in the search box in the new window and then click "submit filter"</li>
<li>This will filter out all the nodes. You can work moving and editing only whole ways</li>
<li>When you are done, you can bring the nodes back by unchecking the filter in the filter dialogue.</li>
</ol>
<p>bonus track: explore the other buttons in the filter for "invert" and "hide" the objects.</p>
