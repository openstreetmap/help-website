<p>Footways or streets tagged with <code>area=yes</code> is a reasonably common way to tagging paved expanses in plazas etc. This could potentially be used here is nothing more specific is available.</p>
<p>Tall platforms for observation are <code>man_made=tower</code> and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:tower:type%3Dobservation"><code>tower:type=observation</code></a> but this doesn't seem to be what you are driving at.</p>
<p>Pictures or additional explanation of what you're trying to record might be helpful.</p>
