<div class="answer-body">
<p>It might take some time for your changes to be propagated via re-rendering and cache replacement. See <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FAQ#Editing">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/FAQ#Editing</a> (next paragraph w/ question: "I have just made some changes to the map. How do I get to see my changes?) for what you may do to see those changes by invalidating your browser cache.</p>
<p>If this does not help, please include a link showing the area you've edited or link to your changeset.</p>
<p>Are you the OSM user by the same name?</p>
<p>E.g. on this changeset from yesterday <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/146150039#map=19/51.04536/-2.18009">https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/146150039#map=19/51.04536/-2.18009</a> I doo see your change regarding the outline applied on all zoom levels (using a Fastly tile cache in Germany).</p>
