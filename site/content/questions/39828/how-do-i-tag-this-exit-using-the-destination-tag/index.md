<p>I would map it as you describe. This will allow navigation software to mention the words on the sign. Names of cities that are not mentioned on the signs might confuse a driver . Imaging the software would say "turn right to XXX" or ""take the exit to XXX" and you do not see "XXX" on the signs.</p>
