<p>Looks reasonably good. I see you are also tagging some areas where the trees are planted in rows as landuse=forest which is likely correct too.</p>
<p>I happen to like also putting landcover=trees on natural=wood. And if we had any areas managed for wood products I'd do it on landuse=forest. See <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Alandcover%3Dtrees">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag%3Alandcover%3Dtrees</a></p>
<p>Some don't like using landcover=trees but I can't always tell by looking if the trees are they naturally or because of human management. For example, some of your natural=wood areas are in a fairly built up area and those trees might be part of a managed wood lot, maybe an area in a park, etc.</p>
