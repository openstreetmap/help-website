<div class="answer-body">
<ul>
<li>Pan the map to the area you wish to edit</li>
<li>Click the "Edit" button at the top left. (Account and Login is required for this)</li>
<li>Wait a while for the Potlatch-editor to load, then wait for the objects to load. Says: "Loading data..." in the right top corner</li>
<li>Select the woodland in the editor</li>
<li>At the left panel there should be a field that says "Name"</li>
</ul>
<p>if not, then:</p>
<ul>
<li>(Assuming the woodland is still selected) Click "Advanced" at the bottom of the left panel.</li>
<li>Click "Add" at the middle of the left panel.</li>
<li>Type "name" and press return.</li>
<li>Type desired name</li>
</ul>
<p>If you alt-tabbed to here during doing the three last steps the editor might loose focus and act up. If you can't solve it just click "Add" again.</p>
<p>Then just click "Save" in the top left corner and add a comment. (Perhaps the source of the data you provided, perhaps Knowledge?</p>
<p>Then you might head on to the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Beginners%27_Guide">Beginners Guide</a> or perhaps check out some video tutorials at <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7fc-n_GXVgI&amp;feature=related">youtube</a></p>
