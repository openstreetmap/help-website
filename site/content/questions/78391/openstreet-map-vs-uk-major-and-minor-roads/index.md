<p>The major road files looks to be 'A'-roads &amp; motorways which will be covered by motorway, trunk &amp; primary highways in OSM (+ their respective link roads). Also the geometries in the file are very generalised.</p>
<p>The minor road points are much more arbitrary &amp; don't even necessarily cover 'B' roads. Some looks to be monitoring traffic at school sites or known rat runs. I think these might be much harder to use than the major sites, as many are located in dense networks of residential streets and cannot be used to impute traffic volumes on neighbouring roads.</p>
