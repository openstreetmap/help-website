<div class="answer-body">
<p>You could <a href="http://switch2osm.org/serving-tiles/">setup your own slippy map</a> configured to allow higher zooms, or you could use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Vector_tiles">some form of vector rendering</a>. There are a few projects working on this, including <a href="http://www.mapbox.com/blog/vector-tiles/">mappbox</a> and <a href="http://kothic.org/js/">kothic js</a>.</p>
