<p>Hello, I downloaded this week a map of Austria from <a href="http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/">http://garmin.openstreetmap.nl/</a> and installed it on my Garmin-Navigator nüwi 255W. When I tried it out, it became clear, that the navigator couldn´t find the housenumbers of many adresses. I looked it up on the map of openstreetmap.org and saw, that in THIS map all the housenumbers were ok?</p>
<p>Here´s an example screenshot: <img src="http://members.chello.at/~martin.drucker/osm.jpg" alt="example" /></p>
<p>If I search for "Schüttelstraße 27" (which is right of the river), my Garmin nüwi 255W will only find the "Schüttelstraße" as a whole and will send me to the beginning, although you can see, that <span>openstreetmap.org knows the housenumbers</span> of this street.</p>
