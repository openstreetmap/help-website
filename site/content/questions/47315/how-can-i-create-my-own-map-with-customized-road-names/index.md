<p>I produce my own walking maps, I have used several produced by other, Talkytoaster, Openfietsmap etc etc, but none did exactly what i wanted. So i set about producing my own.</p>
<p>I use a combination of Mkgmap, Osmosis and splitter,</p>
<p>Osmosis I use to crop the map, Splitter I use to split the map into sizes Mkgmap can handle Mkgmap produces the map,</p>
<p>The look and feel of the map is really determined by the Style files that MKGMAP uses</p>
