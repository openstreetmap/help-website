<p>Is there a way to indicate that a street has no name, that can be distinguished from a street that has a name, but nobody has entered it yet?</p>
<p>For example, in San Ignacio, Cayo, Belize, the first street East of Pegasus, which runs from Beetle Juice Street to Sirius Street, has no name. I checked with the Town Council, and they said that since no houses face the street, they did not name it.</p>
<p>Is there a way to indicate this in OSM?</p>
