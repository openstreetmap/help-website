<div class="question-body">
<p>Hi</p>
<p>In Bangalore (India), we have a underpass at a location called <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=19/12.95689/77.70127">Marathahalli</a>, in which-</p>
<ol>
<li>The East-West 4-lane Varthur road passes on the upper deck (a massive RCC structure)</li>
<li>The North-South 4-lane Outer Ring Road ("ORR") passes below this deck.</li>
<li>The ORR and Varthur Road are joined through 4 loops (called "service roads"). These service roads climb up to the upper deck's level through ramps.</li>
<li>The upper deck also carries two links that provide a U-turn for the ORR</li>
<li>Pedestrians use the remaining space on the upper deck to cross between the two major roads.</li>
<li>The upper deck also has small traffic islands that separate the above features.</li>
</ol>
<p>At present, the RCC deck is not shown properly:</p>
<ol>
<li>Both halves of the Varthur road are shown as if they pass over two separate bridges.</li>
<li>The links are shown "hanging" (not shown as situated on the upper deck).</li>
<li>The pedestrian area on the upper deck is not shown at all.</li>
</ol>
<p>I would like to know how to depict this construction correctly. Please help!</p>
</div>
