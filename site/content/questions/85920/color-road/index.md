<p>You might want to check <a href="https://www.velomap.org/">velomap</a>. First feature is "High Contrast" ! But it's not free.</p>
<p>On <a href="https://download.bbbike.org/osm/">BBike</a>, there's three styles to choose from.</p>
<p>A lot more choices on the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download">wiki</a>.</p>
<p>You can also learn to make your own maps, and thus tweak the style used, but that's not trivial.</p>
