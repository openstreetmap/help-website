<p>You could tag the separate entrance with <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:entrance">entrance=xyz</a>.</p>
<p>entrance=mail <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/entrance=mail">has been used three times</a> so far, entrance=delivery 184 times and entrance=deliveries 4 times.</p>
