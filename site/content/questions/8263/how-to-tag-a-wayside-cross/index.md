<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you visit our Wiki (<a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org">wiki.openstreetmap.org</a>) you'll find there is a search box in the upper right. Enter "wayside cross" and you will find the answer to your question.</p>
<p>The wiki is generally a good first port of call if you are looking for tagging information. Other methods include a visit to <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org">taginfo.openstreetmap.org</a>, where entering "cross" in the search box also quickly yields the over 20,000 occurrences of wayside crosses in OSM. Finally, even entering "openstreetmap wayside cross" in any web search engine quickly leads to the desired result.</p>
