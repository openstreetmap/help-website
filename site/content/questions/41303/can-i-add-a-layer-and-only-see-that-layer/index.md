<p>yes, try this (the quick'n'dirty solution):</p>
<ol>
<li>go to <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/">http://overpass-turbo.eu/</a></li>
<li>centre the map on your city</li>
<li>click "Wizard"</li>
<li>enter the tag you like to see (e.g. <code>amenity=waste_basket</code>)</li>
<li>hit "build and run"</li>
</ol>
