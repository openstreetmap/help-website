<p>highway is * is wrong here! In the wiki * stands for primary/secondary/tertiary/unclassified/residential/etc. so one of those types that is most suitable you must add there.</p>
<p>BTW: cycleway=lane is enough, this means there are lanes on both sides, instead of cycleway:left=lane AND cycleway:right=lane</p>
