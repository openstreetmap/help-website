<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>It's not very clearly explained on that page, but the photos are loaded by default from the OpenStreetPhoto project. If they don't have any photos for your area, then nothing shows up.</p>
<p>You can either add your photos to that service, or use a different one that also provides a kml file in the correct format. You can make your own kml file for your own photos, as described on the page. When you know what the url for the kml file is, you click the settings button and add it to the correct section.</p>
<p>Currently there is no photomapping support in Potlatch2.</p>
