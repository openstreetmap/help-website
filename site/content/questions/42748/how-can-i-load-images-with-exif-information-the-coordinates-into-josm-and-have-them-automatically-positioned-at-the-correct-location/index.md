<p>I have just found out how to to it.</p>
<ol>
<li>In JOSM right click on the data layer. In the drop down menu click on "convert to GPX- layer.</li>
<li>Load the images using the imagery menu.</li>
<li>Download a fresh version from the OSM server.</li>
<li>Delete the converted GPX layer.</li>
</ol>
