<div class="answer-body">
<p>In addition to the other answers, please <em>only</em> add factual information to OpenStreetMap. Especially:</p>
<ul>
<li>Do not advertise your business. Facts are ok, marketing is not. You can add your telephone number or opening times, but we're not interested in "award-winning", "family owned", "finest in town", "exceptional", "awesome", "beautiful", or any other marketing speak.</li>
<li>This is true for the business name as well. The <code>name</code> tag is for the name and nothing else; if your name so happens to include marketing speak (i.e. if your business is <em>really</em> called "Banana Chip Studios Award-Winning Fine Art and Photography") then shorten it (to "Banana Chip Studios") or you'll be suspected of trying to "spam the map".</li>
<li>Do not add a business where none exists on the ground. We are not interested in the location of your mailbox or your registered address if you do not have a physical presence there (a workshop, a shop, or at least an office).</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p>"go to OpenStreetMap editor" means that you should edit our OpenStreetMap data (apparently coinmap uses it). Read our <span>Beginners%27_guide</span> or <a href="http://learnosm.org/">http://learnosm.org/</a> to learn how to start editing OpenStreetMap. If you feel like you want to start right away, go to <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new">https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new</a> (or click the "sign up" link on the top right on your homepage).</p>
<p>If the shop you want to edit does not exist yet, you need to add it to our data (see the beginner guides for this).</p>
<p>For how to use "tags" see <span>Tag#Use_in_an_editor</span>.</p>
<p>Welcome to OSM!</p>
