<p>As SomeoneElse already noted OSM is not what you seem to think it is ("sat nav part of OSM on my phone"). There are <span>loads of OSM-based services</span> but OSM itself is just a large database of geodata.</p>
<p>If I understand your question correctly you are searching for a voice guidance which accepts a gpx file (containing tracks or routes) as input? Search those <span>lists of software for mobile devices</span> for your requirements (currently you did not even mention which operating system you use). You may also find some similar questions on this help site by searching.</p>
