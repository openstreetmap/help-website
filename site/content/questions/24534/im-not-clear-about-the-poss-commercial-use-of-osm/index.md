<p>I'm preparing walking routes and would like clarity about the licencing of OSM data. I've checked checked out the info on licences but don't fully understand the position. I plan to develop a walking route and document it in the form of a write up plus a map (based on OSM data). This document could be: (i) placed on the web for anyone to download at any time for free (ii) included with others in a booklet which would offered for sale I guess that (i) is acceptable under the OSM licence (is that right?), and am not sure about (ii). Any explanation welcome! But if I've missed something that's already on the site somewhere - apologies, and please point me at it. Thanks in advance.</p>
