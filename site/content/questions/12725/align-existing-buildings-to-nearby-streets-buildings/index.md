<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is the way I do it in JOSM:</p>
<ul>
<li>Select the building</li>
<li>Press and hold the Ctrl key</li>
<li>Select two nodes from a street or whatever you want align to</li>
<li>Release the Ctrl key</li>
<li>Press Q or select Orthogonalize Shape in the Tools menu</li>
</ul>
<p>That works for buildings with right angles. The building will be orthogonalized at the same time if you haven't done that before.</p>
<p>Summary:</p>
<ul>
<li>Select an object and two nodes and press Q</li>
</ul>
