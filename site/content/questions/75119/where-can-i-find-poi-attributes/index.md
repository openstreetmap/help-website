<p>Are you looking for additional attributes that come along hotels? Then have a look at the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:tourism%3Dhotel">hotels wike page</a> for some documented tag and taginfo to find<a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/tags/tourism=hotel#combinations">actually used combinations</a>.</p>
<p>Or are you just looking how all kinds of POIs are generally tagged? Then browse the wiki.You could start with <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:amenity">amenity</a>, <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:tourism">tourism</a> and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:leisure">leisure</a>.</p>
