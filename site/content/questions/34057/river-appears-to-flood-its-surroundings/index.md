<p>Possible, likely cause: Someone has broken the <span>coastline</span> somewhere.</p>
<p>The problem is solved (by someone fixing whatever) since at latest 2014-06-18 06:56:14 UTC. Both sample locations reported in this question look good to me, now. See the <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/pipermail/talk/2014-June/thread.html#70037">mailing list thread</a>.</p>
<p>If there are still "flooded" areas somewhere just wait longer or use <span>/dirty</span>. You may also simply see an older cached vesion of the map part due to your browser's cache, so refresh your cache by reloading the map page with ctrl+F5 keys.</p>
