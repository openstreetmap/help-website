<div class="answer-body">
<p>You probably typed [ALT]+Z by mistake.</p>
<p>The building tool has several modes, one of which is aimed at circular buildings.</p>
<p>[CTRL]+[ALT]+B will open the preferences panel to switch back to rectangle mode. Alternatively you can use [ALT]+R or the corresponding item in the <em>Data</em> menu.</p>
<p>More information is available <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/JOSM/Plugins/BuildingsTools">on the wiki</a>.</p>
