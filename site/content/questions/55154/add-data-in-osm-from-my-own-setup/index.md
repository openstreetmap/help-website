<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is no off the shelf way to do what you want, it is "a simple matter of programming", but not totally trivial. In particular you need to consider how you synchronize IDs of new objects that you have created on your system.</p>
<p>You could have a look at POSM <a href="https://github.com/posm">https://github.com/posm</a> for a possible approach (IMHO unlikely to be practical in any area with existing data and concurrent editing).</p>
