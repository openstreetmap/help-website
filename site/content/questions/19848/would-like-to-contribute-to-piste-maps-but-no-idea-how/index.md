<p>Hi and welcome lumihei! You either could just upload your GPS tracks to help others a bit, or, much better, you use that info and your (mind) notes to directly create (if they are already in our data) or modify (if they are already in our data and you think your position data/meta information is better/more accurate) those pistes.</p>
<p>Read about that piste project and the specific tagging involved and see our <span>Beginner's guide</span> for a general how to (including how to upload GPS data). A FAQ is right here or at <span>FAQ in our wiki</span> (and the linked pages on top) or just search our wiki.</p>
<p>Please comment if that was clear enough for your. You also may make your initial question more specific. If you have different questions which are not already answered at help.osm.org then please ask a new question.</p>
