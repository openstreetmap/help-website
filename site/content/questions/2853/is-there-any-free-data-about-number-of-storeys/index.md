<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can tag any buildings you map with <code>building:levels=*</code> to record the number of storeys they have.</p>
<p>If you'd like to encourage others to record the number of storeys a building has, you could document the tag in the wiki, and submit patches to the major editors to include the tag in their presets (or submit feature requests at least).</p>
