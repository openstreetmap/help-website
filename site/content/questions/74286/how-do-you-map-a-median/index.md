<div class="answer-body">
<p>Medians are normally implicit. Dual carriageways (divided highways) are usually mapped as a separate way for each direction.</p>
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:kerb">Kerbs</a>, <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Landuse">landuse</a> and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Vegetation">vegetation</a> can be mapped as normal.</p>
