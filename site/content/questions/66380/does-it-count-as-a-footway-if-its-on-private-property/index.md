<div class="answer-body">
<p>Private <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dpath">paths</a>/<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dfootway">footways</a> can be mapped, and there are a range of <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:access">access tags</a> to indicate to what extent the public are allowed in.</p>
<p>Mapping objects that most mappers can't get to or see can lead to <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Good_practice#Verifiability">verifiability</a> issues though, so some discretion is advised.</p>
