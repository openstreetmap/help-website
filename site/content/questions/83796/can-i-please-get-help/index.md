<p>Hi.</p>
<p>It's hard to know if an area lacks mapped car parks. But it's quite easy to highlight the already mapped ones, if you check an area you know well, you should see the gaps easily.</p>
<p>For example this <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/?q=LyoKVGhpcyBoYcSGYmVlbiBnxI1lcmF0ZWQgYnkgdGhlIG92xJJwxIlzLXR1cmJvIHdpemFyZC7EgsSdxJ9yaWdpbmFsIHNlxLBjaMSsxIk6CsOiwoDCnGFtxI1pdHk9xKNya8S6ZyDFiMKdCiovCltvdXQ6anNvbl1bdGnFjMWfxaEyNV07Ci8vxI_ElMSdciByZXN1bHRzCigKICDFsyBxdcSSxJrFknQgZm9yOsWXxYnFi8WNxY_FkcSwxZRuZ8WYxoMgbm9kZVsixpVuxY55Ij0ixZLGmmciXSh7e2LEqnh9fSnFscaEd2F5xqTGpsaoxqrGrMaZxZXGsMayxrTGtsa4xrrGnsW6bMSUacWlx4DFjManxY_Hg8atx4bGscazxrVvxrfGucWxx4zGhnDEt27GjcW6xbzFvsaAxazEmMahecWxPsWxx6tza2XEvXF0Ow&amp;c=Aj5cC1vGYS">query on overpass-turbo</a> will display <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dparking"><code>amenity=parking</code></a> in the area you're looking at (click "Run" at the top left to load the data, try a not too big area, otherwise it will get really slow or even crash).</p>
<p>With this <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/?q=LyoKVGhpcyBoYcSGYmVlbiBnxI1lcmF0ZWQgYnkgdGhlIG92xJJwxIlzLXR1cmJvIHdpemFyZC7EgsSdxJ9yaWdpbmFsIHNlxLBjaMSsxIk6CsOiwoDCnGFtxI1pdHk9xKNya8S6ZyDFiMKdCiovCltvdXQ6anNvbl1bdGnFjMWfxaEyNV07Ci8vxI_ElMSdciByZXN1bHRzCigKICDFsyBxdcSSxJrFknQgZm9yOsWXxYnFi8WNxY_FkcSwxZRuZ8WYxoMgbm9kZVsixpVuxY55Ij0ixZLGmmciXSh7e2LEqnh9fSnFscaEd2F5xqTGpsaoxqrGrMaZxZXGsMayxrTGtsa4xrrGnsW6bMSUacWlx4DFjManxY_Hg8atx4bGscazxrVvxrfGuca7xoTGnsa9xr_HhMWTxZU6x49uZceHx5vHisefCseMxoZwxLduxo3FusW8xb7GgMWsxJjGoXnFsT7Fsce7c2tlxL1xdDs&amp;c=Aj5bMH1_MS">other query</a>, you will also get street-side parking (<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:parking:lane"><code>parking:lane=*</code></a>).</p>
<p>While re-reading your question, I thought that maybe you were looking for <code>amenity=parking</code> that lacks <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:parking"><code>parking=*</code></a> tag. Here is <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/?Q=%5Bout%3Ajson%5D%5Btimeout%3A25%5D%3B%0A%0A(%0A%20%20nwr%5B%22amenity%22%3D%22parking%22%5D%5B!%22parking%22%5D(%7B%7Bbbox%7D%7D)%3B%0A)%3B%0A%0Aout%20body%3B%0A%3E%3B%0Aout%20skel%20qt%3B&amp;C=34.74605;-92.28964;18">another query</a> to look for that.</p>
<p>It seems that none of the car parks in the area (around the CARTI Cancer center you mentioned) have the wanted tag. Indeed they don't have any other tag than <code>amenity=parking</code> ! I like to add at least <code>fee=</code>, <code>access=</code> and <code>opening_hours=</code> when I can find the relevant information. You will find a lot more details on the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dparking"><code>amenity=parking</code></a> wiki page.</p>
<p>Hope this helps.</p>
<p>Best regards.</p>
