<p>If you down load a trace from OSM traces list it will have an upload date, and in the datafile it will have timestamps unfortunately unless the trace has a good description it will be hard to find ones of an area, before you can begin to look at the dates. I have found some of my, up to 8 years ago traces, to find out when and where I went on a particular path, It did solve my query but it was time consuming. It would be nice if we could identify trace date form an editor.
