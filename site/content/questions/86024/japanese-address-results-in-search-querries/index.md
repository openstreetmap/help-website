<p>Fairly new here, I am trying to add data to the Japanese map. Adding and editing data works great, but search results show addresses incorrectly:</p>
<ol>
<li>A street name is displayed. This is rarely used in JP. The official standard is a PrefectureCityWardBlocknumberBuildingnumber style. (Yes, in that order, no spaces, just one line).</li>
<li>Users searching for a Japanese language item get result lists consisting mainly of Latin characters. Of course, Japanese characters are required here.</li>
</ol>
<p>Example: Searching for トヨタ自動車　(=Toyota Motor), one of the results list as "Factory トヨタ自動車　元町工場, national route 419, 鴻ノ巣町四丁目, Toyota, Aichi Prefecture, 471-0833, Japan"</p>
<p>Correct would be "Factory トヨタ自動車（株）元町工場, 愛知県豊田市元町1, 471-8573, Japan"</p>
<p>Source: <a href="https://global.toyota/jp/company/profile/facilities/manufacturing-jp/motomachi.html">https://global.toyota/jp/company/profile/facilities/manufacturing-jp/motomachi.html</a></p>
<p>I have played around with address tags and browser settings, but to no avail. How can I get the results to display correctly in queries? Please advise.</p>
