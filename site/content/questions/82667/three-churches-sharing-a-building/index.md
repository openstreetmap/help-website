<div class="answer-body">
<p>That's one of those topics where our tagging scheme is unfortunately not providing a really good answer. In my opinion the best way to do it is to separate building and communities.</p>
<p>Tag the building with <code>building=church</code> and all those tags that are common (address, opening hours, ...). Then create separate nodes inside the building for the churches with <code>amenity=place_of_worship</code> and all the related tags. If there is clearly one main church using the building and the other five are merely minor guests you may want to put the main church also on the building and not into a separate node.</p>
<p>This was quite recently discussed on the Tagging mailing list. You can <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/pipermail/tagging/2021-November/062873.html">read through that thread</a> as well.</p>
