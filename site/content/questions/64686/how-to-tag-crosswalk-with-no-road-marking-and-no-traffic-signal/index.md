<div class="answer-body">
<p>Use the second tag set: <code>highway=crossing</code> and <code>crossing=unmarked</code></p>
<p><code>crossing=uncontrolled</code> is <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:crossing">defined</a> as "A generic crossing with no traffic-signals of any type, just road markings; e.g., zebra-crossings." You have no markings in your example.</p>
<p>Note: <code>crossing_1</code> is no valid (aka usual) key.</p>
