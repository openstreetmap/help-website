<p>Disclaimer: we do not dispense legal advice and if you need that you should be consulting your legal counsel. Further: the help site is likely the worst place imaginable to discuss business plans.</p>
<p>Does this counts as derivative database -&gt; yes</p>
<p>and do I have to distribute it? -&gt; no (see below)</p>
<p>Our distribution license, the ODbL, requires you to make derived data available on request if either that data has been publicly used, or if a produced work created from such data is publicly used. If you don't publicly use your data there is no such requirement.</p>
<p>Note: if we are talking about a small number of roads this might be an extract that is not substantial and as such not subject to the license terms.</p>
