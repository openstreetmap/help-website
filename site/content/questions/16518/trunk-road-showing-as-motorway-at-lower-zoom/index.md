<p>It was changed from motorway to trunk on 22 September, but some tiles have not been re-rendered since 17 September. Eventually the rendering engine will catch up with the contents of the underlying database. You may be able to speed this up by refreshing the display in your browser a few times.</p>
<p>Steve</p>
