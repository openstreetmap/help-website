<p>Seeing more and more of these getting constructed every year. No idea how to tag them.</p>
<p>There are many buildings where I live that have a single purpose: storing people's belongings for a temporary period of time. These are often called "public storage units". They are usually long buildings with multiple garage units. Some are multistory, climate-controlled structures.</p>
<p>I have made several attempts to research the wiki and the help pages, but I have not found anything to help, except "boat storage". Can anyone here share what worked for them? Or should I do building=retail and name="Public Storage" and be done with it?</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>I had a look at what people map this sort of thing as a month or so ago (see <a href="https://github.com/SomeoneElseOSM/SomeoneElse-style/blob/master/style.lua#L1989">here</a>).</p>
<p>"<code>amenity=self_storage</code>", "<code>amenity=storage</code>", "<code>shop=storage_rental</code>" and "<code>amenity=storage_rental</code>" were all used in the UK; internationally there may be more keys. Try searching for the name of the company in <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/">taginfo</a> and have a look at what people tag things with that name as.</p>
