<div class="answer-body">
<p>Make sure you're in "select" mode (the topmost mode button), then select the way. Look at the message in the bottom right corner, it says: "Shift-Ctr to rotate selected". While holding Shift and Ctrl, Drag one corner of your polygon and it will rotate around its centre.</p>
