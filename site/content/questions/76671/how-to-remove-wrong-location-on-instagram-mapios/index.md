<div class="answer-body">
<p>Instagram / Facebook do not use POI (point of interest) data from OpenStreetMap (this is a very very frequent FAQ), so you will need to ask Facebook to correct the data.</p>
<p>Naturally, you should still check that the location is correct on OpenStreetMap, and that the restaurant is present at all, for those applications that directly use OpenStreetMap.</p>
<p>Is this <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/5924475163#map=19/35.83972/51.01398">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/5924475163#map=19/35.83972/51.01398</a> your restaurant and in the correct location?</p>
