<div class="answer-body">
<p>It's not critical that TIGER be retained, especially if there are serious problems with alignment in addition to small road segments. Just be sure to replace it with good data such as you mention which were obtained with GPS traces.</p>
<p>If the TIGER data is aligned well, you may create longer ways by combining segments of the same road:</p>
<ul>
<li>JOSM: Highlight multiple segments and press "C" (Combine)</li>
<li>Potlatch 1: Click one way to select it, then hold Ctrl while clicking the other.<br />
</li>
<li>Potlatch 2: Select all the ways you want to merge (use ctrl-click for this, or command-click if you're on a Mac), then click the 'Join' chainlink icon in the toolbox.</li>
</ul>
