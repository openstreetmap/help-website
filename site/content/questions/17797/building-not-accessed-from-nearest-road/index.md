<p>Things that may help:</p>
<ol>
<li>Draw the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dservice">highway=service</a> road from Bath Road to the hotel.</li>
<li>Put <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Entrance">entrance</a>=main on the main entrance.</li>
</ol>
<p>Note that whether or not these things <em>actually</em> <em>help</em> depends on the navigation software that you use OSM data with.</p>
