<div class="answer-body">
<p>Some mapping applications allow you to hold down the "Ctrl" key on your keyboard while using the scroll wheel on your mouse to zoom in or out. This can allow for a finer level of control when zooming.</p>
<p>Many mapping applications also have a zoom slider or zoom controls located in the user interface. You can use this to zoom in or out at a specific level, or to adjust the zoom level gradually.</p>
<p>Some mapping applications allow you to use a pinch gesture on a touch screen device to zoom in or out. This can also provide a finer level of control when zooming.</p>
