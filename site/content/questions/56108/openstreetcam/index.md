<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetCam is a product of <a href="http://www.telenav.com/">Telenav</a>. It's an app that allows people to continuously take pictures while driving, walking, cycling. Those pictures are then uploaded to a website from where they can be used by mappers to improve OpenStreetMap. Telenav is offering this service because they are using OpenStreetMap data in their navigation software.</p>
<p>A similar product is <a href="https://www.mapillary.com/">Mapillary</a>.</p>
