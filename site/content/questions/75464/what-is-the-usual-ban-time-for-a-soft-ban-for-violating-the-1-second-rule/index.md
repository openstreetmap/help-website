<p>Forever ;)</p>
<p>While that's not true (but should be), see <a href="https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/nominatim/">https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/nominatim/</a></p>
<p>But for any serious business you should either <a href="http://nominatim.org/release-docs/latest/admin/Installation/">setup your own nominatim instance</a> or use one of the providers or services listed <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Search_engines">here</a>, <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Commercial_OSM_Software_and_Services">here</a> or <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Nominatim#Alternatives_.2F_Third-party_providers">here</a>.</p>
<p>The nominatim instances run by OpenStreetMap are run by volunteers on donated hardware and may get overloaded or may stop serving you anytime and are not recommended to be used for a regular business endeavor.</p>
