<p>Hi, Your 4 changesets are here:- <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/StevenRen/history#map=7/52.566/-105.681">https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/StevenRen/history#map=7/52.566/-105.681</a></p>
<p>Click on a changeset to open it. You can then see that all features added in the changeset are appearing on the OSM map rendering. I've looked through all your changes and everything is OK. OSM is open to everyone, you approve the data when you upload it, hopefully, it's all to OSM guidelines and good practice. If not, again hopefully someone will notice and made amends.</p>
<p>If you're looking to see your changes in an application other than OSM directly then there may well be a delay in your app getting up-to-date data from OSM. you should address this problem to the particular app admins.</p>
