<div class="answer-body">
<p>We cannot update the bing aerial images ourselves. See <a href="/questions/3522/">image-of-bing-is-very-old</a>. You may enter your location at <span>Bing/Priorities</span> (new account needed) but that is just an idea. In the end: please just wait for bing updating.</p>
<p>In the meantime we have some other providers of aerial imagery which we are allowed to use: just look at the imagery menu in your editor – likely you will find e.g. Mapbox Sat there. If you are lucky it is newer.</p>
<p>But please do not use unallowed imagery, e.g. google's satellite imagery (for mapping / your edits / your additions). We are not allowed to use it, so you should not.</p>
<p>Last option if you cannot find an allowed up-to-date imagery source: Use another <span>mapping technique</span> (e.g. gps traces) to add the shape of a new road to our maps.</p>
