<div class="answer-body">
<p><code>amenity=social_facility</code> + <code>social_facility:for=senior</code>, where you need to check whether it is a <code>social_facility=</code> of <code>=assisted_living</code>, <code>=group_home</code>, or <code>=nursing_home</code>.</p>
