<p>I guessing highway=pedestrian is not appropriate because this isn't a case where the pavement is a wider street per the wording here: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dfootway">highway=footway</a> Here's an example of <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/334121288">area:highway usage.</a></p>

<div class="comment-text">
<p>For the examples given, highway=pedestrian would be completely wrong. That tag describes a pedestrianized-street, or in other words, a street that's primarily used by pedestrians but can also be used by authorized vehicles. The courtyard of a school or the area around a pool certainly do not fit this description. highway=footway+area=yes or area:highway=footway would be the best options.</p>
</div>
