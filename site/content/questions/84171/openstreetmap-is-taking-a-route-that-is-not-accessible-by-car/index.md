<p>I believe this may be related to this rather strange "barrier=yes" node on White Hart Avenue: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/443274874">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/443274874</a></p>
<p>It looks like OSRM, at least with default settings on the OSM home page, refuses to route past this so-called barrier, even if this forces the use of road tagged as access=private - you can see this by trying to route over a very short distance on either side of the node: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/directions?engine=fossgis_osrm_car&amp;route=51.49817%2C0.10176%3B51.49856%2C0.10199#map=16/51.4950/0.0959" title="OSRM link">OSRM example</a></p>
<p>From the imagery this barrier doesn't seem to correspond to anything in the real world and could likely be deleted. Or perhaps it was meant to refer to a barrier adjoining the street rather than on the street itself (it looks like there might be some kind of gate to the east of that point).</p>
