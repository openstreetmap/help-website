<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is one example where we do a comparison based on official data from local autorities or data sources with the OSM data. As result, we have lists of streets or housenumbers that may be missing in OSM. But this webservice <a href="http://regio-osm.de">regio-osm.de</a> is based on data for Germany or some areas around, and not for other continents/countries. And the webservice itself seems to be only in german language.</p>
<p>So you should ask yourself first: Do you have any official data that you can compare against OSM?</p>
