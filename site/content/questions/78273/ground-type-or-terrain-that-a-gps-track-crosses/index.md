<p><a href="https://maps.openrouteservice.org/">OpenRouteService</a> shows you the surfaces you are covering with the calculated routes and you can also upload an existing GPX track which the tool will try to match to the roads. I just tried it and shows you the surfaces by percentages and also visualizes them on the map.</p>
<p>Other routing services might do the same.</p>
