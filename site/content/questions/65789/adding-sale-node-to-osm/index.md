<div class="answer-body">
<p>If this is not a regular shop that sells goods at a reduced price then the answer is a simple: no.</p>
<p>OSM is a dataset of more or less permanent objects and facilities. As you say you can use OSM as a backdrop to displaying a sale location, for example with umap.openstreetmap.fr, but it is not something that belongs directly in OSM.</p>
