<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p><code>bicycle=yes</code> if there are signs or road markings indicating that bicycle traffic should use this crossing, or if you know for a fact that a bicycle lane or bike path crosses here. <code>bicycle=no</code> if there are signs or road markings indicating that bicycle traffic should not use this crossing. Otherwise no need to do any bicycle-specific tagging.</p>
<p>Despite not including <code>bicycle=no</code> in the "translation" for zebra crossings, the wiki does say that <code>crossing=zebra</code> is for pedestrians only, so I never tag <code>zebra</code> unless there's a reason to believe that bike crossing is prohibited. The major difference between zebra crossings and pelican crossing, by the way, is that pelican crossings have signal lights to indicate when pedestrians may cross (we call them "walk/don't walk" signs in the USA) and zebra crossings do not.</p>
