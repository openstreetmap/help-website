<p>Under "Windows" there is a selection for "Map Paint Styles". Clicking on it will add it to the side bar area (not sure the official name). Once there you can use the check boxes to select the display style. Maybe one of them, like "wire frame" will be close to what you are looking for.</p>

<div class="comment-text">
<p>"<code>key:name</code>" matches all elements that have "key" as one of their keys and "name" as a part of that key's value. To match all elements that have a name, you should use the filter string "<code>name=*</code>".</p>
<p>I'm not sure whether that's ideal for your use case, though, because it will also disable/hide highways and buildings that have a name. Based on your original question - showing only buildings and highways -, I would have suggested using</p>
<pre><code>building=* OR highway=*</code></pre>
<p>as a filter, and use the "inverse filter" checkbox.</p>
</div>
