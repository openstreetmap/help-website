<p><a href="http://daveh.dev.openstreetmap.org/garmin/Lambertus/latest/README">http://daveh.dev.openstreetmap.org/garmin/Lambertus/latest/README</a></p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>Probably the easiest to follow set of instructions that I've seen are <a href="http://www.dcrainmaker.com/2013/05/download-garmin-705800810.html">these</a>. As far as I can tell from reading <a href="https://buy.garmin.com/en-US/US/on-the-road/automotive/2013-line/nuvi-42/prod120957.html">this</a>, you'll need to get a microSD card if you don't already have one. As North America is quite large, I'd try to load a smaller map based around your local area first before retrying with a larger one.</p>
<p>There are actually <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin">lots of different styles</a> of map created from OSM data for Garmin devices, but the site linked to from the above instructions is probably the easiest to use. It might also help to have a look at <a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/search/?q=nuvi+america&amp;Submit=search&amp;t=question">these previous questions</a> - there may be answers there that are useful.</p>
