<div class="answer-body">
<p>Map tiles are a "produced work". You can release them using any license you like.</p>
<p>The viral part of the ODbL only kicks in when you create a "derived work". E.g. when you combine OSM data with another dataset into a new "database".</p>
<p>Try plugging "ODbL produced derived" into your search engine of choice and you will find many lengthy explanations/discussions.</p>
