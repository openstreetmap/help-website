<p>An alternative is to download <a href="http://www.javawa.nl/jdm_en.html">JaVaWa Device Manager</a>. Click on the "Manage Maps" button to find tools that will allow you to rename the maps on your device.</p>
<p>The stable mate <a href="http://www.javawa.nl/gmtk_en.html">JaVaWa GMTK</a> provides this functionality for managing maps installed on your computer (Mapsource / Basecamp).</p>
