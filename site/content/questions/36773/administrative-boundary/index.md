<div class="comment-text">
<p>To create sub-national boundaries, you have to create new relations of type "boundary=administrative" like for the country but with a higher admin_level (3 in your case). Then attach the ways in this relation. Note that some of the ways can be attached to more than one boundary relation when they are used for several levels (country borders are also regional borders).</p>
