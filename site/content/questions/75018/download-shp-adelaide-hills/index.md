<div class="answer-body">
<p>It's easier to use Overpass-turbo and accept Geojson output. I would recommend a series of thematic layer downloads (e.g., highways, water etc).</p>
<p>Alternatively download pre-built shapefiles for Australia from <a href="https://download.geofabrik.de/australia-oceania.html">Geofabrik Downloads</a>. You'll need to clip them to your area of interest. <a href="https://extract.bbbike.org/?lang=en&amp;sw_lng=137.97&amp;sw_lat=-35.869&amp;ne_lng=141.83&amp;ne_lat=-33.519&amp;format=shp.zip&amp;oi=0&amp;city=adelaide%20hills&amp;layers=B000T">BBBike</a> may also be suitable for your purposes as it provides similar shapefiles to those from Geofabrik with custom bounding boxes.</p>
