<p>"lat/long positions" to "Country/State/City name" is reverse geocoding. See <span>Nominatim in our docu wiki</span>. You could use a nominatim service to convert the lat/lon positions to a text string. Please comply to the <span>usage policy</span> of the service which you are using (if you do not set up your own Nominatim server). As mentioned there, the <span>OSM copyright</span> also needs to be respected.</p>
