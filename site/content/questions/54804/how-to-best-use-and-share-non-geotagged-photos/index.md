<div class="answer-body">
<p>You may be able to geo-tag them if you recorded a GPX trace at the same time, assuming the clock on the camera had been set to GPS time or you know the error. Pics can be located with JOSM or Prune and several other apps which use the GPX time stamps to fix the pictures. You can manually do this with Prune. With prune you can see the time of each GPX point on an OSM map just sync the time within the pics data. A clever trick if you can locate one pic, is to retrieve the time error and use this correction to Geo-fix the others.</p>
</div>
