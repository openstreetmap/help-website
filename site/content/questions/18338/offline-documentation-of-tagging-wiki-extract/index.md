<p><a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org">Taginfo</a> does a relatively good job of spidering the Wiki and storing tag documentation in a SQLite database. That database can be downloaded in full from <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/download/taginfo-wiki.db.bz2">http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/download/taginfo-wiki.db.bz2</a> - the only thing missing is a viewer for this in JOSM.</p>
