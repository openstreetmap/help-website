<p>Ah, okay, I understand: you entered a wrong email (and the correct user name) but you cannot sign in (as I proposed in my first comment) since the account is not activated (via email). You also cannot change your display name of the working account since the desired display name is blocked by the half registration (I tested that).</p>
<p>I <em>guess</em> such not-activiated registrations are cancelled after some amount of time (e.g. two weeks). Yes, that is no solution for you - indeed you need a manual deletion of the non-activated account (with the wrong email address). Please send an email to the osm.org admins asking for deletion of the not-activated account <em>Attilio_A_Romita</em>: See <span>FAQ#How can I close my account?</span> for the address.
