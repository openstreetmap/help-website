<div class="answer-body">
<p>if an area is only accessible by authorized personnel, you can map it as access=private. Not knowing the exact extent of an area is something that cannot be solved with the current OSM tools/tags.</p>
<p>Please note that "foreigners" normally means people from another area, often meaning people from another country. AFAIK there is no tag to indicate access for such group.</p>
