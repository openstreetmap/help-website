<p>No, that is not possible. Most maps are generated by producing image files for a given zoom level following fixed rules. You might find some other style (<a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Using_OpenStreetMap#Ready-made_online_maps)">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Using_OpenStreetMap#Ready-made_online_maps)</a> that produces more details for your desired zoom level.</p>
