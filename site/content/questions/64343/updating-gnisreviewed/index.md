<div class="answer-body">
<p>IMHO either removing GNIS tags or setting gnis:reviewed=yes are both valid actions. The GNIS import was a long time ago and it is rather unlikely that the source will ever be used again. In general anything imported from GNIS with <em>(historic)</em> in the name should have priority for review as that is really just a mispelling of <em>(wrong)</em> :-).</p>
