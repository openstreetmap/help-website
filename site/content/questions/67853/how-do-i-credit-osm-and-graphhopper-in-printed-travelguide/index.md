<div class="answer-body">
<p>In addition to the copyright page, I recommend the Legal FAQ's <a href="https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Licence/Licence_and_Legal_FAQ#How_should_I_attribute_you.3F">"How should I attribute you?"</a> section as a resource on how to attribute OSM. It has this to say on the placement of attribution for printed works:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>For a printed map, the credit should appear beside the map if that is where other such credits appear, and/or in the "acknowledgements" section of the publication (often at the start of a book or magazine).</p>
</blockquote>
<p>I unfortunately don't know what kind of attribution GraphHopper expects on top of OSM's. If this is unclear, you might want to contact GraphHopper's developers.</p>
