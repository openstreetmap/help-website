<p>Does your JOSM remote control via HTTP<strong>S</strong> work with other sites? E.g. with osm.org? I guess not.</p>
<ol>
<li>Did you enable HTTPS in the JOSM remote control settings?</li>
<li>Did you once accept the JOSM certificate for this connection in your browser (see <a href="https://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Preferences/RemoteControl">https://josm.openstreetmap.de/wiki/Help/Preferences/RemoteControl</a> )?</li>
</ol>

<p>I solved the problem. Click with your right mouse button on the JOSM link in Keepright and choose "open link in a new window". You will see that this page has no secure connection. If you make an exception for this page, so it works without certificate, you will have the connection between Keepright and JOSM again working.</p>
