<div class="answer-body">
<p>I don't think that you have mapped it wrongly - it's a <a href="https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto/issues/2457">known problem</a> with that map style. See also <a href="https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto/issues/2428">this issue</a>. Mapnik (the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mapnik">software</a>) is what actually decides where to place icons. There are ways within the stylesheet (or resulting XML) to influence where icons and labels go, but if a change were made it'd need careful testing to see what it broke elsewhere.</p>
