<p>The large difference between the east and west is an artifact of incomplete or bad <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/CanVec">CanVec</a> imports where the east has been done, but the west hasn't.</p>
<p>The small squares are probably also inaccurate, as most CanVec tree data tends to be outdated, but the overall appearance wouldn't have changed much.</p>
