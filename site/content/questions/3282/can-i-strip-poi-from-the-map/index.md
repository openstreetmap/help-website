<div class="answer-body">
<p>To get a map without POIs you have some ready made solutions:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://maps.cloudmade.com/editor">Cloudmade styleditor</a> has many styles, one example <a href="http://maps.cloudmade.com/?lat=51.508315&amp;lng=-0.124712&amp;zoom=14&amp;styleId=12569">"Fresh without POIs"</a>, just search to find them all.</li>
<li>Wikimedia <a href="https://wiki.toolserver.org/view/OpenStreetMap">Toolserver</a> has the default osm style <a href="http://toolserver.org/~osm/styles/?zoom=16&amp;lat=52.51356&amp;lon=13.38112&amp;layers=0000F0FF0000F0FFF0B00F">rendered in black and white without POI</a></li>
</ul>
<p>If these are not enough, you have to spend sometime to make your own stylesheet in mapnik, you can base it on the Toolserver stylesheet perhaps. There are webservices that let you render your own map look at <a href="http://tiledrawer.com/">tiledrawer</a>.</p>
</div>
