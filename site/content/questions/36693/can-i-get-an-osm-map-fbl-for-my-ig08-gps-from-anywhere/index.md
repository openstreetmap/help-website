<p>The format used by IGO seems to be proprietary and the conversion from OSM to it has to be performed by the manufacturer, at least there doesn't seem to be a free conversion program available.</p>
<p>You should likely contact <a href="http://www.igonavigation.com/map-updates-and-extras">http://www.igonavigation.com/map-updates-and-extras</a> . See <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IGO_%28software%29">http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IGO_%28software%29</a> for more information.</p>
