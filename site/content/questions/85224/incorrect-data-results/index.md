<p>It seems as if the city district can only be found if you search for: Кировский район, Каза́нь</p>
<p>see: <a href="https://nominatim.osm.org/search.php?q=%D0%9A%D0%B8%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9+%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD%2C+%D0%9A%D0%B0%D0%B7%D0%B0%CC%81%D0%BD%D1%8C&amp;format=json">https://nominatim.osm.org/search.php?q=%D0%9A%D0%B8%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9+%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD%2C+%D0%9A%D0%B0%D0%B7%D0%B0%CC%81%D0%BD%D1%8C&amp;format=json</a></p>
<p>As nominatim cannot expand the район and the district is tagged with Кировский район, it does not find the district while just searching for Кировский.</p>
<p>You could add an alt_name of Кировский to the Кировский район relation here: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/2133463">https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/2133463</a></p>
