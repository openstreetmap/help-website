<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetMap has a sizable community in Iran.</p>
<p>There are many different servers under the openstreetmap.org domain and they are in many different locations and have different IP addresses. Some of these IP addresses depend on your location (like tile.openstreetmap.org), others (like api.openstreetmap.org) can also change in the course of administrative operations.</p>
<p>So the addresses are not "dynamic" as such but still it will not be possible for you to hard-code an exception for a specific IP.</p>
