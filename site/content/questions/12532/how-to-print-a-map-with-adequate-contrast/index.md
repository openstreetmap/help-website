<p>I would suggest you export your area to SVG and then print it. (You might need a plugin if you wish to use Ifranview, but you can print it from your browser).</p>
<p>To export to SVG:</p>
<ul>
<li>Zoom in to your area of interest</li>
<li>Click "Export" in the upper taskbar</li>
<li>Select the "Map Image (shows standard layer)"-option</li>
<li>Change the "Format" to SVG</li>
<li>Click "Export" and you'll get a file</li>
</ul>
