<div class="answer-body">
<p>Information such as state House and Senate districts is not normally added directly to OpenStreetMap because it is not a physical feature and cannot be verified by other mappers.</p>
<p>To create a map display, the district information could be displayed on top of OpenStreetMap tiles using a package such as <a href="http://openlayers.org/">OpenLayers</a> or <a href="http://leafletjs.com/">Leaflet</a> and the official district boundaries obtained from the state of Tennessee.</p>
