<p>In the real world the following has happened</p>
<p>Pound Stretcher (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076385">way 479076385</a>) and PetHut (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076386">way 479076386</a>) shared a building on Tyldesley Road. These two businesses vacated and B&amp;M Bargins moved its business from Shuttle Street (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479074651">way 479074651</a>) and occupies the entire building on Tyldesley Road. The building on Shuttle Street still stands but is vacant.</p>
<p><strong>What is considered the best way to handle this situation in OSM?</strong> Can <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076385">way 479076385</a> and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076386">479076386</a> be merged? Should I take one and delete the other? Or should <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076385">way 479076385</a> and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479076386">479076386</a> be deleted and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/479074651">way 479074651</a> be moved from Shuttle Street to Tyldesley Road with a new way been created for the vacant building?</p>
