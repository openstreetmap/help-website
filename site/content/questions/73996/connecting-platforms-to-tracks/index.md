<p>Generally, there is no connection between a platform and a railway (or highway). Route relations are used to indicate that traffic on the railway (highway) uses a certain platform for allowing passengers to board/disembark by line.</p>
<p>Are you familiar with the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Public_transport">wiki page on mapping public transport</a>?</p>
