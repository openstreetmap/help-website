<p>I am not sure what you mean by "shoddily" here.</p>
<p>No, you should not delete them. Remember that the one who did them would be quite disappointed if his work gets deleted. They are not wrong - there are buildings, right? You could improve them. Also remember that the aerial imagery could have been worse in the past – so drawing more accurately would not have been possible.</p>
<p>Just contacting <span>the one</span> to whom the toes in question belong to could help (at least if the user is still active – look at his edits). Tell him what you think about the buildings. Maybe why you think that they better should not have been drawn like this at all. :-)<br />
</p>
