<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>Half of your mentioned tags obviously won't define a music school: <em>shop=music</em>, <em>shop=musical_instrument</em> and (a single) <em>type=audio</em> clearly don't define a school. Likewise <em>amenity=studio</em> just seems wrong.</p>
<p>So the remaining tags are <em>amenity=music_school</em>, <em>education=music</em> and <em>training=music</em>.</p>
<p><em>training</em> has just a <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Proposed_features/training">pretty empty proposal</a> whereas both <em>amenity=music_school</em> and <em>education=music</em> are suggested in the <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/DE:How_to_map_a">german</a>, <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/ID:How_to_map_a">indonesian</a> and <a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Pl:How_to_map_a">polish</a> "how to map a" wiki pages. <em>amenity=music_school</em> is also quite similar to <em><a href="http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dschool">amenity=school</a></em> and <em><a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=amenity%3Ddriving_school">amenity=driving_school</a></em> whereas <em>education</em> seems to be the better category.</p>
<p>My recommendation is to add both <em>amenity=music_school</em> and <em>education=music</em> for now.</p>
</div>
