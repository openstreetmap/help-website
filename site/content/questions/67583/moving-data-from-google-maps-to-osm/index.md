<p>Unfortunately, Google Maps cannot legally be used as a source for adding data to the OpenStreetMap database. Please stick with the imagery providers who have granted us permission to use their content for OSM contributions (many of which will be available as background layers in OSM editing tools) or your own on-the-ground research.</p>
<p>(I hope I didn't misunderstand your question; correct me if I did.)</p>
