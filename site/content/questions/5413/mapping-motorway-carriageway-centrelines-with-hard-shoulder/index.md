<div class="question-body">
<p>Where is the centreline of a motorway carrage way that has a hard shoulder? Do you just consider the normall running lanes, so for a three lane carrageway draw the centre of the middle lane. Or do you also include the hard shoulder and (in the 3 lane case) draw the line along white line separating lanes 1 and 2?<br />
With recent high resolution imagery (Bing) you can see detail down to the white line markings so this level of accuracy can easily be achieved.</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>I'd say the way tagged as <code>highway=motorway</code> should go along the center of the regular lanes so in this case along the center of the middle lane.</p>
<p>However I would suggest that you don't realign the way if another mapper used the white line between lanes 1 and 2. It doesn't improve accuracy much, is a lot of work and might annoy the other mapper.</p>
