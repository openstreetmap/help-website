<p>Tag info can be used to see what others have used, or get ideas to make a new one. This is what key= warning lists. <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/warning">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/warning</a> But Danger and Hazard could also be used with deep water or drowning risk for example. Your heading " warning = danger_of_drowning " seems good to me.</p>
