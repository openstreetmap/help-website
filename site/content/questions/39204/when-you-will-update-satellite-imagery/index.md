<div class="answer-body">
<p>OSM does not update satellite imagery, ever (for lack of satellites). We use imagery from third-party providers that have granted permission to us; Yandex and Google are not among them. In most parts of the world the best imagery available to us is either Bing or the MapBox satellite imagery, and we have no influence on when they update.</p>
