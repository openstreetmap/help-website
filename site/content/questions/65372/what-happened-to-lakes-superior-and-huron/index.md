<div class="answer-body">
<p>The relations that define the lakes were broken during the refresh of the zoomed out tiles, so the lakes didn't render. That refresh happens once a month or when a new version of the style is deployed.</p>
<p>The data has been fixed, the tiles just haven't been refreshed yet.</p>
<p>(tile update frequency per <a href="https://lists.openstreetmap.org/pipermail/talk/2018-August/081233.html">https://lists.openstreetmap.org/pipermail/talk/2018-August/081233.html</a> )</p>
