<p>Changes to map basically go through two stages, 1) the way is edited and when saved it enters the database 2) The current database is then used by a rendering tool to draw a map, different rendering tools give different styles and may include or choose to leave out some data. There may also be some delay between an edit and a render that shows your changes.</p>
