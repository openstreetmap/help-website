<p>Typical, after posting the question, I take a fresh look and find it.</p>
<p>Hover over <strong>Layer</strong> icon (top left)</p>
<p>Click pencil to <strong>Edit</strong></p>
<p>In right menu click on <strong>Advanced Actions</strong></p>
<p>Click <strong>Delete</strong></p>
