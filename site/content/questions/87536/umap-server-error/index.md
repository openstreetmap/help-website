<p><a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/23197/milkyloutch">@MilkyLoutch</a>, this is well known issue, did you changed your OSM account recently ? My guess is that that new name is the same username as your Github one. Given that OSM does not expose the email (which may be a good thing overall), uMap cannot merge automatically those accounts. So at this time, it just… crashes… I've made the merge by hand.</p>
