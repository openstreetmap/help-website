<div class="answer-body">
<p>At <a href="http://osm.org">osm.org</a>, enter into the search box<br />
<em>restaurant [Name of your town]</em><br />
The same search on <a href="http://www.openlinkmap.org/">openlinkmap</a> gives a nicer looking result.</p>
<p>I am not sure if one of this sites support geolocation by browser but on the other hand you didn't tell your hardware/software setup.</p>
