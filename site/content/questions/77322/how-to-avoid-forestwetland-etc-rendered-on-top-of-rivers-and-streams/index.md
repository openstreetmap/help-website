<div class="answer-body">
<p>Until a few years ago water bodies were mapped the way you would like to see it. Back then the maintainers of the style for the main map on www.openstreetmap.org decided to change the rendering to encourage mappers to exclude water bodies from landuse areas. A questionable direction in my opinion as I think of (at least smaller) rivers and lakes being part of a forest or wetland. Anyway, that's how it is and you may or may not follow the strategy to cut water bodies out of landuses.</p>
