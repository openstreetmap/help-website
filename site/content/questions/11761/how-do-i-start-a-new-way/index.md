<p>In JOSM, I would click on the junction node and hit G, this would unglue the roads and allow you to adjust the length and direction. To join the roads back after adjustments, make sure the roads to be joined are near each other and click J. Do play around and it would be great if you could go to cities like Berlin or Paris to see the dense roads draw by fellow users. This will allow you learn faster too.</p>
<p>Happy mapping !</p>
