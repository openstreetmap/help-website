<div class="question-body">
<p>Can two ways share a node if they do not intersect? For example, <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/2998872922">node 2998872922</a> is part of <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/26231131">way 26231131</a> and part of <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/29509614">way 29509614</a>. However, these two ways <em>do not actually intersect</em>: way 29509614 is just a bridge over way 26231131 (and the node appears at the lat-long coordinates where they cross, even though they don't intersect).</p>
<p>Is this correctly represented on OSM? Or is this an editing error?</p>
