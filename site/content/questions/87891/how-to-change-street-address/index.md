<div class="answer-body">
<p>If Zillow were willing to share their data with OSM you would be able to do it through any number of editors. As they don't appear in the Wiki it is highly unlikely that they do this.</p>
<p>Other maps normally have a copyright and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Copyright">can't be used</a> without explicit permission or if they are released under a suitable licence (or they're really really old).</p>
<p>Zillow appears to use Google maps for their base map which also <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Google">explicitly prohibits</a> the use of their maps, imagery etc. for making maps.</p>
<p>If you want to go out there and personally survey the additional addresses then you can provide that info to OSM, but other than that we're very limited in what we can use.</p>
