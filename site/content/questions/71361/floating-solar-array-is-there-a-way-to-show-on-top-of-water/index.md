<p>There are at least two other floating solar arrays mapped:</p>
<ul>
<li>Godley Reservoir (<a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/523645138">https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/523645138</a>): mapped as a 3 MW generator.</li>

</ul>
<p>Neither of these shows up on the standard Carto-CSS style, although level tags are used. Both do show up on OpenInfraMap.</p>

<p>That's not really how the "layer" tag works in the OSM Carto style (or at least wasn't the last time I looked at it).
