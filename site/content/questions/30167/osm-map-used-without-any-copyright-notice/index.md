<p>See <span>Lacking_proper_attribution</span> (you need a new account for the wiki if you want to add it to the page). However, quite easy what to do now, just continue your emailing - mail mapometer. They even use the <a href="http://tile.openstreetmap.org/7/63/39.png">osm.org tile server</a> (which is somehow okay, <span>and</span> somehow not). However, nice that they offer OSM! Use a positive tone in your email (e.g. mention this).</p>
<p>And, yes, I cannot see a attribution, too (just "© Mapometer Ltd 2013." and "using Google maps"). "OpenSM" is a quite uncommon abbreviation which should either be OSM or OpenStreetMap, but that is unrelated to the license issue.</p>
