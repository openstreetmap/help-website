<p>How are you viewing it? There should be a zoom in and out control to the top left (+ at the top to zoom in, - at the bottom to zoom out; the arrows above move the view around, or you can click and drag to pan). Double clicking should also zoom in. You should also be able to shift+click and drag a rectangle to zoom to that area.</p>
<p>But if you are viewing on a mobile device this might be different.</p>
