<div class="answer-body">
<p>Umap is not geared up for concurrent editing. You'll probbably just be overwriting each other's changes.</p>
<p>It'd be a nice feature to have, but it's also far from trivial (to do right). I'm not aware of any similar map-editing package with a concurrent editing feature.</p>
<p>Do you really need concurrent editing of the same map ? How about taking turn editing, or sharing an editing session on one (possibly remote) screen, or having each studen follow the same editing tutorial on their own map ?</p>
