<div class="answer-body">
<p>OSM doesn't have any satellite imagery of its own, but several providers have given permission to use their imagery to improve OpenStreetMap.</p>
<p>Of these providers Mapbox is the only one I'm aware of that has <a href="https://labs.mapbox.com/bites/00240/#3/0.00/0.00">an interface</a> to request fresh imagery. I think they tend to purchase in large batches, so it may be quite a while before they act on it.</p>
