<p>Roads in OpenStreetMap are classified by their importance in the road network and/or their intended use. <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/RU:Key:highway">Our wiki describes the different categories</a>. In a general map this looks like this: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=12/50.8469/24.3215">https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=12/50.8469/24.3215</a>.</p>
<p>You should describe a bit more what you intend to do. Do you need a onetime map of a specific area? Do you need a map that updates frequently? Is it something you want to share in the web or in an app?</p>
