<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/1600087">The reservoir relation</a> was edited 23 days ago with a changeset comment saying that they were correcting the broken reservoir. I downloaded the relation into JOSM and confirmed that there are no errors. The reason it isn't showing up at lower zoom levels is just that the renderer hasn't re-rendered at the lower zoom levels yet since the broken relation was fixed.</p>
