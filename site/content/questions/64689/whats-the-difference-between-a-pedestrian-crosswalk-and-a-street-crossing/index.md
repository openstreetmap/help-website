<p>I assume you're referring to the presets in iD. If that's the case, here are the tags that are applied by each preset:</p>
<p>Pedestrian Crosswalk: highway=crossing + crossing=zebra</p>
<p>Street Crossing: highway=crossing</p>
<p>These tags can be seen by selecting the preset and then expanding the "All tags" section.</p>
