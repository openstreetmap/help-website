<div class="answer-body">
<p>There have been many discussions about disputed borders over the years, one of which resulted in the official position <a href="https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/w/images/d/d8/DisputedTerritoriesInformation.pdf">here</a>.</p>
<p>There have been many discussions about how to store conflicting claims and while several competing claims may be stored in the database, what is normally shown on the "default" map is what is thought to be the extent of actual "on the ground" control of an area. People can and do make other renderings of the database with their preferred interpretations.</p>
