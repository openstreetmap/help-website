<p>Apps I have installed show the second link location:</p>
<ul>
<li>OsmAnd: ice cream cone,</li>
<li>Maps.me: a generic shop,</li>
<li>Magic Earth: generic shop.</li>
<li>Vespucci shows nothing special</li>

<p>As to the naming convention. I can see this one going either way. Ice cream parlors and kiosks tend to take a bulk item and dispense it for the individuals in a similar way to <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity%3Dfuel">amenity=fuel</a> (but tastier).</p>

Similar to the distinction between shop=alcohol vs amenity=bar.</p>
