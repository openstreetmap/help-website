<p>I believe <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/73390291">https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/73390291</a> is correctly saved in the database as a two-way road. There is no tag that would make it oneway.</p>
<p>The arrow you see in your screenshot may be from an out-of-date tile. That's probably also the reason the road near the north of your screenshot looks odd (the letters "ad" on their own) - one tile was probably not yet synced with recent changes. It all looks OK when I look at the map in my browser now.</p>
