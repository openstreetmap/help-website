<p>In some cases railway lines are converted into cycle paths and these are easy as bikerouter.de takes care of that.</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>I am not sure what you mean by a "lifted railway". I think you are referring to a former railway line where the tracks have been removed but that the right of way is still visible and it is obvious that it was previously a railway. In that case the railway lifecycle tagging would be appropriate. See: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Railways#Life-cycle">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Railways#Life-cycle</a></p>
