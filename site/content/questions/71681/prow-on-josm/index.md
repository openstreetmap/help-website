<div class="answer-body">
<p>Nick Whitelegg's <a href="http://www.mapthepaths.org.uk/">Map the Paths</a> allows direct editing of some attributes.</p>
<p>Robert Whittaker's <a href="https://osm.mathmos.net/prow/progress/worcs/">PRoW tools</a> has Worcestershire reports.</p>
<p>I'm not aware of a WMS layer, but NLS 1:25k out-of-copyright is often a good way to find paths.</p>
