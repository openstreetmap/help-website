<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you want to add your business details to OpenStreetMap, the easiest way to do it is with the dedicated site at <a href="http://onosm.org">onosm.org</a>.</p>
<p>Note that OpenStreetMap is a geographical directory of the world, not a business directory, so we can only list objective facts. So "we are a cafe, our name is Fred's Cafe, and we are located on High Street, Burnley" is good, but "we sell the best coffee in Burnley" isn't.</p>

<p><a href="https://osmybiz.osm.ch/#/18/47.40614/8.39856">OSMyBIZ</a> is a dedicated web app for adding businesses to OpenStreetMap. New (as in not present on OSM yet) business are directly added to the OpenStreetMap data, changes are currently stored as OSM Notes with structured content.</p>
<p>See <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSMyBiz">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSMyBiz</a> for more information.</p>
