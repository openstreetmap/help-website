<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are many different services hosting tiles, each with their own usage policy. Check out the wiki pages on <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tile_servers">tile servers</a> and <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Commercial_OSM_Software_and_Services">commercial service providers</a> and find their individual usage policies on their web sites. For openstreetmap.org <a href="https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/tiles/">you will indeed need permission from the system administrator</a> for heavy usage.</p>
