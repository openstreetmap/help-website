<div class="answer-body">
<p>Your changes are from Friday (2-3 days ago). Your new data is simply not included in the ORS data yet and it is likely that the Garmin maps have not been updated (which ones are you using').</p>
<p>If you check the upper right hand corner of the ORS site, you will see that the data was last updated on the 18th October (thanks to EdLoach for pointing that out).</p>
<p>Don't forget that the standard "Mapnik" style map on the main OSM site updates extremely fast and that the same cannot be expected from all other services.</p>
