<p>In OpenStreetMap ways are split whenever one of the properties of the way changes. These properties could be any number of things including: name, surface, number of lanes, whether the way is lit or whether that portion is part of a route.</p>
<p>Depending on what you're looking at the splits may be perfectly correct.</p>
