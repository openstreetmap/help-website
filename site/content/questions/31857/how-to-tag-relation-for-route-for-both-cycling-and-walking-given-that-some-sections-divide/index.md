<li>The route divides in places so hikers and cyclists are recommended to take separate paths.</li>
<li>Sections of the cycle route and joint route are rough and suitable mainly for mountain bikes (not 'cycleway')</li>

</ul>
<p>(And yes this is a signed route with website etc - not just a virtual route)</p>
