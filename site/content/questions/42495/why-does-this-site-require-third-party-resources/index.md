<div class="question-body">
<p>When I use this site, most things work fine, but I'm forced to load resources from google.com and ajax.googleapis.com for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>actual submission of questions (otherwise, nothing happens when hitting "Ask your question"),</li>
<li>tag and duplicate question suggestions, preview (just a blank space is shown in their place).</li>
</ul>
<p>Why compromise user privacy by loading stuff from Google? Surely the required resources could be stored locally on this domain. Is there a configuration option for this, or would it need to be added upstream? Does OSM maintain local changes to OSQA? Where are settings and local alterations kept?</p>
<p>(I use NoScript to see and tailor what resources are used from where.)</p>
