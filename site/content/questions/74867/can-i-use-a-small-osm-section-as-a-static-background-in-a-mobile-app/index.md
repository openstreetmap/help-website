<p>Tiles (just in the "standard" style) from openstreetmap.org are available on CC BY-SA 2.0 terms, given you are not actively downloading images (you would need to follow the relevant <a href="https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/tiles/=">usage policy</a> if you were), you simply need to follow the attribution guidelines on <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright">https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright</a> and <a href="https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Licence/Licence_and_Legal_FAQ#What_do_you_mean_by_.22Attribution.22.3F">the legal FAQ</a>.</p>
