<p>It wasn't your road - it was another one someone added elsewhere that broke the renderer.</p>
<p>You can request a rerendering of an individual tile by right clicking the map, selecting "view image", and then adding "/dirty" to the end of the URL. It'll display "Tile submitted for rendering". The time taken for the actual rendering depends on how busy things are, but right now it's pretty quick.</p>
