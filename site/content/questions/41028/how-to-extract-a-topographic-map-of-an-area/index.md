<div class="answer-body">
<p>Generally, the share (export) function on openstreetmap.org only relates to the 'Standard' style. Since other styles aren't hosted on the core OSM servers, it's not possible for the site to create the exported images.</p>
<p>If you say what area you're interested in, and provide some details about your technical skill level, people will be able to advise you further.</p>
