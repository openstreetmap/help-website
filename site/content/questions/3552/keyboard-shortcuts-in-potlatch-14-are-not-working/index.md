<p>The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Potlatch/Keyboard_shortcuts">Potlatch/Keyboard shortcuts</a> wiki page says:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>At present, shortcuts may vary or be non-functional for non-UK/US keyboards.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>I suggest you try the Potlatch 2 editor and see if that works better for you. I'm not sure it's relevant for this specific issue, but also check that you are running an up to date version of the <a href="http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/">Adobe Flash Player</a> used by the Potlatch editors.</p>
