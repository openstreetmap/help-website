<p>There are a number of companies that provide <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tile_servers">map tiles based on OpenStreetmap data</a>. Some have a free tier, most are paid. One of them will probably be able to give you what you need.</p>
<p>Alternately you can download the software to <a href="https://switch2osm.org/serving-tiles/">generate the tiles on your own server</a>.</p>
