<div class="answer-body">
<p>The last location is stored in your local browser via a cookie. The cookie refers to <em>www.openstreetmap.org</em> and its name is <em>_osm_location</em>. If you want to clear your last position <em>once</em> then delete the cookie or just browse to a different location. If you want to disallow it every time then you probably have to disallow cookies for the whole domain. This should be possible with most browsers:</p>
<ul>
<li>Firefox: <a href="https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences">Enable and disable cookies that websites use to track your preferences</a></li>
<li>Opera: <a href="http://www.opera.com/help/tutorials/security/cookies/">Cookie tips</a></li>
<li>Chrome: <a href="https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647">Manage your cookies and site data</a></li>
<li>Internet Explorer: <a href="http://windows.microsoft.com/is-is/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookies">Block or allow cookies</a></li>
</ul>
