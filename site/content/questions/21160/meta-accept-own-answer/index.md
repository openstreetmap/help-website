<p>I thank SK53 for his help, however I've come back and answered my own question.</p>
<p>This leaves me with two options:</p>
<ul>
<li>Leave it unanswered and clutter this site.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>or</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Accept SK53's answer and it is marked green thrown to the top making it harder for someone else to get the information they want.</li>

<p>Neither option seems reasonable.</p>
<h2 id="enabling-self-answering">Enabling Self Answering</h2>
<p>Pros: Less clutter and easier to find answer</p>
<p>Cons: Perhaps people can gain useless points, SO doesn't give rep for self-acceptance.</p>
<p>I submit the pros far outweigh the cons.</p>
