<p>Welcome to OpenStreetMap.</p>
<p>With respect, adding a node for an entire country really is not the sort of thing you should be doing as your first change to OSM, and trying to tell the highly experienced sysadmins about ontological correctness is not going to win you many friends fast!</p>
<p>I'd recommend you start small with some edits around your home town, and get involved with the project for a few months before making potentially troublesome changes such as this.</p>
