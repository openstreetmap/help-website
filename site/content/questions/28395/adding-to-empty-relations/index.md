<div class="answer-body">
<p>A manual download is required. Open the <em>File -&gt; Download object</em> dialog, then select <em>relation</em> as <em>object type</em> and type the relation ID into the <em>Object ID</em> field. Afterwards the relation should get listed in the relations widget.</p>
