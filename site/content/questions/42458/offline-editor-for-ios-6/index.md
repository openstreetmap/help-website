<p>I do not have an iOS device, however...</p>
<p>you may find one by trying/reading specs from this list <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Apple_iOS#OpenStreetMap_editing_features">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Apple_iOS#OpenStreetMap_editing_features</a></p>
<p>If you just want to make notes on geographical places and have a map display see <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Apple_iOS#Track_making_features">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Apple_iOS#Track_making_features</a> and sort for "geo-tagged notes" (or photos). Many map apps support that. While I am saying "photos" – as far as I know the standard Apple camera app saves your current location into a photo's meta info, so maybe just taking photos would be enough for you.</p>
