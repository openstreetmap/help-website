<div class="answer-body">
<p>This is a forum for getting practical advice, not for starting debates about design or for requesting enhancements: the mailing lists and <a href="http://trac.openstreetmap.org/">trac</a> are better for those, respectively.</p>
<p>But for what it's worth, there's simply already lots of stuff on the top and not necessarily any room for more. Two things you can do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Make sure you always log in - this removes some of the blurb from the left of the screen</li>
<li>Simply press 'Tab' in your browser to move to the first input field, i.e. the search box</li>
</ul>
