<div class="answer-body">
<p>To see the revision history of an item on the standard OSM map:</p>
<ul>
<li>Zoom in to the area of interest.</li>
<li>Select "browse map data" on the edit menu (click the little triangle to the right). Nodes will highlight in green, ways in black.</li>
<li>Click on the item that you're interested in.<br />
</li>
<li>Click "details"</li>
<li>Click "view history"</li>
</ul>
