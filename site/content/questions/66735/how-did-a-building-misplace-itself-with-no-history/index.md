<p>Spotted a strange thing today: A building, <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/250440049">way 250440049</a>, moved about 20 meters SSW, overlapping other buildings and distorting the shapes of the two buildings it's connected to on the sides, <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/250440047">way 250440047</a> and <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/250440051">way 250440051</a>.</p>
<p>Nothing too crazy about that. I've been mapping in the area so I assumed I'd selected and nudged it by accident. But there's no history of me or anyone else doing this -- all three ways are at version 1, haven't been modified since their original creation 5 years ago.</p>
<p>I swear these buildings were all correct very recently -- you can still see the proper layout in the <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=19/40.67233/-73.97696&amp;layers=TN">Transport Map layer</a>, which hasn't updated (yet?)</p>
<p>Obviously I can just push the stray building back into place, but first, what's going on here? Anyone have a clue?</p>
