<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hi <a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/13842/rdtracker">@RDTracker</a>, iD does not support direct editing of point coordinates. Here is a ticket that explains why: <a href="https://github.com/openstreetmap/iD/issues/3088">https://github.com/openstreetmap/iD/issues/3088</a></p>
<p>The main reason is to prevent people from accidentally (or intentionally) creating areas or lines that span huge areas of the map.</p>
