<p>OK, I am answering my own question, with my older account I just rediscovered...</p>
<p>"<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shared_Zone">Shared zone</a>" is apparently what a <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dliving_street">living zone</a> is called in Australia and New-Zealand.</p>
<p>So this should be tagged as <code>highway=living_street</code>.</p>
<p>I created a redirection on the OSM wiki for contributors who are in the same situation.</p>
