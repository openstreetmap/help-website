<div class="answer-body">
<p>If you've made some errors on the map, there's no need to panic, but it's best to try to get them fixed as soon as possible.</p>
<p>If you know the changeset that the changes went into, someone in the community can do a revert.</p>
<p>If you don't know, then let someone know your username, and they can dig up the changeset. It's probably best not to make any more changes until this is resolved to make the changeset easier to find.</p>
<p>This process is straightforward but can take a while, so it may be best to have someone on IRC give you a hand.</p>
