<div class="answer-body">
<p>Any remnants of the old building complex can be added as <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:historic%3Druins">ruins</a> etc., but items with no remaining traces should not be.</p>
<p>This information is welcome in <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Open_Historical_Map">Open Historical Map</a>.</p>
