<p>Hi there</p>
<p>I added some public book cases and some free boxes (give boxes). The bookcases were included into the map and visible after a couple of days. But not the free boxes. They are still not visible on the map with their icons and names. Why not?</p>
<p>When I right click on the area where it is at and then choose "Query features" then it appears on the list over items nearby. So, apparently it is there on the map, but not showing for some reason.</p>
<p>Example: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/11023089963">https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/11023089963</a></p>
<p>Even more strange is that they actually do appear on the umap over bookcases and give boxes:</p>
<p><a href="https://umap.openstreetmap.fr/en/map/give_box_406244#13/55.6956/12.5816">https://umap.openstreetmap.fr/en/map/give_box_406244#13/55.6956/12.5816</a></p>
<p>But only not on the "main" map openstreetmap.org</p>
