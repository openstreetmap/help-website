<p>Is that the correct way to tag them? Shouldn't they just be <code>natural=wood</code>, without an additional <code>leisure=park</code>? They're not individual parks after all...</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>Typically you don't nest polygons of the same type. So a park within a park is not generally accepted tagging. Both of your examples are also tagged as natural=wood which is fine, no problem with having a wood within a park. (Though I prefer landcover=trees even if that is not generally accepted nor rendered at this time.)</p>
