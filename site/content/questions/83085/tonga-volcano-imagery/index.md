<div class="answer-body">
<p>MAXAR often distributes disaster imagery <a href="https://www.maxar.com/open-data">separately</a>, nothing for Tonga yet though.</p>
<p>The first imagery available for OSM use is normally <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Sentinel-2">Sentinel 2</a> but it can be rather involved to get it into an editor as the commercial providers don't generally permit their latest processed versions to be used in OSM.</p>
<p>FWIW, JOSM is giving "Metadata Capture Date: 7/21/2010-7/21/2010" for Bing imagery in the vicinity of Hunga Tonga.</p>
