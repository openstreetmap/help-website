<div class="answer-body">
<p>As always I have to point out that this site is not the right place to ask for legal advice (which you should get from your own counsel).</p>
<p>Please refer to</p>
<p><a href="http://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/License">http://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/License</a></p>
<p>and</p>
<p><a href="http://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/License/Community_Guidelines">http://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/License/Community_Guidelines</a></p>
<p>for guidance from the licensor of OSM data.</p>
<p>Further: with the exception of self-produced tiles you will likely need to follow attribution requirements from your service provider.</p>
<p>If you have further questions please send them to legal-questions@osmfoundation.org</p>
