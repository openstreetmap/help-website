<p>Click on the map layer button to the right, this displays a sidebar with a group of three checkboxes at the bottom. The middle one controls the notes display.</p>
<p>With the checkbox set, you see the notes, when you turn off the checkbox, the markers (red with white cross) for the notes should be gone.</p>
