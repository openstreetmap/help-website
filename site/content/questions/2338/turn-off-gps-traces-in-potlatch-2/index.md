<div class="answer-body">
<p>Nothing. As yet there isn't a simple way of turning the traces off in P2 (though I think you're mistaken when you say that P1 does that; the difference is that P1 wipes out the previous load when you pan to a new area and load tracks, whereas P2 doesn't).</p>
<p>However, for now, if you do want to turn them off you can do it this way:</p>
<ul>
<li>Go to 'Vector file...' in the Background menu</li>
<li>Select the 'GPS tracks' entry</li>
<li>Click 'Delete'</li>
</ul>
