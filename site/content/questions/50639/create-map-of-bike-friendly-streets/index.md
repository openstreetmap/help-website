<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can't create a custom layer on OpenStreetMap. However you can view a bicycle map on <a href="http://www.opencyclemap.org/">OpenCycleMap</a>, but it does not highlight hills. You could download a map of your city with <a href="https://mapzen.com/data/metro-extracts/">Mapzen</a> and then use QGIS to create your own map how you like it. The data may not be extensive enough for your needs however.</p>
