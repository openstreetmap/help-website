<p>Finally! I found the way :-) Ok, here are the steps how to make the happy marriage of 2 different worlds:</p>
<ol>
<li>create a route in Google Maps for example</li>
<li>export it to KML</li>
<li>use GpsBabel to convert KML into GPX</li>
<li>use FTPServer to upload GPX into /scard/osmand/track</li>
<li>run OsmAnd</li>
<li>define view / GPX / select your uploaded GPX track</li>
</ol>
<p>Voila. You will see your track over OSM map.</p>
