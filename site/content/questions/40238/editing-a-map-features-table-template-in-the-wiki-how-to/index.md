<div class="answer-body">
<p>There is a link to help you with editing just below the long table …</p>
<ol>
<li>follow it (then you are at the template's page)</li>
<li>click "edit" (at top of the page – those on the page or from the template's docu)</li>
<li>add your entry (<span>example</span>) (alphabetic ordering seems to be useful)</li>
<li>use the preview (since editing tables easily leads to errors because of their syntax)</li>
<li>save</li>
</ol>
<p>General help about editing templates is there: <span>Help:Template</span> (<span>quick version</span>). And <span>there about tables</span>.</p>
