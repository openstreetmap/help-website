<td><div class="item-right">
<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are two schemes in OpenStreetMap to tag cycle roads. Basically, you tag the road with the usual highway classification like <code>highway=residential</code> and then you additionally add <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:bicycle_road"><code>bicycle_road=yes</code></a> or <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:cyclestreet"><code>cyclestreet=yes</code></a>. In Germany predominantly the former is used, in the Netherlands and Belgium the latter. You might want to read around the respective wiki pages a bit to see what fits better in Austria. Looking at taginfo I think bicycle_road is more popular in Austria.</p>
