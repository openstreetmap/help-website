<p>It is probably just your browser caching the map tile images. You may not have zoomed all the way in before you edited the name so when you zoomed in after editing your browser fetched the new image but lower zooms are being displayed from your cache.</p>
<p>Try forcing a refresh in your browser (usually shift-F5) or clearing your cache and then reloading the page.</p>
