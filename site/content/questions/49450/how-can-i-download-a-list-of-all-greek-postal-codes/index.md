<p>I fear that this information is not yet in OpenStreetMap. I just ran some Overpass Queries. The <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/fVG">first one</a> learned me that not all admin boundaries of level 9 are mapped yet. With the <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/fVG">second</a> one, I cannot find any postal code boundaries in Greece at all.</p>
<p>Without the boundary information of postal codes you cannot associated a street with a postal code. In some countries, postal codes (or ZIP codes) do not represent an area at all. I don't know which system is used in Greece.</p>
<p>I also wonder why you need a text file. You need a file that describes areas and properties of those areas, examples are shapefiles or GeoJSON. With a file that just lists postal codes without additional information you won't be able to associated it with street and cities</p>
