<div class="answer-body">
<p>The <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:building">building tag</a> clearly needs to be tagged to a building. It should either be <code>building=yes</code> or reflect what the building was built do be, what it looks like. It could be something like <code>building=house</code>, <code>building=cabin</code> or <code>building=bungalow</code>. And you are right the tourism tag should then either be tagged on an individual building or an individual building including its grounds or to the perimeter of a group of buildings if they belong to each other in some kind, not on all of those options.</p>
<p>If the individual buildings have some kind of reference add a <code>name</code> or <code>ref</code> tag to them.</p>
