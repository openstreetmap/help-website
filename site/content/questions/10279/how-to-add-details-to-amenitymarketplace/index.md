<p>How can one add more details to a marketplace? The tag amenity=marketplace does not say anything about the things one can buy there. I would like to add information for someone who e.g. "wants to buy meat", which cannot be accomplished on every marketplace. The Wiki does not give any info on further defining them.</p>
<p>is it ok to use it in combination with shop=*? Then one can even add different sections of markets (like greengrocer, butcher, clothes, car parts etc.) and group them with a site relation to one big market.</p>
<p>e.g. amenity=marketplace shop=butcher name=Khortsis Bazroba (=meat market)</p>
<p>Here in Georgia, there are many markets selling all kinds of things, and they are an important place for people buying their everyday things and more.</p>
