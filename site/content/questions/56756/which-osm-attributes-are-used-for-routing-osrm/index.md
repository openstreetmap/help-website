<div class="answer-body">
<p>OSRM will use the tags specified in the profile that you pass to osrm-extract. The size of the predefined profiles, like <a href="https://github.com/Project-OSRM/osrm-backend/blob/master/profiles/car.lua">car.lua</a> sort of limits the usefulness of this answer, but the most important keys for routing are <code>highway=*</code> and <code>access=*</code>. You can see that lots of other tags are also accounted for, but those are by far the most important ones.</p>
</div>
