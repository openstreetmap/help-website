<p>I think the postcodes for The Netherlands are imported into Nominatim and are not part of the OSM data, see <a href="http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128717184">http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128717184</a> . This means that the data cannot be updated by a mapper. You have to contact the Nominatim maintainers.</p>
<p>If you compare this with a Belgian postcode, see <a href="http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128098718">http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=128098718</a> , you will notice that there is a boundary relation with a clickable link.</p>
