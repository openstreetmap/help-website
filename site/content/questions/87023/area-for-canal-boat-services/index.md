<p>I recently made some edits at a marina I am very familiar with:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/134256512">https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/134256512</a></p>
<p>and being my first change, I marked it for review.</p>
<p>However, now that it has been "applied", I notice that the presence of Great Haywood Marine Services has vanished from the displayed map. Therefore, I assume that "boatyard" is not the correct type for their building.</p>
<p>Is "boatyard" supposed to be an area of open land rather than a building? What type should I be using?</p>
<p>Note that the building is capable of housing a boat, but also contains a small office and store room. Neighbouring the building is a white poly-tunnel where boats get sand blasted.</p>
<p>Thanks.</p>

<p>Note that changesets are always immediately "applied". Marking it for review doesn't have any effect on this. It is just an invitation for other mappers to have a look on it. Whether this happens or not, and when, doesn't change anything.</p>

<div class="answer-body">
<p>I think the <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:waterway%3Dboatyard"><code>waterway=boatyard</code></a> would generally be the entire facility where they work on or store the boats and in many cases would include quite a large area where boats are stored 'dry'.</p>
<p>A building over the water where boats are stored "wet" would probably be <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:building%3Dboathouse"><code>building=boathouse</code></a>, but in this case I suspect it <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:building">might be</a> a generic <code>building=industrial</code>.</p>
