<div class="answer-body">
<p>The styles are for use with OpenMapTiles vector tiles.</p>
<p>To use them you need a source of such vector tiles, and a method to serve them to your application/browser (typically vector tiles are converted to images client-side, that is in your browser or app).</p>
<p>Googling for vector tiles will return a lot of material that doesn't make sense to repeat here.</p>
