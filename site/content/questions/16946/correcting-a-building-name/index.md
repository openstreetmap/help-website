<div class="answer-body">
<p>Login to OSM, zoom to the place that needs changing, click the edit tab, make your change and save it. OSM is all about people doing exactly what you describe, looking at an area they know and making it as accurate and up-to-date as they can. If you are sure there is an error, change it. If you are wrong, you, or the next person to spot it, will correct it later. Be bold; make the change.</p>
