<p>Plans can be changed during construction. If you can see the working areas and planters, fine to add <code>construction:amenity=bench</code>, <code>construction:natural=tree</code>, and <code>construction:leisure=playground</code> I guess. Only add the <code>opening_date=</code> on the <code>landuse=construction</code> + <code>construction:leisure=park</code> encompassing them, to simplify your work.</p>
