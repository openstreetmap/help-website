<p>I have been taking photos and videos with my mobile phone while surveying. I don't have a matching GPS trace; at most there might be Wi-Fi triangulation for the beginning of the video. It's hard to find things in the video afterwards, and even harder to find which of the many videos covers a certain area...</p>
<p>Is there any tool that will let me manually add geolocation to different points in the video, and show it as a trace on the map? Ideally it'd allow clicking on the route to jump to the matching part of the video too.</p>
