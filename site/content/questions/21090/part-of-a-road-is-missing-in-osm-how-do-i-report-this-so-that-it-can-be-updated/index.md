<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hi Lee, you can report the missing road (use a meaningful description) with osm.org's "<span>Notes</span>" (or <a href="http://www.mapdust.com">MapDust</a> or in former times with the Notes predecessor <a href="http://openstreetbugs.schokokeks.org">OpenStreetBugs</a>). Sooner or later a OSM volunteer will fix it.</p>
<p>However, if you can spare a bit more time please <em><span>add the road yourself</span></em>! OSM's data can be edited by everybody. The maps will be updated (some minutes to weeks) automatically soon after your data edit.</p>
</div>
