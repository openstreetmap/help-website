<div class="answer-body">
<p>In JOSM you can download the area of interest, search for objects with the tag "addr:postcode"=21709, change the postcode to 21850 then submit the change.</p>
<p>That type of bulk edit can be dangerous and is often frowned upon, so double and triple check that what you are doing is correct.</p>
