<div class="answer-body">
<p>A <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/heb">search</a> for one of the largest such chains in the UK suggests <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=tool#values">shop=tool_hire</a> as probably the most popular value (excluding hardware, doityourself, etc.). For <a href="http://overpass-turbo.eu/s/hec">Sunbelt Rentals</a> itself, previously mapped ones seem to have been mostly hardware or doityourself.</p>
<p>I had a look at all of the values used for shops in that space (in the UK) a few months ago, and for map rendering purposes <a href="https://github.com/SomeoneElseOSM/SomeoneElse-style/blob/master/style.lua#L1035">grouped them together</a>.</p>
