<div class="answer-body">
<p>The minutely replication system that the rendering server uses to keep up with edits seems to have stopped working. See the graph here: <a href="http://munin.openstreetmap.org/openstreetmap/yevaud.openstreetmap/replication_delay.html">http://munin.openstreetmap.org/openstreetmap/yevaud.openstreetmap/replication_delay.html</a></p>
<p>Once that line goes back to zero your road will render. I have no idea why it stopped.</p>
