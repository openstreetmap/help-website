<div class="answer-body">
<p>There are several alternatives already in use for that situation but after researching taginfo I decided to go with</p>
<p>amenity=weigh_station for the node (or way) where the weighing device is and</p>
<p>service=weigh_station for the segment of service road associated with it.</p>
<p>As an American the term weigh_station seems more popular than either weigh_bridge or weightbridge. Note too, that all those choices are in use with and without the underscore character connecting the two words.</p>
<p>YMMV,</p>
<p>Dave</p>
