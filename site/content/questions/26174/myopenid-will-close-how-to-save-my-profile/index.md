<div class="answer-body">
<p>Login to <a href="http://www.openstreetmap.org">http://www.openstreetmap.org</a>, click on your username on upper right corner and choose "My settings". There you can select new OpenID (created first at another provider, of course) and/or change your email/password which you can use for logging in. You need to do that ASAP while your myOpenID still works, of course.</p>
