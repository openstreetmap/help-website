<div class="answer-body">
<p>It sounds like you need to combine the 2 ways into a single way. In which case:</p>
<ol>
<li>Select one way</li>
<li>Hold down <code>Ctrl</code>, and click on the other way</li>
<li>Click on the <code>Merge ways</code> icon in the toolbox (or press <code>J</code>)</li>
<li>Both ways should be combined, so should make the entire boundary of the area</li>
</ol>
