<div class="answer-body">
<p>It <a href="http://www.navmii.com/support/faq#q4">seems</a> that you can download the maps (<a href="http://www.navmii.com/GPSNavigation/Maps">which are created by NavFree but based on OpenStreetMap data</a>) somehow inside the app. However our <span>OSM wiki article about Navfree</span> says: "Maps can be purchased (U.S. and UK are free of charge) from Navmii and stored locally on the device." You <a href="http://www.navmii.com/support/faq#q3">cannot use OSM data directly in Navfree</a>.</p>
<p>Also see <a href="/questions/10603/">i-am-using-navfree-and-china-isnt-on-the-maps-that-i-can-downloadare-china-maps-available</a> and <a href="/questions/10583/">Is it possible to get map of Morocco for my Navfree &amp; how to get it?</a>.</p>
