<div class="answer-body">
<p>OSM normally uses British English; "railway_car" is an American rather than an English term.</p>
<p>The English version term a passenger railway car would be "railway_carriage", which there is some use of:</p>
<p><a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=railway_carriage#values">https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=railway_carriage#values</a></p>
<p>(compare with <a href="https://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=railway_car#values">railway_car</a>)</p>
<p>While "historic" might work for some many will not be; they'll just be old. Also some "stationary railway vehicles" would be something other than cars/carriages.</p>
