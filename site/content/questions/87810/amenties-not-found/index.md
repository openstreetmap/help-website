<p>These two are totally different things. The "wiki" in --import-from-wiki is <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Nominatim/Special_Phrases">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Nominatim/Special_Phrases</a> which is a list of special search terms maintained by the OSM community, whereas the "Wikimedia-importance" is derived from data maintained by the Wikimedia/Wikipedia community, outside of OSM.</p>
