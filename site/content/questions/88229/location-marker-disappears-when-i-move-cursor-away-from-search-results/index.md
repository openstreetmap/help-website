<div class="answer-body">
<p>You just click on the result link that you had been hovering on. Then the map will center on that location. Then you could use the share function from the menu on the right, choose "include marker" to get a share url. This one will show a map with a marker, e.g.:</p>
<p><a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/?mlat=50.0725&amp;mlon=14.4102#map=16/50.0725/14.4102">https://www.openstreetmap.org/?mlat=50.0725&amp;mlon=14.4102#map=16/50.0725/14.4102</a></p>
