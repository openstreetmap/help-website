<p>These cameras likely use a standard GPS chipset but the results will vary. My experience with one was that the accuracy was comparable to a handheld GPS when it got a fix. But the time to obtain a fix from power on would be up to 15 minutes. Also when taking a series of photos while walking around with the camera GPS enabled, the update rate was unacceptable; a photo might be stamped with the previous location even though there was a movement of several meters.</p>
