<div class="answer-body">
<p>OpenStreetMap contributor, emacsen, just announced a new series of OSM videos.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>In honor of OSM's birthday, I present to the OSM community a new series of videos on OpenStreetMap.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCE6296A33CF47955&amp;feature=mh_lolz">http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCE6296A33CF47955&amp;feature=mh_lolz</a></p>
<p>They're a set of Khan Academy style videos. That is they're each short 5-15 minute videos on a particular topic of OpenStreetMap. Right now there's about two hours of video, covering the basic concepts: all the way from nodes and tags, to routing, changesets and rendering.</p>
</blockquote>
