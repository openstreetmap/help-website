<div class="answer-body">
<p>In Potlatch 2, a gas station is indicated the the small gas pump icon in the "Transport" category.<br />
</p>
<p><img src="http://i.imgur.com/Ipbun.png" alt="amenity=fuel in potlatch2" /></p>
<p>In JOSM, follow the Presets » Transport » Car » Fuel, also indicated by a small gas pump icon.<br />
</p>
<p><img src="http://i.imgur.com/b16TM.png" alt="alt text" /></p>
<p>If you are doing things the manual way, use key:amenity; value:fuel to add a gas station to OpenStreetMap.</p>
