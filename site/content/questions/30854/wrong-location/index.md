<div class="answer-body">
<p>I guess you speak about your "Home Location" in your OSM user profile.</p>
<ul>
<li>go to "My settings" screen (click upper right button with your user name, then "My settings")</li>
<li>scroll down and find the small map with a marker on your location</li>
<li>select the box "Update home location when I click on the map?"</li>
<li>click on the small map the new location</li>
<li>save</li>
</ul>
