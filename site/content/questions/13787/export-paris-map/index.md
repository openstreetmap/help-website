<p>You don't actually tell us what error(s) you are getting, but a wild guess on my behalf would be that the problems are due to the large amount of data present in the area.</p>
<p>A possible solution would be to render the area yourself. For example with Maperitive (<a href="http://maperitive.net/)">http://maperitive.net/)</a> or TileMill (<a href="http://mapbox.com/tilemill/).">http://mapbox.com/tilemill/).</a> The former will work directly with a city level extract of OSM data.</p>
