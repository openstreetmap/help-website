<p>Hi Cascafico, it depends on the editor you’re using. JOSM offers you a range of 7 or more views, but even they differ in view. I always survey and take some ways or crossings as references to check the alignment with a GPS, surveying is the best way to improve OSM to be a better image or map with greater and more details. If you re using areal views, there is only one option check them to find the best fit. But if you can’t find any sufficient image to control, just leave it as is and wait for later accurate data.</p>
