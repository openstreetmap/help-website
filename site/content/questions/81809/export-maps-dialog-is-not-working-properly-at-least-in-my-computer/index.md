<p>The Export dialog is for exporting raw data, not images.</p>
<p>You need the Share dialog - there should be an icon in the list at the right of your screen. This gives you an option to share as an Image, which in turn offers a number of formats incluidng PNG.</p>
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Export">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Export</a></p>
