<div class="answer-body">
<p>Objects with <code>craft=*</code> tags are not specifically rendered on the standard OpenStreetMap-Carto style. Your node <em>does</em> show up on the map, as "8570", its house number. If the brewery sells beer for consumption on the premises then I guess it would be acceptable to add an <code>amenity=pub</code> tag.</p>
<p>Potential future rendering of <code>craft</code> icons is discussed here <a href="https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto/pull/3126">https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto/pull/3126</a> and apparently is looking for someone to work on it.</p>
