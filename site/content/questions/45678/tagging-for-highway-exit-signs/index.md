<p>For mapping highway destinations/exits see:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:destination">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:destination</a></li>

<li>As always: look at existing highway objects at other places (well mapped places) to see how other mappers handled this situation there.</li>
</ul>
