<p>Hello,</p>
<p>There are a lot of amenities that are not on the surface. Shops, offices, restaurants, cinemas... they could all be in street underpasses, subway stations and other kinds of underground tunnels.</p>
<p>The first question is how to determine the exact location of these objects... GPS is not reliable underground. However photo-tagging, getting the tunnel construction plan (if available to general public) are all good to go.</p>
<p>The second question is how to tag such amenities? What to add so it will be clear that the amenity is underground? <em>underground=yes</em> ? First I though of <em>layer=-1</em>, but this is more for rendering purposes.</p>
<p>Any ideas here?</p>
</div>
