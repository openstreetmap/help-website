<div class="answer-body">
<p>Combinations of different barriers are pretty common near me. Personally, I'd just pick the most appropriate one, i.e. if something's a hedge and a wall, but "more like" a wall, I'd add barrier=wall and ignore barrier=hedge. You could do something similar for fence type. A quick look at taginfo suggests that semicoloned values <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/fence_type#values">aren't much used</a>.</p>
