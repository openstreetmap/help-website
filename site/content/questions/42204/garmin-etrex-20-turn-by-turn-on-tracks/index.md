<p>Try to convert your <em>track</em> to a <em>route</em>, e.g. by using gpsbabel. For <em>routes</em> your Garmin device will provide turn by turn navigation. However calculating a 200 km route on a Garmin Etrex will take ages, better split up the track into several sections. And try to reduce the number of points per route, gpsbabel can also help you with that.</p>
