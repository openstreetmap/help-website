<div class="answer-body">
<p>On <span>openstreetmap.org</span>, if you click on the stack of books icon at the right of the map, tick the “Map data” box (<span>help on this</span>) and then select the Southampton to East Cowes ferry route you get a <span>listing of the object</span> that shows that it is a member of the relation “NCN National Cycle Route 23”. You can add further ferry routes (or roads or paths) to cycle route relations by the “add to relation” command that editors generally provide.</p>

<p>Finally I found the solution (in Potlatch):</p>
<ul>
<li>select existing ferry route;</li>
<li>click Advanced properties;</li>
<li>click Add to..;</li>
<li>select NCN route (I'm guessing this is listed because it's nearby);</li>
<li>click Select;</li>
</ul>
<p>and the NCN route is added, and will hopefully show up on the updated map in a few days...;</p>
