<div class="answer-body">
<p>Hello,</p>
<p>For the second one, if you <a href="http://nom.gpslogistic.net/nominatim/reverse.php?format=xml&amp;lat=43.46841&amp;lon=24.1625754&amp;zoom=18&amp;addressdetails=1&amp;accept-language=bg-BG">append "&amp;accept-language=bg-BG" to your URL</a>, then the names will appear in the original Bulgarian (except the country code, which is always in ASCII by definition).</p>
<p>For the first problem, I think it might be because Sofia doesn't have a boundary line at the moment that indicates exactly where the city limits are, so Nominatim doesn't know which points are in Sofia the city and which aren't. There's <a href="http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?pid=462119">a conversation going on right now on the forum</a> about adding that boundary, and I very much want to create it soon, exactly for this reason.</p>
<p>I hope this helps.</p>
