<p>OK so your question is not so much "how can I display Thai names in English on the main map" (answer: you can't) as "how can I easily load an area I'm viewing on thaimap.osm-tools.org into JOSM" (answer: right click on Permalink, "copy link location", go to JOSM's download dialog, tab "Bounding Box", paste permalink, click download). You could also ask Stefan to add an "edit in JOSM" link which would only require a small change to his web site.</p>
