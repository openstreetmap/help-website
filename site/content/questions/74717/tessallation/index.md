<p>No, there is currently no way to get ways as polygons, tesellated or not, without you doing the processing.</p>
<p>What you see in the tiles, depend on rules in the "style" that is used to create a bit mapped image from the data, and width etc of the lines is determined rather late in the process (so current implementations of vector tiles wouldn't help you either).</p>
