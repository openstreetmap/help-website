<div class="answer-body">
<p>Probably you mean the scale of the map in the upper left corner which currently displays in "Nautical" units in your JOSM. To change try one of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>in the JOSM settings (F12): third tab ("map settings") → first tab → last setting.</li>
<li>right click the ruler box (if you have it there) in the status bar of JOSM and change to "metric".</li>
</ul>

<p>Apparently "kbl" is the German abbreviation for the measurement unit "cable" (1/10 of a nautical mile), and is used as the smaller-scale unit when JOSM is set to the Nautical Mile system. That is, if the scale is showing distances less than a nautical mile, it displays it in cables or "kbl".</p>
