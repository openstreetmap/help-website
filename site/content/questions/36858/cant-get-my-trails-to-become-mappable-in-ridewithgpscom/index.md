<p>If the trails show up at some zoom levels and not at other zoom levels it just means that the tiles at some of the zoom levels have not been updated yet. Less information is rendered at zoomed out levels too so the trails will not be visible at lower zoom levels . The maps on the app you are using will have updated maps eventually so you may like to check their update policy. I would wait a day or so and check the osm again.</p>
