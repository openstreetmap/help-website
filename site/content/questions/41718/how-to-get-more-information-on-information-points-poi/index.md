<div class="answer-body">
<p>Let's imagine you've got something you want to look at, like here:</p>
<p><span>www.openstreetmap.org/#map=19/53.15148/-1.47073</span></p>
<ol>
<li>Click the question mark at the right.</li>
<li>Click near the thing you're interested in.</li>
<li>A list of features near your click appears at the left - click the one that you want.</li>
</ol>
<p>That gets you to <span>here</span>, which in this case includes opening hours.</p>
