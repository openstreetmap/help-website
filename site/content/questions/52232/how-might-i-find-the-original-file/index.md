<p>Geofabrik sells <a href="http://www.geofabrik.de/data/shapefiles.html">continent-wide shapefiles</a>. You might also want to check <a href="http://learnosm.org/en/osm-data/getting-data/">learnosm</a> and <a href="http://openstreetmapdata.com/data">openstreetmapdata</a>, but it doesn't look like they have Europe-wide files. Or you could <a href="https://duckduckgo.com/?q=europe+shapefile">search for "europe shapefile"</a> and you'll find a lot of hits, although most of them won't be OSM data.</p>
