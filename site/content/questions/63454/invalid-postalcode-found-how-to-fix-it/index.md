<p>Hi,</p>
<p>go to <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/">https://www.openstreetmap.org/</a> and enter “Moreton View Tower and Villas” into the address search bar. It will take you to this building on the map.</p>
<p>Press the “Edit” button at the top of the screen and follow the instructions.</p>
<p>If you don’t want to do all that for some reason you can also use the button to the right of the map to add a note to the map. Please explain what needs to be changed and why and some friendly mapper will take care of it.</p>
