<p>This and other imagery is already available for OSM. See Grant's <a href="https://blog.openstreetmap.org/2013/01/01/new-aerial-imagery-south-africa/">diary post</a> from 2013 and the <a href="https://osmlab.github.io/editor-layer-index/">browsable map</a> of the Editor Imagery Index (ELI). These should be available natively in editors which use ELI.</p>
