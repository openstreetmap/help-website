<p>hi moraes,</p>
<p>If the nodes carry the same lat lon they are really at the same spot. The renderer has to make a choice which of the nodes gets visible. Don’t change or mess with the lat lon values just to get them visible. You’re not supposed to tag for the renderer, just to make things visible.</p>
