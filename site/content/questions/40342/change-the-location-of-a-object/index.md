<div class="answer-body">
<p>Register on OpenStreetMap if you haven't already: <a href="https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new">https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/new</a></p>
<p>If you have a username, log in.</p>
<p>Find the place of your business, and click Edit.</p>
<p>If your business is represented by a dot, just click it and move to the place where you want it to be.</p>
<p>If your business is on a building, remove the tags of your business on that building, and add them to a building where your business is now.</p>
<p>Click Save two times.</p>
