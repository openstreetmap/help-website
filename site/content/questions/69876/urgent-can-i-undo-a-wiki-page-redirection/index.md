<p><a href="https://help.openstreetmap.org/users/13210/babri">@babri</a>: Do you want the page title to appear as کوهنوردی or Fa:کوهنوردی? In either case, you need to move Fa:Hiking to that new title. (The option to move a page is available from the "More ▽" menu on the top right of the wiki page.)</p>
<p>You are correct that there needs to be a redirect from Fa:Hiking to that new page title. This is required for the language selector to work. The wiki will generally create such a redirect automatically once you move the page.</p>
