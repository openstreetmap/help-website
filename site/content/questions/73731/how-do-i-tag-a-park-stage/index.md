<p>Probably <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:leisure%3Dbandstand"><code>leisure=bandstand</code></a> with <code>building=roof</code>.</p>
<p>I think <code>theatre:type=open_air</code> would be a bit more substantial/permanent structure both for the stage and the seating.</p>
