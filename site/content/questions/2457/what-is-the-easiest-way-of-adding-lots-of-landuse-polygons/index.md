<div class="answer-body">
<p>In JOSM, you can achieve that with some tricks:</p>
<ol>
<li>select the coast way and copy it in the clipboard</li>
<li>create a new layer (menu File), paste the content of the clipboard in this new layer</li>
<li>select this copied path the two junction points</li>
<li>split the path (key P)</li>
<li>select the path you need and copy it in the clipboard</li>
<li>switch back to your orginal layer, and paste the content of the clipboard</li>
<li>you can now join this copied path with your cliff border</li>
<li>run the validation tool and fix the duplicate nodes</li>
</ol>
<p>It's a little bit tricky, but I haven't found another way to properly duplicate the wanted path.</p>
