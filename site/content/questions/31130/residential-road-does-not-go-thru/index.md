<div class="question-body">
<p>There is a road in my town that shows up on the map as a thru road from one intersection (intersection 1) to the next intersection (intersection 2). That is not the case.</p>
<p>In the midpoint between intersection 1 and intersection 2 is a permanent barrier (a guardrail) that prevents a person attempting to navigate thru.</p>
<p>The break or barrier on this road can be seen on the satellite image, but the road layer is drawn as a thru road.</p>
<p>What is the best way to illustrate or change this issue on the map using the tools available?</p>
<p>Please note, I am new to OpenStreetMap and attempting my first correction.</p>
