<p>Hi there's no legal way to shortcut a mapping by copying those maps. Please read the legal rules for OSM at <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal#Copyright_violation">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal#Copyright_violation</a> The presented maps are usable as an overview and backup, plaese make a picture or 2 just to remember the situation. But you got to see and note, picture, or map every shop in the mall yourself by using a GPS if possible or any other means. Since you’re using JOSM you even could measure the front of a shop and draw it up to 0,02 m.</p>
