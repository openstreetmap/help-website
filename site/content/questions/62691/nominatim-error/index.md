<p>That message suggests that you're actually using IPV6, whether you are intending to or not. You're getting a hard fail after 10 seconds, not a "go away" message. I'd suggest the routing / name resolution settings etc. at your end are the problem.</p>
<p>However, as Richard says, it's definitely worth asking yourself whether nominatim.openstreetmap.org is what you should be pointing an app at. Why did you choose that instead of another nominatim provider?</p>
