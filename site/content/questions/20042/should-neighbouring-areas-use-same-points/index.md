<p>For your <em>second question about two different landuse areas with a street between them</em>, please see: <span>is-it-okay-if-landuse-borders-share-nodes-with-streets-rivers</span>.</p>
<p>The arguments given therein (and in the similar/duplicate) linked questions should make the answer to your <em>first question (two landuse areas without a street between)</em> nearly clear. I do not know how up to date this is.</p>
