<p>The place to log bugs is <a href="https://trac.openstreetmap.org/">trac</a>. Your login there is supposed** to be the same as your login for the main <a href="http://osm.org">osm.org</a> site, and <a href="http://forum.osm.org">forum.osm.org</a> (only the wiki is different). Presumably you've tried the "Forgot your password" link? Did you perhaps use OpenID (perhaps associated with your Google account) to sign up?</p>
<p>** but if you can't login here, you may not be able to there.</p>
