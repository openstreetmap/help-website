<p>If you are using Leaflet.js, you could use maxBounds.</p>
<p>Example:</p>
<pre><code>var boundedMap=L.map....
boundedMap.setMaxBounds([[34.161818,-118.591919],[33.683211, -117.734985]]);</code></pre>
<p>to restrict a map.</p>
<p>See: <a href="https://leafletjs.com/reference.html#map-setmaxbounds">https://leafletjs.com/reference.html#map-setmaxbounds</a></p>
