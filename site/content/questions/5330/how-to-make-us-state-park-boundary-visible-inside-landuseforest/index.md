<div class="question-body">
<p>I have mapped a US state park that is mostly a wooded area. The park boundary has these tags:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>boundary=national_park</p>
<p>admin_level=2</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This renders as a green dashed line.</p>
<p>Most of the park is wooded, however in many places the wooded area extends outside the park boundary, so I have added another area tagged as:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>landuse=forest</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This is rendered as a green area which makes it impossible to see the park boundary.</p>
<p>Has anyone else experienced this? is there a way to make the rendered map more useful by showing the park boundary?</p>
<p>Larry</p>
