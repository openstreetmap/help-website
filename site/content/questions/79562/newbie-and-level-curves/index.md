<div class="answer-body">
<p>I think you might be talking about height contours. These are not in OpenStreetMap at all. You have to generate them from other data sources. How exactly you would do that and what tools you would use depends on what you need to achieve. Various people have documented their approaches on the wiki e.g. here <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Contour_relief_maps_using_mapnik">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Contour_relief_maps_using_mapnik</a> - there is even a tool that will generate something that looks like OSM data from SRTM: <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Srtm2Osm">https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Srtm2Osm</a></p>
