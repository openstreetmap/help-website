<div class="answer-body">
<p>You need to add the missing section of the hiking trail back as a relation member.</p>
<ul>
<li>No need to draw another path, just put the existing one in.</li>
<li>If the osm way is longer than the trail section, split the osm way (by clicking on a node and selecting the scisors).</li>
<li>To add the way to the relation, select the way and click on the '+' at the very bottom of the left panel, and choose the hiking relation.</li>
</ul>

<p>By definition, if a cut is needed (because the attributes change, a route relation connects there, etc), you have to do the cut.</p>
<p>Otherwise, it's better not to cut (or even to join) the ways, unless the way is very big (the threshold varies and is very subjective, but often around 1000 nodes or 50km).</p>
