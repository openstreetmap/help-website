<div class="answer-body">
<p>You can add <code>designation=public_bridleway</code> to the bridleway section in addition to any other <code>highway=*</code> tag to show the legal status of that section. The <code>highway=bridleway</code> tag is just a shorthand way of showing a track that's designated a bridleway.</p>
