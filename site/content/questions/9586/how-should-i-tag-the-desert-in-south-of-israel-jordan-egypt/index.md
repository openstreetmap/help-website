<p>That proposal looks good to me (altough I'm not sure why they use "type=" instead of "semi_arid=").</p>
<p>On <a href="http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/search?q=desert">taginfo</a> you'll find 99 occurences of "natural=desert" and 20 of "desert=*". That may not be very widespread, but to me it looks plenty enough to use.</p>
