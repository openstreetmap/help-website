<p>Hi.</p>
<p>When you choose a background layer from the menu on the right side of the screen, while editing, and click save, that background will become the new default background of your map.</p>
<p>The icon <img src="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/w/images/thumb/1/1d/Icon-Background.png/30px-Icon-Background.png" alt="background" /> is the same than in the other menu but with permanent effect.</p>
