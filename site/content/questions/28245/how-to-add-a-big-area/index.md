<p>If you still wanted to use Potlatch 2, then you could draw portions of the border, and add them to a relation for the refuge. Probably it'd make the most sense to make it a type=multipolygon relation, and add relevant tags to the relation. On each way, you'd add them to the relation, and give them a role of outer (or inner).</p>
