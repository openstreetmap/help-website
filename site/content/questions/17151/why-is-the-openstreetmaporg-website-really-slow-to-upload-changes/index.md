<div class="answer-body">
<p>The speed of the upload depends on many other factors, like your available bandwidth, the network "distance" from you to the OSM servers, the size of the changes being uploaded, the load on the OSM servers (it is said that on Sunday afternoons they get congested...), etc.</p>
<p>A few of my largest changesets (~8k items hand traced from Bing imagery) took more than 10 minutes to upload...</p>
<p>I would consider normal to wait a few minutes for any larger changeset to upload, except when errors are returned.</p>
<p>What is "takes forever" to you?</p>
