<p>In JOSM editor you can load geotagged photos and it will place them where they were taken. You can sequence through the in the order they were taken. Depending on how closely spaced the photos are or not you might be able to reconstruct your travel route.</p>
<p>I don't know if the iD editor has that capability or not.</p>
