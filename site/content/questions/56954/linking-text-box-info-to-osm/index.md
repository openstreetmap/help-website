<div class="answer-body">
<p>While it is not completely clear what you want to do, I suspect you could submit your address data to a <a href="https://github.com/komoot/photon">Photon</a> instance and display your mouse pointer of whatever at the location of the best match (Photon will return the results as geo-json whcih should be simply to process in JS).</p>
<p>Note: address coverage in OSM varies wildly with country and region, you will need to check if it is sufficient for your purposes regardless of the technical implementation.</p>
