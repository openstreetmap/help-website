<div class="answer-body">
<p><a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:access"><code>access</code></a> isn't suitable since it defines legal restrictions for modes of transport. Instead use <a href="https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:min_age"><code>min_age</code></a>, for your specific case <code>min_age=18</code>.</p>
